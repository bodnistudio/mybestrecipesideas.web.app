##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Carrot Banana Smoothie:

1. Get 11/2 cup chopped carrot
1. Prepare 1/4 cup grated coconut
1. Take 1 ripe banana chopped
1. Prepare 2-4 Dates as per your sweetness level or 1/4 cup sugar
1. Take 1 cup milk
1. Take 1 cardamom
1. Take 1/2 cup water to adjust consistency




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Healthy Carrot Banana Smoothie:

1. In a Mixer jar or Blender, add all the ingredients and grind to coarse mixture without adding milk.
1. Then add milk and blend well. Add water if required to adjust consistency.
1. You can add sugar to replace Dates if you want. Add Ice cubes and blend again.
1. Now the Delicious and healthy Carrot Banana Smoothie is ready to drink.
