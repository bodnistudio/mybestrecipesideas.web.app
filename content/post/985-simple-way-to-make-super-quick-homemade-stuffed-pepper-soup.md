##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:

1. Get 1 lb sweet Italian sausage or meat of your liking
1. Prepare 1 onion diced
1. Take 3 whole peppers of your liking
1. Prepare 2 box sometimes 3 of chicken broth or stock
1. Prepare 1 large Can crushed tomatoes
1. Prepare 1/2 cup rice sometimes 1 cup depending how thick you want it
1. Prepare  grated parmesan cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:

1. Preheat oven to 350. Clean and half your peppers, roast then until tender about 15 to 20 min.
1. Cook your meat on the stove top in a pot. Add onion to meat while cooking. drain meat leaving a little bit of the liquid for flavor.
1. Add chicken broth and bring to a slight boil, then turn down to simmer.
1. Cook your rice according to the package then add to the pot as much or little as you want the more you add the thicker it will become more like a stew than a soup.
1. Pour the whole can of crushed tomatoes in the soup and stir everything together.
1. Take peppers out of oven and cut up or dive up and add to the pot.
1. Tho it off with parmesan cheese and serve.
1. Use salt and pepper as desired
