##### The ingredients needed to make Polish Beef Pasta:

1. Make ready 1 packages Wheat pasta noodles
1. Make ready 1 lb Ground beef
1. Make ready 1 cup Sliced polish sausage
1. Take 1 cup Shredded cheese
1. Get 1/2 cup diced onion
1. Get 2 clove sliced garlic
1. Take 2 can Ragu traditional sauce
1. Make ready 3 dash pepper, salt, garlic, dill weed, and onion powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Polish Beef Pasta:

1. Bring 5 cups of water to boil an add pasta
1. In 8&#34; skillet brown ground beef
1. Add onion an seasoning to ground beef once its has absorbed all of its own grease.
1. Add polish sausage and a about 1/2 cup ragu sauce to meat mixture an cool down until sliced onions are tender.
1. Drain pasta and add meat mixture
1. Turn to medium heat and add both Cans of ragu (high enough to see bubbles through the sauce)
1. Add shredded cheese an cover. Turn off the flames, serve and enjoy!!!!
