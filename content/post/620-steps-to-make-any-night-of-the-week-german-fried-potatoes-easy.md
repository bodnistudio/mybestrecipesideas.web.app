##### The ingredients needed to make German Fried Potatoes (Easy):

1. Take 3 lbs Red Creamer Potatoes
1. Get 1 Apple
1. Make ready 1 Med. Sweet Onion
1. Make ready 4 Slices Bacon
1. Make ready  Caraway Seed
1. Make ready  Salt and Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make German Fried Potatoes (Easy):

1. Slice potatoes, 1/4 inch or less. Halve onion and then thinly slice each half. Core apple and slice into 3/8 inch wedges. Chop bacon into small pieces.
1. Place potatoes in bowl, cover with plastic wrap and microwave for 9 minutes. While potatoes in microwave, cook bacon in large cast iron skillet.
1. Put cooked potatoes in skillet with bacon and toss to coat with bacon fat. Add Caraway, salt, and pepper to taste.
1. Add apples and onions, reduce heat. Turn mixture infrequently until potatoes are brown, onions are carmalized and apples are soft.
1. Ready to serve!
1. Ready to eat!
