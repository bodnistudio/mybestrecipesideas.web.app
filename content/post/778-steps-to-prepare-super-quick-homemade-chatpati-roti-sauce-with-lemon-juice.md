##### Steps to make Chatpati roti sauce with Lemon juice:

1. Collect the ingredients.Wash and cut the mix vegetables.
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, add cumin seeds. Lets it crackle, add onion, saute for few seconds, add mix vegetables.
1. Add salt, black pepper, mix well. Cover it and cook for five minutes.
1. Add tomato sauce, red chilli sauce, mix well. Cook for few seconds. Turn off the flame. Stuffing is ready..
1. Spread tomato sauce on roti, fill the stuffing in the middle. Roll it. Chatpati roti is ready.
1. For lemon juice...Squeeze lemon for juice.
1. Take a bowl, add 4 tbsp powdered, 2 tbsp lemon juice, half teaspoon black salt and 1/4 tsp salt.
1. Add 2 cups water. Mix well. Serve in cups and decorate with sliced lemon.
1. Chatpati roti sauce with lemon juice us ready to serve. Enjoy!!!!!
