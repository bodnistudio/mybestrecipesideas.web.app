##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage Meatball Sandwich w/Balsamic Glaze:

1. Prepare 3 Bianco and Sons, Garlic and Cheese Sausage
1. Take 1 cup Italian seasoned Bread Crumbs
1. Prepare 1 tsp chopped Garlic
1. Get 1/4 cup chopped fresh Basil
1. Get 1/2 cup Freshly grated Parmesan Cheese
1. Take 2 eggs
1. Take 2 tblsp of extra virgin olive oil
1. Prepare 12 Cherry tomatoes
1. Prepare 1 Bottle Balsamic Vinegar Glaze
1. Prepare 12 Slices Fresh Mozzarella cheese
1. Get 4 hoagie rolls
1. Get 1 Tbsp chopped fresh Basil for topping
1. Get 1 Tbsp chopped onion




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sausage Meatball Sandwich w/Balsamic Glaze:

1. Take sausage out of casings and crumble in a medium bowl, add, bread crumbs, cheese, basil, olive oil and eggs mix to combine and make golf size meatballs, you can place in oven, grill or fry in a pan,
1. While meatballs are cooking skewer tomatoes and brush with oil and place on Grill or put in oven till softened but not mushy
1. Take hoagie rolll or whatever roll you like and toast slightly, slice or keep meatball whole. Put tomatoes on roll then mozzarella then meatballs sprinkle some sluced fresh basil and swirl balsamic glaze on top,,enjoy. ðŸ˜Š
