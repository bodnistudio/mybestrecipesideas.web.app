1. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1. Get 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1. Take 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1. Prepare 6 cloves garlic, minced
1. Prepare 2 cans (10.5 ounce) cream of mushroom soup
