##### The ingredients needed to make MacDaddy&#39;s Chicken and garlic mushroom Pot pie:

1. Make ready 3 large chicken breasts
1. Get 2 cans cream or chicken soup
1. Get 1 basket pre sliced musbrooms
1. Prepare 1 bag diced hash brown potatoes
1. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
1. Make ready 2 tbsp copped garlic
1. Take 1/4 cup milk
1. Prepare 1 cup sour cream
1. Make ready 1 garlic salt and pepper to taste
1. Take 2 pies crusts top and bottom
1. Make ready 1 bag of diced frozen carrots and peas




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make MacDaddy&#39;s Chicken and garlic mushroom Pot pie:

1. Cut 3 chicken breasts into quarter inch square pieces and cook in frying pan. Add garlic salt if you so desire.
1. Saute diced onion diced garlic and mushrooms in large frying pan you can use butter or olive oil.
1. Remove cooked chicken chunks and add frozen diced hash browns to the pan and cook throughly.
1. Add soup and frozen veggies to mushroom onion garlic mixture and simmer on low heat.
1. When potatoes are done add them along with chicken milk and sour cream and mix thoroughly.
1. Preheat oven to 375
1. Spoon mixture into two pie crusts and cook for 30 minutes or until pies are nice and brown
