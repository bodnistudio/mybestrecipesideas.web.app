##### The ingredients needed to make Steamed Eggs:

1. Make ready 3 large eggs
1. Prepare 100 ml water (warm)
1. Get 100 ml Low fat Milk
1. Make ready 50 ml Condensed Milk
1. Make ready 1/8 tsp Black Pepper Power
1. Get 1/5 tsp Salt
1. Prepare  Sprinkle of Chicken stock granules
1. Take  Fried garlic with oil
1. Get  Spring onions, chopped
1. Prepare  Chinese celery, finely chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Steamed Eggs:

1. Crack eggs into a bowl.
1. Beat lightly and and water, low fat milk and Condensed milk into the bowl. Beat and mix well.
1. Add seasoning. Salt, Black pepper, Chinese celery and the chicken stock.
1. Used a heatproof bowls when you steam the eggs. Cover the top with a wrap.
1. Steam for 30 minutes or so.
1. When the eggs is ready sprinkle the fried garlic with oil on top of the eggs together with spring onions.
