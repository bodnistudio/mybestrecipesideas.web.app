##### The ingredients needed to make Espresso Crusted Chicken:

1. Make ready 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1. Make ready 1/2 C espresso powder
1. Prepare 1 t fresh thyme; minced
1. Make ready 1 t ground cardamom
1. Prepare 1/2 t ground celery seed
1. Take 1/2 t ground coriander seed
1. Make ready 1 T turbinado sugar
1. Take 1/2 C honey
1. Prepare 1/4 t onion powder
1. Make ready 1/4 t garlic powder
1. Prepare 2 t brown sugar
1. Take  evoo; as needed

##### Instructions to make Espresso Crusted Chicken:

1. Toss chicken with enough oil to coat. Season with everything except honey and turbindao sugar. Bake at 350° for approximately 45 minutes or until thermometer reaches 165°.
1. Pour honey atop chicken and sprinkle turbinado sugar during last 5 minutes of baking.
