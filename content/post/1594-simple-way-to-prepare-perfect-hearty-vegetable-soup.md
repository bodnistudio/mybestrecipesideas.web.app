##### Instructions to make Hearty Vegetable Soup:

1. Bring a pot of water to boil and add vegetables, garlic, salt and pepper. Allow to boil for 2 hours, topping with water as needed.
1. Add in chopped parsley and celery and continue to boil for another 1-2 hours. Partially liquidize soup with a hand blender to make a semi-smooth texture.
1. In a separate pot, boil soup mix till lentils/barley are soft. Add to soup.
1. Mix soup powders with water to make a smooth paste and add to soup. Allow soup to thicken slightly (desired thickness).
