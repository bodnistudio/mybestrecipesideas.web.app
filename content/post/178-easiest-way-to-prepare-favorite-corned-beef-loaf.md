##### The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef Loaf:

1. Make ready 1 can corned beef
1. Take 1 head garlic chopped
1. Get 2 onions, sliced
1. Get 5 eggs, beaten
1. Get 5 tbsp flour
1. Get 1 tsp cornstarch
1. Prepare  salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Corned Beef Loaf:

1. Saute&#39; onion, garlic and corned beef. Add salt and pepper to taste. Let it cool.
1. Mix egg, flour, and cornstarch. Beat well. Add salt and pepper according to your taste. Then fold mixture into corned beef.
1. Put in a loaf pan, grease pan with oil first before putting the loaf mixture. Bake for about 25 mins at 300°F. Serve with bread.
