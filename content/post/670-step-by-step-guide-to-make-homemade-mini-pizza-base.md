##### The ingredients needed to make Mini Pizza base:

1. Take 2 cup maida/All purpose flour
1. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Get 1 tsp baking powder
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 1 cup whisked curd
1. Make ready As needed Water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mini Pizza base:

1. In a large bowl put maida + salt + baking powder + baking soda + sugar and mix it.then in center put curd + 1 tsp oil and mix very well with hands and by adding water knead a soft dough.
1. Apply 1 tbsp oil to the dough and wrap the bowl with sealing wrap and keep aside for 30 mins.
1. Remove sealing wrap and again knead dough for 5 mins.divide the dough into 10 equal balls and place into plate or tray and keep aside for 5 to 8 mins.
1. Roll it with rolling pin or with hands by litely pushing.make like a small thick paratha.noch with fok.
1. Take a deep bottomed pot put some salt and preheat it for 10mins.then place rack. On rack put plate and on plate place your base.cover the pot and cook for 5 mins on low flame.after 5mins remove your base and put another one.similarly bake all the base.
