##### Instructions to make Instant Pizza:

1. In a bowl take flour along with all dry ingredients &amp; knead with curd only.Cover the dough with muslin cloth and rest for 15 min.
1. Till then preheat a pan for 8-10 min at medium flame with salt and put a stand into this.
1. Knead the dough one more time and Divide it into 2 parts.Roll the dough as how thick you want the base. Put the base in a greased plate and bake in pan for 8-10 min.
1. Apply pizza sauce over the base then spread cheese.Now put veggies with some salt, black pepper and alots of cheese.Now put it in a pan and cover until the cheese melt.
