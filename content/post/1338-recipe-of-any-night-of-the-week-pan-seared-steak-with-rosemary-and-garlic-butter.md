##### The ingredients needed to make Pan-Seared Steak with Rosemary and Garlic Butter:

1. Prepare 200 g steak 1 1/2 inches thick
1. Get 3 cloves garlic finely diced
1. Take 2 rosemary sticks
1. Get 3-4 tablespoons butter
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon olive oil optional
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon white wine
1. Prepare to taste salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pan-Seared Steak with Rosemary and Garlic Butter:

1. Rub the steak with some salt and pepper. Heat the oil in a sauce pan and brown the steak on both sides. Wipe off the excess oil.
1. Add the butter, rosemary and the garlic and let the steak cook to your desire, rare, med rare or well done.
1. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
1. Enjoy
