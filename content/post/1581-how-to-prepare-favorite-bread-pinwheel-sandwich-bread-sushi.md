##### The ingredients needed to make Bread pinwheel Sandwich/Bread Sushi:

1. Make ready 12 slices wholemeal bread
1. Take 12 tsp butter
1. Prepare 12 slies cheese
1. Take 1/2 cucumber cut lenghtwise
1. Take 1 small carrot sliced lenghtwise with slicer
1. Prepare 1/4 beet root cut into thin strip
1. Prepare 1/2 cup lettuce shredded
1. Make ready 1/2 cup mixed salad leaves washed. (Spinach, kale, rocket leaves)
1. Make ready 1/4 red bell pepper cut into thin strips

##### Instructions to make Bread pinwheel Sandwich/Bread Sushi:

1. Wash, cut different veggies into stips lenghtwise. Cut the edges of the bread.
1. Roll the bread very thin with a rolling pin. Apply butter to the bread slices.
1. Put cheese slice, shredded lettuce, cucumber strip, red bell pepper strip, carrot slices, beetroot strip, In some bread slice I have also placed the salad leaves.
1. Roll the bread tightly into a log. Cut into slices and serve.
