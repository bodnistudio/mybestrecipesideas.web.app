##### Steps to make Banana Ice Cream:

1. Get your ripe bananas, peel them, and slice them into smaller pieces.
1. Spread the banana pieces on a plate so that they don&#39;t overlap. Put it in the freezer until the banana has frozen (approximately 2 hours).
1. After the banana has frozen put it in the food processor and blend until it becomes a smooth puree.
1. If you want, you can add whatever flavour you want and blend it in.
1. Put the mixture in a plastic container and place in freezer for an hour.
