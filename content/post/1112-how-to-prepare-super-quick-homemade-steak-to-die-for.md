##### The ingredients needed to make Steak to Die For:

1. Get 2 sirlon center cut steaks
1. Get 1 salt&amp;pepper as desired
1. Get 1/3 cup soy sauce
1. Take 2 cup plain steak sauce
1. Get 1 cup greek salad dressing
1. Make ready 1/2 can mushrooms, drained

##### Steps to make Steak to Die For:

1. tenderize steaks with a fork by stabbing the meat several times all over. this is my secret!
1. lightly salt &amp; pepper steaks before sitting them aside. whisk together all your liquids.
1. in a baking dish, pour a thin layer of marinade, then put the steaks on top. pour in the rest of the marinade &amp; refrigerate for 2-3 hours
1. after marinading for the FULL 2 HOURS AT LEAST, remove steaks from fridge &amp; sear in skillet/grill over charcoal to desired temperature. IMPORTANT: if you would like to use the marinade as a sauce, be sure to put it in the skillet &amp; heat at least until bubbling to kill the raw meat bacteria! if you do so, this is when to sautee your mushrooms. enjoy.
