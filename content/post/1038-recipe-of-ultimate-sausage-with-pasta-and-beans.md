##### Steps to make Sausage with Pasta and Beans:

1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
1. Spray large skillet or soup pot lightly with oil. Brown sliced sausage on medium high heat until both sides are browned. Drain grease.
1. Turn heat down to medium.
1. Add tomatoes, green beans, kidney beans, and mushrooms and/or black olives, if desired. Stir to combine ingredients.
1. Cook uncovered about 5-10 minutes, or until mixture is heated through, stirring occasionally.
1. Add pasta and toss gently.
1. Top with cheese. Cover and leave on heat until cheese melts slightly.
1. Refrigerate leftovers.
