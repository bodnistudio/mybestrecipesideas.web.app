##### The ingredients needed to make TL&#39;s Rice Krispie Treat Birthday Cake Batter Cookies:

1. Take 1 packages instant vanilla pudding (3.4 oz)
1. Get 1 1/2 stick butter
1. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
1. Take 2 eggs
1. Take 1 tsp vanilla
1. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
1. Take 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1. Prepare 3 cup rice krispie treats cereal
1. Make ready 1/4 cup assorted color jimmies or sprinkles




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make TL&#39;s Rice Krispie Treat Birthday Cake Batter Cookies:

1. In bowl beat butter and sugar until fluffy. Add pudding mix. Add eggs and vanilla. Keep mixing until well blended.
1. Add flour and baking  soda. When all mixed nicely add rice krispies and the sprinkles.
1. Heat oven to 350degrees F. Place cookies 2 inches apart on greased or parchment lined sheets. Bake about 8-10 min. Enjoy!
