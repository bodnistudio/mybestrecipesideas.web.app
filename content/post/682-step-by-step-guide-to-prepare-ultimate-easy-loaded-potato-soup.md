##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Loaded Potato Soup:

1. Get 30 oz Bag frozen hash browns, squares
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Chopped onion
1. Get 2 can chicken broth (14.5 oz)
1. Take 1 can cream of chicken soup
1. Prepare 1 packages cream cheese, softened
1. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper, to taste
1. Prepare 1 Shredded cheese
1. Take 1 Cooked, crumbled bacon
1. Take 1 Green onion




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Loaded Potato Soup:

1. Combine all ingredients (except cream cheese, shredded cheese, bacon, and green onion) into crockpot.
1. Mix well and cook on low 5-7 hours
1. Cube and add cream cheese. Cook about 30 minutes.
1. Stir well until soup is smooth.
1. Spoon into bowls and add toppings such as shredded cheese, bacon, and green onion
