<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 6-7 cups of thinly sliced and peeled apples. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of packed brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 teaspoon of cinammon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tablespoons of lemon juice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Pie Crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 cups of all-purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 teaspoon of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2/3 cup of shortening. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 5-6 tablespoons of ice water. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3 tablespoons of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tablespoons of fresh milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tablespoons of granulated sugar. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In a large bowl, combine the apples, sugar, water, cinnamon and nutmeg; toss to coat. Let stand for 15 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt; cut in shortening until crumbly. Gradually add water, tossing with a fork until dough forms a ball. Divide in half, making one half slightly larger..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			On a lightly floured surface, roll out larger portion of pastry to fit a 9-in. pie plate. Transfer pastry to plate; trim even with edge of plate. Add filling; dot with butter. Roll out remaining pastry; make a lattice crust..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Trim, seal and flute edges. Brush with milk; sprinkle with sugar. Cover edges loosely with foil..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 400° for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350°; bake 40-50 minutes longer or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly. Cool on a wire rack..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
