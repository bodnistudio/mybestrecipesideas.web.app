##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Baked Salmon:

1. Take 4 salmon filets  (1 lb total weight)
1. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp seasoned salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp dill weed dried
1. Prepare 2 tsps cooking oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Baked Salmon:

1. Preheat oven to 450° F.
1. In a small bowl, mix all seasonings together.
1. Place fish, skin-side down, in a lightly greased baking pan. Lightly brush with oil. Sprinkle seasoning mixture evenly over fish.
