##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Thin Crust:

1. Make ready 100 gr flour
1. Get 1 tsp yeast
1. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
1. Get  Water
1. Make ready  Topping:
1. Take  Tomato sauce (ketchup)
1. Get  Chinese cabbage (chopped)
1. Take  Saussages (chopped)
1. Get  Corn
1. Make ready  Meat (minced)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pizza Thin Crust:

1. Mix yeast, sugar and little bit water to activate the yeast
1. Mix flour, activated yeast, a pinch of salt and water. Then make dough. Then wrap the dough
1. Rest the dough for about 1 hour or more in warm place
1. Add little bit oil or butter on pizza pan
1. Roll the dough into round shape then add tomato sauce, add chinese cabbage, meat, saussages, corn.place in pan or oven (i used pizza pan). Bake for about 15 minutes
1. Serve it
