##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Potato &amp; Veggie Skillet:

1. Take 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp of bacon fat (or olive oil)
1. Take 5 Or 6 medium potatoes, small to medium dices
1. Get 1 Bell pepper, diced large
1. Make ready 4 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
1. Make ready 1 1/3 cup mushrooms, sliced thick
1. Take bunch parsley, chopped (for garnish)
1. Get 1 onion, chopped
1. Take  salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp dried basil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Potato &amp; Veggie Skillet:

1. Heat bacon fat (or oil) in cast iron skillet. Add your onion and garlic, sautee until translucent.
1. Add your mushrooms, cook for 3 min.
1. Add your potatoes, and cook for 5 min on medium-high. Season with salt and pepper and basil.
1. Turn the heat down to medium-low, add your diced peppers and let cook on medium-low  for about 30 min.
1. Stir occasionally until potatoes are cooked through and mushrooms are deep brown.
1. Add chopped parsley and serve.
