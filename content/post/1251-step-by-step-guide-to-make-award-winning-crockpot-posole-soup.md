##### The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Posole Soup:

1. Get 2 lb Beef Chuck Roast
1. Make ready 5 cup Water
1. Take 4 Ancho Chiles (Seeds and Stems Removed) Can be found dehydrated and packaged this is what I used.
1. Prepare 1 1/2 Onion (Medium Sized; 1/2in Diced)
1. Get 3 1/2 Garlic Cloves (Good Sized)
1. Take 2 tsp Salt
1. Take 1/4 tsp Cumin
1. Make ready 1 tsp Mexican Oregano
1. Take 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
1. Make ready 29 oz Canned Yellow Hominy; Drained
1. Prepare 15 oz Canned White Hominy; Drained
1. Prepare 1 can Small Salsa Verde (Green Sauce)
1. Prepare 3 Avocados
1. Take 1 head Cabbage
1. Get 1 bunch Radishes
1. Take 3 slice Lemons (Wedge Sliced)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Crockpot Posole Soup:

1. Add Water (Add a little extra if needed) and Beef Roast to the slow cooker, Cover on high for 3 hours.
1. At the end of 3 hours, take out the Roast and add the salt, Mexican oregano, cumin, black pepper, Salsa Verde, ancho chiles, garlic,and onion to the water.
1. Turn the slow cooker to low, cover, and continue to simmer on low for 3 more hours.
1. Meanwhile, when the Roast is cool enough to handle, chop it up into regular-sized pieces and reserve it to add back to the soup later.
1. After the 3 hours on low have elapsed, fish the chiles out of the broth and add them to a blender with a 1/2 cup of broth from the crock pot.
1. Blend until smooth (about a minute or so) and add back to the crock, along with the Roast Meat.
1. Lastly, Dump in your cans of hominy, season to taste (I added Garlic Salt to taste), cover, and let it all warm up another 20-30 minutes before serving. (Or until preferred heat temperature)
1. For Garnish; Cut up the Avocados into long slices, Cabbage, and Radishes and put whatever you like on your soup bowl, Then top with a squeezed wedge of lemon juice. Enjoy! :)
