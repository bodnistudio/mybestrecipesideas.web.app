##### The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Taco Soup:

1. Get 2 can Mixed beans (kidney, black, pinto)
1. Make ready 1 Tomato diced
1. Make ready 1 can Sweet corn
1. Make ready 1 can Vegetable broth
1. Get 4 clove Garlic chopped
1. Make ready 1 Celery stalk diced
1. Make ready 2 medium carrots diced
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Sweet onion diced
1. Take 3 Green onions diced
1. Prepare 1 Handful cilantro chopped
1. Get 1 Sour cream
1. Get 1 Blue corn chips broken into small pieces
1. Get 4 tbsp Mrs. Dash fiesta lime seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vegetarian Taco Soup:

1. Mix together all ingredients except sour cream and chips in large pot.
1. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
1. Place soup in bowl and add a spoonful of sour cream (or to taste).
1. Garnish with chips and fresh cilantro.
