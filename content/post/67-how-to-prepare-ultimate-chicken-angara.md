##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Angara:

1. Take 350 gr chicken (with bones curry cut pieces)
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp coriander seeds
1. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
1. Take 10 peppercorns
1. Get 3 cloves
1. Prepare 1 cinnamon stick 1
1. Get 2 1/2 cardamom
1. Take 2 dry red chillies
1. Take 125 grams curd
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste
1. Prepare 1 tomato
1. Prepare 1 onion (fried)
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp red food coloring
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp oil
1. Get 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Angara:

1. Dry roast 1 ½ tsp coriander seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 10 peppercorns, 3 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 ½ cardamom and 2 dry red chillies on a pan for 1 minute.
1. Grind to make a fine powder.
1. In a large bowl take 125 grams of curd and add 1 ½ tsp Ginger Garlic Paste and the grinded powder and mix well
1. Now take a chopped fried onion and a tomato and grind to make a paste.
1. Now marinate the Chicken with the ½ tsp Salt, curd mixture and ¼ tsp Red food colour
1. Cover and set aside for 1 hour.
1. After an hour heat 1 ½ tbsp oil and add the marinated chicken do not add the marinate.
1. Cook on high flame for 5 minutes and then add the marinate.
1. Cook for 5 minutes on medium flame and add the onion and tomato paste
1. Add 1 tsp of Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder and cook for 2 mins on medium flame.
1. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes
1. Serve hot and enjoy.
