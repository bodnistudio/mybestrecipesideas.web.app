##### The ingredients needed to make Rich Tomato Soup:

1. Take 4 Tomatoes big
1. Make ready 1 Carrot
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
1. Make ready  Bay leaf 1
1. Prepare 1 Black cardamom
1. Make ready Cloves 4
1. Make ready 6 Black pepper
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Get 1 tbsp Cornflour
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sugar
1. Make ready 4 cups Water
1. Get 4 tsp Cream fresh for 4 cups
1. Get 2 tbsp Bread croutons




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Rich Tomato Soup:

1. Cut tomatoes and carrot in pieces.Put in a cooker with 2 glasses of water and all the sabut spices and salt. Add ginger and garlic paste.
1. Boil it 4 or 5 whistles.Cool down and seive.
1. Take out all sabut masalas and discard them. Grind tomatoes in a mixie.
1. Mix with seived water and keep for boiling.
1. Mix cornflour in 2 tbsp water and add to the boiling soup.It will become thick.Now add black pepper to taste.
1. Pour into bowls.Decorate with bread croutons and cream.
