##### The ingredients needed to make Double Chocolate Espresso Cookies:

1. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
1. Get 1/4 cup coconut flour
1. Take 1 tsp baking powder
1. Take 1/4 cup coconut flakes
1. Take 5 tsp powdered instant espresso
1. Prepare 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 2 sticks butters, softened
1. Prepare 2 tbsp vanilla extract
1. Get 1 tsp coffee extract
1. Take 1 cup white sugar
1. Get 1 cup packed brown sugar
1. Get 2 large eggs, beaten to a fluff
1. Make ready 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1. Take 2 cups chocolate chips
1. Get 2 cups powdered sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Double Chocolate Espresso Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 375. Mix the flour, baking powder, coconut flakes, espresso powder and salt in a bowl and set aside.
1. Combine butter, cocoa, vanilla and coffe extracts, eggs and both sugars. Mix with a hand or stand mixer until you get an even consistency.
1. Stir flour and chocolate chips in small batches, making sure to distribute the fiest batch evenly before adding another.
1. On a greased cookie pan lined with parchment paper distribute cookies (about 1 tbsp dough per cookie)
1. Cook in oven for 10 minutes
1. Remove and top with powdered sugar
