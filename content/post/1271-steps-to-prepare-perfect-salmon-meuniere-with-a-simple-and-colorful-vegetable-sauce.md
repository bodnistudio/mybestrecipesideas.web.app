##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Meunière with a Simple and Colorful Vegetable Sauce:

1. Take 2 pieces Raw salmon
1. Prepare 1 Plain flour
1. Prepare 1 Butter
1. Take  ~Vegetable Sauce~
1. Take 1/2 tbsp ★Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp ★Mayonnaise
1. Make ready 1/3 cucumber Cucumber
1. Prepare 1/2 Red bell pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Salmon Meunière with a Simple and Colorful Vegetable Sauce:

1. Coat the salmon in flour.
1. Cook the salmon in a preheated pan coated with oil. Once one side has turned golden brown, add butter to taste, flip the salmon over, and cook until it has cooked all the way through to the center.
1. Prepare the sauce while the salmon is cooking. First, finely chop the cucumber and paprika peppers, and lightly squeeze out the water.
1. Add ★, soy sauce, and mayonnaise, and mix thoroughly together. Put it on top of the cooked salmon and it is done!
