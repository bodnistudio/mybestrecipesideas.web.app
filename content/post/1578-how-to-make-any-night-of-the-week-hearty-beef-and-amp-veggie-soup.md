##### The ingredients needed to make Hearty Beef &amp; Veggie Soup:

1. Prepare 1 1/2 lbs stew meat
1. Get 2 large garlic cloves, minced
1. Take 3/4 cup red wine
1. Get 3/4 cup marsala
1. Get 2 tsp olive oil
1. Get 8 oz canned mushrooms, drained
1. Take 14 oz can diced tomatoes, undrained
1. Make ready 14 oz beef broth
1. Prepare 8 small potatoes, unpeeled, scrubbed, cut into wedges
1. Take 16 oz baby carrots
1. Take 2 stalks celery, cut into small pieces
1. Get 2 tbsp water
1. Prepare 1 tbsp cornstarch




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Hearty Beef &amp; Veggie Soup:

1. Combine stew meat, garlic, red wine, marsala, &amp; olive oil in a gallon ziploc bag. Close bag. Shake or squish around. Refrigerate for 2 hours to overnight.
1. Place beef mixture, mushrooms, &amp; tomatoes in Dutch oven or large pot. Add beef broth. Stir together. Bring to a boil over high heat. Cover. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 1 hr.
1. Add potatoes, carrots, &amp; celery. Cover. Simmer for 30 minutes.
1. Remove beef &amp; vegetables from pot with slotted spoon into a large bowl. Cover &amp; set aside.
1. Stir water &amp; cornstarch in small bowl until smooth. Pour cornstarch mixture into pot. Increase heat to medium. Cook &amp; stir until sauce is slightly thickened.
1. Return beef &amp; vegetables to sauce. Cover. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 30 minutes. Serve.
