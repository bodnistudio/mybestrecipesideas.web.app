##### The ingredients needed to make Mug Cake with ice cream:

1. Take 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1. Prepare 1/4 cup white sugar
1. Get 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1. Make ready 3 tablespoons milk
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons oil
1. Get 1 pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mug Cake with ice cream:

1. In a cup, mix all the dry ingredients.
1. Add milk and oil.
1. Whisk thoroughly to a batter consistency and microwave for 2mins.
1. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
