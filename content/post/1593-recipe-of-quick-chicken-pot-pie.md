<h3>Instructions to make chicken pot pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			preheat oven to 400°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			line pie plate with 1 crust.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			sauté chicken in olive oil. season with salt &amp; pepper.  lightly brown, then remove from pan &amp; chop..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			deglaze pan with sherry &amp; add onions. cook 2-3 min, then add celery, carrots, &amp; peas.  Cook 4-5 min, until carrots are just tender. stir in cheese spread &amp; return chicken to pan.  add a little milk if the mixture is too thick.  heat until warmed through..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			pour into crust lined pie plate.  top with second crust, crimping edges &amp; poking a few holes in top..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			bake in 400 oven for 30 minutes, until crust is golden.  let cool a bit before slicing.  yum!.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			substitute lightly steamed broccoli florets for the peas &amp; celery &amp; add grated pepperjack for a fun twist!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
