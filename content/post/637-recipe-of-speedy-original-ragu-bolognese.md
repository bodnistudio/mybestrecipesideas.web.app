##### Steps to make Original Ragù Bolognese:

1. Finely chop fresh bacon, onions, carrots and celery and put them in a large pan with olive oil on a low heat, lightly fry
1. Add ground beef and let it cook until it sizzles
1. Simmer with dry white wine on medium heat until reduced
1. Add tomato puree/passata, lower the heat and cook covered for 110 minutes. (If the sauce gets too dry you can add hot stock)
1. Season to taste with salt and pepper
1. Add milk and cook for 10 minutes until absorbed
