##### The ingredients needed to make Ice Cream Bread:

1. Take 2 cup any flavor softend ice cream (I used chocolate)
1. Prepare 1 cup self rising flour (not all purpose)
1. Prepare 1 chocolate chips or any other extras (I used peanut butter chips)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Ice Cream Bread:

1. pre-heat oven to 350°F
1. spray loaf pan with oil and flour
1. put softend ice cream in a bowl with the self rising flour mix until just moistend
1. add your chips or extras and stir lighty
1. spoon batter into pan, smooth out and place in center of rack until tooth pick comes out clean when inserted
1. take out and cool in pan for 5 min then take out of pan and finish cooling on a wire rack
1. cut and enjoy!
