##### The ingredients needed to make Hopscotch No-Bake Cookies:

1. Prepare 6 oz butterscotch chips
1. Take 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
1. Make ready 2 cups mini marshmallows
1. Prepare 1 cup potato sticks




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Hopscotch No-Bake Cookies:

1. In a sauce pan melt butterscotch chips and peanut butter over low heat stirring constantly.
1. Add marshmallows and potato sticks, stir to combine.
1. Drop spoonfuls onto parchment paper and let cool.
