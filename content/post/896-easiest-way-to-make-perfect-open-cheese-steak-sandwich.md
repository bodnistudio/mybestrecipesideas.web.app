##### The ingredients needed to make Open Cheese Steak Sandwich:

1. Take 1 gluten free baguette or sub roll
1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Take 1 onion sliced
1. Take 1 pepper sliced
1. Get 1 garlic clove minced
1. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
1. Prepare 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1. Make ready 250 g beef strips
1. Prepare 2 tbsps dairy free cheese grated




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Open Cheese Steak Sandwich:

1. Add the olive oil, onion, pepper, garlic, brown sugar and apple cider vinegar in a shallow pan over a medium heat - Sauté for 5 minutes - Add in the beef steak strips and saute for 4 minutes - Set aside
1. Cut the roll in half lengthways and place outside downwards on a baking sheet - Top with the steak mix and dairy free cheese - Heat under a low grill until the cheese has melted - Serve with salad and fries
