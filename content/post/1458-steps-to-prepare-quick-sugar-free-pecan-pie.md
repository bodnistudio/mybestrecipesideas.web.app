<h3>The ingredients needed to make Sugar Free Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Ingredients. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of 9 inch  pie crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cup of pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3/4 cup of splenda. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3 of eggs, slightly beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of butter, melted. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/3 cup of agave syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tbsp of flour or 1 tablespoon cornstarch. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Sugar Free Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			preheat oven to 375°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			prrriick pie crust with fork and cover edges with foil to prevent over browning.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			mix eggs, agave, splenda, and flour in medium bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			add pecans and butter , stir well.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			pour into pie shell.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			bake at 375°F for 60 minutes or until a knife comes clean when inserted.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			remove foil from edges,  bake an additional 10 to 15 minutes until edges are golden.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			cool, serve, and enjoy..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
