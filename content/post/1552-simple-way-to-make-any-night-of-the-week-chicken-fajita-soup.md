##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajita soup:

1. Make ready 2 cups chicken broth
1. Prepare 2 cups water
1. Make ready 2 cups cooked chicken breast
1. Take 1 green or red peppers cut in small pieces
1. Get 2 oz cream cheese
1. Get 1/2 cup Salsa
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup shredded cheese taco flavored
1. Prepare pinch crushed red peppers
1. Prepare pinch chili powder
1. Prepare 1/2 cup tomatoes chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Fajita soup:

1. Put all ingredients in a pot except both cheeses and heat til peppers and tomatoes are soft.
1. Next add in cream cheese and stir to mix with soup.
1. Last add in the shredded cheese. I used taco flavored cheese but u can add any that you like. Stir to make sure cheese doesn&#39;t stick to bottom of pot. Simmer 5 minutes.
1. You can garish with extra cheese or with chips and serve hot.
