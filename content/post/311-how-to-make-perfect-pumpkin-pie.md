##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:

1. Make ready 1 (29 ounce) can pumpkin
1. Prepare 1.5 cups sugar
1. Take 4 eggs
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Take 2 tsp ground cinnamon
1. Get 1 tsp ground ginger
1. Make ready .5 tsp ground cloves
1. Take 2 (12 ounce) cans evaporated milk
1. Get 2 deep dish raw pie crusts




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie:

1. Preheat oven to 425
1. Combine pumpkin, sugar, eggs. Stir in salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves. Add evaporated milk while stirring. Pour into pie shells.
1. Bake on cookie sheet for 15 minutes.
1. Reduce oven temp to 350, bake for 45 minutes.
1. Makes 2 pies.
