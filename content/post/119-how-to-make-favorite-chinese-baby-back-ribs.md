##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Baby Back Ribs:

1. Make ready 2 racks of baby back ribs
1. Prepare  dry rub
1. Get 2 cup dark brown sugar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp salt
1. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
1. Take 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 3 tsp 5-spice powder
1. Take 1 zest of 1 lime, 1 orange &amp; 1 lemon
1. Take  braising liquid
1. Prepare 2 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
1. Get 1 juice of 1 lime, 1 orange &amp; 1 lemon
1. Make ready 1/4 cup red wine vinegar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chinese Baby Back Ribs:

1. Mix rub ingredients together well. Place 2 rib racks in large roasting pan. Cover completely with rub mixture. Cover pan with foil and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight (I rub my ribs the day before I want to make them).
1. When ready to cook, bring ribs to room temperature. Pour in braising liquid and cover tightly with foil. Bake in preheated, 250°F oven, for 4 hours.
1. Remove from oven. Set rib rack on platter, and pour braising liquids from roasting pan to large sauce pan. Return ribs to roasting pan, bone side up, and place back in oven. Boil the braising liquids, stirring frequently, until reduced by half and thickened into a sauce (about 20 minutes).
1. Brush the bone side of rib racks with sauce and broil for about 3 minutes keeping an eye on them so the sauce doesn&#39;t burn. Flip ribs over and brush sauce well over ribs. Broil for 3 to 4 minutes.
