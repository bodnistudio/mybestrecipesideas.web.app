##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Ice-cream:

1. Get  Milk
1. Take  Custard powder  (vanilla)
1. Take  Sugar
1. Get  big mango cut into small cubes
1. Take  Fresh cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Mango Ice-cream:

1. Take 1/4 cup milk, mix custard powder in it and keep aside. Heat rest of the milk in a thick bottom pan. Add the required sugar to it.
1. When milk starts boiling, add custard powder mixture to it, stirring continuously. On low flame bring it to boil with constant stirring till Custard thickens. Allow it to cool completely.
1. Add the chopped mango cubes and fresh cream in a mixer and make thick puree. Add the puree to the cooled custard mixture and mix well with whisker or hand blender.
1. Now transfer it to a container. Cover it with foil paper or cover it with polythene cover and then close the lid of container. Freeze it for 3 hours.
1. Take it out after 3 hours and whip again with a hand blender. With this process, the ice-cream becomes creamy and soft. Again place it in the freezer and freeze it for 8 to 10 hours till it is completely set.
