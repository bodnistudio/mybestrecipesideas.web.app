##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Scalloped Potatoes and Ham:

1. Make ready 6 sliced potatoes
1. Get 2 can of cream of mushroom
1. Prepare 1 diced ham




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Scalloped Potatoes and Ham:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Slice potatoes.
1. Pour two 10.75 ounce or close to  cans of cream of mushroom in a pan. Fill each can 3/4 of a way with milk.  Add to cream of mushroom and stir on low until mixed well.
1. Layer potatoes add ham and pour sauce over.  Repeat until the potatoes are gone.  Top with ham.
1. Cook on 350 for 60 mins or until brown on top.
