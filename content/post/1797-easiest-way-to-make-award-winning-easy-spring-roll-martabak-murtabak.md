##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Spring Roll Martabak/Murtabak:

1. Take 8 spring rolls
1. Make ready 2 eggs (beaten)
1. Prepare 1 spring onion (chopped)
1. Take 3 saussages/meat (chopped)
1. Make ready 2 chopped cayenne pepper (optional)
1. Prepare 1 tsp buillon powder
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready  Water
1. Prepare  Vegetable oil for frying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Spring Roll Martabak/Murtabak:

1. Mix beaten egg, chopped spring onion, salt, buillon powder, cayenne pepper, saussages.
1. Heat the pan and vegetable oil then saute step 1 until cooked
1. Prepare spring roll add mixed sauted eggs as filling.
1. Glue the edges of spring rolls using water. Fold it like an envelope.
1. Fry until golden brown then serve it
