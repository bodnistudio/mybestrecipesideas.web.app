##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Sausage Shrimp Vegetables and Rice Mini Loaves:

1. Get 1 Package Bear Creek Creamy Chicken Rice Mix prepared as package instructions direct. Set aside. You will only need 3 - 4 cups of the Rice
1. Prepare 16 Medium to large Shrimp
1. Get 2 (4 oz) Salmon Filets cut into cubes
1. Make ready 2 Cooked Johnsonville Irish Garlic Sausages from a 5 pk cut into thick slices and quartered
1. Make ready 1 medium Vidalia Onion diced
1. Make ready 1 cup Green Peppers diced
1. Prepare  Dust with Garlic powder and Sweet Paprika powder
1. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
1. Get 6 Eggs beaten
1. Get 2-2 1/2 cups shredded Sharp Cheddar and Monterey Jack Cheese blend




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Salmon Sausage Shrimp Vegetables and Rice Mini Loaves:

1. Preheat oven to 400°
1. Grease a 10 x 14 inch 8 Loaf Mini Loaf Pan
1. Add about 1/2 inch of the Rice Mix for each of the 8 Loaves
1. Assemble by dispersing all ingredients evenly and in order of the ingredients list to each of the 8 loaves
1. Cook in the Preheated Oven for about 45 minutes, or until the Cheese is melted and golden brown
1. Cool 5 - 10 minutes then serve. Enjoy
