##### The ingredients needed to make Oxtail Soup in Slow Cooker:

1. Take 500 grams oxtail
1. Take 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
1. Get 3 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and chopped
1. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, smashed
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1. Get 1/4 tsp grated mace
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground coriander
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Get 2 cups beef broth (or 2 cups of water with bullion cubes)
1. Get 1 tbsp chopped celery




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Oxtail Soup in Slow Cooker:

1. Put everything except chopped celery in slow cooker.
1. Cook on HIGH for 3-4 hours or LOW 6-8 hours.
1. Set to WARM and put chopped celery about 15 minutes before serving.
