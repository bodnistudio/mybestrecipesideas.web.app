##### The ingredients needed to make Low Carb Breakfast Balls:

1. Take 2 lb ground pork sausage
1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
1. Take 3 eggs
1. Take 2 tbsp dried onion flakes
1. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 1/2 lb sharp cheddar cheese shredded




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Low Carb Breakfast Balls:

1. Mix all ingredients together with your hands or electric mixer.
1. Form into 40 or 50 balls, about 1 to 1 1/2 inch each and place on a cookie sheet.
1. Bake at 375 for about 25 minutes.
1. Let cool before storing or freezing.
1. For Scotch eggs, mold raw mixture around peeled hard-boiled eggs and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
