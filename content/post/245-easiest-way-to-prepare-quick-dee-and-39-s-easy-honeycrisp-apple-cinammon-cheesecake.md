##### The ingredients needed to make Dee&#39;s Easy Honeycrisp Apple cinammon cheesecake:

1. Take 3 packages cream cheese
1. Take 2 can sweetened condensed milk
1. Get 1 can evaporated milk
1. Make ready 1 apple
1. Take 1 cinammon
1. Get 1 graham cracker crust




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Dee&#39;s Easy Honeycrisp Apple cinammon cheesecake:

1. preheat oven to 350°F
1. in blender- blend creamcheese, condensed milk, and evaporated milk until smooth.
1. cut Apple into smaller pieces. Just small enough to blend in blender
1. pour cheesecake batter in another container. Pour back into blender 2 cups of cheesecake batter.
1. blend the apple with the 2 cups of cheesecake.
1. add a little of cinammon while blender is going.
1. pour into the crust
1. bake for 45 min- 1hr (depending on your oven)
