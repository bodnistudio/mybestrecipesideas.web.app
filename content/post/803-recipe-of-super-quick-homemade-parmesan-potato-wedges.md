##### The ingredients needed to make Parmesan Potato Wedges:

1. Prepare 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Olive Oil
1. Get 3 medium potatoes




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Parmesan Potato Wedges:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Cut each potato into 8 wedges
1. Put parmesan cheese, italian seasoning, and salt in a ziploc bag. Close bag and shake well to mix ingredients.
1. Add 1tablespoon on olive oil to parmesan cheese. Mix well
1. Toss potato wedges in 1/2 tbsp on olive oil. Add potato wedges to the bag and shake well. Make sure all wedges are coated the cheese.
1. Lay wedges flat on a pre-oiled pan.
1. Bake until potatoes can be easily pierced with a fork(About 25 mins dependin on size of wedges)
