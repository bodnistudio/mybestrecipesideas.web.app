##### The ingredients needed to make Date Filled Sandwich Choco Chips Cookies:

1. Get  For choco chips cookies :-
1. Get 1 cups All purpose flour
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Butter
1. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
1. Take 1 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Cocoa powder
1. Take 1/4 cup powdered sugar
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Liquid milk
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Make ready 1/2 cup choco chips
1. Get  For date filling:-
1. Prepare 1 cup deseeded dates
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
1. Get 4 tbsp water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Date Filled Sandwich Choco Chips Cookies:

1. Combine filling ingredients in a saucepan.
1. Cook on a medium flame until thickened.
1. Keep aside.
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
1. Combine all the dry ingredients by the sieve.
1. Beat the butter in another bowl.
1. Whisk with hand blender till it becomes soft and creamy.
1. Then add sugar and beat till it becomes fluffy.
1. Add vanilla extract and milk.
1. Next add the dry ingredients, stirring continuously.
1. Add choco chips.
1. If dough looks soft and sticky, refrigerate it about 30 minutes.
1. Place it on the cookie sheet so that they do not stick to each other.
1. Bake them for 10 mintues.
1. Let it cool into the sheet for 15 minutes.
1. Sandwich the dates between two cookies.
1. Enjoy it.
