##### The ingredients needed to make Ranch Sausage Balls:

1. Make ready 1 lb sausage
1. Prepare 1 packages 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup bisquick
1. Get 1/4 cup ranch dressing
1. Prepare 3 tbsp dried chives
1. Take 2 tbsp onion flakes
1. Prepare 2 tbsp crushed red pepper
1. Take 3 dash garlic powder
1. Take 3 dash Worcestershire sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Ranch Sausage Balls:

1. let sausage and cheese come to room temperature
1. preheat oven to 375°
1. mix all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl, you may add a few tablespoons of water or more bisquick to get mixture to the right consistency.
1. roll into walnut sized balls and place onto cookie sheets sprayed with cooking spray
1. bake for 20 minutes
1. turn oven to broil for 1-3 minutes until the tops of the balls are browned
1. cool and enjoy :-)
1. makes 4-5 dozen
