##### The ingredients needed to make American Tacos:

1. Prepare  ground turkey or black beans
1. Get  brown sugar, packed
1. Make ready  soy sauce
1. Prepare  sesame oil
1. Make ready  crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste)
1. Make ready  ground ginger
1. Make ready  olive oil
1. Take  garlic, minced
1. Take  green onions, thinly sliced
1. Take  Cooked rice, for serving

##### Instructions to make American Tacos:

1. In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, and ginger.
1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook stirring constantly until fragrant, about 1 minute.
1. Add ground turkey and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble turkey as it cooks; drain excess fat.
1. Stir in soy sauce mixture and green onions until well combined, allowing to simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes.
1. Plate and top as you wish, serve immediately with rice and Enjoy!
