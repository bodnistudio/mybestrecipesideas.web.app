##### Instructions to make Easy breakfast crescent ring:

1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
1. Scramble sausage over medium heat till done.. Drain grease
1. Add eggs and seasoning to sausage scramble till well cooked
1. Arrange cresent rolls (unrolled) with point out like a sun with widest side slightly overlapping on a baking sheet wrapped in foil
1. Cover overlapping base with eggs and sausage mixture and fold points over base to create your ring
1. Bake for 12-15 minutes enjoy!!!!
