<h3>The ingredients needed to make Coconut Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 9 inch of baked pie shell. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Pie ingredients. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 tablespoons of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 5 tablespoons of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 teaspoon of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cups of milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of egg yolks slightly beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cups of coconut. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tablespoon of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of Meringue ingredients. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 of egg whites. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 teaspoons of sugar. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Coconut Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Bake 9&#34; pie shell as directed..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In top of double boiler combine sugar, flour and salt..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add milk, and egg yolks. Stir until smooth..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add 1 cup coconut..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cook over boiling water 5 minutes or until thick. Cover reduce heat cook 10 more minutes. Remove from heat.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add vanilla and butter. Stir..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool slightly, pour into baked pie shell..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Make meringue by beating 3 egg whites until stiff, add 3 teaspoons sugar 1 teaspoon at a time and beat well after each teaspoon is added..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top filling with meringue and sprinkle with coconut..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
