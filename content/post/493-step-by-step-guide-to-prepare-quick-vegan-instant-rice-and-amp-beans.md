##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Instant Rice &amp; Beans:

1. Make ready 1/2 Can Pinto Beans (Drained)
1. Prepare 1/2 Can Diced Tomatoes (Drained)
1. Make ready 2 Cups Vegtable Broth
1. Prepare 2 Cups Instant Rice
1. Get 1/2 Tb Cumin
1. Get 1/2 Tb Onion Powder
1. Prepare 1/2 Tb Garlic Powder
1. Make ready 1/2 Tsp Salt

##### Steps to make Vegan Instant Rice &amp; Beans:

1. Tap a medium sized pot and add in Olive oil. Once olive oil is heated add in onion and Garlic. Sautee onion until they start to turn translucent.
1. Once vegtables are done being sauteed add in Pinto beans and tomatoes. Add in spices and stir until all ingredients are combined.
1. Add in 2 cups of Vegtable Broth and bring to rapid boil. Once rapid boil is reached add in 2 cups of instant rice. Stir two-three times to ensure instant rice is fully mixed with ingredients. Remove from heat and let rice sit for 5min or until thickened.
