##### The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Soup:

1. Take  Main Dish
1. Take 1 lb homemade or frozen mini meatballs
1. Make ready 3 large carrots
1. Make ready 1 tbsp dried oregano
1. Prepare 1 cup acini di pepe pasta uncooked
1. Take 1 packages frozen spinach
1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Take 8 cup low sodium chicken broth
1. Take 1 cayenne pepper to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Slow Cooker Italian Wedding Soup:

1. In a 5- to 6-quart slow cooker, combine broth, carrots, dried oregano (if using),1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Gently add meatballs.Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 6 hours or on high-heat setting for 3 hours, stirring in pasta and spinach during the last 20 minutes of cooking. Ladle into bowls. If desired, garnish with oregano sprigs. Soup will thicken upon standing.
