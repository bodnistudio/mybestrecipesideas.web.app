##### The ingredients needed to make Cold Coffee:

1. Take 1/3 cup milk

1. Get 1 tsp white (or brown) sugar
1. Get 1 tsp cocoa powder

##### Instructions to make Cold Coffee:

1. Take out blender/mixer
