##### The ingredients needed to make Mackarel Sushi:

1. Take 1 Mackerel (fillet)
1. Take  few Salt
1. Take 180 cc Cider Vinegar
1. Make ready 60 g Sugar
1. Take 10 g Salt
1. Make ready 10 cm Kombu (Kelp)
1. Make ready 200 cc Japanese rice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mackarel Sushi:

1. Remove stickiness mackerel skin by using a knife.
1. Put few salt on the dry surface of mackerel and leave 10 mins then wipe water by kitchen paper.
1. Make marinede sauce. Mix vinegar, sugar and salt well untill dissolve.
1. Place Kombu on a tray then put mackerel on Kombu and pour half of marinade sauce. At last covered by clean sheet. Please try to remove air as much as possible. Stay in the ref for more than 4 hours or you can use vacuum machine to shorter time.
1. Make rice and while rice is hot mix with falf of marinade sauce then cool it down quickly.
1. Now let&#39;s finish dish up. Place clean film on the bamboo mat. Put half of rice and mackerel then roll it up. Cut and serve it.
