##### The ingredients needed to make Masala Papad Chaat:

1. Take 3-4 moong daal papad
1. Make ready 2 chopped onion(barik)
1. Take 2 chopped tomato(barik)
1. Prepare 1 cup boondi
1. Make ready 1/2 cup bhujia
1. Get 1/4 cup pomegranate
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp lal mirch
1. Take 1 tsp chaat masala
1. Get 1 lenon juice
1. Take 1/4 cup chopped coriander




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Masala Papad Chaat:

1. First in a mixing bowl add onion, tomatoes,boondi, bhija, salt, mirch, lemon juice, some coriander.mix well.
1. Then roast papad in microwave(you can cut then roast) for 1 min.
1. Take out of it.Spread boondi mix on it. sprinkle some chaat masala.
1. Garnish with bhujia, coriander, pomegranate.
