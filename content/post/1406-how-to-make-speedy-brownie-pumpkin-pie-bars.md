<h3>The ingredients needed to make Brownie Pumpkin Pie Bars:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 box of of your favorite brownie mix (prepare as box instructions indicate) I love Ghiradelli. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 8 oz of cream cheese,softened. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 15 oz of canned pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of light brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 2/5 oz of box vanilla instant pudding mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 tsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 8 oz of tub of cool whip. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Brownie Pumpkin Pie Bars:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350. Spray or grease an 8x8 baking pan..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour prepared brownie mix in pan..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			With an electric mixer, beat on medium speed next 6 ingredients. Pour over brownie mix gently. Spread to edges of pan with the back of a spoon..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 35-45 min or until knife inserted in center comes out clean..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine cool whip and cinnamon. When COMPLETELY cool (I refrigerated mine overnight) spread cinnamon cool whip on top..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
