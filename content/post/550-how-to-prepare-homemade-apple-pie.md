<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 2 of Frozen pie crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 6 of green apples (if you use any other type of apple you must use more flour). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 tbsp of all-purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tbsp of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 dash of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3/4 tsp of of pumpkin spice &amp; ginger (opt). </li>
	
</ol>

<h3>Steps to make Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt and optional ingredients..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Stir in apples until covered..
