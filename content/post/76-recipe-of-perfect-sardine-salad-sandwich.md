##### The ingredients needed to make Sardine Salad Sandwich:

1. Take 1 can sardines, flavor your choice
1. Take 1 slice sweet onion
1. Get 1 garlic powder
1. Get  Hot sauce
1. Take  ground black pepper
1. Make ready 3 tbsp mayonnaise




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sardine Salad Sandwich:

1. Sardines from 1 can minus oil, this is just what I had on hand.
1. Remove backbone if you dislike them. Place in a cereal/soup bowl
1. Finally dice your onion, add to bowl with sardines
1. Add garlic powder, I give it a couple sprinkles, maybe half a teaspoon.
1. Add hot sauce (here I used crushed red pepper) and a grind or two of black pepper
1. Using a fork, mash it all together, breaking up everything
1. When it&#39;s mashed to your liking, add mayonnaise
1. Mix and mash the mayonnaise, until it&#39;s sort of smooth
1. Serve. I happen to like it open face, a nice crusty bread is good, I only had Italian bread. I garnished it with a bit of chives (green onion, cilantro, parsley all work, or nothing at all) and enjoy. Makes two sandwiches.
1. NOTES - Sometimes I put horseradish on the bread, sometimes I toast the bread. This also lends itself to modifications for the way you like it. My dad never used hot sauce, I happen to like a little heat.
