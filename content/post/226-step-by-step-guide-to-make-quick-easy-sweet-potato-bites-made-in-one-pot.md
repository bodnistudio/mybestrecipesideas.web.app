##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Sweet Potato Bites Made in One Pot:

1. Take 4 small Sweet potatos
1. Make ready 3 tbsp Heavy cream (or milk)
1. Prepare 20 grams Butter or margarine
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Sugar (soft light brown sugar if available)
1. Make ready 1 Egg yolk (including for the glaze)
1. Get 1 Vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Sweet Potato Bites Made in One Pot:

1. Peel the sweet potatoes, and cut into 2 cm cubes. Soak in water for a while. Transfer to a pot, and boil for about 15 minutes.
1. Drain the water, and mash with a masher. You can pound with a pestle, too!
1. Add the heavy cream, butter, egg yolk (save some for the glaze), and vanilla extract, then mix over low heat.
1. When the mixture becomes moist, let it cool a little while. Wet your hands, and form into your preferred shapes.
1. Brush the egg yolk on the surface for the glaze. You can just pour on top if you don&#39;t have a pastry brush!
1. Bake in the oven for 5-10 minutes, and it&#39;s done!
