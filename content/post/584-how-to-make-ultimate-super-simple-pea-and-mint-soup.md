##### The ingredients needed to make Super-simple Pea and Mint Soup:

1. Take 200 grams frozen peas
1. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 1 small onion
1. Prepare 1 bunch fresh mint
1. Make ready 300 ml vegetable bouillon
1. Prepare 1 clove garlic
1. Take 2 tbsp fresh yogurt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Super-simple Pea and Mint Soup:

1. Chop the garlic and onion and fry in the olive oil until soft
1. Add the peas and the vegetable bouillon and enough water to cover the peas
1. Bring to the boil then turn down the heat to simmer for around 10 minutes
1. Chop the mint, leaving a few leaves aside to serve
1. Add the mint to the pan and cook for 2 minutes
1. Use an immersion (or regular) blender to puree the soup
1. Serve hot and place the yogurt on top with some of the mint leaves
