##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin and banana smoothie:

1. Prepare 1 banana, frozen
1. Prepare 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
1. Make ready 1 cup almond milk
1. Prepare 2 tbsp yogurt
1. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
1. Get 4 tbsp oats
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Make ready 1 tbsp honey


For this smoothie you can use frozen or fresh bananas. Frozen bananas will create a more frothy texture, which I prefer. You definitely need to be sure to add ice if you are using fresh bananas because both the pumpkin and the bananas are at room temperature and no one likes a warm smoothie! This healthy smoothie recipe has all the flavor of a pumpkin spice latte without all the sugar.

##### Instructions to make Pumpkin and banana smoothie:

1. Blend together all the ingredients until smooth.
1. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
