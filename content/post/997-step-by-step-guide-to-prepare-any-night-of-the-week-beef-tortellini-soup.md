##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Tortellini soup:

1. Take 1 lb Stew beef
1. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped onion
1. Take 1/2 cup chopped carrots
1. Take 1/2 cup chopped celery
1. Take 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
1. Get 2 tsp sugar
1. Make ready 1 (14.5) oz. can diced tomatoes undrained or a (10 oz.) Can Rotel diced tomatoes and green chilies
1. Take 1 quart beef stock
1. Make ready 1 tsp dried basil leaves
1. Take 2 cup frozen cheese filled tortellini




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Beef Tortellini soup:

1. In crock pot add the beef (seasoned with salt and pepper), onion, carrot, celery, garlic, sugar, tomatoes, &amp; beef stock in order listed.
1. cover and cook on low heat for 8 to 9 hours
1. about 25 minutes before serving, stir in basil and frozen tortellini. turn crockpot to high and cook for about 25 minutes.
