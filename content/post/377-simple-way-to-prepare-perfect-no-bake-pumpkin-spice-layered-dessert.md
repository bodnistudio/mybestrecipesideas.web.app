<h3>The ingredients needed to make No Bake Pumpkin Spice layered Dessert:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/2 cup of graham cracker crumbs. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of melted butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 can of pumpkin (15 oz). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of pkg.3.4oz each jello vanilla instant pudding divided. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of 8 oz. tub cool whip thawed - divided. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/2 cup of plus 1 Tbsp. cold milk divided. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 8 of kraft carmels. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make No Bake Pumpkin Spice layered Dessert:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Mix graham crumbs and butter. Press onto bottom of 13 x9 dish..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Whisk pumpkin 1 pk. Pudding mix and spice in large bowl until blended. Stir in 2 cups cool whip spread over crust..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Beat remaining pudding and 1 and 1/2 cups milk in medium bowl with wisk 2 min. Spread over first layer. Cover with remaing cool whip. Refrigerate 4 hours..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Microwave carmels and remaing milk on high 30 seconds. Stir until carmels are completely melted and blended. Drizzle over before serving..
			
			
		</li>

</ol>
