<h3>The ingredients needed to make No Bake Marshmallow Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 packages of marshmallows large or small (10 oz). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 can of canned pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 cup of cool whip. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of graham cracker crust (9 in). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make No Bake Marshmallow Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In a large saucepan, insert marshmallows, pumpkin, and spices (to taste) over medium heat until melted..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Lower heat and fold cool whip in until mixed throughly..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spoon into crust and refridgerate covered for 3 hours or until set..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
