##### Instructions to make Easy &amp; Hearty Vegetable Stew (vegan):

1. Dice onion and carrots
1. Put oil into pot, followed by the diced onion and carrots - sautee all together.
1. When onions are semi transparent, add wine, followed by vegetable stock.
1. Add diced potatoes, and bring to a boil
1. Open and drain corn, garbanzo, black and kidney beans. Rinse and add to pot
1. When potatoes are soft enough to stick with a fork, add cabbage
1. Cover and let simmer for about 2 minutes. Let simmer longer if you want softer cabbage.
1. Add spinach to pot just before serving
1. Enjoy!
