<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Raisins Preserve Jam:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 of green apples (cut into small cubes). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp of cinnamon powder. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 tsp of nutmeg powder. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/8 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tbs of lemon juice. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 of orange (take the juice and the zest). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 30 gr of raisins. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tsp of plain jelly powder. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Apple Pie Raisins Preserve Jam:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Peel and cut the apples into tiny cubes, add the rest of the ingredients, and stir them well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Boil the mixture with high heat until the soft bubbles appear, and then low the heat..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Strain and take out the froth bubbles using ladle, try to strain them as much as possible..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Simmer and stir it occasionally with low heat around 20 - 25 more minutes, or until the colour turns into dark brown caramel like, and the liquid becomes lesser and a bit thicken..
			
			
		</li>
