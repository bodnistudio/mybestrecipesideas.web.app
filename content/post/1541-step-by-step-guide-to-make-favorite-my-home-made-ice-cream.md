##### The ingredients needed to make My home made ice cream:

1. Get 1 Spoon Custard powder
1. Prepare 250 Ml Milk
1. Get 150 Ml Whipping cream
1. Get 5 Spoon Icing sugar
1. Take 1 teaspoon Essence




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make My home made ice cream:

1. In a bowl add the custard powder and the icing sugar then add a little of the milk and add your essence (Vanilla,Strawberry,Coconut or whichever essence you wish to use for chocolate ice cream add the cocoa at this point)
1. In a pan boil the remaining milk then add the custard mixture and bowl it for 1 minute
1. Remove the boiled mixture from the heat and Add some water to a basin and insert your pan to the water allowing it to cool
1. Whip your whipping cream until it reaches it peak - mix the custard mixture with the whipped cream
1. Put the mixture in the fridge for one hour remove it from the fridge and whisk with a fork if you have fruits or nuts if you have them and mix well
1. Return it to the fridge and let it freeze for about 3 hours.Ready to take
