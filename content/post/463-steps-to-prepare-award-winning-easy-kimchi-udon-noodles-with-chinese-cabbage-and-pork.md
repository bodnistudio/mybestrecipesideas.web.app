##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Kimchi Udon Noodles with Chinese Cabbage and Pork:

1. Make ready 1 pack Boiled udon noodles
1. Take 1 large leaf Chinese cabbage
1. Prepare 70 to 80 grams Pork (thinly sliced)
1. Take 85 ml Mentsuyu (3x concentrate)
1. Make ready 400 ml Water
1. Take 1 tbsp Kimchi base seasoning
1. Make ready 1 tbsp White ground sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Kimchi Udon Noodles with Chinese Cabbage and Pork:

1. Cut up the cabbage. Heat up the water and add a dash of sake (not listed in the ingredients). Add the pork and cabbage and simmer while skimming off the scum properly. Add the mentsuyu sauce and the kimchi seasoning. Add the udon noodles and heat through. Finish by adding the ground sesame seeds.
