##### The ingredients needed to make Bacon Potato Soup:

1. Get 3 each Green scallions
1. Make ready 1 envelope Frozen broccoli
1. Prepare 3 Red potatoes
1. Get  Bacon
1. Prepare 1 tsp Dry parsley
1. Prepare  Salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1 tsp Italian seasoning
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Olive oil
1. Get 1/2 cup Lemon juice
1. Get 1/2 cup Tomato paste
1. Take 1 Green plantain

##### Instructions to make Bacon Potato Soup:

1. Add all chopped veggies and bacon in a big pot with water and let it boil
1. Than add your lemon juice and olive oil. Make sure you stir them all together.
1. Add some salt and pepper and some tomatoes paste for some color and tasting .
