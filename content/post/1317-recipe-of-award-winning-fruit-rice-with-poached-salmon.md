##### The ingredients needed to make Fruit Rice with Poached Salmon:

1. Make ready 2 portions Salmon Steaks
1. Take 1 cup Basmati Rice
1. Make ready 1 medium Green Apple
1. Take 2 Satsuma Oranges
1. Prepare 2 small Pears
1. Take  Black Pepper
1. Get  1 microwave Rice Steamer




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fruit Rice with Poached Salmon:

1. Wash Salmon of any scales. Place in a deep fry pan. Add enough water to almost cover the steaks and start to poach, with a little Black Pepper and Salt.
1. Rinse the Basmati rice under running water to remove excess starch. Place into a microwave steamer, if you have one, or a medium saucepan, if not.
1. Peel Oranges, pull apart segments and remove any pips. Dice into small pieces, add to steamer or pot. (If using a pot, you will need to cook the rice for about 10-15 minutes until rice is fluffy and water is absorbed.)
1. Wash Pears, slice into 4 sections and remove core and stalks. Then dice into small pieces and add to steamer.
1. Wash Apple, cut into 4 sections, remove core and stalk. Dice into small pieces and add to steamer.
1. Stir the fruits into the rice, then cover with boiling water. Microwave for 6 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, turn over Salmon gently so the other side can poach.
1. Remove steamer from the microwave, stir well and return to cook for another 6 minutes, until rice is fluffy and water is absorbed.
1. Drain Salmon and put on a plate. Drain any liquid from the rice and add. This rice can also be eaten cold, as an accompanying dish to cold meats. Please post pictures if you try this!
