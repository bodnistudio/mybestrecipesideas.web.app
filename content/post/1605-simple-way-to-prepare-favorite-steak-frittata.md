##### The ingredients needed to make Steak Frittata:

1. Get 1 leftover steak cut into bite size pieces
1. Prepare 1 cup slices leftover sautéed mushrooms
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp butter
1. Prepare 1/2 red onion, thin sliced
1. Take 1 jalapeño, seeded, thin sliced
1. Make ready 1 cup grated smoked gruyere cheese
1. Get 8 eggs, slightly beaten
1. Prepare 2-3 Tsp your favorite hot sauce
1. Prepare to taste Salt &amp; pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1. Take 1 large avocado, sliced
1. Prepare 1 Tomato, seeded, diced




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Steak Frittata:

1. Gather all your ingredients, prep to cook
1. Heat 12&#34; skillet on medium high heat. Add butter, onions and jalapeños. Season salt &amp; pepper.
1. After veggies are soft, add in leftover mushrooms. Stir to combine, add salt &amp; pepper. Heat through.
1. Now add diced steak.
1. Cover everything with the scrambled eggs.
1. Drizzle your hot sauce over the eggs. Salt &amp; pepper.
1. Last, sprinkle the grated cheese over the eggs.
1. Turn heat down to medium low, cover and let cook to set for about 10-15 minutes.
1. Once cooked, top with sliced avocados and diced tomatoes. Adjust salt &amp; pepper to taste. Sprinkle dried oregano. Enjoy.
