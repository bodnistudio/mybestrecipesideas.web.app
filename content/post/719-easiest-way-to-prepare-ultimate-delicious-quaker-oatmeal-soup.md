##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious Quaker Oatmeal Soup:

1. Get 1/2 kg lamb, cut into cubes
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups oats, for example Quaker oat meal
1. Take 1 onion, chopped
1. Take 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1. Get 1 chicken broth cubes, for example maggi chicken cube
1. Take 1 tablespoon tomato sauce
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1. Take  white pepper
1. Prepare  black pepper
1. Take  salt
1. Prepare  lemon wedges for serving




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Delicious Quaker Oatmeal Soup:

1. In a pressure cooker, fry the onion in vegetable oil until golden. Add the lamb cubes and stir for few minutes until half done.
1. Add the tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, chicken stock cube and 2 cups of water. Close the cooker and cook meat on medium heat for almost 30 minutes until it is done.
1. Remove pressure cooker from heat and wait until all steam escapes before opening it.
1. Open the cooker, add the Quaker oat meal, white pepper, black pepper, salt and water if needed. Put the cooker back on low heat while continuing to stir, until the oatmeal is cooked, for about 20 minutes.
1. Serve the soup hot with a squeeze of lemon.
