##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Teriyaki:

1. Prepare  Fried Chicken
1. Make ready 2 1/2 tbps cornstarch
1. Make ready 2 1/2 tbps fish sauce
1. Make ready  Ground black pepper
1. Take  Flour
1. Make ready  Teriyaki sauce
1. Take 1 clove garlic, grated
1. Get 1 clove size ginger, grated
1. Take 1 pc red sili, chopped
1. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce
1. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp cornstarch dissolve in 2 tsp water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken Teriyaki:

1. Add Chicken, cornstarch, fish sauce and pepper. Mix.
1. Dredge the marinated chicken into flour.
1. Fry the chicken on medium flame setting until golden brown.
1. When the meat becomes golden brown, strain and set aside.
1. Saute garlic, ginger and sili.
1. Add soy sauce.
1. Add brown sugar then dissolve.
1. When sugar is dissolved, add cornstarch and stir until desired thickness.
1. Add the fried chicken meat and mix well.
1. Add green sili and sesame seed before serving.
