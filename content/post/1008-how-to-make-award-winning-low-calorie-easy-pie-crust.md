##### The ingredients needed to make Low Calorie Easy Pie Crust:

1. Get  Cake flour (or all-purpose flour)
1. Prepare  Butter
1. Make ready  Salt
1. Take  Chilled water
1. Prepare  Strong flour (for dusting)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Low Calorie Easy Pie Crust:

1. Cube the butter, then chill. Sift flour on your work space.
1. Add butter and salt to the dough, and using a dough scraper, chop the butter into the flour. Add the chilled water and mix the dough with cutting motions. After bringing it together, let the dough chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
1. Dust flour onto a working surface and roll out the dough into a narrow strip. Fold the top and bottom into the middle, rotate 90°, then fold into fourths (bring the top and bottom ends into the center, then fold in half) and chill for 30 minutes.
1. Repeat Step 3, chill the dough, then it&#39;s ready to use!!
