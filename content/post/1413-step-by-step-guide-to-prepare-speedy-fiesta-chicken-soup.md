##### The ingredients needed to make Fiesta Chicken Soup:

1. Take 2 tbsp vegetable oil
1. Take 1 onion, chopped
1. Get 3 clove garlic diced
1. Prepare 2 jalepenos chopped
1. Get 1 cup beef broth
1. Make ready 4 cup chicken broth
1. Prepare 1 cup water
1. Get 1 tbsp chili powder
1. Get 1 tbsp oregano
1. Take 1 tbsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 can diced tomatoes or fresh tomatoes and tomatoes sauce
1. Take 1 can corn
1. Prepare 1 can black beans rinsed
1. Get 2 cup cooked shredded chicken

##### Steps to make Fiesta Chicken Soup:

1. Sautee garlic onions and jalepenos until soft
1. Add seasoning, tomatos, chicken broth, beef broth, and water.  Simmer for 30 minutes, taste, add more seasoning if necessary.
1. Add corn, beans, and chicken. Simmer for another 15 minutes.
