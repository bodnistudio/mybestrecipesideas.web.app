##### The ingredients needed to make Lazy Simple Pizza:

1. Get 120 gr flour
1. Take 1 tsp yeast
1. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
1. Get  Water
1. Take 1 pinch salt
1. Prepare  Toppings:
1. Make ready 4 tbsp tomato sauce
1. Make ready  Corn
1. Take  Saussages




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Lazy Simple Pizza:

1. Mix yeast, sugar and little bit water to activate the yeast
1. Mix flour, activated yeast, a pinch of salt and water. Then make dough. Then wrap the dough
1. Rest the dough for about 1 hour or more until double in size in warm place
1. After 1 hour, punch the dough then mix with little bit vegetable oil (about 1 tsp)
1. Rub little bit butter on pan. Roll the dough into round shape then add 4 tbsp tomato sauce, add saussages and corn. Bake for about 15-20 minutes
1. Serve it
