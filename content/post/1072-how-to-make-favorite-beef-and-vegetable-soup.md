##### The ingredients needed to make Beef and Vegetable Soup:

1. Take 1 kg stewing beef on the bone, cut into 5cm cubes
1. Get 1 large onion, chopped
1. Get 1 medium tomato, diced
1. Take 1 medium turnip, chopped into 0,5-1cm cubes
1. Prepare 1 medium potato, chopped into 0,5-1cm cubes
1. Take 1 medium carrot, chopped into 0,5-1cm cubes
1. Make ready 1 stalk celery with leaves, diced
1. Make ready 1 leek, cut in half and then sliced
1. Get 2 liters chicken stock
1. Get 0,5 liter water
1. Prepare 250 ml dry soup mix, soaked for 30 minutes
1. Make ready 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
1. Prepare 50 ml olive oil
1. Take to taste salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Beef and Vegetable Soup:

1. Preheat a big pot on the stovetop to medium high heat
1. Lightly season beef with salt and pepper. Brown in the pot with half of the olive oil. You might have to do this in 2-3 batches. Set the beef aside.
1. In the same pot add the remainder of the olive oil and brown the onions for about 5 minutes. Add the tomato and fry for a further 2 minutes
1. Put the beef back in the pot with the stock and water. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 1,5 hours over a medium low heat, covered. Stirring every 15 minutes
1. Now add everything else including salt and pepper. Excluding the parsley. Bring to the boil and simmer for 1 hour covered. Stirring every 15 minutes. Cook for a further 30 minutes uncovered.
1. Stir in the parsley and remove from heat. Let it rest for 30 minutes before serving.
