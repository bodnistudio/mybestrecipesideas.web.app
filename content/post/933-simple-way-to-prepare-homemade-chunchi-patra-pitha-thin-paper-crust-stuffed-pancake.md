##### The ingredients needed to make Chunchi Patra Pitha/Thin paper crust Stuffed Pancake:

1. Make ready  basmati rice
1. Prepare  grated coconut
1. Make ready  As per your taste Jaggery or sugar
1. Get  cardamom powder
1. Get  Salt
1. Get  As required Oil or ghee for grease on tawa only




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chunchi Patra Pitha/Thin paper crust Stuffed Pancake:

1. Soak rice for around 2 hours &amp; grind it.Make a fine paste &amp; add salt &amp; water into that &amp; batter should be water like consistency
1. For stuffing heat a pan &amp; fry grated coconut,jaggery together in low flame till it becomes brown in colour.
1. Finally add cardamom powder &amp; your stuffing is ready.
1. Put a tawa on gas stove.Grease oil or ghee on tawa,then put gas stove on medium flame.
1. Take a thin clean cloth &amp; dip it into the already prepared batter &amp; make a cross symbol on tawa.
1. Put a little coconut stuffing on the middle of the cross &amp; make it four fold as a hanky.
1. Serve it hot.
