##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Noodle Soup:

1. Take 12 oz Of Wide Egg Noodles
1. Prepare 2 cup mixed vegetables (your choice)
1. Get 4 tsp Knorr Chicken Flavor Bouillon
1. Get 1 dash salt
1. Prepare 1/2 onion
1. Make ready 1 clove of garlic
1. Get 2 lb cut cubes of chicken breast




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Noodle Soup:

1. boil water in a pot
1. add pasta in boiling water &amp; add dash of salt
1. Stir pasta occasionally till fully cooked
1. Drain pasta well
1. Add 12 cups of water in a separate pot.
1. ADD onion, clove of garlic &amp; 4 teaspoons of Knorr Chicken flavor and cubs of chicken breast.
1. Cook chicken for 35 min in medium heat.
1. Remove onion &amp; garlic
1. Add drained pasta mixed vegetables in pot of boiled chicken
1. stir, cook 8 min and serve.
