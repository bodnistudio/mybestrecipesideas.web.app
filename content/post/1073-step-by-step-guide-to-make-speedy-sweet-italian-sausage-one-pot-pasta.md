##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Italian Sausage One Pot Pasta:

1. Get 3 1/2 cup Chicken broth
1. Prepare 12 oz Uncooked pasta noodles
1. Make ready 1 packages Sweet italian sausage
1. Get 2 tsp (Or 3) olive oil
1. Prepare 1 can Diced tomatoes (drain most of the juice)
1. Make ready 2 clove Minced garlic
1. Prepare 1 tsp Ground white pepper (black pepper is fine if you don&#39;t have white pepper)
1. Get 1 tsp Basil
1. Get 1/3 packages Chopped frozen onions (or 1/2 chopped onion)
1. Prepare 1 cup Frozen broccoli or any other veg
1. Get 1/2 bunch Spinach
1. Take 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sweet Italian Sausage One Pot Pasta:

1. In a pot, add chicken broth and heat on high. As the broth is heating, add all ingredients but the Parmesan and bring to a boil.
1. Stirring frequently, cook approximately 8-10 minutes or until pasta is cooked through. Any excess liquid will dissipate after sitting for a couple minutes.
1. Add Parmesan cheese and mix through. Enjoy!
