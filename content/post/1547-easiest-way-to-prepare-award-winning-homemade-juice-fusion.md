##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade Juice Fusion:

1. Make ready 6 each six large carrots
1. Take 3 3/4 cup 100% apple juice
1. Take 3 cup fresh spinach
1. Take 2 each two ends of the fresh spinach
1. Take 1/2 can fruit cocktail with juice
1. Prepare 1 can unsweetened applesauce
1. Get 1 each one cucumber
1. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
1. Prepare 1 each one whole banana, peeled




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Homemade Juice Fusion:

1. Cut ends off of cucumber and carrots, discard ends
1. Cut all fruits and vegetables into the size of your juicer
1. Juice everything into a container according to your juicer size
1. Pour juice into pitcher
1. Add sugar
1. Stir well but don&#39;t mind the bubbles when you pour in sugar
1. Chill completely
1. Serve
