##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Chinese Cabbage and Cod Steamed in Sake:

##### Steps to make Easy Chinese Cabbage and Cod Steamed in Sake:

1. Chop the Chinese cabbage and bacon to 1 cm wide pieces. Spread the cabbage evenly in a frying pan.
1. Place the mushrooms, fish, bacon (in that order) on top and sprinkle on the seasonings marked with a ☆ (the sake should sprinkled directly on the fish).
1. Cover. Heat over medium-low to medium heat and steam for 10 minutes.
