##### Instructions to make Sausage meat Plait:

1. Set temp to 180°C ( fan oven )
1. Prepare a baking tray by greasing and flouring or lining with parchment/greaseproof paper
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the spices to the sausage meat and mix well. Add freshly ground pepper and a grind of salt.
1. Roughly chop the prunes and the chestnuts and add to the sausage meat.
1. Fry the chopped bacon until golden, then add the chopped shallots and the crushed garlic. Cook until soft. Add to the sausagemeat and mix well.
1. Roll pastry on a floured board to about 3mm thick.
1. Fold the pastry into thirds and crease lightly. Open out flat and cut the outside thirds diagonally from the edge to the crease.
1. Fill the middle third with the sausage meat mix and press flat.
1. Lay the outside pastry flaps over the filling and tuck in at the top and bottom.
1. Place the plait onto the baking sheet. Brush with beaten egg. Cook for 12 - 15 minutes.
