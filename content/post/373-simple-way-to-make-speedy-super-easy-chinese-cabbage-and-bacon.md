##### The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Chinese Cabbage and Bacon:

1. Take  Chinese or nappa cabbage
1. Get  to 6 slices  Bacon
1. Make ready  Soup stock cube
1. Take  Water
1. Prepare  Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Super Easy Chinese Cabbage and Bacon:

1. Chop the Chinese cabbage into 5 cm squares. Do the same with the bacon.
1. Put the Chinese cabbage into a large pot, like an earthen pot. I recommend you put the pieces with the hard stems in at the bottom.
1. Put bacon on top and add water. Cover the pot and steam-cook. Stir once or twice while it&#39;s cooking.
1. When the Chinese cabbage releases water and becomes soft, remove the lid. Dissolve in the soup stock cube and continue to simmer for 2 to 3 minutes. To finish, season with salt and pepper to your liking.
