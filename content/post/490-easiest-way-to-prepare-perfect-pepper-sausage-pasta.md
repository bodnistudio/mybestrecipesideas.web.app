##### The ingredients needed to make Pepper Sausage Pasta:

1. Get 1/2 box angel hair
1. Get 1 large onion
1. Take 2 clove garlic
1. Get 3 bell peppers, green red yellow
1. Take 2 large sausages
1. Prepare 4 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 1 herbs to taste (oregano, basil, parsley)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pepper Sausage Pasta:

1. Boil water with a teaspoon of salt.
1. Cut peppers and onion into strips. cut sausage into bite sized cylinders.
1. Begin sautéing garlic, onion, peppers, and sausage with 2 tablespoons olive oil.
1. When water is boiling add half box of angel hair. bring back to boil and cook for 5 minutes.
1. When sausage is brown and vegetables tender remove from heat.
1. When angel hair is done drain water.
1. In a large serving bowl toss angel hair with 2 tablespoons olive oil and desired herbs. add sausage and vegetables.
