<h3>The ingredients needed to make Lemon Blueberry Pie with Strudel Topping:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Lemon Filling. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of fresh or bottled lemon juice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare Pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 tablespoon of cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 4 of large eggs, well beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of Strudel Topping. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of all purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/3 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get Pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 5 tablespoon of butter, melted and cooled. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Blueberry Layer. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 cups of fresh or frozen blueberries. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/3 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 Tablespoons of Cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare Pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tablespoons of lemon juice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 9 inch of deep dish pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>

<h3>Instructions to make Lemon Blueberry Pie with Strudel Topping:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425 degrees..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake crust for 10 minutes. Place on rack to cool..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix blueberries, cornstartch, salt, sugar and lemon juice. Heat until bubbly and thickens. Set aside..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Turn oven to 375. While oven is reducing in temperature, melt the butter. Add lemon juice..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix the flour, salt, sugar, cornstarch and add to the melted butter and lemon juice. Mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add eggs and mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into prepared crust and bake for 20 to 25 minutes. The filling should be jiggly in the center..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix your cooled melted butter with flour, sugar and cornstarch. Mix until crumbly and sandy..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spoon blueberry mixture on top of lemon pie evenly..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle strudel topping over blueberries..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake additional 20 minutes or until strudel topping is golden brown..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool on wire rack completely before slicing..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Servealone, with whipped topping or vanilla ice cream..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
