##### The ingredients needed to make Steak Crustini:

1. Get 1 steak (marinated in your favorite)
1. Take 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Get 1 packages mushrooms
1. Get 1 loaf fresh French bread with garlic cloves
1. Get 1 stick smoked mozzarella
1. Prepare 1 packages blue cheese
1. Take  olive oil
1. Take  herb butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Steak Crustini:

1. Grill steak until medium rare, then let rest.
1. Sauté mushrooms and onions in olive oil
1. Slice bread and lightly butter
1. Slice steak, place strip of steak, onions and mushrooms on bread. Top with either cheese
1. Put under medium broil until cheese is golden
