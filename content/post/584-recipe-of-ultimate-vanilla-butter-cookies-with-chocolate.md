##### The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Butter Cookies with chocolate:

1. Take 125 gm unsalted butter/white butter
1. Prepare 1/3 cup caster sugar
1. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Get 1 cup Maida
1. Get 2 tbsp milk
1. Prepare 1/2 cup melted dark chocolate
1. Take  few crushed almonds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vanilla Butter Cookies with chocolate:

1. Beat the butter, sugar and vanilla essence in a bowl till soft and fluffy then mix maida in it. Add the milk and mix well to make soft dough. If the mixture is a little dry, add an extra 1 tbsp of milk.
1. Divide the dough into balls and then place the balls in paper cups, place all cookies in a baking try and bake in a pre-heated oven at 180°c for 15-20 minute.
1. Remove the biscuits from the oven and set aside to cool for about 5 minutes on a wire rack to cool completely.
1. Now top with melted dark chocolate and sprinkle some crushed almonds.
1. Serve your guest this mouth melting cookies.
