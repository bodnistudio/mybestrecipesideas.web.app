##### The ingredients needed to make Jeremiah’s Candied Pumpkin:

1. Get 1 8-10” pumpkin
1. Prepare 1/2 stick butter (1/4 cup)
1. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
1. Get 1/2 tbsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp nutmeg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Jeremiah’s Candied Pumpkin:

1. Scrub the pumpkin surface clean and remove the stem.
1. Halve the pumpkin and clean out seeds and strings; reserve the seeds
1. Melt the butter and mix in the sugar and other seasonings.
1. Baste the flesh (inside) with the mixture.
1. Bake at 350° for one hour.
1. Cut the pumpkin in wedges and serve. Remove the rind before eating.
