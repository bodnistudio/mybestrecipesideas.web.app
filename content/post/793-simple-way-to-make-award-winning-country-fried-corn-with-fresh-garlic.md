##### The ingredients needed to make Country Fried Corn with Fresh Garlic:

1. Get 10 ears fresh corn on cob
1. Take 4 Tbsp butter
1. Take 4 cloves garlic
1. Take 1/4 cup parsley chopped or 2 Tbsp dried
1. Take 1 tsp cracked black pepper
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Country Fried Corn with Fresh Garlic:

1. Shuck and clean ears of corn.
1. Cut kernels off cob. Invert a bowl inside the skillet to make it easier to cut the kernels off.
1. Take knife blade and press at top of cob and run down cob to extract the starches. This helps thicken the corn.
1. If you have a garlic press, press the fresh garlic cloves through and set aside. Or finely chop with knife.
1. Add butter, finely minced garlic, salt and pepper. Cool over medium heat stirring occasionally to keep from sticking.
1. Corn cooks quickly so should be done in 20 minutes. Sprinkle with more parsley. Ready to serve.
1. If you like cream style corn, at this point stir in 1/2 cup cream and 2 additional tbsp butter and heat through. Enjoy
