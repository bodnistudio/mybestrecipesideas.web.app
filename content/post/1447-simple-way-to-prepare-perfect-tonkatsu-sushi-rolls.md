##### The ingredients needed to make Tonkatsu Sushi Rolls:

1. Take 1 cutlet Tonkatsu pork cutlet
1. Take 400 grams Hot cooked rice
1. Get 2 sheets Nori seaweed
1. Make ready 200 grams Cabbage
1. Take 1 tsp Salt (to rub into the cabbage)
1. Prepare 1 Sauce
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Honey mustard sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Tonkatsu Sushi Rolls:

1. Cut the tonkatsu into four long stick and toss with the sauce. If you&#39;re using cold rice,  microwave.
1. Shred the cabbage and rub with salt until soft. Drain excess water, and combine with honey mustard sauce.
1. On a sushi mat, place your nori sheet, half of the rice, half of the cabbage, and 2 sticks of tonkatsu. Roll it up tightly. Make two.
1. Set it aside for a while before slicing. It could be troublesome, but if you clean your knife with a damp cloth each time you slice it, the cross-section will look nice.
