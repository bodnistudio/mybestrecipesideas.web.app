##### The ingredients needed to make Bratwurst with Bell Peppers and Sauerkraut:

1. Take 1 tsp caraway seeds, grounded
1. Take 1/2 tsp celery seeds, grounded
1. Make ready 2 T EVOO
1. Make ready 1 lb bratwurst
1. Take 1 yellow onion, chopped
1. Make ready 2 green bell peppers, seeded and sliced
1. Take 1/4 tsp sea salt
1. Prepare to taste black pepper
1. Take 2 c sauerkraut, drained




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Bratwurst with Bell Peppers and Sauerkraut:

1. In large skillet, heat oil.  Cook sausage until barely pink.
1. Add onion, peppers and seasonings. Cover and cook until onion, peppers and sausage is done.
1. Add sauerkraut and toss before serving.
