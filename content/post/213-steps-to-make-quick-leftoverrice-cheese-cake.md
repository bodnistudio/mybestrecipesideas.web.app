##### Steps to make #leftover#Rice cheese cake:

1. Mash together the cottage cheese and rice.
1. Then add the sugar and essence.
1. Then add in the cream. Mix well and make a smooth paste.
1. Heat the butter till it melts.
1. Add to the mixtures in butter.
1. Pass the mixture through a wire mash.
1. Line a mould or cake tin. Pour the mixture into it.
1. Add a mix nuts and mix well. Refrigerate.
1. Remould and cut into wedges.
1. To serve garnish each wedge with grated chocolate and a cherry.
1. Serve chilled.
