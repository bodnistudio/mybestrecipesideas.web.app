##### The ingredients needed to make White Chicken Pizza:

1. Make ready 1 can Pillsbury pizza dough
1. Get  pizza toppings
1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts, cooked prior and diced
1. Get 1 onion, chopped
1. Take  shredded mozzarella
1. Get  crust toppings
1. Make ready 1 stick butter or margarine (I use imperial)
1. Get  garlic powder, onion powder, italian seasoning, pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make White Chicken Pizza:

1. Preheat oven to 400°
1. Roll out pizza dough to desired thickness and shape.
1. Melt the butter and add the seasonings to taste. Once mixed, brush the pizza dough with the mixture and place in oven for about 7-8 minutes.
1. Take out of the oven and lightly brush again with the same mixture. Add your chicken, onions, and cheese. Place in oven for another 10 minutes.
1. Take out, lightly brush the crust, cut, and ENJOY!!!!
