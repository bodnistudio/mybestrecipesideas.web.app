##### The ingredients needed to make Italian Caprese &amp; Chicken Sausage Mini Pizza:

1. Make ready 4 pita bread pockets
1. Prepare 1 Gilbert&#39;s caprese chicken sausage link
1. Take 1 pesto spread
1. Make ready 10 small fresh mozzarella balls
1. Get 5 grape tomatoes
1. Get 1 fresh basil
1. Make ready 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
1. Take 1 kosher salt
1. Take 1 cup balsamic vinaigrette (OPTIONAL)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Italian Caprese &amp; Chicken Sausage Mini Pizza:

1. Take a cookie tray, and scatter kosher salt on the tray.
1. Take each pita bread, and with a brush, spread the olive oil on both sides of the bread, leaving a nice layer without any puddles. Do this for each pizza.
1. With a spoon, take the pesto and spread it on each of the pita breads creating a base layer.
1. Take the chicken sausage link, and cut it into quarter-inch discs, enough to put 4-5 discs on each bread.
1. Cut the grape tomatoes into quarters, and alternate the tomato quarters with the chicken sausage discs.  The idea is to have it look like a star pattern.
1. Take the mozzarella balls, and cut them each in half. Place about 5-6 halves on each pita bread; one on each sausage disc and one in the middle.
1. Bake at 375°F for about 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted fully.
1. When the pitas come out of the oven, top with freshly sliced basil.  (Optional: add a small drizzle of olive oil and balsamic vinaigrette on top)
1. Serve hot, &amp; enjoy!
