##### Steps to make Easy chicken Lo Mein:

1. Cut your chicken breast in chunks. Season chicken with salt and pepper only. In a skillet with olive oil cook chicken until no longer pink
1. Set cooked chicken aside; remove from skillet. Mix in a bowl your sauces n red pepper flakes
1. In same skillet add more olive oil..and throw in your cut up celery chunks n broccoli and saute until desired tenderness
