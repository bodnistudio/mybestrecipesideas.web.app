##### The ingredients needed to make Sauteed Sugar Snap Peas:

1. Take 300 grams of Sugar Snap Peas
1. Prepare 1 Salt
1. Make ready 1 Black Pepper
1. Prepare 2 tbsp of Cooking Oil
1. Prepare 3 clove of Garlic, crushed




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sauteed Sugar Snap Peas:

1. Wash and clean The Sugar Snap Peas.
1. Heat the oil in a Frying Pan ( I used Teflon).
1. Put the garlic in the pan and sauteed it until it smells good.
1. Put in the Sugar Snap Peas and add salt and Black Pepper.
1. Saute for around 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Serve.
