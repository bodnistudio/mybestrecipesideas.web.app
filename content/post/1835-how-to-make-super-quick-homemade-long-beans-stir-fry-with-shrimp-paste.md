##### The ingredients needed to make Long Beans Stir Fry with Shrimp Paste:

1. Make ready 300 gr long beans, washed and chopped
1. Make ready 200 ml water
1. Make ready  Salt and pepper
1. Take  Grind in pestle and mortar to make a rough paste:
1. Prepare 1 tomato, sliced
1. Take 2 red chilli peppers
1. Prepare 5 shallots
1. Get 2 garlics
1. Get 1/2 block shrimp paste (around 1/2 tbsp)
1. Get 2 cm galangal
1. Get 2 tsp palm sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Long Beans Stir Fry with Shrimp Paste:

1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet. Saute the rough paste in medium heat until fragrant.
1. Add chopped long beans to the wok. Season with salt and pepper. Stir for a minute.
1. Add water and stir. Taste season if needed.
1. Reduce heat. Let it simmer until beans soften. Best served warm with a sprinkle of fried sliced shallots.
