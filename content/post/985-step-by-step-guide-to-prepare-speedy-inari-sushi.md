##### The ingredients needed to make Inari Sushi:

1. Prepare 10 pieces Aburaage (squares)
1. Prepare 150 to 200 ml Water
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Prepare 700 grams ● Cooked rice
1. Take 60 ml ● Sushi vinegar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Inari Sushi:

1. Cut the aburaage in half.
1. Pour hot water to drain the excess oil.
1. Put the bean curd, water, sugar and soy sauce in a pan, use aluminum foil as a drop lid and cook it slowly over a low flame.
1. Cook until the liquid is almost gone then turn off the heat. Stuff the sushi rice into the bean curd when the rice has cooled down. Done.
