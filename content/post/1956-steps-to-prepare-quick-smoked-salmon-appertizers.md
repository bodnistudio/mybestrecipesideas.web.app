##### Steps to make Smoked Salmon Appertizers:

1. Preheat oven to 150 (gas fan forced) spray baking tray with cooking oil.    . Slice potatoes to round disks about 1mm thick ( I left the skins on) place on  tray season with salt and pepper..drizzel all potatoes with olive oil.. bake in oven for 20-25 mins
1. After 20-25 check potatoes--prick with a knife. If soft they are done
1. While potatoes r cooling a little. Slice in thick strips the smoked salmon.
1. After potatoes have cooled spread generously with cream cheese. Add the cut smoked salmon &amp; arrange creativly...top with a sprig of dill and enjoy
