##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Vegetables On Rosemary Skewers:

1. Make ready  Fresh sprigs of rosemary
1. Prepare  Baby white potatoes
1. Take  Mini heirloom tomatoes
1. Take  White mushrooms
1. Make ready  Orange bell pepper
1. Get  White onion
1. Make ready  Chopstick/stick
1. Prepare  Seasonings of choice, such as garlic powder, black pepper, chili powder, etc.

##### Instructions to make Roasted Vegetables On Rosemary Skewers:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet.
1. Wash your vegetables and rosemary sprigs.
1. Cut the vegetables into pieces of relatively the same size. Cut the potatoes into fours, the tomatoes in half, the mushrooms into fours, and the bell pepper and onion into uniform square pieces of relative size to the other vegetables. Feel free to omit/add vegetables as you prefer.
1. Run the chopstick through your vegetables (slowly) so that you have a hole to run the rosemary through.
1. Skew the vegetables onto your rosemary through the holes in the SAME direction as the leaves go. Vary the vegetables in any order of your choice.
1. Place on lined sheet and add seasonings to taste. Roast in the oven, uncovered, for 35 minutes.
1. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool for at least 10 minutes.
