##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun Chicken and Turkey Sausage Spaghetti:

1. Get 1 cup onion, chopped
1. Get 1 packages Turkey Sausage
1. Get 3 chicken breasts
1. Get 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
1. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 tsp black pepper
1. Take 2 packages Velveta Queso Blanca Cheese
1. Make ready 1 packages whole wheat pasta
1. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil

##### Instructions to make Cajun Chicken and Turkey Sausage Spaghetti:

1. First boil your pasta for 15 minutes on high heat add a little salt to flavor your noodles and olive oil so they won&#39;t stick together
1. Chop up your chicken turkey sausage and onion and cook them all together in a pan with a little butter then add your garlic salt pepper and cayenne pepper
1. Add your cheese sauce once the noodles are finish cooking then add your sautéed chicken sausage and onion to your pasta and enjoy
