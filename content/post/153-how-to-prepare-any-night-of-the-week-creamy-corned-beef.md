##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Corned Beef:

1. Make ready 1 can Corned Beef

1. Prepare 1 Onion
1. Take 2/3 can All Purpose Cream

##### Steps to make Creamy Corned Beef:

1. Dice the onion and garlic.
1. Pour the oil into the pre-heated pan.
1. Cook the garlic first, then the onion.
1. Fry the Corned Beef for 2-3 minutes.
