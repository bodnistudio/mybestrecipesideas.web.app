<h3>The ingredients needed to make East Texas Pecan Sweet Potato Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of preheat oven to 350 degrees. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of frozen 9&#34; deep dish prepared pie shell. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 cup of mashed sweet potato @ room temperature (about 2-3 medium size). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of melted unsalted butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 of large eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/3 cup of roughly chopped toasted pecan pieces. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tbsp of all purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3/4 cup of half n half cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3/4 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of table salt. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make East Texas Pecan Sweet Potato Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Toast pecan pieces in a pan over medium heat, until you can smell them. Roughly 3-5 minutes. Set aside..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cream mashed sweet potato, melted butter and eggs together until very smooth. I use a stand mixer..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add all remaining ingredients including toasted chopped pecans and blend together until mixture is very smooth..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into frozen pie shell and bake at 350&#34; for approximately 50-60 minutes until pie custard is set. Pie will be done when a wooden toothpick comes out clean when inserted..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			You can boil your sweet potatoes or even use canned. I prefer fresh baked sweet potatoes. I like to serve this pie well chilled and keep it stored in the refrigerator. Garnish with whipped cream and toasted pecan pieces. Enjoy!!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
