##### The ingredients needed to make Broccoli and Dried Shrimps Stir-Fry (6 Ingredients):

1. Get 1 cup broccoli florets
1. Make ready 4 tbsp dried shrimps
1. Get 1 tbsp honey
1. Get 3 garlic, crushed, sliced thinly
1. Take 1 tsp oil
1. Prepare 50 ml water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Broccoli and Dried Shrimps Stir-Fry (6 Ingredients):

1. Soak the dried shrimps, after five minutes or so, remove the water. If you want less salty shrimps, soak for at least 15 minutes.
1. Heat the pan with oil. Stir-fry the garlic, then add dried shrimps, then broccoli florets. Add honey.
1. Stir from time to time. After a couple of minutes, pour the water inside, lower the heat, and cover.
1. When the broccoli florets have changed color, it is done!
