##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Sweet Potatoes Stewed in Orange Juice:

1. Take 200 ml Orange juice
1. Take 4 tbsp Sugar
1. Take 3 tbsp Honey
1. Prepare 1 tsp Lemon juice (optional)
1. Prepare 1 Gardenia seed (optional)
1. Take 2 Sweet potatoes (choose thin, narrow ones)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Sweet Potatoes Stewed in Orange Juice:

1. Rinse the sweet potatoes well, slice into rounds, then soak in water. If you skip this step, the color will not turn bright.
1. Add all of the ingredients to a sauce pan, bring to a boil on high heat, then simmer for about 10 minutes on low heat. Cover with a drop lid to prevent the potato slices from breaking apart while simmering.
1. When a skewer goes all the way through the sweet potatoes, remove the gardenia seed, and then it&#39;s ready to serve. They are delicious served either hot or cold. When serving cold, let them cool in the stewed liquid.
1. Enjoy them either hot or cold.
