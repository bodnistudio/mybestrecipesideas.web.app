##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Pecan Pie:

1. Get 1 refrigerated pie crust
1. Make ready 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1. Get 3 tbsp milk
1. Take 4 eggs
1. Get 3 tbsp butter (melted)
1. Take 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
1. Take 1 cup dark corn syrup
1. Get 1 cup sugar
1. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1. Get 1/4 tsp ground Allspice
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup pecan halves




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chocolate Pecan Pie:

1. Line 9-inch deep dish pie plate with pie crust. Bake in preheated 425°F oven for 7 minutes. Remove crust from oven. Reduce temperature to 325.
1. Meanwhile, microwave chocolate chips and milk in medium microwavable bowl on HIGH 1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Stir until smooth. Pour chocolate evenly over crust.
1. Beat eggs in large bowl. Add remaining ingredients; mix well. Slowly pour mixture over chocolate layer. Place ring of foil around edges of crust to prevent over-browning.
1. Bake 55 to 60 minutes or until filling is puffed and center is still soft enough to move when shaken gently. Cool completly on wire rack.
