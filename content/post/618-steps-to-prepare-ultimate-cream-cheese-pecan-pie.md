<h3>The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 8 oz of cream cheese, softened. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of egg, beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2/3 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 10 inch of unbaked deep dish pie shell. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/4 cup of chopped pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of light corn syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>

<h3>Steps to make Cream Cheese Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>

		<li>

		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread mixture over bottom of pie shell..

		</li>
	
		<li>
