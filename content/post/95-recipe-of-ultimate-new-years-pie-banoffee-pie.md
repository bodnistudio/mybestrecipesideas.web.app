<ol>

		<li>{Take 250 g of soft butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 250 g of caster sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 8 pcs. of ripe bananas. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 300 ml of whipped cream. </li>
