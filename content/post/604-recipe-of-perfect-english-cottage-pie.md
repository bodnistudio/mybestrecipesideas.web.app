##### The ingredients needed to make English Cottage Pie:

1. Get 1 kg King Edward Potatoes
1. Get 500 grams Lean Steak Mince Meat
1. Get 6 tbsp Milk
1. Make ready 3 White Mushrooms
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
1. Take 1 Green Bell Pepper
1. Make ready 1 Carrott
1. Make ready 1 clove Garlic
1. Get 1/2 Red Onion
1. Make ready 400 ml Beef Stock
1. Prepare 100 grams Grated Cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make English Cottage Pie:

1. Preheat oven to No 5
1. Peel &amp; boil potatoes, drain potatoes in collinder, then add milk &amp; butter to pot, reheat until butter has melted. place boiled potatoes back in pot and mash well
1. Chop onion, mushroom, pepper &amp; carrotts, crush garlic
1. In new pot add olive oil bring to low heat, add carrotts, onion, pepper, mushrooms &amp; garlic fry for 5 mins
1. Add lean mince meat &amp; beef stock to onion, mushroom &amp; carrott mix pot. stir well &amp; cook until meat is brown, 15 mins
1. Place meat mixture into oven baking dish, cover with mash potatoes &amp; sprinkle over grated cheese
1. Place on middle oven shelf for 30 mins until surface is golden brown
