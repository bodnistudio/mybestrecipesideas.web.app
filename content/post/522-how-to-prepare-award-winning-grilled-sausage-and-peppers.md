##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Sausage And Peppers:

1. Get  sausage and peppers
1. Prepare 1 packages Italian turkey sausages, 1 1/4 pounds
1. Take 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced thin
1. Get 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced thin
1. Get 1/2 yellow bell pepper, sliced thin
1. Make ready 1 large Vidalia onion, sliced
1. Take 8 fresh sage leaves ( optional )




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Grilled Sausage And Peppers:

1. I used a gas grill and preheated grill and grill pan on high heat for 10 minutes.
1. Once hot, turn heat to a medium heat.
1. Place sausages on grill or grill pan. Cook five minutes then turn.
1. Cook another five minutes then turn again. This time place the sausages in opposite direction to achieve a cross hatch grill mark.
1. Now on griddle side of pan place peppers, onions and sage if using.
1. Cook sausages in same turning pattern for another 10 minutes.  Stir peppers and onions occasionally.
1. Sausages are done when internal temperature reaches 165°F Fahrenheit.
1. Serve with buns or over rice or enjoy just as it is!
