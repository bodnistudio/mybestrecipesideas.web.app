##### The ingredients needed to make Besan Halwa:

1. Take 2 cups besan (gram flour)
1. Prepare 1/2 cup water
1. Prepare 1 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1 cup ghee
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp powdered cardamom
1. Prepare 1 tbsp grated dried coconut
1. Make ready 5 grated almonds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Besan Halwa:

1. Heat ghee in a pan and fry the besan till it’s light brown.
1. Add the cardamom powder.
1. Make sugar syrup by adding sugar to boiling water. Stir till the syrup is thick.
1. Add the sugar syrup to besan and stir well and make sure there are no lumps.
1. Transfer the mixture into a greased plate or vessel. Garnish with grated nuts and dried coconut powder and leave it to set till the mixture is completely cool.
