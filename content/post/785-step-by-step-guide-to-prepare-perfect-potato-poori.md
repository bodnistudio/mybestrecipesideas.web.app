##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Poori:

1. Take 250 grams all-purpose flour
1. Make ready 1 large Potato
1. Prepare 1 pinch pepper
1. Get 1 pinch salt
1. Take 3 tbsp oil or butter
1. Prepare 1 water
1. Make ready 1 cup oil to deep fry




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Potato Poori:

1. First take a pan put some water,salt, tumeric powder and potatoes (cut into small pieces) and let the potatoes to boil. (take 2 or 3 large potatoes)
1. Until it boils mix the flour,salt,pepper,oil or butter (use butter for good tatse)
1. Smash the potatoes when they are fully boiled.
1. Mix the smashed potato with the flour mix.
1. Then add little bit of water and knead until it becomes a soft dough.
1. Then make small balls out of the dough.
1. Use a flat surface and put some flour on it. Then keep a small dough and flatten it using the hand
1. Next take a rolling pin and roll it.
1. Heat some oil in a pan to deep fry.
1. When the oil is ready add the rolled poori into the pan and fry until it&#39;s golden colour.
1. Serve with a curry. Or with an egg like I have done..
