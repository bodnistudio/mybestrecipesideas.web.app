<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/8 tsp of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 cup of pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 each of pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Mix dry ingredients.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Beat eggs slightly in a bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Slowly add pumpkin and dry ingredients mixing with a spoon..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add evaporated milk and mix.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into pie crust (homemade or store bought)..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 425 for 15 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Lower temperature to 350 and bake an additional 30-40 minutes until a knife comes out clean when poked..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Let cool for 2 hours before refrigerating..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add toasted pumpkin seeds on top (optional). I toasted mine with olive oil, salt, sugar, cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
