##### The ingredients needed to make Ginger Vegan Fried Rice:

1. Take 1 dash pepper
1. Take 2 cup dice green bean
1. Prepare 4 cup cooked rice
1. Prepare 1 cup pounded ginger more if you like strong ginger flavour
1. Take 4 tbsp light soy sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Ginger Vegan Fried Rice:

1. Cooked rice as usual set aside
1. In pan with oil lightly brown ginger and green bean for about 3 minute till lightly brown but not burn then add cooked rice and light soy sauce mix well for another 2 minute
1. Top with dash of black pepper
