##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Potato in Sweet Soy Sauce:

1. Take 4 pieces chicken
1. Get 2 potatoes, cut into several pieces
1. Prepare 3 cloves garlics, smashed and chopped
1. Take  Some salt
1. Take  Some pepper
1. Make ready  Chicken powder
1. Get as needed Some water,
1. Take  Some sweet soy sauce
1. Make ready  Some oil

##### Steps to make Chicken and Potato in Sweet Soy Sauce:

1. Add the oil in a pan. Then fry the potato pieces until cooked through and golden brown. Take them out and set aside.
1. Then add about 2 tbsp of oil in a pan. Add the garlics and stir until fragrant.
1. Then add the chicken. Stir to combine and wait until the turn in colour.
1. Then add some water. Let it simmer.
1. Add in the potatoes. Mix again.
1. Add in the seasonings and soy sauce. Taste test and let them penetrate through.
1. Turn off the heat when everything is ready.
1. Enjoy.
