<h3>The ingredients needed to make Easy Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 can of (30 oz.) Libby&#39;s Easy Pumpkin Pie Mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 can of (5 fl. oz.) Carnation Fat Free Evaporated Milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 large of eggs, beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of unbaked 9-inch deep dish pie shell. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Easy Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix pie mix, evaporated milk, and eggs in large bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into pie shell.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 15 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Reduce temperature to 350°F and bake for an additional 50-60 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool on wire rack for 2 hours.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
