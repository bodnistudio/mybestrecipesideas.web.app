##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage Lasagna:

1. Get 16 oz gluten-free oven ready lasagna noodles
1. Take 1 lb Italian sausage, casings removed
1. Take 1/2 tsp onion powder
1. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, minced
1. Take 32 oz ricotta cheese
1. Prepare 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1. Make ready 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1. Take 2 Large eggs, lightly beaten
1. Make ready 1 tbsp dried basil
1. Make ready 48 oz pasta sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sausage Lasagna:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook sausage. Add onion powder &amp; garlic towards the end of cooking. Once meat is done. Drain.
1. In large bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, eggs, &amp; basil until well blended.
1. Spread thin layer of sauce in bottom of 9 x 13 baking dish. Top with layer of noodles. Cover with layer of cheese mixture. Sprinkle with some sausage. Add layer of sauce. Repeat layering. End with a layer of sauce.
1. Cover with foil. Bake for 45 minutes. Uncover. Bake 15 minutes longer. Let stand 5 minutes.
1. Serve.
1. Notes: Can use noodles that are not oven ready. If so, cook them according to package directions. Drain. Rinse with cool water. Drain again. Then ready for layering. Also, you don&#39;t have to use gluten free noodles. Can use regular noodles with no other changes.
