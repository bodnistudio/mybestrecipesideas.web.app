<h3>The ingredients needed to make Honey Baked Chicken Breast:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of chicken breasts; bone-in. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tbsp of honey. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tbsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of white pepper. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of vegetable oil; as needed. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Honey Baked Chicken Breast:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Toss chicken with enough oil to cover. Season with pumpkin pie spice and salt. Bake at 350°for approximately one hour or until thermometer reaches 165°. Glaze chicken with honey during last five minutes of cooking..
			
			
		</li>
