##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Potato Chip Casserole:

1. Take 1 can chicken, drained (10 oz)
1. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken soup
1. Prepare 1 can early peas, drained
1. Make ready 2 cup crushed plain potato chips, divided
1. Prepare 1 shredded cheese (desired amt)
1. Take 1/3 cup milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Potato Chip Casserole:

1. preheat oven to 350
1. in 8x8 casserole dish, flake chicken. Stir in soup and milk, mix until smooth.
1. add peas and 1 cup crushed chips, mix well.
1. bake at 350 for 15 mins.
1. remove from oven. pour remaining chips on top and sprinkle with cheese.
1. return to oven and bake an additional 10 mins or until cheese has melted.
