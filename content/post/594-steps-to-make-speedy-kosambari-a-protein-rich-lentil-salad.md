##### The ingredients needed to make Kosambari A Protein Rich Lentil Salad:

1. Make ready 1/2 cup yellow Moong Dal(split Green Gram)
1. Take 1/2 cup grated orange Carrot
1. Prepare 1/2 cup finely chopped Cucumber
1. Prepare 1/4 cup grated fresh Coconut
1. Get 1/4 cup Pomegranate pearls
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp finely chopped green chillies
1. Prepare 2 tsp Lemon Juice
1. Make ready 2 tsp coriander finely chopped
1. Prepare to taste Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Kosambari A Protein Rich Lentil Salad:

1. Firstly, wash properly and soak the yellow Moong dal in enough water for 1-2 hours. Then drain off the excess water.
1. Now in a bowl, add strained dal, grated carrots, chopped cucumber and combine well. Add salt, lemon juice, chopped coriander leaves, green chillies, pomegranate pearls and grated coconut too. Give a good mix.
1. Serve this salad immediately with your main course meal or enjoy this as a healthy snack!
