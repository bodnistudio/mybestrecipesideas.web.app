##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Green Beans:

1. Prepare 2 cup fresh green beans, trimmed and washed
1. Prepare 1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
1. Prepare 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1. Get 1/2 tsp ground ginger or 1 tsp. fresh
1. Take 2 clove garlic, minced
1. Take 1 sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chinese Green Beans:

1. Place green beans in saucepan. Add about a half a cup of water, steam for about 15 minutes on medium or until they are almost done.
1. While they are cooking, in a small sauce pan add soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic. Cook on medium for 5 minutes. Turn off heat until green beans are done.
1. When green beans are done, drain the water. Add sauce and simmer for 5 minutes. Top with sesame seeds.
