##### Instructions to make Easy Pumpkin Spice &#34;Doughnuts&#34;:

1. Lightly flour your work surface.
1. Roll out pizza dough with a dusted rolling pin into one long log.
1. Cut the dough every inch to make a bunch of small pieces.
1. Deep fry at 350° for approximately 2 minutes or until dough floats.
1. Remove all dough balls at once and place into a large mixing bowl. Immediately add the butter and toss. Then add the pumpkin pie spice and brown sugar. Toss.
