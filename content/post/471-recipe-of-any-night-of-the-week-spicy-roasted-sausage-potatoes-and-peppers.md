##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Roasted Sausage Potatoes and Peppers:

1. Prepare 1 lb potatoes
1. Prepare 12 oz fully cooked link sausage
1. Prepare 1 medium Onion wedged
1. Get 1 medium red pepper wedged
1. Take 1 medium green pepper, cut in 1 in pieces
1. Make ready 1/2 cup pickled pepper rings
1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 1 tsp Creole seasoning
1. Get 1/4 tsp pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Roasted Sausage Potatoes and Peppers:

1. Preheat oven to 400. In large bowl, combine potatoes, sausage, onion, red pepper, green pepper, and pepper rings. Mix oil, creole seasoning and pepper, drizzle over potato mixture.
1. Transfer to baking pan coated with cooking spray. Roast 30-35 min or until veggies are tender.
