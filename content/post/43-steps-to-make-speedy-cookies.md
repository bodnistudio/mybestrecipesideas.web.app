##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies:

1. Make ready 2 cup maida / all-purpose flour
1. Get 1 cup butter
1. Get 3/4 tsp baking soda
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt
1. Get 2 cups choco chips
1. Take 2 eggs
1. Take 3/4 cup brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
1. Get 1/3 cup white sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cookies:

1. Mix butter sugar eggs and essence until fluffy with hand mixer.
1. Add dry ingredients and mix gently
1. Whisk in chocolate chips
1. Make balls and place it evenly in the baking sheet
1. Leave gaps in between the balls to flatten
1. Bake in 160 degree for 16 minutes.
1. Cool down on the baking sheet.serve warm
