<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Hand Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of - 15 oz . can pumpkin ( not pie filling). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of white sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tbsp of cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tsp of water. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of pkg. refrigerated pie crust ( I made my own ). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of flour for dusting. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Hand Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425ยบ. In a medium bowl.  Whisk together pumpkin,  both sugars, spices, 1 egg and cornstarch.  Stir till well mixed..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			On a floured surface lay out pie crust. Cut out rounds. An empty  15oz. Tin works perfectly.Reroll scraps same thickness. You should get about 15 pies..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Using rounds pair them up. Then place about 1 Tbsp. Of filling in the center of rounds. Top with second round. Pinch &amp; seal edges all around with a fork. Place on non stick cookie sheet..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Beat second egg with water to make egg wash. Brush each pie with the wash. Sprinkle each pie with sugar. You can use colored sugar. If you want to. I mixed white and orange together..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 425ยบ . Until golden brown about 12-15 minutes.  Mine took 10 minutes.let pies cool store in airtight container..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
