##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Augratin Potatoes:

1. Make ready 1 packages steamfresh broccoli florets
1. Get 4 can sliced new potatoes(drained)
1. Make ready 1 packages skinnless smoked sausage sliced
1. Prepare 4 tbsp butter or margarine
1. Take 4 tbsp a/p flour
1. Take 2 cup milk
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp garlic powder
1. Take  salt and pepper
1. Prepare 2 cup shredded 3 cheddar  cheese blend




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Augratin Potatoes:

1. Preheat oven to 375°. In the microwave steam your broccoli florets.
1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and blend in flour.
1. Gradually add in milk stirring constantly until thickened.
1. Add garlic powder, salt and pepper and 1/2 cup cheese.
1. In a 9x13 casserole dish add drained potatoes, broccoli, sausage and the  sauce.
1. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake uncovered for 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
