##### Instructions to make Deep dish breakfast pizza:

1. Once dough is ready, grease skillet with bacon grease.
1. Press dough into bottom of pan and up sides. Bake according to directions. Mine called for 8-10 minutes on 450.
1. Cover bottom with gravy.
1. Layer on eggs, crumbles sausage,  and bacon. I know I said 1lb of bacon, but that much never makes on the pizza. It always seems to disappear.
