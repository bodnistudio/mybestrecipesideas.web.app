##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Joy (Cornflake Cookies):

1. Prepare 2 tablespoon honey
1. Make ready 50 gram butter
1. Take 150 grams cornflakes gently crushed to smaller pieces
1. Make ready  Coloured sprinkles or hundreds-and-thousands




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Honey Joy (Cornflake Cookies):

1. Over gentle heat, melt butter and honey in saucepan until the mixture is frothy
1. Add crushed cornflakes into the honey butter mixture and stir to coat the cornflakes evenly.
1. Spoon cornflakes into individual mini baking cases and top with coloured sprinkles. Bake at preheated oven of 150°C for 10-12 minutes, or until the cornflakes turn a darker shade of golden. Once cooled, store them in an airtight container.
