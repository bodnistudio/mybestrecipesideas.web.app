##### Steps to make Potato and Leek Soup:

1. In 3 qt sauce pan simmer potatoes in chicken broth until very tender
1. In a skillet melt butter and sauté leeks until tender, do not brown
1. When potatoes are tender, mash in broth, add leeks and cream
