##### The ingredients needed to make Unique Onion Gratin Soup Made with Sandwich Bread:

1. Prepare 1 Onion
1. Prepare 1 packages Half-strips of bacon
1. Make ready 15 grams Butter
1. Take 1 slice Sandwich bread
1. Take 500 ml Water
1. Make ready 2 cubes Soup stock cube
1. Prepare 1 dash Salt
1. Make ready 1 Black pepper
1. Get 1 Shredded or sliced cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Unique Onion Gratin Soup Made with Sandwich Bread:

1. Mince or thinly slice the onion.
1. Cut the bacon into slices about 5 mm thick.
1. In a small sacuepan, heat the butter. Add the onions and stir over low heat, cooking slowly until they turn amber in colour.
1. Add the bacon to the pot. Separate the slices and cook until they are crispy.
1. Add the water. When it comes to a boil, add in the soup stock cubes, salt, and black pepper to taste.
1. While working through Step 5, cut the bread into 9 separate pieces and toast.  Preheat the oven to 430째F/220째C.
1. Place the hot soup into individual heat-safe bowls. Add the bread and then the cheese. Quickly bake into the oven for 10 minutes.
1. Enjoy the soup while it is piping hot.
