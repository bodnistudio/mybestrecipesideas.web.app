1. Mix the other half of Taco seasoning  to your pizza crust dough, blending it without  working  it to much.
1. Preheat your oven to 450  degrees.
1. Spread your pizza  crust  dough onto a greased  cookie sheet, and cook for ten minutes.
1. Take pizza crust out of oven, spread ground  beef mixture  on top, sprinkle  Taco cheese on top and put back in oven for another  10 minutes.
