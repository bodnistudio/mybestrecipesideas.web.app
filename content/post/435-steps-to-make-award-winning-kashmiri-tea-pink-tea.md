##### Instructions to make Kashmiri tea /pink tea:

1. Take a pan add cold water to it then add green tea, cinnamon stick, green cardamom, star anise and boil till the water becomes less then add 2 cups of cold water and again bring it to a boil and add salt and baking soda. Now with a spoon beat and add ice then bring it to a boil. Then add sugar and boil it for 10 minutes in a medium flame.
1. Drain kawa and keep aside. You can also keep the kawa in the fridge for ten days and use it. Then take 3 tbsp of kawa and mix with one cup of milk then boil it.
1. Take a pan add milk and bring it to a boil.
1. Take a cup add 1/4 kawa, 1/3 milk, boil it and now pour it in the glass and add some dry fruits on top for garnishing.
