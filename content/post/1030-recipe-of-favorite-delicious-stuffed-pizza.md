##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious Stuffed Pizza:

1. Make ready 1 Pillsbury Pizza Crust
1. Get 1/2 can of Pizza sauce
1. Take 6 Morezzela sticks (I use ficello)
1. Get 15 slice Pepperonni
1. Take 1 You can add mushrooms, onions and peppers at taste
1. Take 1/2 block of 300g Mozzarela cheese (grated)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Delicious Stuffed Pizza:

1. Put the Pillsbury crust on a 12 inches pizza pan.
1. Pull the crust approximatively 1 inch outside the pan.
1. With the 1 inch of crust, roll the crust around the mozzarella sticks.
1. Put the Pizza sauce in the middle of the crust.
1. Put the 4/5 of your cheese on the Pizza.
1. Add pepperonies.
1. Put the rest of your cheese on top.
1. Bake the Pizza (425°F) for 12 to 16 minutes.
