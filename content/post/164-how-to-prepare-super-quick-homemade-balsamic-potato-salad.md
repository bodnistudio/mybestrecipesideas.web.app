##### Steps to make Balsamic Potato Salad:

1. Whisk together vinegar, olive oil, sun-dried tomato oil, mustard, salt and pepper; toss with potato mixture. Let stand for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with basil.
1. Serve and enjoy!
