##### The ingredients needed to make Rigatoni With Sausage And Kale:

1. Take 2 cup Uncooked rigatoni
1. Prepare 1 tsp Table salt, or to taste, for cooking pasta
1. Prepare 8 oz Uncooked sweet Italian turkey sausage
1. Prepare 4 cup Uncooked kale, roughly chopped into bite-size pieces
1. Take 1 cup Canned chicken broth
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Black pepper
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Table salt
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Rigatoni With Sausage And Kale:

1. Cook rigatoni in salted water according to package directions; drain.
1. Meanwhile, remove sausage from casing and cook in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat, breaking up as it cooks, about 3 minutes. Add kale; cook, stirring frequently, until limp, 3-5 minutes.
1. Add broth to skillet and scrape up browned bits with a wooden spoon; season with salt and pepper. Cover, reduce heat to low; cook until kale is tender, 5-10 minutes. Stir in rigatoni; heat through.
1. Sprinkle each serving with about 2 tbsp of cheese before serving.
