1. Drain the mushrooms and add its soaking water to the soup pot. Slice the mushrooms, throw away the tough stems and add to the soup pot.
1. Add in the green onions, celery and cabbage and cook for 15 minutes before putting in the noodles. Then, simmer for 10 more minutes and serve immediately.
