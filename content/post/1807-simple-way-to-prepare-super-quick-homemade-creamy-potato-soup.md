##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Potato Soup:

1. Get 3 lbs red potatoes, peeled and cubed
1. Prepare 4 carrots, thinly sliced
1. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
1. Prepare 3 cloves garlic
1. Get 1 Tbsp oregano
1. Prepare 1 Tbsp basil
1. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
1. Take 8 cups chicken broth
1. Take 1 lb spicy Italian sausage
1. Prepare 1 pkg bacon
1. Prepare 1 pkg cream cheese
1. Prepare 1/2 cup sour cream
1. Take 1/2 cup Shredded cheese
1. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken
1. Make ready 1 cup milk
1. Get 1/4 cup flour




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Creamy Potato Soup:

1. Chop bacon, cook in skillet. Remove from skillet and set aside a small amount for later, then cook sausage. Remove sausage and sauté onions and garlic. Place everything in large pot
1. Add chicken broth and spices to large pot, bring to a boil. Add potatoes and carrots and boil on medium until cooked through, about 15 minutes.
1. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, Shredded cheese, cream of chicken and milk in separate bowl. Add to pot and simmer for another 12 minutes. Add flour to desired thickness. Serve with Shredded cheese and bacon. Green onions optional
