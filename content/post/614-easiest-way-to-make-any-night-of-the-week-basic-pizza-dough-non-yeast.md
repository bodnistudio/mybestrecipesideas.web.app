1. Make ready 1/2 cup milk

##### Instructions to make Basic Pizza Dough Non Yeast:

1. Basic non yeast pizza dough
1. Sieve then flour and add salt in a bowl then make a well in the middle
1. In seperate bowl beat egg with oil and milk
1. Add egg mixture unto flour and mix into soft dough
1. Knead lightly on flour surface then divide into 2 medium or 3 small size pizza on an oiled pizza tray or pizza stone
1. This point the pizza dough can be wrap up and freeze up to a week
1. Roll thinly and place pizza on baking stone then add tomato paste then top fresh slice tomato
1. Add ham and chedder cheese then top some olive oil
1. Bake at 200 C for 15 to 20 minute depending on the thickness and size of the pizza, half way through add some fresh mint and continue to bake
