##### Instructions to make Fresh tomato Sauce:

1. Saute tomatoes and olive oil in a saute pan on medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring on occasion.
1. Add garlic, salt and herbs. Stir, lower heat and simmer for another 10-15 minutes. Depending on how much moisture is in the tomatoes you may need to add some water to the sauce if needed.
1. Boil several quarts of water in a large stock pot. Add a large pinch of salt to the water as it nears a boil. Cook pasta as directed. Take care not to over cook fresh pasta. Strain and return to stick pot.
1. Place pasta in pasta bowls, top with tomato sauce and serve with grated parmesan cheese.
