##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Apple Streusel Muffins:

1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1. Get 2 cup sugar
1. Get 1 tbsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
1. Make ready 2 eggs lightly beaten
1. Make ready 1 cup libby&#39;s solid pack pumpkin
1. Make ready 1/2 cup vegetable oil
1. Get 2 cup peeled finely chopped apples
1. Prepare 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
1. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
1. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1. Take 4 tsp cut in 4 tsp. butter

##### Steps to make Pumpkin Apple Streusel Muffins:

1. In a large bowl, combine first five ingredients; set aside.
1. In medium bowl, combine eggs, pumpkin and oil.  Add liquid ingredients to dry.
1. Stir just until moistened.  Stir in apples.  Spoon batter into greased or paper lined cups filling 3/4 full.
1. In small bowl, combine streusel toppings, cut in the 4 tsp. butter until mixture is crumbly.  Sprinkle over batter.  Bake in pre-heated 350°F oven for 35 - 40 minutes or till knife inserted comes out clean.
1. For 6 giant muffins, increase baking time for 40 - 45 minutes.
