##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Sausage Pasta:

1. Make ready 1 1/2 lb Italian Sausage
1. Prepare 1 box FarFalle (Bow Tie) pasta
1. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil
1. Get 4 tbsp minced garlic (fresh or jar)
1. Make ready 1 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
1. Make ready 4 cup Baby Spinach




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Spinach and Sausage Pasta:

1. Follow directions and cook pasta according to box drain and set aside
1. In a large skillet add olive oil, garlic and break up sausage. Cook on medium heat covered until meat is just about done (about 10 mins) stirring occasionally.
1. Reduce heat and stir in pasta, washed spinach stir well. Last add Parmesan cheese and stir. Turn off heat and cover. Let stand 2 mins to allow cheese to melt. Serve with home made garlic bread :)&#39;
