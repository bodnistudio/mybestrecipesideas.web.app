##### Steps to make Sabudana khichdi:

1. Wash and soak sabudana in a seive, overnight. Keep a plate filled with water under the seive
1. Boil potatoes and chop it in cubes. Powder groundnuts and grated coconut.
1. Heat a non-stick pan. Add 2tsp ghee and 1tsp oil, jeera, curry leaves, chillies and cashews.
1. Add chopped potatoes, salt, pepper, sugar and ground nut powder and mix well.
1. Add sabudana, lemon juice and grated coconut, reserving some for decoration. Mix thoroughly and cook for 5minutes more.
1. Keep it covered for 5minutes. Put sabudana khichdi in a plate. Sprinkle some coriander leaves, banana and pomegranate. Serve right away with some traits like banana pomegranate raita.
