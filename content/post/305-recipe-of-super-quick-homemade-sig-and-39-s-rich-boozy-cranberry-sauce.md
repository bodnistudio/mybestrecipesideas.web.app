##### The ingredients needed to make Sig&#39;s Rich, Boozy Cranberry Sauce:

1. Get 1 large cup of water
1. Get 2 tbsp runny honey (to taste)
1. Make ready 3 tbsp dark brown sugar (to taste)
1. Get 2 tbsp cherry brandy or other fruit liqueur
1. Take 2 tbsp port
