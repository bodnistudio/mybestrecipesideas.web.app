##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Karaage for Bento:

1. Make ready 280 grams Chicken breast or thigh
1. Take 1 tbsp ●Soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp ●Sake
1. Take 1 tsp ●Mirin
1. Make ready 1 tsp ●Garlic (grated)
1. Prepare 1 tsp ●Ginger (grated)
1. Take 2 tbsp or more ◎Cake flour
1. Get 2 tbsp or more ◎Katakuriko
1. Get 1 Vegetable oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken Karaage for Bento:

1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Mix the ● ingredients together. Marinade the chicken in the ● ingredients for 20-30 minutes.
1. Put the ◎ ingredients in a plastic bag, and add the chicken. Shake the bag to coat the chicken, then remove the excess flour.
1. Deep fry in oil at 170C.
