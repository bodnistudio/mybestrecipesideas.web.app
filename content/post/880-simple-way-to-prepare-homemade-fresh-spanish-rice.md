##### The ingredients needed to make Fresh Spanish Rice:

1. Prepare 1 cup long grain rice
1. Get 2 cup steaming hot water
1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
1. Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
1. Take 2 cup chicken broth
1. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp butter
1. Take 1/2 bunch cilantro, minced
1. Prepare 2 medium-large tomatoes, chopped
1. Prepare tsp salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fresh Spanish Rice:

1. Heat water, pour over rice in a heat proof bowl, and let rest for 20 minutes.
1. Heat oil and garlic in a pan.
1. Add drained and rinsed rice. And suate a little before adding the pepper &amp; salt.
1. Pour in broth and bring to a simmer, cover and reduce heat.
1. After about 20 minutes, uncover add butter and stir.
1. Mix in fresh cilantro and tomatoes
