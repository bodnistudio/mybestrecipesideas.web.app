##### The ingredients needed to make Szechuan Chicken and Vegetables Over Rice:

1. Take  light sesame oil
1. Take  thawed, boneless, skinless chicken breast tenders
1. Prepare  dried red pepper flakes
1. Take  package fresh stir fry vegetables
1. Make ready  low-sodium teriyaki sauce
1. Get  brown rice, dry, not instant




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Szechuan Chicken and Vegetables Over Rice:

1. Begin cooking brown rice according to package instructions.
1. Meanwhile, heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
1. Add chicken and sprinkle with pepper flakes; stir fry for 3 minutes
1. Add vegetables and teriyaki sauce.
1. Stir fry for 5 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender and chicken is thoroughly cooked (no longer pink and internal temperature reaches 165°F).
1. Keep warm until rice is complete.
1. For 1 serving: pour 1 cup of chicken mix over 2/3 cup cooked rice.
