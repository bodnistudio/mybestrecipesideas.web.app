##### The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Tomato Soup:

1. Prepare 1 28oz Can of Diced Tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 8oz Can of Tomato Sauce
1. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh finely chopped basil
1. Prepare 3 clove Minced Garlic
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Salt
1. Prepare 1 tsp pepper
1. Make ready 1 Medium White Onion-diced
1. Make ready 1 cup Heavy Cream or Half &amp; Half
1. Take 4 cup Chicken broth
1. Get 2 cup Shredded Parmesan
1. Make ready  Roux
1. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
1. Take 1/4 cup flour
1. Get 1 cup heavy cream or half&amp;half




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Crockpot Tomato Soup:

1. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and next 7 ingredients (through chicken broth) into crockpot. Cover and cook for 2 hours on High or 4-8 on Low. Occasionally stir and scrape down sides.
1. About 30-40 minutes before serving transfer to a blender or food processor and puree until smooth. Then return to crockpot.
1. Once blended prepare roux: In a saucepan melt butter on medium heat. Add flour, mix until clumpy, continuously stirring for about a minute until flour is blended. Slowly whisk in heavy cream or half&amp;half adding a little at a time.
1. Pour roux and parmesan cheese into crockpot and mix. Allow to cook another 20-30 minutes until cheese is completely melted and give it a good stir before serving
1. Top with additional basil and parmesan cheese if so desired and serve with crackers!
