##### Instructions to make Pork Steak Tagalog w Steamed Onions and Mushrooms:

1. In a bowl place meat marinade then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 5 hours. Slice onions in rings and drain mushrooms but save juice.
1. Heat pan in med-hi and oil cook meat until lightly brown then take out put aside. Take mushrooms juice and deglaze and scrape bottom of pan then return meat pour in marinade. Place mushrooms and onions on top to steam place finger chilies cover. Then simmer for few minutes.
1. Turn off heat and serve w hot rice.
