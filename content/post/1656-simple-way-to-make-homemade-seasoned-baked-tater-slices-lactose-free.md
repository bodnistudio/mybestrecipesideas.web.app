##### Instructions to make Seasoned Baked Tater Slices (Lactose Free):

1. Add a little Earthbalance butter to pan.  Add sliced potatos.  Cook over medium heat, and flip to cook both sides of potato slices.
1. While potatos are cooking, season with Garlic Powder, Oregano, Onion Powder, Greek Blend Seasoning and Basil.
