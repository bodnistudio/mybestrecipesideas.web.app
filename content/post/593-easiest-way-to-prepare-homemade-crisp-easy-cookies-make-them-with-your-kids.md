##### The ingredients needed to make Crisp Easy Cookies - Make Them with Your Kids:

1. Get 150 grams Cake flour
1. Get 80 grams Margarine
1. Get 50 grams Sugar (I recommend powdered sugar )
1. Make ready 2 Plastic bag

##### Steps to make Crisp Easy Cookies - Make Them with Your Kids:

1. Put the margarine in a plastic bag and knead from the outside of the bag It&#39;s ready once it&#39;s nice and soft.
1. In another plastic bag, combine the cake flour and sugar, twist the opening, then shake it up well.
1. Add the second bag of shaken flour to the bag of margarine, then knead it again It&#39;s ready when it&#39;s no longer floury.
1. Roll it out to a 5 mm thickness, then cut out cookies with your favorite cookie cutters Rolling the dough flat while still in the bag to minimize clean up ( )
1. You could also squeeze the dough with your hands.
1. Bake for 25 minutes in an oven preheated to 170℃. They&#39;re done.
1. If you substitute a portion of the flour with cocoa powder, you can make chocolate cookies.
