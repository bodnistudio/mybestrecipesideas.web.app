##### The ingredients needed to make Nanbanzuke Fish Marinade:

1. Prepare 10 small, young mutsu or any white fish
1. Take 1 Cucumber
1. Make ready 1/2 Red bell pepper
1. Make ready 50 ml Vinegar
1. Get 2 Tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
1. Prepare  Flour as needed
1. Make ready  Red Chili pepper if you like 1 pepper
1. Take 2 cups Oil for frying about




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Nanbanzuke Fish Marinade:

1. Fillet and clean fish if needed. If using a large fillet, cut into smaller pieces. Season with salt and lightly dust with flour.
1. Heat oil on medium-high. Once hot, fry fish for 2-3 minutes on each side until crispy and golden brown.
1. Slice cucumber and red pepper and place all vegetables into a tupperware container. Mix the vinegar, sugar and soy sauce together in small bowl.
1. Lay the fried fish over top vegetables and pour over the marinade dressing. Add the chili pepper. Marinate overnight in fridge, mixing occasionally.
