##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Spicy Ramen w/ Leftover Steak:

1. Make ready 1/4 Cup chopped steak
1. Get 1 tsp olive oil
1. Get 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
1. Get 1/8 Cup Brown Sugar
1. Get 2 tbs Garlic Chili Sauce
1. Get 1 pkg Ramen Noodles (discard flavor packet)
1. Get  Sliced green onions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Quick Spicy Ramen w/ Leftover Steak:

1. Cook Ramen noodles in boiling water for three minutes. Drain and set aside.
1. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a medium skillet. Add steak and warm for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Add brown sugar, soy sauce and chili garlic sauce to skillet and stir to combine. Reduce heat and simmer for five minutes.
1. Add noodles to skillet and toss to coat. Sprinkle with green onions.
