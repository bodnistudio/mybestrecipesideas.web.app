##### Instructions to make Crockpot Venison BBQ:

1. Marinate venison in barbecue sauce, garlic salt, and onion powder.
1. Turn on slow cooker to low, add water, then add venison mixture and mix until level.
1. Top this off with more barbecue sauce if wanted.
1. Cook on low for 4-6 hours.
1. Once done cooking, scoop out meat and chop on cutting board.
1. Add meat back to sauce once chopped.
1. Enjoy!
