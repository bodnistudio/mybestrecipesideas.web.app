##### The ingredients needed to make Buttered Sweet Potatoes with Sweet Red Beans and Vanilla Ice Cream:

1. Make ready 400 grams Sweet potato
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Margarine
1. Make ready 1 (to taste), about 1/3 teaspoon  Shio-koji (or table salt)
1. Get 1 Vanilla ice cream
1. Make ready 1 Anko




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Buttered Sweet Potatoes with Sweet Red Beans and Vanilla Ice Cream:

1. This time, I used small, 100 g sweet potatoes. Peel if desired and cut into 1 cm slices. If using large sweet potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces.
1. Soak in a bit of water and drain. Put in a heatproof bowl, wrap with plastic, and microwave at 600 W for about 2:30 minutes.
1. Put the butter-flavored margarine and shio-koji in a pot. Add the sweet potatoes and turn the heat on medium. Sauté until browned.
1. When the sweet potatoes are crispy and is fragrant, transfer to a dish.
1. Top with ice cream and anko.
1. It&#39;s up to you, but if you top the sweet potatoes with the ice cream and anko while still hot, then you can eat it as it coats the sweet potatoes. It&#39;s so yummy!
