##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Bianca:

1. Get 10 g olive oil
1. Prepare 5 g salt

1. Take 50 g water
1. Make ready  cheese as much as you want
1. Make ready  anchovy

##### Steps to make Pizza Bianca:
