##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Roasted Potatoes:

1. Take 8-12 baby rooster potatoes
1. Make ready 2 1/2 Tbls butter
1. Get  Red Robin season salt
1. Make ready  Garlic and Herb seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Simple Roasted Potatoes:

1. Clean potatoes and poke each potato twice
1. Microwave potatoes 5-6 minutes until starting to soften
1. Start melting butter in frying pan and Cut each potato in half
1. Add potatoes to the pan and sprinkle seasonings to your taste
1. Fry till potatoes start to crisp on the cut ends
1. Enjoy!!
