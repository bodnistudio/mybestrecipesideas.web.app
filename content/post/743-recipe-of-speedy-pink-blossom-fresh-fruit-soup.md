##### The ingredients needed to make Pink Blossom Fresh Fruit Soup:

1. Take 2 apples (cut into cubes, soak them into a salt water)
1. Get 2 oranges (cut into cubes)
1. Prepare 125 gr strawberries
1. Make ready 1 can fruit cocktail (don&#39;t use the syrup)
1. Take 1 soursop (seedless)
1. Prepare 1 red dragon fruit (cut into cubes)
1. Get 2 tbs basil seeds (soak with 1 cup of hot water)
1. Make ready 1 litre low fat milk
1. Make ready 80 ml condense milk (could be less or more)
1. Prepare 1 tsp banana essence
1. Take  Simple syrup:
1. Prepare 250 gr sugar
1. Prepare 250 ml water
1. Get 1 bowl ice cubes




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pink Blossom Fresh Fruit Soup:

1. Prepare all of your fruits, pour all of them into one big bowl.
1. Meanwhile prepare your basil seeds by soaking them into hot water and let it sit for a while for later purpose.
1. Into your fruits bowl, add the fruit cocktail without the syrup, basil seeds, milk, condense milk, banana essence, simple syrup (adjust into your sweetness level).
1. Add some ice cubes to make it less thick and make it cooler. Put it into your chiller about 1 until 2 hours before you serve it.
