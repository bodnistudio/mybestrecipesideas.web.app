##### Instructions to make Mango Ice Cream:

1. Wash and peel the mangoes, cut into pieces
1. Now blend the mangoes in a mixer and make mango purée.
1. Freeze the mango purée for 3-4 hrs in the freezer
1. After that put the mango purée in the food processor jar and churn it for 2-3 minutes.
1. Now add sugar,cream,vanilla essence and saffron strands soaked in milk. Keep churning for 10-15 mints or till the mango purée is thickened and turning into a smooth mixture. Now pour the batter into a bowl or ice cream tin having a lid. Cover the ice cream with a cling wrap before putting the lid.
1. Refrigerate overnight or for5-6 hrs.
1. Take out of refrigerator and the ice cream is ready to serve.
