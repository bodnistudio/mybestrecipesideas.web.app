##### The ingredients needed to make Maque Choux, Tomatoes and Corn:

1. Prepare 2 large vine ripened tomatoes
1. Prepare 1/4 cup bacon drippings
1. Get 1/4 cup butter
1. Take To taste salt and pepper
1. Get 1 pint water
1. Prepare 1 medium green bell pepper

##### Steps to make Maque Choux, Tomatoes and Corn:

1. Drain the juices of the corn. Boil the water in a small pot, and put the tomatoes into the water for 15 seconds remove and peel and dice the tomatoes.
1. Wash and slice the celery. Dice the onion. Chop the bell pepper.
1. Heat the drippings and butter. Add all the ingredients except the tomatoes, to the pan.
1. When the corn mixture has sautéed for 15-20 minutes add the tomatoes.
