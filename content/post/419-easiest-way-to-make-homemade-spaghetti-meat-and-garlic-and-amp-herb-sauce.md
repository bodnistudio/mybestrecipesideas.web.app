##### Instructions to make Spaghetti, Meat, and Garlic &amp; Herb sauce:

1. Boil enough water to cook spaghetti, add salt and 1/4 of an onion
1. Reduce the heat to medium, and cook spaghetti (follow directions on box; or cook until tender).
1. Remove cooked spaghetti and wash and drain with cool water (set aside)
1. Place ground beef in a hot pan with 1/4 of a cup of water over medium-high heat
1. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder (about 1 tsp each; taste and add more if necessary)
1. While meat cooks, chop onion and garlic into small cube pieces
1. Once meat has browned and water is almost dried (optional: remove excess oil from pan) add onion and garlic
1. Once onions are clear in color, add sauce and cover, allow to boil...once bubbly reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 5-10 minutes
1. Either serve meat sauce on top of spaghetti or mix spaghetti and meat sauce in a deep pan or dish
