##### The ingredients needed to make Probiotic Pomegranate Juice with Yakult:

1. Prepare 1/2 Pomegranate
1. Prepare 1 bottle yakult
1. Prepare as needed water
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon Basil seed (soaked)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Probiotic Pomegranate Juice with Yakult:

1. Soak Basil seeds in water. Remove pomegranate seeds.
1. Take pomegranate seeds and a cup of water and put it in the juicer jar. Blend. Sieve and remove the hard seed particles.
1. Take a serving glass, add soaked basil seeds, pour yakult, pomegranate juice and serve immediately.
