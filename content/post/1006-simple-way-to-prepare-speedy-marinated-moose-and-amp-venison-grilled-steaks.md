##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Moose &amp; Venison Grilled Steaks:

1. Make ready 4 Wild game Steaks....will work for almost all wild game steaks
1. Take 1 cup olive oil
1. Take 1 tbsp salt
1. Take 1/2 tbsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1/4 tbsp crushed red pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Marinated Moose &amp; Venison Grilled Steaks:

1. combine salt &amp; peppers in bowl &amp; sprinkle evenly onto steaks....
1. put steaks into a sealed bag with the olive oil &amp; let marinade overnight or minimum of 4 hours
1. have grill on high heat &amp; cook until rare or medium rare....do not over cook or meat will be dry &amp; not taste good....wild game has hardly any fat &amp; is best if not overcooked
1. plate &amp; serve
