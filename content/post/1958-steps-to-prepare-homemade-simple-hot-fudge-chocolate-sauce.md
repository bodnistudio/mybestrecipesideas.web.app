##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Hot Fudge Chocolate Sauce:

1. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
1. Make ready 5 tsp cocoa
1. Get 1 8 oz. can elaborated milk
1. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Simple Hot Fudge Chocolate Sauce:

1. combine sugar and cocoa in a sauce pan and mix to break up cocoa clumps
1. add canned milk and stir
1. cook on high, stiring constantly (watch carefully because this stuff boils oven easily)
1. bring to a vigorous boil and turn heat down to low
1. simmer, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens slightly...between 15 and 30 minutes usually
1. remove from heat and serve hot over ice cream
1. NOTE: If you want to eliminate the butter and use low fat canned milk to reduce calories, it&#39;s still delicious.
