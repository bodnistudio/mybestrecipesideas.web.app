##### The ingredients needed to make Pomegrante Juice:

1. Take 1 kg pomegranate (about 5 large)
1. Prepare 1 lemon (or 4 oranges or 3 to 4 kiwis or any citrus food)
1. Make ready 1-2 inches ginger(if making in a slow juicer)
1. Get 2-3 pinches Himalayn salt (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pomegrante Juice:

1. 1.Add the mixed fruits to your blender or juicer chute. If making in blender, fill only half of jar.
1. 2. Pulse and a few times until the pomegranate seeds are crushed.
1. 3.Pass through a strainer and collect the juice in a large bowl. If making for baby, pass it through a muslin cloth placed over the strainer.
1. 4. Serve pomegranate juice immediately.
