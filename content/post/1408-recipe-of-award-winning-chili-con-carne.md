##### Steps to make Chili con Carne:

1. While these are browning add remaining ingredients except the meat into a large pot set on low.
1. Add the browned onions and peppers into the pot.
1. Dice the flap meat into small pieces and brown in same pan as onions.
1. Simmer for 30 minutes or longer
1. Serve with shredded cheese, diced onions, or crackers on top. Also can be served over white rice.
1. If you want to thicken with corn starch add the corn starch to a small amount of cold water and stir before adding to chili or it will be lumpy.
