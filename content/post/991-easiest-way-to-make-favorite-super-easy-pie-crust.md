##### Steps to make Super Easy Pie Crust:

1. Sift the flour and salt together 3 or 4 times. Put into a bowl.
1. Chill the oil and milk in the refrigerator in the same cup.
1. Put all of the chilled oil and milk into the flour, quickly combine and bring it all together.
1. Once brought together, use your bare hands to form into a ball. Don&#39;t knead it!
