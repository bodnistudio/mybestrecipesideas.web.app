##### The ingredients needed to make Boudin Balls:

1. Get 2 eggs
1. Make ready 1 cup Milk
1. Make ready 1 Flour or Cajun Fish fry
1. Make ready 1 box of Boudin
1. Take 1 tbsp Cajun Seasoning
1. Make ready 1 Peanut Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Boudin Balls:

1. Mix eggs in a bowl with milk and stir
1. Cut boudin into smal squares you can rol them into balls if you want.
1. Have breading flour or fish fry in a container to bread (I use plastic bags )
1. Heat peanut oil in pan or fryer to 350°F
1. Take boudin balls and dip in milk-eggs mixture fully coat.
1. Then drop into grease for 10min at most or until golden brown color
