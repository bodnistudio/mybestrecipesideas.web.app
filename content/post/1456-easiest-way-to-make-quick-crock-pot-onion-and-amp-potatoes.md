##### Steps to make Crock Pot Onion &amp; Potatoes:

1. Rinse &amp; peel potatoes. You can cut them into smaller slices if you prefer. Place inside of crock. Cut onion &amp; place large slices inside crock filling in the holes.
1. Season to taste. I usually add pepper &amp; garlic powder. Drizzle a generous amount of Butter infused Olive Oil overtop. If you don&#39;t have access to Butter infused Oil; regular Olive Oil will work but add a dash of Butter flavored seasonings.
1. Cook on High 4-6 hours or Low 6-8 hours. If you&#39;ve cut them into smaller pieces you&#39;ll need to adjust your cooking time; as they won&#39;t need to cook as long. Ensure potatoes are fully cooked before serving. I stir potatoes every so often to ensure they don&#39;t stick to the side of the crock. Enjoy!
