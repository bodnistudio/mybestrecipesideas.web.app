<h3>The ingredients needed to make Banoffee Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 5 of ripe cavendish banana. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 can of condensed milk or caramel in tin can. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 box of cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 5 tbsp of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 box of graham crackers (crushed). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 tbsp of lemon juice or vinegar (optional). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of water (optional). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Banoffee Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Melt butter on low fire (set aside about 5 tablespoons) and add sugar. Pour 3/4 of the condensed milk and stir. Continue stirring (make sure it is on low fire as it burns easily) for 10-15 minutes or until light brown. Another way to make toffee/caramel is to put unopened condensed milk (can) in a pot of water and bring to boil on low fire (make sure it is submerged in water) for 2 hours. Let it cool for 45 minutes and then open can..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix crushed graham crackers (set aside a few tablespoons for decoration) with remaining melted butter and press on to the base of the cake pan. Make sure to cover holes/gaps. Slice bananas (set aside a few slices for decoration) and arrange on top of the graham cracker base/pie crust..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour toffee/caramel on top of sliced bananas and spread evenly..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Whisk/beat cream and remaining conedensed milk until stiff. Pour on top of toffee..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Decorate top with remaining crushed grahams and sliced bananas. To keep sliced bananas from turning brown, soak them in water with lemon juice or vinegar first. Chill for 3-4 hours and serve..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
