##### The ingredients needed to make Golden Potato Soup:

1. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Canola oil
1. Take 3 large Potatos, peeled and cubed
1. Get 1/2 tsp Garlic salt
1. Take 1/4 tsp Pepper
1. Prepare 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) Chicken broth
1. Make ready 1 large Onion, chopped
1. Get 1/4 cup Crumbled cooked bacon
1. Make ready 1 Garlic clove, minced
1. Prepare 1 tsp Dried rosemary, crushed
1. Get 1 tsp Dried thyme
1. Make ready 1 cup Water
1. Make ready 2 tsp Chicken bouillon granules
1. Prepare 1 cup Heavy whipping cream
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Cracker Barrell Cheddar Cheese, shredded




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Golden Potato Soup:

1. In lg saucepan, heat butter &amp; oil until butter is melted. Add potatoes, garlic salt and pepper. Cook over med heat for 10 min or until potatoes are tender &amp; lightly browned.
1. Stir in broth, onion, bacon, garlic, rosemary and thyme. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover &amp; simmer for 20 minutes. Stir in the water and bouillon. Gradually stir in cream; heat through. Add cheese, stirring until melted.
