##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun Sausage &amp; Rice:

1. Get 3 C. Cooked Brown Rice
1. Take 1/3 C. Onion, diced
1. Get 1 Green Bell Pepper, diced
1. Get 1 Red Bell Pepper, diced
1. Get 4 oz. Kale, chopped
1. Make ready 1 tsp. Garlic, minced
1. Make ready 1 Can Red Beans, drained &amp; rinsed
1. Make ready 1 Turkey Smoked Sausage, halved &amp; sliced
1. Take 2 tsp. Cajun Seasoning (I used Tony Chachere&#39;s Creole Seasoning)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cajun Sausage &amp; Rice:

1. In a skillet, on medium heat, cook onion, both bell peppers and garlic.
1. After about 8 minutes add kale, cook another 6 minutes.
1. Add sausage and beans. Toss together well and cook for about 10 minutes until warmed through.
1. Add rice and seasoning. Mix together well. Heat through another 10 minutes. Enjoy!
