##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Chinese Chive and Chicken Breast Fritters:

1. Prepare 2 Chicken breasts
1. Prepare 1 bunch Chinese chives
1. Take 200 grams Plain flour
1. Get 300 ml Water
1. Prepare 1 Egg
1. Take 1 tbsp Sesame oil
1. Take 2 tsp Salt and pepper
1. Take 1/2 tsp Grated garlic
1. Take 1 Kimchi




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Chinese Chive and Chicken Breast Fritters:

1. Chop up the Chinese chives into about 1cm long pieces.
1. Roughly chop up the chicken.
1. Chop it up again a little with your knife.
1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl.
1. Mix it all up. At this point you can put half the mixture in a separate bowl and add some kimchi to it, which will be delicious too.
1. Put spoonfuls of the mixture in a heated frying pan.
1. Turn occasionally until golden brown on both sides, and it&#39;s done.
1. In our house, we eat the kimchi fritters dipped in a sauce made with ponzu sauce with a little rayu spicy chili sesame oil in it.
1. We eat the regular fritters with mayonnaise-flavored soy sauce.
