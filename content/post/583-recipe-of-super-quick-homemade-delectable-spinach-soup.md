##### The ingredients needed to make Delectable Spinach Soup:

1. Prepare 1 packages whole leaf spinach
1. Make ready 3 white potatoes
1. Take 1/2 packages cauliflower
1. Make ready 1/2 packages broccoli florets
1. Make ready 1 vegetable bullion cube
1. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Get 1/4 tsp celery seed
1. Make ready 1 pinch garlic powder
1. Take 1/2 can evaporated milk
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup water
1. Get 3 tbsp butter
1. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Delectable Spinach Soup:

1. Add water to a saucepan and add bouillon cube. Peel potatoes and cut into chunks. Bring ingredients to a boil. Add garlic powder.
1. Add cauliflower and broccoli. Top with spinach and stir. Let vegetables come to a rolling boil. Add celery seed and butter.
1. Slowly add milk, folding vegetables into the mixture constantly. Let all ingredients come to a boil. Add salt and pepper to taste. Turn down heat to simmer, add cheddar cheese. Serve warm. Enjoy!!!
