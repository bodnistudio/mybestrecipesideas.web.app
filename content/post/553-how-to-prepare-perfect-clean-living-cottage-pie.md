##### Instructions to make Clean living cottage pie:

1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large frying pan
1. Fry the onion for 15 mins until soft
1. Add bacon and carrots to the pan and fry until bacon is cooked and carrots are soft about 10 mins
1. Add minced beef to the pan and saute till brown
1. Season with salt and pepper
1. Add stock and cook down until 60% has evaporated
1. Place cooked cauliflower, carrot and sweet potato in a food processor and puree with the remaining olive oil until smooth
1. Pour minced beef mixture into oven dish
1. Pour mash over the top
1. Bake at 200c for 30mins
