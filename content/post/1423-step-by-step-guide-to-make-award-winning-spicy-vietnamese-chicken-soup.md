##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Vietnamese Chicken Soup:

1. Get 1 oz cellophane noodles
1. Get 8 oz boneless skinless chicken breasts
1. Take 1 tbsp peanut oil
1. Make ready 2 tsp minced garlic
1. Make ready 2 tsp minced ginger
1. Take 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1. Prepare 28 oz Chicken broth
1. Prepare 2 tbsp fish sauce
1. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh chopped cilantro
1. Get 1 tbsp fresh chopped basil
1. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh chopped green onions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Vietnamese Chicken Soup:

1. Soak noodles in hot water.
1. Thinly cut boneless skinless chicken breasts into thin strips.
1. Heat oil in deep skillet on Medium high heat.  Add the chicken, garlic, ginger and red pepper flakes.
1. Cook until chicken is brown. Add chicken broth and fish sauce. Boil for 8 minutes.
1. Drain noodles and cut shorter.
1. Place noodles on bottom of a bowl. Pour soup over noodles. Then sprinkle cilantro, green onions and basil on top.
1. OPTIONAL: Add Siracha Sauce
