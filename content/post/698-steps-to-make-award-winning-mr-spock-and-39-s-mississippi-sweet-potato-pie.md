##### The ingredients needed to make Mr. Spock&#39;s Mississippi Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Make ready 4 oz butter
1. Take 2 cup cooked and mashed sweet potatos (2 cups = 3.25 potatos)
1. Take 2 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1 can evaporated milk (5 ounce)
1. Get 1 tsp vanilla
1. Make ready 3 each eggs
1. Get 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 1 each deep dish pie shell (or 2 shallow) unbaked
1. Take 1 pinch nutmeg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Mr. Spock&#39;s Mississippi Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Peel and boil potatos
1. Mix butter, potato&#39;s, sugar, and evaporated milk until well blended
1. Add vanilla, eggs, nutmeg and cinnamon, mix well
1. Pour into pie shell
1. Bake in oven at 350°F for 1 hour or until set.
