##### Instructions to make Chocolate ice cream:

1. Firstly take a bowl and add 1 cup chilled milk. Add cocoa powder and mix it well. Make a lump free mixture.
1. Heat a kadai and add milk. Boil it on a low flame.
1. After one boil, add sugar in a milk and boil it. Boil the milk for at least 15 minutes.
1. Now add cocoa powder mixture in the kadai and boil again for 5 minutes.
1. Now turn off the flame and let it cool down completely.
1. Now pour the whipping cream in another bowl and whip it with a hand mixer. Whip it until it turns fluffy and light.
1. Now add whipped cream in a ice cream mixture and mix it.
1. Pour the mixture in a air tight container and cover it with cling sheet. Then cover the container with lid. Freeze it for 8-10 hours.
1. Now again beat the ice cream and again freeze it same way for 8-10 hours.
1. Then scoop the ice cream in a serving bowl. Garnish it with chocolate syrup and mint leaves. The delicious chocolate ice cream is ready.
