##### The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Pork Shoulder Blade Steaks:

1. Take 4 thick pork shoulder blade steaks
1. Take 1 Olive Oil
1. Take 1 Salt
1. Make ready 1 Pepper
1. Get 2 tbsp onion powder
1. Get 1 tbsp garlic powder
1. Get 2 cup ketchup
1. Take 2 cup chicken broth

##### Instructions to make Crockpot Pork Shoulder Blade Steaks:

1. Salt &amp; pepper both sides of steaks.
1. Heat oil in pan on medium high.
1. Sear both sides of steaks.
1. Mix last four ingredients together in a bowl.
1. Pour small amount of mixture in bottom of crockpot.
1. Place steaks in crockpot and pour remaining mixture over them.
