##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Ice Cream from Biscuits:

1. Make ready 2 small pack of any chocolate biscuits
1. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
1. Take 1 cup chilled milk
1. Get 2 tbsp milk powder (optional)
1. Take 1/2 cup fresh cream/malai
1. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla essence (optional)
1. Take 1 tbsp Choco Chips for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chocolate Ice Cream from Biscuits:

1. Crush biscuits and add to a grinding jar. Add sugar and make fine powder. Now add chilled milk, cream, essence and milk powder.
1. Pulse the mixture until mixed well and will get a creamy texture. Now take it out into a air tight container, cover it with cling film and close the lid. Freeze for 4 hrs until firm.
1. Take it out again in a jar and churn it again for giving it more creamy texture. Now take in same container and fold in some Choco chips. Cover with cling film and close the lid. Freeze overnight. And here you go... very easy and quick Chocolate ice cream is ready at home...
