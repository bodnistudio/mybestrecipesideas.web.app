##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage With Brown Onion Gravy.:

1. Prepare 4 tsp olive oil
1. Get 4 medium onions, cut in half and sliced
1. Make ready 2 chillies, chopped
1. Get 4 clove garlic, thinly sliced
1. Prepare 1 can tomatoes, peeled and diced
1. Get 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
1. Get 2 tsp dried origanum
1. Get 2 tsp onion powder
1. Get 3 tsp salt
1. Take 4 tsp ground black pepper
1. Make ready 50 grams brown onion soup mix
1. Get 250 ml fresh cream
1. Prepare 250 ml vegetable stock
1. Make ready 1 1/2 kg sausage, use your favourite one




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sausage With Brown Onion Gravy.:

1. Brown the onions with the garlic and chillies in a preheated pot with the olive oil.
1. Add the tomatoes, green bell pepper, origanum, onion powder, salt and pepper. Simmer for 60 minutes.
1. Mix together your brown onion soup mix, cream and vegetable stock. Add to the onions. Simmer for 15mins.
1. In the meantime fry the sausage in a frying pan, then cut to portion size and set aside
1. Add the sausage to the onion mixture and simmer for a further 10 minutes
