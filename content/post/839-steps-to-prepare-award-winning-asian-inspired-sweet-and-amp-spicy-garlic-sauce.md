##### The ingredients needed to make Asian-inspired Sweet &amp; Spicy Garlic Sauce:

1. Make ready 1 tbsp sesame oil
1. Make ready 6 clove garlic (finely minced)
1. Get 2 tbsp sriracha
1. Make ready 7 tbsp soy sauce
1. Make ready 5 tbsp white vinegar
1. Get 3 tbsp sugar
1. Prepare 1 tsp ginger (paste or finely minced)
1. Take 2 tsp cornstarch
1. Get 2 tsp water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Asian-inspired Sweet &amp; Spicy Garlic Sauce:

1. Heat sesame oil in a small saucepan over low heat. Add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté until fragrant.
1. Add the sriracha, soy sauce, white vinegar and sugar to the saucepan. Bring to a simmer.
1. While bringing the sauce to a simmer, mix the cornstarch and water together in a small mixing bowl until smooth.
1. Add the cornstarch mixture to the simmering sauce and quickly whisk well.
1. Simmer until sauce will coat the back of a spoon or until your desired consistency is reached.
