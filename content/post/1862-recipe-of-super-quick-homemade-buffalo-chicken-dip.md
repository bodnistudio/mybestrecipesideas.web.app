##### The ingredients needed to make Buffalo Chicken Dip:

1. Prepare 2-10 oz. cans chicken
1. Prepare 1 cup ranch dressing
1. Get 1 cup buffalo wing sauce
1. Make ready 2-8 oz. bricks cream cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Buffalo Chicken Dip:

1. Preheat oven to 350.
1. Drain chicken well and combine all ingredients.
1. Place mixture in 8x8 baking dish.
1. Bake for 20- 25 minutes, or until bubbly around sides of dish.
1. Remove from oven and serve with your favorite dippers!
