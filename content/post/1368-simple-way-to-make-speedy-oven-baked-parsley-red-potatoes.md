##### The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Parsley Red Potatoes:

1. Make ready  In a 9x13 pan
1. Prepare  butter, cubed (1 stick)
1. Make ready  red potatoes, washed, and chopped into large even chunks (about 6)
1. Get  onion, chopped
1. Prepare  salt
1. Get  pepper
1. Take  chopped fresh parsley




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Oven Baked Parsley Red Potatoes:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F
1. Place pan with cubed butter in the oven to heat up dish and melt butter
1. Take out of oven after 10 minutes and add the potatoes, onion, salt and pepper. Toss with slotted spoon in butter.
1. Bake for 40 minutes, or until tender.
1. After baked, add parsley and toss to mix. Enjoy! I made these for the side dish to my Italian Meatloaf.
