##### The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Cheesy Potato Soup:

1. Prepare 6 cups peeled and diced potatoes
1. Get .5 cups diced onion
1. Get 32 ounces chicken broth
1. Take 1 cup water
1. Take 3 Tablespoons flour
1. Get 1 cup milk
1. Make ready 8 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
1. Get to taste salt, pepper, garlic powder, parsley, and thyme
1. Make ready  sliced carrots optional




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Crock Pot Cheesy Potato Soup:

1. Peel and cut potatoes.
1. Dice onion.
1. Place potatoes and onion (carrots optional) in crock pot.
1. Add broth, water, and seasonings.
1. Cook on LOW for 6-7 hours.
1. Mix together the flour, salt, pepper, and milk in a bowl.
1. Pour into crock pot and cook on HIGH 20-30 minutes.
1. When thickened, add cheese and stir until melted.
