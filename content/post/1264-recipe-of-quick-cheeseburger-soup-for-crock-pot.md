##### The ingredients needed to make Cheeseburger Soup for Crock Pot:

1. Take 1 lb lean ground beef
1. Make ready 1 cup onion
1. Prepare 3 clove garlic
1. Take 14 oz diced tomatoes (can)
1. Make ready 1 cup baby carrots, chopped
1. Prepare 1/2 cup celery, chopped
1. Make ready 2 cup potatoes,  peeled, diced
1. Get 4 cup chicken broth
1. Take 8 oz light cream cheese (1 brick)
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp parsley
1. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 cup milk
1. Prepare 1/4 cup flour
1. Take 2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1. Make ready 6 slice bacon, cooked,  crumbled




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cheeseburger Soup for Crock Pot:

1. Brown ground beef with onion, drain
1. Combine ingredients in crockpot, EXCEPT for milk, flour and cheddar cheese
1. Stirr together and cook on low for 7 hours
1. In the last 10 minutes combine milk and flour together, whisk until there is no more clumps
1. Add the flour milk mixture to the crock pot, stirr
1. Add the cheddar cheese, the soup is ready when all the cheese has melted
1. Serve with crumbled bacon
