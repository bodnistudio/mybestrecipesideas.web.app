##### The ingredients needed to make Candied Bacon Wrapped Potatoes:

1. Make ready 1 lb potatoes, cut into 1 inch pieces
1. Take 1 lb thick-cut bacon, cut into thirds
1. Prepare 1/3 cup brown sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Candied Bacon Wrapped Potatoes:

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
1. Put the potatoes in a medium pot, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil.
1. Once the water begins to boil, cook the potatoes for 4 minutes, until you can stick a fork into them without too much resistance.
1. Drain the potatoes.
1. Toss bacon with brown sugar.
1. Wrap each potato bite in a piece of bacon, securing it with a toothpick.
1. Put the potatoes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil spaced an inch or two apart.
1. Cook the potatoes for 30 minutes, turning halfway.
1. Serve with sour cream.
