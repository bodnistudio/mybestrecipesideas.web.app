##### Steps to make Matcha Green Tea Latte:

1. Completely dissolve sugar into hot water to create a simple syrup. Set aside.
1. Sift matcha powder and to it, add hot water. Whisk vigorously with a bamboo whisk until frothy. If you do not have a bamboo whisk, you may use a mason jar and shake vigorously until frothy.
1. Add simple syrup to the matcha concentrate.
1. Fill glass with ice cubes and add milk. Pour matcha on top of the milk, creating a two-tone layer. You may or may not put all the concentrate, adjust to your preference. Stir and enjoy.
