##### The ingredients needed to make Steamed Cod fish salad:

1. Make ready 1/2 lb cod fish
1. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
1. Make ready 1 medium chopped onion
1. Get 1/4 cup water
1. Get 1 dash salt and pepper to taste
1. Take 1 chopped broccoli
1. Get 1 large carrot shredded
1. Get 1/2 large pepper chopped
1. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
1. Make ready 1/2 medium cabbage chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Steamed Cod fish salad:

1. boil cod fish in water for 5-7 minutes to remove some of the salt
1. cut or shred codfish into small pieces
1. saute cod fish, onions, peppers, garlic in butter.
1. add vegetables, water and stir
1. cover pan and cook on low heat till vegetables are tender
1. add black pepper and salt if needed
1. other types of vegetables can be added or substituted
