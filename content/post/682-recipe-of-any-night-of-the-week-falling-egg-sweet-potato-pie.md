<h3>The ingredients needed to make Falling Egg Sweet Potato Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 4 of sweet potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of white sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 6 oz of unsalted sweet cream butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 1/2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of eggs beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Falling Egg Sweet Potato Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			preheat oven to 400°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			boil sweet potatoes about 45 min (or until fork goes through w/o getting stuck).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			drain potatoes and peel under cold water.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			mash potatoes; add all ingredients (mixing after each).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			pour into crust evenly.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			bake @ 400°F for 10 min.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			reduce temperature to 375°F; bake 1 hour.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
