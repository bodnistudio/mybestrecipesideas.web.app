##### The ingredients needed to make Slamming Potato Soup:

1. Prepare 32 oz chicken broth
1. Prepare 1 can cream of chicken
1. Prepare 1 packages cube frozen hashbrowns
1. Make ready 1/2 cup bacon bits
1. Take 6 green onions chopped up finely
1. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Slamming Potato Soup:

1. Bring chicken broth to a boil and add your hashbrowns and cook until hashbrowns are soft.
1. Remove heat and add cream of chicken and stir well.
1. Add bacon bits, green onions and cheese stir it well. Serve it with crackers or biscuits.
