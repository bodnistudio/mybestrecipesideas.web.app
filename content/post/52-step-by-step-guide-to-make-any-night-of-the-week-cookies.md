##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies:

1. Make ready 125 g butter
1. Get 55 g caster sugar plus extra to sprinkle
1. Make ready 180 g plain flour




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cookies:

1. Heat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5. -  - Beat the butter and the sugar together until smooth. -  - Stir in the flour to get a smooth dough. Turn onto a work surface and gently roll out until the dough is 1cm to ½in thick. -  - Cut into hearts, fingers or any shape you like and place onto a baking tray. Sprinkle with caster sugar and chill in the fridge for 20 minutes. -  - Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes, or until pale golden-brown. Set aside to cool on a wire rack.
