##### The ingredients needed to make Canned Spaghetti Sauce:

1. Take 12 # of tomatoes
1. Make ready 2 T olive oil
1. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
1. Prepare 2 large onions (chopped)
1. Make ready 2 large bell peppers (chopped)
1. Get 2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 tsp black pepper
1. Make ready 6-8 cloves garlic
1. Make ready 2 T oregano
1. Make ready 2 T basil
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1. Take 1 tsp thyme
1. Prepare 3 tsp fennel seeds
1. Take 2 stalks celery, minced




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Canned Spaghetti Sauce:

1. I use a huge turkey roaster..... Dump all the tomatoes in the roaster... Allow to cook overnight at 200°
1. Mince bell pepper, celery, and onion... saute in butter....add to tomato mixture..
1. Allow tomatoes veggie mix to cook for 3-4 hours.... This is what I call stewed tomatoes.... They can be canned at this point in a pressure canner for 25 minutes at 10# of pressure..
1. If making spaghetti sauce add the additional ingredients and cook again overnight....
1. Is ready to can....this recipe made 14qts of sauce....pressure can for 25 minutes at 10# of pressure...
