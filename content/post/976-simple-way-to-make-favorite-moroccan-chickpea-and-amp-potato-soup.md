##### The ingredients needed to make Moroccan Chickpea &amp; Potato Soup:

1. Make ready 1 cup chickpeas, soaked
1. Prepare 1 cup potatoes, cut into cubes
1. Take 1/2 cup parsley, minced
1. Prepare 1/2 cup onions, grated
1. Get 8 cups beef broth
1. Prepare 3 teaspoons olive oil
1. Get 1/4 cup lemon juice
1. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon tomato paste
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon saffron
1. Get  salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Moroccan Chickpea &amp; Potato Soup:

1. Drain the chickpeas and place in a saucepan with the parsley, onions, beef broth, black pepper and a pinch of salt. Cook over low heat for 1 hour.
1. Then add the tomato paste and let the mixture boil for another hour. Then add in the potatoes and continue cooking for an additional 30minutes.
1. Finally add the lemon juice and salt to taste. Serve the soup warm.
