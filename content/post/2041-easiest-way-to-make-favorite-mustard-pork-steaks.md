##### The ingredients needed to make Mustard Pork Steaks:

1. Make ready 4 good size Pork Steaks
1. Get 1/4 cup White wine
1. Take 2 tsp Djion Mustard
1. Prepare 2 tsp Wholegrain mustard
1. Take 1/4 cup Single cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Mustard Pork Steaks:

1. In a slow cooker, mix the wine and both mustards together. Dip the four steaks into it on both sides, then lay them flat.
1. Cook for 4 hours on high.
1. When finished cooking, take out the steaks add to a serving bowl. Then add the cream to the juice in the slow cooker and stir in. Pour over the steaks and serve
1. Nice served with boiled new potatoes and peas.
