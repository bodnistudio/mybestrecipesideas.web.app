##### The ingredients needed to make Garden Fresh Spaghetti Sauce:

1. Prepare 4 medium onions, chopped
1. Prepare 1-1/4 tsp pepper
1. Get 1/2 cup olive oil
1. Get 4 garlic cloves, minced
1. Make ready 12 cups chopped peeled fresh tomatoes
1. Make ready 3 bay leaves
1. Take 4 tsp salt
1. Take 2 tsp dried oregano
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp dried basil
1. Take 2 (6 oz) tomato paste
1. Prepare 1/3 cup packed brown sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Garden Fresh Spaghetti Sauce:

1. In a large Dutch oven, saute the onions and the pepper in oil until onions are tender.
1. Add garlic, tomatoes, bay leaves,salt, oregano, and basil.
1. Simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
1. Add tomato paste and brown sugar; simmer 1 hour longer.
1. Remove bay leaves.
1. Serve over pasta!
