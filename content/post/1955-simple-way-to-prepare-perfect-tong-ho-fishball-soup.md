##### The ingredients needed to make Tong Ho Fishball Soup:

1. Take 2 cups chicken stock
1. Make ready 3 cups water
1. Take 3 bunches tong ho
1. Get 16 fishballs, raw or cooked
1. Take 1 tsp fish sauce
1. Prepare to taste Salt and white pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tong Ho Fishball Soup:

1. Combine water and chicken stock and bring to a rolling boil, then add fish balls
1. Once fish balls have risen to the surface of the soup, add tong ho and seasoning. Cook for several minutes until tong ho is fully cooked. Serve immediately.
