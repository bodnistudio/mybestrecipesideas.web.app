##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Chicken Penne:

1. Prepare 250 grams Penne pasta
1. Make ready 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped into cubes
1. Take 1/2 small broccoli, cut into small pieces
1. Take 540 ml Tomato, garlic and onion pasta sauce
1. Take 2 Carrots, sliced
1. Make ready 1 cup mozzarella cheese
1. Take 1 Enough parmesan cheese to sprinkle generously on top
1. Prepare 2 Skinless chicken breast
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Garlic salt
1. Take 2 tbsp Dried basil
1. Make ready 1 Pepper to season




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Tomato Chicken Penne:

1. Boil water and add pasta.
1. Chop chicken into cubes and cook in large fry pan until brown.
1. Once chicken has browned, add the pasta sauce and cut up vegetables.
1. Let sit until carrots are soft and pasta is cooked.
1. Once its cooked, add the mozzarella and mix in and transfer to a rectangular baking dish. Sprinkle the parmesan and extra mozzarella (if needed) on top and place in a preheated oven. Let it sit in the oven until cheese has thoroughly melted through and a little crispy at the edges.
