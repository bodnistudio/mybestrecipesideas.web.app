##### The ingredients needed to make Reese’s Cookie 🍪Dough Cheesecake:

1. Prepare 2 packages cream cheese (8 ounce), softened
1. Prepare 1/2 cup white sugar
1. Take 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1. Take 2 eggs
1. Take 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
1. Take 3/4 cup Reeses’s cookie dough
1. Make ready 1 graham cracker crust




<!--inarticleads2-->

1. Break up part of the cookie dough, about 1/2 cup, and stir into cream cheese mixture.......
