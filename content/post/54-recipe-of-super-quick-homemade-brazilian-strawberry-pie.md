<h3>The ingredients needed to make Brazilian Strawberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take  of Dough. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of egg yolk (beaten). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 100 g of margarine. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tbsp of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tbsp of baking powder. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of Filling. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 700 ml of milk (I used almond milk). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 can of condensed milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 4 tbsp of cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of egg yolks (beaten). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Toppings. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Strawberries (cut in half). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Strawberry Jello (use it when still soft). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Brazilian Strawberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Mix all ingredients to make the dough and place it in the pie dish.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Make holes in the bottom of the dough with a fork and bake it at 400F for 15-20 min.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Make the pie filling by combining the milk, condensed milk, and eggs in a saucepan. Mix it constantly at medium high heat.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Once it starts boiling, add the cornstarch (dissolved in milk) and keep mixing it until thick.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add the cream filling into the pie dough using a strainer.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add the strawberries on top of the cream, then the jello.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Refrigerate for a few hours, and enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
