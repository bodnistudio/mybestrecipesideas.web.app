##### Steps to make Homemade Iced Tea:

1. Bring the water to a boil. And turn off the heat
1. Add teabags to the water and let them steep for 10 min
1. Remove the teabags
1. Add the sugar to the pot and stir to dissolve
1. Fill your pitcher with the ice. If using a glass pitcher make sure its heat proof or it will crack and break. Or use plastic
1. Cut the Lemon in half and squeeze into your pitcher of ice
1. Pour the tea over the ice and stir
1. Enjoy!
