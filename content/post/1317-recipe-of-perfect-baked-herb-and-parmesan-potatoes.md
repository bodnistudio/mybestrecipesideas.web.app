##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Herb And Parmesan Potatoes:

1. Take 4 Baby Red Potatoes
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Oregano
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Parsley
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Rosemary
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Olive Oil
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Thyme
1. Take 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
1. Make ready  Parmesan Cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Baked Herb And Parmesan Potatoes:

1. Gather ingredients
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
1. Slice potatoes thin. Then add to the bowl with all of ingredients except parm cheese.
1. Spread evenly in a baking dish.
1. Add the parm cheese as much as you like :)
1. Heat in the oven for 20 mins.
