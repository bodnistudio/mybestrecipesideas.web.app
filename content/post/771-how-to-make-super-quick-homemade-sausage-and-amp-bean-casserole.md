##### Instructions to make Sausage &amp; Bean Casserole:

1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the onions, cooking over a low heat for 5 minutes. Add the garlic, bacon and sausages and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Transfer from the pan to the slowcooker and add the beans and stock - cook on low for 6 hours.
1. Shortly before serving, preheat the grill. Toast the bread on one side before turning and topping with the grated cheese and toasting again until the cheese has melted.
1. Serve casserole topped with toasted cheese bread!
