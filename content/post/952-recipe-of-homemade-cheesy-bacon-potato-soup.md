##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Bacon Potato Soup:

1. Take 10 tbsp Butter
1. Make ready 3/4 large Onion
1. Take 12 slice Thick cut cherry smoked bacon
1. Get 8 Red potatoes
1. Take 6 Russet potatoes
1. Take 12 oz Heavy cream
1. Take 3 tbsp Minced garlic
1. Take 1 can Frito jalape?o cheddar dip
1. Make ready 8 oz Sharp cheddar cheese
1. Get 5 as needed Cayenne, paprika, chives, salt, pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cheesy Bacon Potato Soup:

1. Cut up onion and butter and simmer in soup pot.
1. Cook bacon and let cool
1. As bacon is cooling peel and slice up all potatoes. Add heavy cream and water
1. Add bacon and garlic and turn up the heat. Stir occasionally for about 30 minutes.
1. Add in cheeses and stir until well blended/melted. Add in any desired spices. I used only the 5 listed. Turn down heat and continue cooking 30 minutes
1. Mash potatoes somewhat to creat smaller pieces and thicken up soup. Enjoy!
