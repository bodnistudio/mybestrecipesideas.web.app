##### Instructions to make Swiss Chard and Potatoes:

1. Clean and chop potatoes. Place in saucepan of water. Salt water. Add potatoes. Boil until soft.
1. Clean and chop Swiss Chard.
1. Chop garlic and onion. Add to pan with oil. Sautee until garlic and onion are a little soft. Add Swiss Chard and sautee Add salt. Cook until chard is soft.
1. When potatoes are soft drain. Add to Swiss Chard. Drain and add garbanzos. Cook 5 minutes.
1. Sprinkle red pepper flakes and pumpkin seeds. Sprinkle parmagian.
1. Serve with whole wheat bread. Vegan butter and fresh salad.
