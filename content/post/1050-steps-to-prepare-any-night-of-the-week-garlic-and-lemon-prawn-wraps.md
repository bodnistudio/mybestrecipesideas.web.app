##### Instructions to make Garlic and lemon prawn wraps:

1. First chop up all the vegetables in to small chunks and mix together in a bowl, add a little salt
1. Put prawns into a bowl and cover them with with 2 tea spoons of garlic paste and add a drizzle of lemon and herb sauce (add as much as you like based on personal preference) then add a dash of paprika and mix all together so that the prawns and equally covered
1. Put the prawns with all the sauce onto a frying pan and fry on a medium heat for approx 4 mins, turning them over half way through
1. Get your wrap and put a thin layer of mayo, sour cream and paprika across it, then add the prawns and vegetables, wrap up and enjoy!
