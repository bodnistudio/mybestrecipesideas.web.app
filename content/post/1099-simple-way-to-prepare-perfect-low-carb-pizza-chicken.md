##### The ingredients needed to make Low Carb Pizza Chicken:

1. Make ready 6 pcs boneless skinless chicken breast, pounded thin
1. Prepare 6 oz Tomato sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp garlic salt
1. Make ready 2 tsp italian seasoning
1. Take 4 oz shredded cheese (whatever you like)
1. Take  Your choice of pizza toppings




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Low Carb Pizza Chicken:

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
1. Spray large oven safe pan with olive oil. Arrange chicken pieces in one layer.
1. Sprinkle garlic salt and Italian seasoning generously over chicken.
1. Spread tomato sauce over chicken
1. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese. Add whatever pizza toppings you like.
1. Bake uncovered for 18 minutes
1. Finish under the broiler for 2-3 minutes until the cheese bubbles and the pepperoni gets crispy (if you used it).
1. Serve :)
