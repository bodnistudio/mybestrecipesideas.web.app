##### Instructions to make Diet chocolate Pudding♥:

1. Add sugar substitute and cocoa powder and mix well.
1. Pour the rest of the milk in a bowl and dissolve the cornflour. Make sure there are no lumps.
1. When the milk is simmering and add the remainder of the milk into the pan while mixing continiously. Stir until it thickens.
1. Turn off the stove and allow to cool down.
1. While it is cooling place the biscuits in a plastic bag and crush them.

1. Chill in the fridge for 2 hours and serve.
