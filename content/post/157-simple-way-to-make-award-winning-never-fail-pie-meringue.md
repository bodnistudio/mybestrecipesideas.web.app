##### The ingredients needed to make Never Fail  Pie Meringue:

1. Prepare 1 tablespoon cornstarch
1. Get 6 tablespoons sugar
1. Take 1/2 cup water
1. Make ready 3 egg whites
1. Make ready  few grains of salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Never Fail  Pie Meringue:

1. Mix cornstarch, sugar, water and salt. Cook until thick and clear. Cool slightly.
1. Beat egg whites until frothy. Continue beating while slowly adding cooked mixture. Continue beating for 5 minutes.
1. Cover your creation with the meringue. Bake at 450 degrees fahrenheit for 5-7 minutes until golden
