##### Instructions to make Steak Dinner for Couple:

1. Marinate ribeye with seasoning. Peel and slice potatoes, boil in 20’.
1. Put oil in a pan, wait until it is hot, set medium heat. Get ribeye in pan, fried 8 mins each side.
1. Prepare letucce, carrot and fruits in a bowl
1. Take all of cooked potatoes to a bowl. Add milk and cheese, mash well.
1. Take the ribs to dish, clean the pan. Put butter into, then garlic. After, add flour, milk and a pinch of seasoning. Stir them with a whisk until boiled. Pour the dressing on the steak.
1. Scoop mashed potato on the same dish with steak. Enjoy a delicious dinner!
