##### The ingredients needed to make For Clumsy People Easy! Sweet Potato Bites:

1. Get 400 grams Asian sweet potato (1 large)
1. Take 40 grams Sugar
1. Get 100 ml Heavy cream
1. Take 1 Egg yolk
1. Make ready 1 dash Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make For Clumsy People Easy! Sweet Potato Bites:

1. Slice the potato, soak briefly in water then microwave until soft.
1. Add the sugar, heavy cream and salt, and mash the potatoes.
1. Using an ice cream scoop, take one scoop of the potato paste and shape. Repeat the process.
1. Brush the surface with an beaten egg yolk and bake in the oven until golden brown.
