<h3>The ingredients needed to make Dutch Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Prepared pie crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 6 of medium apples, peeled, cored and sliced. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 tsp of allspice. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Topping. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of soft butter. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Dutch Apple Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven 350.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put apple slices, spices and sugar into bowl. Mix.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour slices into pie crust.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In same bowl, add flour and sugar. Mix with fork or pastry blender.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add butter and blend into course crumbs.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour on top of apple slices.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake 45-55 minutes or until apples are soft and topping is golden brown.
			
			
		</li>
