##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Banana Sushi!:

1. Get 1 Whole Wheat Tortilla
1. Prepare 1 Banana
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Peanut Butter
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cinnamon Powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Healthy Banana Sushi!:

1. Spread all the PB (can add more or less if you like!) Evenly around the whole tortilla
1. Place banana on one end of the pb coated tortilla
1. Sprinkle half of the cinnamon all over the inside of the tortilla and banana.
1. Tightly roll the banana into the tortilla like sushi!
1. Cut up into small slices
1. Sprinkle with remaining cinnamon and enjoy!
