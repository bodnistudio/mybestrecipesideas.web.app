##### The ingredients needed to make Beetroot Poha:

1. Get 1 cup poha
1. Get 1 small grated beetroot
1. Take 1 small onion, chopped
1. Make ready 1-2 tbsp peas, boiled
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1. Get 5-6 curry leaves
1. Make ready 1 greenchilli
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Peanuts
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Prepare 1 tbsp coriender leaves
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Pomegranate krenels
1. Make ready 1 lemon juice
1. Take 1-2 tsp oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Beetroot Poha:

1. Firstly take a strainer, Wash poha 1-2 times by gently swifting them with your fingers under running water. Drain the excess water through a strainer.keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, when they start splutter, add onion saute until onion turns translucent. add green chillies, peas, curry leaves, peanuts saute for few seconds.
1. Add soaked poha mix gently.
1. Cook for 2–3 minutes. Add lemon juice, grated beetroot and finally chopped coriander leaves, Saute for 2 minutes switch off the flame.
1. Sprinkle Pomegranate kernels &amp; garnish ready to serve.
