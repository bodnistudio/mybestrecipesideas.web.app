##### The ingredients needed to make Italian Chicken Bake (LOW CARB):

1. Take 2 cup shredded chicken
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 tsp Italian seasoning
1. Get 1 cup tomato sauce
1. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese
1. Make ready 1/2 cup parmesan cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Italian Chicken Bake (LOW CARB):

1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. Place shredded chicken in the bottom of a greased glass casserole dish.
1. Mic all remaining ingredients minus parmesan cheese
1. Pour tomato sauce mixture over chicken.
1. Top with parmesan cheese
1. Bake for 25 - 30 minutes or until casserole begins to bubble
