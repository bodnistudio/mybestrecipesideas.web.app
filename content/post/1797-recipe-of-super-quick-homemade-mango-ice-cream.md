##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Ice Cream:

1. Get 1 Cup Mango Pulp
1. Take 1/2 Cup Milk Powder
1. Take 1/2 Cup Cream
1. Take 1/4 Cup Milk
1. Take to taste Sugar powder according
1. Prepare 1 Tablespoon tooti fruitti
1. Take as required Cherries, Chocolate Sauce to decorate




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mango Ice Cream:

1. Put half of the pulp in the blender and blend.
1. Again add rest of the pulp and blend. Lastly add milk powder, milk, cream and blend again to mix everything nicely. Don&#39;t blend excess otherwise cream will release oil.
1. Now pour this mix in a box. Spread evenly and cover with cling film. Keep in a deep freezer for at least 2 hours. After 2 hours again churn this mix in a blender.
1. Again pour this in the same box and decorate with tooti frooti. Keep this in deep freezer overnight.
1. Next day arrange Mango Ice creams in bowls. Decorate with Cherry and drizzle with Chocolate Sauce and enjoy with your kids.
