##### The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter &amp; Ice Cream Pie:

1. Make ready  crust mix
1. Prepare 6 tbsp Creamy or Crunchy Peanut Butter
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Rice Krispies
1. Take 1 tsp chocolate syrup
1. Get  Filling
1. Get 1/4 gallon Ice Cream (your choice, I used banana nut)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Peanut Butter &amp; Ice Cream Pie:

1. Melt peanut butter and syrup in microwave for 10 sec.
1. Blend peanut butter and Rice Krispies together
1. Spread crust in baking glass or freezer container
1. Spread a thick even layer of ice cream on top of crust (thaw a little to make more manageable)
1. place back in freezer for a couple of hours Cut and enjoy
