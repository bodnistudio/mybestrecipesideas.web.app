##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Slow Cooker Chili:

1. Prepare  Lean Chop Meat
1. Make ready  Big cans of Crushed Tomatos
1. Make ready  Big can of Red Kidney Beans (Drained and Washed)
1. Prepare  Medium cloves garlic minced (More or Less to your taste)
1. Get  Big yellow onion diced (More or Less to your taste)
1. Prepare  Ground Cumin to taste
1. Prepare  Chili Powder to taste
1. Make ready  Salt and Pepper
1. Prepare  Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Slow Cooker Chili:

1. Heat some Oil in a deep pan. Put onions and garlic in to brown.
1. Add chopped meat to the browned onions. As the meat browns add chili powder, cumin, salt &amp; pepper to taste. Let it cook completely.
1. Add the meat mixture to the crock pot. - Add approximately 3 cans of crushed tomatoes. Enough to cover the meat.   - You can add your beans now or an hour before it&#39;s done. Cook high 4 hours.
1. You could put it over rice, elbow pasta or plain. You could add sour cream, chedder cheese or whatever you like. Enjoy!
