<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 3/4 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 tsp of cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 tsp of all spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 of eggs +1 yolk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of evaporated milk. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Combine sugar with all the spices.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Whisk together eggs and Pumpkin in another bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add spice mixture slowly.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add milk, whisk until smooth.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into tarts or pie.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 15 minutes at 200 degrees...and then Or 35 minutes at 175 degrees (the jury is out on this one) Add a crust guard for second baking session..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
