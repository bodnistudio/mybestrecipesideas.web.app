##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Cellophane Noodle Salad:

1. Take 25 grams Cellophane noodles
1. Make ready 1/2 Carrot
1. Make ready 1/2 Cucumber
1. Get 2 slice Ham
1. Prepare 1 as much (to taste) Wood ear mushrooms
1. Take 1 as much (to taste) White sesame seeds
1. Take 1 tbsp ● Soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp ● Vinegar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ● Sesame oil
1. Make ready 1 ● Sugar
1. Prepare 1 ● Japanese mustard




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chinese Cellophane Noodle Salad:

1. Cook the cellophane noodles according to the instructions on the package. Run under water and then drain well. Rehydrate the wood ear mushrooms, discard the hard parts, and slice thinly.
1. Julienne the vegetables and ham slices to 4-5 cm and blanch the carrot.
1. Mix the ● ingredients in a bow. Also add the wood ear mushrooms (Step 1), the vegetables (Step 2), the ham, and toss.
1. Serve on a plate, and scatter with white sesame seeds on top.
