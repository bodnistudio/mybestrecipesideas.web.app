##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Hamburger Steaks:

1. Get 300 grams Ground meat - mix beef and pork
1. Make ready 1/2 Onion
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Panko
1. Get 1 Egg
1. Take 1 Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 Nutmeg
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Ketchup
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ★Japanese Worcestershire Sauce
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ★Ketchup
1. Prepare 4 leaves Lettuce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Hamburger Steaks:

1. Finely chop the onion. Mix the ground meat with the onion, panko, egg, salt and pepper, nutmeg, ketchup, and mayonnaise in a bowl.
1. Knead very well with your hands. Divide into two portions and form into patties. Pan fry over medium heat.
1. When both sides are browned, add a little sake to the pan, cover with a lid and steam-fry. (Medium heat)
1. When they are cooked through, serve on a bed of lettuce.
1. Add the ★ ingredients to the pan and heat through. Pour the sauce over the hamburger steaks and they&#39;re done.
