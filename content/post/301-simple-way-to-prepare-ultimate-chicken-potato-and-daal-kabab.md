##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Potato and Daal Kabab:

1. Get 1 cup Daal Chaana (boiled and mashed)
1. Prepare 3 potatoes (boiled mashed)
1. Make ready 1 cup boiled chicken
1. Prepare 1 oinon finely chopped
1. Prepare 2 green chilli chopped
1. Take 1 tsp chicken powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
1. Make ready 1 tsp white pepper powder
1. Get 1 tbsp red chilli powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds (zeera)
1. Make ready 1 tsp Garlic powder
1. Make ready to taste Salt
1. Make ready 1 bread slice
1. Take 3 tbsp green oinon chopped
1. Get  Oil for frying
1. Prepare  Egg for coating




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken Potato and Daal Kabab:

1. Boiled Daal Chaana and mashed.
1. Mashed the Boiled potatoes.
1. Oinon and green chilli chopped.
1. Add Daal, mashed potatoes, green chilli and oinon mix well.
1. Add dry ingredients.
1. Soak the bread slice in milk and mashed it and add the mixture.
1. Add shredded boiled chicken.
1. Add green oinon and mix. well.
1. Make Kabab from this mixture coat in egg and shallow fry.
1. Serve hot with any dip or ketchup.
