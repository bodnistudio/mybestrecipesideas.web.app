##### Steps to make Pizza &amp; tomato sauce:

1. Add olive oil, salt and flour and mix it, should pull away from bowl if it‚Äôs too sticky you can always add a little flour
1. Split ball of dough in to two and let it rest in bowl, covered with a kitchen towel about 15-20 min
1. After this sprinkle flour on counter then roll out dough on top of floured counter top until it gets pretty thin or about the size of your pizza pan.
1. Sprinkle corn meal on pizza pan and lay rolled out dough on top.

1. Pour can of crushed tomato and tomato paste put herbs, salt and sugar mix with spoon and let simmer
