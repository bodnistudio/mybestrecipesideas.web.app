##### The ingredients needed to make Easy and Festive! Avocado and Salmon Tartare:

1. Prepare 1/2 Avocado
1. Prepare 100 grams Fresh salmon (sashimi grade)
1. Make ready 1 Tomato, small
1. Get 1/2 Cucumber
1. Prepare 1/4 Onion
1. Prepare 1 to 1 1/2 tablespooon Olive oil
1. Make ready 1 generous amount Krazy Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy and Festive! Avocado and Salmon Tartare:

1. Dice all the ingredients. (Mince the onion.)
1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl, and toss with olive oil and Krazy Salt. Chill in the refrigerator.
1. Serving Suggestion Using a small dish for a mold, pack the tartare in the dish, then upturn it in the center of your serving platter. Scatter mixed leaves dressed with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Then serve!!
