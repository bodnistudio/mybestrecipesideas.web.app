##### The ingredients needed to make Microwaved Onion Gratin-style Soup:

1. Make ready 1 Onion
1. Get 20 grams Butter
1. Make ready 4 cubes Consommé cubes (or kombu based dashi stock - 2 tablespoons)
1. Take 80 grams Easily melting cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Microwaved Onion Gratin-style Soup:

1. These are the only ingredients.
1. Slice the onion and top with butter.
1. Cover with a lid or plastic wrap and microwave (using the &#34;boil root vegetables&#34; setting, if available).
1. Add the cheese and consommé and microwave (using the same setting) again.
1. The cheese and onion will begin to burn slightly on the edges and turn golden brown.
1. Stir once and then microwave uncovered until it becomes cooked to your desired taste.
1. It will equal about 160 g, so separate into 8 portions.
1. Add one portion to a cup, pour in some hot water, mix very well, and you&#39;re done.
1. For a vegetarian version, instead of consommé, use 2 tablespoons of kombu dashi and it will still be delicious.
