1. Prepare 1/2 cup Garlic alfredo sauce( I used the kind from the jar)
1. Prepare 1 cup Shredded Swiss cheese
1. Get 1/4 lb deli ham sliced thin and chopped
1. Take 10 Breaded chicken nuggets,thawed and cut into half inch pieces
1. Prepare 2 cup Shredded mozzarella cheese

##### Instructions to make Chicken Cordon Bleu Pizza:
