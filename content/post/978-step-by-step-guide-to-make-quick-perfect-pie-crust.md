<h3>Instructions to make Perfect Pie Crust:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			mix flour, sugar,  and salt.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			dice the very cold butter into cubes. add to flour mix and stir leaving butter in large cubes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			add water slowly and mix until dough just forms. leave butter chunks in dough and mix only as needed. this creates the flaky awesome crust that you deserve!.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			roll dough by hand into a ball. store for at least 3hrs in fridge before use..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			this will make enough for one 9in pie and top. vodka is optional, it breaks down the gluten and makes a better crust in my opinion..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			to use; divide into 2 balls and roll out for top and bottom crust. I typically dont pre-bake the bottom but do brush with egg white and top with sanding sugar to make the presentation glorious..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
