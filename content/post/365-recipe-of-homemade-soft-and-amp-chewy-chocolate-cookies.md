##### The ingredients needed to make Soft &amp; Chewy Chocolate Cookies:

1. Take 113 gr butter (room temperature)
1. Prepare 100 gr white sugar
1. Get 100 gr brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 egg
1. Make ready 20 gr cocoa powder
1. Prepare 172 gr flour
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Get  White chocolate chunks




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Soft &amp; Chewy Chocolate Cookies:

1. In a bowl, well mix together butter, white sugar and brown sugar then add egg, mix until creamy (spatula / whisk mix is ok)
1. Pour dry ingredients; flour+cocoa powder, baking powder and baking soda
1. Add white chocolate chunks and mix dough with spatula
1. Divide dough into small portions (30-40 gr each, scoop by the rounded tablespoon onto the prepared sheet pan. Give some space
1. Pre-heat oven then bake the cookies at 180 deg C around 7-10 minutes. Let cool on the pan for a few minutes then use a cooling rack to cool cookies completely.
1. Enjoy! Store baked cookies in a jar at room temperature.
