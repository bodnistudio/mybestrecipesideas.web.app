1. Butter cream frosting: Firstly beat butter with milk then gradually add sugar. Divide this mixer in 2 portions. In one portion add 2 tbsp cocoa powder n mix it well. In another portion add coffee mixer(1tsp instant coffee mix with 2 tbsp of water) &amp; mix it well.
1. Cheesy frosting: Beat butter n cream cheese together n then add vanilla essence n add icing sugar gradually. Then divide into three portion add colours of ur choice.
