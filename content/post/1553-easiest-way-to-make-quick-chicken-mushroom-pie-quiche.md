##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken mushroom pie (quiche):

1. Make ready 100 grams cheese of choice
1. Make ready 1 cup chicken pieces
1. Prepare 8 Tbsp water
1. Take  salt &amp; pepper
1. Prepare  turmeric, vinegar (optional)

##### Steps to make Chicken mushroom pie (quiche):

1. Put the flour in a working surface add butter and start working it. - Make sure all the flour is incorporated with the butter.
1. Add the water and assemble it as a dough ( do not knead ). - Put the dough aside to rest for 30 mins.
1. In a freezing bag put the chicken salt pepper and turmeric or other spices of your choice and some vinegar , shake it well and let it sit in the fridge.
1. After 30 mins put the dough in a working surface and put it in a pie pan cook for 25 mins on 175°C
1. In a pan add some oil and let the chicken cook. - Add the mushrooms and stir fry. - Remove it once its done.
1. Get the pie out of the oven add a layer of mushroom and chicken mixture. - Beat the eggs with the milk and add it on top spread the grated cheese on top. - And put in the oven for about 30-40 minutes.
