##### The ingredients needed to make Lemonade Pie:

1. Make ready  finely crushed graham crackers
1. Take  Butter or margarine,  melted
1. Make ready  sugar
1. Prepare  (4 cups) vanilla ice cream, softened
1. Get  can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
1. Get  few drops of yellow food coloring
1. Prepare  lemon peel for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Lemonade Pie:

1. Heat oven to 350°
1. In medium bowl, mix first 3 ingredients. Press graham cracker mixture firmly against bottom and side of 9 inch pie plate. Bake about 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool crust 30 minutes.
1. In large bowl, use mixer and mix ice cream, lemonade concentrate and food coloring. Add ice cream mixture in cooled pie crust.
1. Freeze about 4 hours or until firm. Let stand at room temperature a few minutes before cutting. Garnish with lemon peel. Store left overs in freezer.
