<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In small bowl, mix sugar, salt, cinnamon,  ginger,  and cloves.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In large bowl, beat eggs.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>

		</li>
	
		<li>

		</li>
