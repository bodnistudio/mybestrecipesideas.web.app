<h3>The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Pecan pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 oz of Unsweetened Chocolate. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3 tbsp of Butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3/4 cup of Sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of Corn Syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3 of Eggs, Beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of Vanilla. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of Couresly chopped Pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of 9&#34; Unbaked Pie Crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Chocolate Pecan pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Melt Butter and Chocolate in double bioler.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine Corn syrup and sugar in a heavy sauce pan. Bring to a boil, boil for 2 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Stir in chocolate mixture. Pour Slowly into eggs, stirring constantly.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add vanilla and Pecans, mix well. Pour into Pie Crust.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 45 to 50 minutes until center is not soft.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool serve with whipped cream if wanted.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
