##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Herbal Chicken Soup (Dun Ji Tang):

1. Take 1 pc Whole Chicken Leg (bone in)
1. Get 1 tsp Salt
1. Make ready 1.5 litres Water (or as needed)
1. Make ready  Dun Ji Tang herbs;
1. Get 6 g Astragalus Root (Bei Chi 北芪)
1. Prepare 15 g Codonopsis (Dang Shen 党参)
1. Get 11 g Chinese Yam (Huai Shan 淮山)
1. Get 23 g Solomon&#39;s Seal (Yu Zhu 玉竹)
1. Take 23 g Wolfberries (Goji Zi 枸杞子)
1. Take  Optional
1. Take 20 g Red Dates
1. Take 20 g Dried Longan




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chinese Herbal Chicken Soup (Dun Ji Tang):

1. CHICKEN; rinse the chicken. Dry the chicken with kitchen towels and then salt it. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
1. STEW; In a pot, place in all the Herbs as well as Water and chicken. Bring it to a boil and then turn down the heat to a simmer. REMOVE any scum from the surface and let the soup cook for 1 hour or until the liquid has been reduced by half.
1. SERVE; turn off the heat, remove the chicken and let it rest for 10 minutes. Then, chop the chicken into serving size. Taste the soup to see if it needs to be seasoned. Serve in a bowl.
