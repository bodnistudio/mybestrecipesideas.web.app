<h3>The ingredients needed to make Jeremiah’s Candied Pumpkin:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of 8-10” pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 stick of butter (1/4 cup). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tbsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of nutmeg. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Jeremiah’s Candied Pumpkin:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Scrub the pumpkin surface clean and remove the stem..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Halve the pumpkin and clean out seeds and strings; reserve the seeds.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Melt the butter and mix in the sugar and other seasonings..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Baste the flesh (inside) with the mixture..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 350° for one hour..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut the pumpkin in wedges and serve. Remove the rind before eating..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
