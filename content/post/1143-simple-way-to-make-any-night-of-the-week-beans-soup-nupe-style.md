##### The ingredients needed to make Beans Soup (Nupe Style):

1. Take 1/2 cup beans
1. Prepare  Red oil
1. Prepare to taste Maggi,
1. Take  Grounded crayfish
1. Get  Water
1. Make ready  Blended pepper
1. Take  Onions
1. Prepare  Locust beans (daddawa, iru, dawadawa)
1. Prepare  Dry fish (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Beans Soup (Nupe Style):

1. Pulse the beans in an electric blender; this makes it easier for the back of the beans to come off.
1. Wash the beans until it is completely clean.
1. Put it in a pot, pour water to cover the beans, add potash (optional) and allow to boil for 45 minutes or more or until it is completely soft and mushy (add water if necessary).
1. Add chopped onions and locust beans, cook for a few minutes.
1. Add the dry fish, if using (ensure it is cleaned and soaked in hot water before using).
1. Add the red oil and use a cooking broom to whisk the soup.
1. Allow to boil on low heat for 5-7 minutes.
1. You can serve with beef/chicken/catfish stew and tuwon shinkafa or any swallow of your choice (we always use tuwon shinkafa and pepper-based catfish stew).
