##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Good Morning Quinoa, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Get  Breakfast Bake
1. Take 160 ml hot water
1. Take 80 ml freshly brewed coffee
1. Make ready 2 tbsp pumpkin puree
1. Get 2 tbsp maple syrup
1. Take 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice mix (recipe in my profile)
1. Take 1 tsp coconut oil
1. Take 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1. Get 40 grams dry quinoa
1. Make ready  Topping
1. Take 45 grams pecan nuts, chopped
1. Make ready 3 tbsp maple syrup
1. Get 3 tbsp ground almonds
1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp coconut flour
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp coconut oil
1. Take 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Get 1 pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vickys Good Morning Quinoa, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Preheat the oven to gas 4 / 180C / 350°F and lightly grease a casserole dish with a lid
1. Mix the main bake ingredients together - add the quinoa in last after mixing the liquids together first
1. Cover and bake for 45 minutes until most of the liquid has been sucked up by the quinoa
1. While the casserole is baking, mix together the topping ingredients and keep in the fridge until the 45 minutes cooking time is up
1. Remove the dish from the oven, sprinkle the topping over and bake uncovered for a further 15 minutes or until golden
1. Serve warm
