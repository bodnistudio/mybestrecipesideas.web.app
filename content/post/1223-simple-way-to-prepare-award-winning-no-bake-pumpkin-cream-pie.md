<h3>The ingredients needed to make No Bake Pumpkin Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of small boxes vanilla instant pudding mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cups of milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tablespoon of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tub (8 oz) of thawed cool whip. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of crushed pecans for garnish if desired. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of ready to use Graham cracker pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make No Bake Pumpkin Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Combine dry pudding mix, milk, pumpkin puree, and pumpkin pie spice in mixing bowl. Beat with hand held mixer until thick and creamy..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread 1 1/2 cups of the mixture into the pie crust..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			To the remaining mix add 1 1/2 cups cool whip and stir until combined. Spread over the pudding layer in the crust. Spread remaining cool whip on top for garnish..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
