##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Margherita Pizza:

1. Take 4 oz precooked shredded chicken
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
1. Make ready 1 pinch Chipotle &amp; Roasted Garlic
1. Prepare 1 bag (14 oz) Primo Taglio Pizza Dough
1. Take 1 Roma Tomato, Halved and sliced
1. Get 1/3 cup pizza sauce
1. Make ready to taste sweet and purple basil, chiffonaded
1. Prepare 1/3 cup Mozzarella
1. Take 1/3 cup Mozzarella cheese
1. Take 1/4 cup parmesan-Romano blend




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Margherita Pizza:

1. Pre heat oven to 450 degrees
1. In a skillet, place the precooked chicken and Italian Seasoning and cook and stir for 2 minutes. Do not over cook.
1. Stretch the pizza dough to fit a 12&#34;pizza pan. Place the pizza sauce on the dough, leaving a quarter inch rim.
1. Place 1/2 of the cheese blend on the pizza. Place the chicken, basil, and tomato. Cover with remaining cheese.
1. Cook for 12-14 minutes, depending on oven.
1. Eat and enjoy :-)
