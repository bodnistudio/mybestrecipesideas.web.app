##### The ingredients needed to make Protein Bhel:

1. Take 1 cup moong sprouts
1. Make ready 1 cup boiled red channa
1. Take 2-3 tbsp finely chopped cucumber
1. Get 2-3 tbsp finely chopped ivy gourd (tindi)
1. Prepare 2-3 tbsp finely chopped red bell pepper
1. Get to taste Salt
1. Prepare to taste Black rock salt
1. Take to taste Lemon juice
1. Prepare to taste Chaat masala
1. Take leaves Finely chopped fresh coriander
1. Get  Pomegranate pearls to garnish

##### Steps to make Protein Bhel:

1. In mixing bowl add sprouts moong and boiled red channa.
1. Add chopped cucumber,ivy gourd, red bell pepper,salt, black rock salt, lemon juice and chaat masala. Mix well.
1. Transfer in 2 serving bowls. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and pomegranate. Serve/Relish Delicious Protein Bhel immediately.
