##### The ingredients needed to make Ice Cream Biscuit Cake:

1. Get 1 Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream (or else)
1. Take 1 packages Oreo
1. Prepare 1 packages Milk Biscuit
1. Prepare 10 tbsp of margarin/ butter

##### Steps to make Ice Cream Biscuit Cake:

1. Crush the Oreo &amp; Milk Biscuit with blender or manually
1. Melt the 10 tablespoon of margrain/ butter
1. Pour the melted margarin/ butter into Oreo &amp; Biscuit dough, stir it.
1. Put the dough into the tupperware (plastic lunch box)
1. Put the Ice Cream on the topside of the biscuit dough.
1. Put into freezer about 30 minutes or for an hour.
1. After that, slice it with every size you Love.
