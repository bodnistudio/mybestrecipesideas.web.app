##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Aloo tuk:

1. Take 4 big potatoes
1. Make ready To taste salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
1. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
1. Take 1/2 tsp lemon juice
1. Make ready 2 tsp pomegranate to garnish
1. Get 2 tsp Coriander leaves
1. Take as needed oil to shallow fry




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Aloo tuk:

1. Peel, wash and cut potatoes in cubes
1. Boil cubes in salt water till parboiled
1. Heatup kadai add oil and press potatoes a little with palm
1. Fry potatoes on low flame so that they become crispy
1. Take them out and sprinkle salt, red chilli and black pepper powder
1. Finally sprinkle lemon juice, pomegranate and coriander leaves to enjoy aloo tuk
