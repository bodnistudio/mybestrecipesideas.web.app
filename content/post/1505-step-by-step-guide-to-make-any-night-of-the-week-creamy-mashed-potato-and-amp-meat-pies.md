##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Mashed Potato &amp; Meat Pies:

1. Prepare 100 grams Ground meat
1. Prepare 1/2 Onion
1. Take 1 Potato (medium)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Water
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive oil
1. Prepare 1 ●Salt and pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp ● Japanese-style Worcestershire sauce (Chunou)
1. Take 2 tbsp ●Ketchup
1. Take 2 to 3 tablespoons 35% fat fresh cream
1. Make ready 1 bag Frozen puff pastry
1. Get 1 dash Grated cheese
1. Prepare 1 dash Parsley




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Creamy Mashed Potato &amp; Meat Pies:

1. Defrost the frozen puff pastry at room temperature. Prepare 1 large and 1 small cup. Cut 12 pieces out of the pie sheets (3 per sheet) using the large cup.
1. Cut small circles out of the middles of 6 of the large circles you cut in step 1. Moisten the perimeters of the uncut circles, and place the rings made of pastry dough on top.
1. Put the pies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and brush the rims with beaten egg. Bake at 390°F/200°C for 15-20 minutes. Transfer the pie shells to a rack to cool.
1. Peel the potato and cut into small pieces. Put the pieces on a microwave safe dish, sprinkle 1 tablespoon of water and cover with plastic wrap. Microwave at 600W for 3-4 minutes.
1. Mash the cooked potato from Step 4 using a masher. Add salt, pepper, and cream, and mix well.
1. Chop the onion finely. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and stir fry the onion, then the ground meat. Add the ● ingredients to season.
1. Spoon the mashed potato into the cooled pie shells from Step 3, add the ground meat mixture, top with grated cheese and parsley and they&#39;re done.
1. I also recommend making these with shrimp and cream of mushroom.
