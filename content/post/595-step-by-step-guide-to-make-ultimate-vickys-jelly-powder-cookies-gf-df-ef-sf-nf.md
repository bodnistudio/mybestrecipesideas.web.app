##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Jelly-Powder Cookies, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Get 240 grams gluten-free /plain flour
1. Make ready 85 grams powdered jelly / jell-o, any flavour
1. Take 1 tbsp cornstarch, heaped
1. Get 3/4 tsp baking powder
1. Take 120 ml melted sunflower spread / safe &#39;butter&#39;
1. Take 60 ml light coconut milk
1. Get  Icing
1. Prepare 115 grams icing/powdered sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp taken from above jelly crystals
1. Prepare 2 tbsp light coconut milk or as required




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vickys Jelly-Powder Cookies, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Preheat the oven to gas 4 / 180C / 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
1. Set aside 1 tablespoon of the jelly crystals in a bowl for making the icing
1. In another bowl mix the flour, cornstarch, baking powder and remaining jelly crystals
1. Add the oil &amp; milk and mix until the dough is thick and evenly coloured
1. Take heaped teaspoons of the dough and roll into inch sized balls. Place on the baking sheet 2 inches apart
1. Flatten the balls with the bottom of a glass and bake for 8 - 9 minutes until the cookie tops are dry
1. Let cool on the baking sheet then transfer to a wire rack
1. To make the icing, add the icing sugar to the bowl with the 1tbsp jelly crystals in it. Add enough milk to make it spreadable but not runny
1. When the cookies have cooled spread a little icing on each and shake some sugar strands on top
1. These are perfect for kids and theme coloured food partys. Green/lime, blackcurrant/purple and orange are perfect colours for a Halloween party
1. Makes 28 - 32 cookies
