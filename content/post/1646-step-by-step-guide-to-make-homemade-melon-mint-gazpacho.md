##### The ingredients needed to make Melon Mint Gazpacho:

1. Take 1 kg Honeydew melon
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Mint leaf
1. Make ready 200 ml Lime juice
1. Get 1 pinch Salt &amp; Pepper to taste
1. Make ready 1 Prawn or Yabbies (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Melon Mint Gazpacho:

1. Place all of ingredients in the blender
1. Blend till become thick and smooth soup
1. Season to taste
