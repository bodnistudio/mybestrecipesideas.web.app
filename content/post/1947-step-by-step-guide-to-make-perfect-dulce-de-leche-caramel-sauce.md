##### The ingredients needed to make Dulce De Leche Caramel Sauce:

1. Take 4 cup milk
1. Get 2 cup white sugar
1. Take 1 tsp baking soda
1. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Dulce De Leche Caramel Sauce:

1. Bring the milk to boil, add the sugar, vanilla and baking soda. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
1. Reduce the heat and wash down the sides of the pan
1. Stir on medium heat until sugar is desolved &amp; it starts to bubble
1. Simmer on medium for about an hour scooping off any froth every 10-15mins
1. At 45min check regularly to avoid burning
1. Once turned dark brown &amp; thick pour through metal seive
1. Allow to cool &amp; thicken. Store in jar in fridge.
