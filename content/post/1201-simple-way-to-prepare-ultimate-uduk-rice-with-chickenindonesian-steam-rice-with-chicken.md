##### The ingredients needed to make Uduk Rice With Chicken(Indonesian Steam Rice With Chicken):

1. Get  Chicken breast
1. Get 2 cups Japanese rice
1. Get 5 cloves garlic (grated)
1. Prepare 2 Indonesian bay leaves
1. Make ready 3 orange leaves
1. Get  Coconut milk
1. Take 1 tbs chicken powder
1. Prepare 2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1. Get  Water
1. Take  Lemong grass




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Uduk Rice With Chicken(Indonesian Steam Rice With Chicken):

1. Wash n cut chicken breast in to small pieces (cube is better) and marinade. I bought chicken which has been marinade from supermarket
1. Heat extra virgin olive oil, grated garlic until fragrant, add chicken and fry it until brown all over. Set aside
1. Mix well coconut milk, chicken powder and water in another bowl
1. Wash and draining the rice, and put the uncooked rice into the rice cooker, add lemonggrass, bay leaf, orange leaf, Chicken and fill up with Mixed coconut milk water n chicken powder untill the third line and mix well, close the cover of rice cooker and press cooking
1. After 20 minutes cooking, stir the rice so that all ingredients are evenly mixed and then cover again and wait untill done
1. After cooking leave it for about 10 more minutes
1. Serve rice with chrispy fried chicken, crackers, sambal and cucumbar, and enjoy it :)
