##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Moist Sweet Potato Muffins:

1. Get 300 grams Asian sweet potato
1. Prepare 100 grams Butter
1. Take 90 grams Sugar
1. Make ready 50 ml Heavy cream (or milk)
1. Get 2 Eggs
1. Make ready 3 tbsp ★Plain flour
1. Prepare 1 tsp ★Baking powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Black sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Moist Sweet Potato Muffins:

1. Shift together the ★ marked ingredients.
1. Melt the butter in the microwave.
1. Peel the potato and cut into 1cm slices. Place on a microwave safe bowl and put in the 600 W microwave for 6 minutes. The potatoes should be pierced easily with a toothpick. Mash the potatoes.
1. Preheat the oven to 180℃
1. Add the sugar and melted butter to the mashed potatoes and combine with a whisk.
1. Add the heavy cream (or milk) and combine.
1. Add the egg and the black sesame seeds and combine.
1. Fold in the shifted ★ingredients until it is mixed in well.
1. Add the butter to the muffin cups and bake in the oven for 20~25 minutes.
1. Done.
