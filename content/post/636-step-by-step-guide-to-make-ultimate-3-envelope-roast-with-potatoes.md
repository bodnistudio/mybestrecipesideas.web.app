##### The ingredients needed to make 3 Envelope Roast with Potatoes:

1. Get 3 lbs beef roast
1. Take 1 envelope dry Italian salad dressing mix
1. Take 1 envelope dry ranch salad dressing mix
1. Get 1 envelope dry brown gravy mix
1. Prepare 2 cups water
1. Take 6-8 medium potatoes




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make 3 Envelope Roast with Potatoes:

1. Put water in a measuring cup that is larger than the amount you are using.  Add and mix both dry salad dressing and gravy mix to the water. Mix until completely blended.
1. Brown roast, if desired. Place meat in slow cooker.
1. Pour water, salad dressing and gravy mixture over roast.  Cook in slow cooker on high for 4 hours or on low for 8 hours.  Cut potatoes into fours and place in slow cook 45 minutes to an hour before dish is done.
