##### The ingredients needed to make Pickled Hot Sausage:

1. Get 1 pack hotdogs
1. Make ready 1 cleaned jar
1. Prepare 2 cups vinegar
1. Take 3/4 cup water
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp salt
1. Prepare 1 Tbsp pepper
1. Make ready 1-2 Tbsp red pepper flakes
1. Make ready  red food dye (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pickled Hot Sausage:

1. Boil water, vinegar, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes &amp; 10 drops of red food dye (optional) until liquid is rolling &amp; salt is dissolved.
1. Prick holes in hotdogs with a fork by poking hotdogs twice, put hotdogs in jar.
1. Pour water in jar &amp; immedatly put lid on &amp; tighten. Let pickle for at least two days. Wait at least a week for super spicy flavor.
