##### The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Rolls:

1. Take  cabbage about 2lbs 2 smaller heads
1. Make ready  diced onion
1. Take  garlic, minced
1. Take  olive oil
1. Take  mushrooms, (optional)
1. Take  ground sirloin
1. Get  salt
1. Take  chopped parsley
1. Prepare  uncooked white rice
1. Prepare  Pomi strained tomatoes or canned tomato puree
1. Take  liquid (beef stock, cabbage water, or combine with some leftover tomato for 3/4 cup)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cabbage Rolls:

1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Cut center core out of cabbage and place, core side down, in boiling water. Cover and simmer for 5minutes. - While cabbage simmers, heat oil in pan over med-high heat and sauté onion and garlic 2/5 minutes until browned. Add mushrooms and cook another 3 minutes. Transfer mixture to a bowl
1. Remove cabbage to a rimmed baking sheet, keeping water in the pot. Gently remove leaves from cabbage, cutting away from the core with a small knife. Set aside the best 16 unbroken leaves and cut off the thick spine in the center for easier rolling. Set all remaining broken or small cabbage leaves aside. (If the core is too firm, return it to boiling water for a few minutes to soften)
1. To cooled onion mixture, add meat, rice, parsley, tomato, salt &amp; pepper. Combine well. - Place about cup of filling in each leaf, roll, and place seam side down in a 13 by 9-inch pan. Pour liquid over rolls and lay some leftover leaves on top Cover pan tightly with foil, and bake for 1 1/2 hours Place pan on a cookie sheet in case it drips  - Remove from oven and let rest 30 minutes or better still, refrigerate overnight. They freeze well
