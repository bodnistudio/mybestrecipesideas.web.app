##### The ingredients needed to make Sweetie&#39;s Meatloaf ?:

1. Get 1/2 small onion
1. Get 1 small green pepper
1. Take 4 oz mushrooms
1. Get 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1. Make ready 1 cup ketchup
1. Take 1/2 packages saltine crackers (sleeve)
1. Take 1 packages pork roll sausage (16 oz.)
1. Get 2 lb ground hamburger
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 dash black pepper, to taste
1. Take 2 large eggs




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sweetie&#39;s Meatloaf ?:

1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. Chop onions, peppers and mushrooms, set to the side
1. Mix ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Add to vegetables.
1. Crush crackers finely ( I used a small Zip Loc baggy but recommend a rolling pin instead of the can I had to use)
1. Mix hamburger, sausage, eggs and crackers in large mixing bowl. Add vegetables with sauce and mix well.
1. Place in 9&#34;x13&#34; baking dish, evenly. Bake for 60 minutes. Enjoy!!
1. 15 minute prep time, serves six to eight
