##### The ingredients needed to make Mamma’ s Chicken Cacciatore:

1. Get 8 chicken thighs bone in and skin on
1. Make ready 1 red pepper diced
1. Prepare 1 orange pepper diced
1. Get 1 yellow pepper diced
1. Take 1 small package baby Bella mushrooms sliced
1. Take 1 small yellow onion diced
1. Get 1 (16 ounce) can cento crushed tomatoes
1. Prepare 1 Tbls olive oil
1. Take 2 dry bay leaves
1. Make ready 2 minced cloves of garlic
1. Make ready  Mrs. Dash Italian blend seasoning to taste
1. Get to taste Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mamma’ s Chicken Cacciatore:

1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
1. Heat olive oil in deep sauté pan and place chicken thighs in and brown on both sides. Remove from pan to plate after browning.
1. Add all veggies garlic and Mrs. dash seasoning to sauté pan and sauté approximately 5 mins.
1. Add crushed tomatoes, bay leaves and additional Mrs. Dash seasoning to taste. Bring to a simmer and add chicken thighs back to the sauté pan.
1. Cover the sauté pan and bring heat to low. Let simmer for an hour stirring approximately every 20 mins. Continue simmering one to one and a half hours low and slow. Chicken should be falling off the bone by then.
1. Serve over rice or any small pasta you wish.
