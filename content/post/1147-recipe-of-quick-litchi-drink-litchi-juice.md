##### The ingredients needed to make Litchi Drink/ Litchi Juice:

1. Take 30 litchis
1. Make ready 7 tbsp powdered sugar
1. Prepare 1 tsp salt
1. Take 240 ml + 500 ml water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Litchi Drink/ Litchi Juice:

1. Peel the skin and remove the seeds of all litchi.
1. In a blending jar, add litchi, powdered sugar, salt and 240 ml water.
1. Blend it well and strain.
1. Add 500 ml water and mix.
1. Keep refrigerate and serve.
