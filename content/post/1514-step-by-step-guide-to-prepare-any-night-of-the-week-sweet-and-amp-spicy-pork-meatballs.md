##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet &amp; Spicy Pork Meatballs:

1. Make ready  meatballs
1. Make ready 1 lb ground pork
1. Prepare 1 tsp grated ginger root
1. Prepare 1 tsp grated garlic
1. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper
1. Prepare 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1. Make ready 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
1. Take 2 oz chopped green chilis (1/2 small can)
1. Make ready 1 packages pork seasoning from Ramen noodles
1. Get  sauce
1. Make ready 3/4 cup pineapple juice
1. Make ready 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1. Get 1/4 cup water
1. Prepare 1 tbsp cornstarch
1. Get 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1. Get 1/4 tsp sriracha
1. Make ready 1 tsp sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sweet &amp; Spicy Pork Meatballs:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine all ingredients for the meatballs in a large bowl and mix until meat mixture is just combined with all the ingredients.
1. Form your meatballs and place on foil lined baking sheet. I used an 1-ounce scoop and made 2 meatballs out of each scoop; about the size of a heaping teaspoon. Bake for 20-25 mins.
1. Combine all ingredients for the sauce in a small saucepan and whisk until thickened, about 5 mins. Pour over meatballs and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve with white rice.
