##### The ingredients needed to make Best Butter Cookies:

1. Get 1 cup butter
1. Take 2/3 cup sugar
1. Get 1 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1. Take 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1. Get 1/4 tsp salt

##### Instructions to make Best Butter Cookies:

1. preheat oven to 350
1. have two seperate bowls for butter, sugar, vanilla, egg mixture and flour and salt mixture
1. cream together butter and sugar
1. add vanilla extract then add egg and mix well
1. slowly add flour and salt mixture to the butter mixture mix well
1. make dough into desired shapes and add to greased cookie sheet make cookies very thin
1. bake cookies 10-12 minutes or till brown on bottom
1. cool for 10 minutes
