##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Pan Cake with Chocolate Ice cream:

1. Take 1 cup All purpose flour
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
1. Prepare 2 tsp sugar powder
1. Take 1 cup milk
1. Make ready 1 scoop chocolate ice cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Banana Pan Cake with Chocolate Ice cream:

1. Take a mixing bowl put all purpose flour,sugar,baking powder mix then add milk and make a semi liquid batter keep for rest for half an hour.
1. After half an hour take a nonstick pan pour some oil and pour batter then see bubbles turn on another side.
1. Serve with scoop of chocolate ice cream.
