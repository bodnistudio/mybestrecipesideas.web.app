##### The ingredients needed to make Italian Dressed Chicken and Potatoes:

1. Prepare 1 Italian Dressing
1. Prepare 1 Boneless Skinless Thin Cut Chicken
1. Get 2 Jalepinos
1. Take 2 clove Garlic
1. Make ready 3 Potatoes
1. Get 1 tbsp Garlic Salt
1. Prepare 3 dash Paprika
1. Take 3 dash Salt and Pepper
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Olive Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Italian Dressed Chicken and Potatoes:

1. Wash chicken then chop up chicken into small peices
1. Add chicken peices to a bowl. Mince garlic and add to chicken. Clean jalepinos and remove seeds, chop up and add to bowl.
1. Add in Italian Dressing to marinate. Cover and refrigerate for 30 mins or longer.
1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
1. Slice potatoes thinly or use food processor to slice.
1. Add oil to baking sheet and lay out potato slices.Season with garlic salt, paprika, salt and pepper.
1. Bake potatoes for 10-20 minutes until soft and crispy. Check and flip potatoes every 5 minutes.
1. While potatoes are cooking, pan fry marinated chicken. Cook chicken in pan until no longer pink and marinate no longer watery.
