##### The ingredients needed to make Dryfruit Chocolate Pizza 🍕 😍:

1. Get 8 inches Pizza base
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Grated White chocolate compound
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate Compound
1. Make ready as required Dryfruits
1. Get as required Chocolate Syrup / Ganache as per requirement
1. Get as required Fresh cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Dryfruit Chocolate Pizza 🍕 😍:

1. Take Pizza Base.
1. Now melt dark chocolate compound and fresh cream in a bowl.
1. Mix them and keep in the fridge for a while so that it sets and becomes ganache.
1. Once it becomes thick a bit spread it on. Pizza base like sauce.
1. After spreading it, grate some white chocolate on it like cheese.
1. Also, sprinkle some dryfruits and drizzle chocolate sauce.
1. Bake at 180 C for 10 mins.
1. Serve hot 🤤
