##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Glazed Salmon:

1. Get 2 Salmon fillets
1. Make ready 2 Limes
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
1. Prepare 3 tsp Sriracha




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Glazed Salmon:

1. Preheat oven to 425
1. Zest and juice the limes
1. Sprinkle zest on the fish
1. Mix the rest of the ingredients in a bowl.
1. Spread the sauce on the fish
1. Bake for about 12-16 minutes or until fish is done
