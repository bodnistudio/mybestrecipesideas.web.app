##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Ham Soup:

1. Get 5-6 large potatos
1. Prepare 1 pack diced ham
1. Take 1 pack bacon
1. Prepare 1 yellow onion
1. Make ready 8 oz cream cheese
1. Make ready 2 cans cream of mushroom
1. Prepare 1 cup milk
1. Take 2 tbsp butter
1. Prepare to taste Salt &amp; pepper
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Garlic powder
1. Make ready 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

##### Instructions to make Potato Ham Soup:

1. Peel &amp; dice potatoes, mince onion &amp; add both to crockpot. Put crockpot on high.
1. In a large mixing bowl beat cream cheese &amp; both cans cream of mushroom &amp; melted butter. Then add to crockpot.
1. Mix &amp; stir thoroughly, then add milk, ham &amp; any seasonings. Cover &amp; cook for 4 hrs
1. When potatoes are almost tender, fry bacon in a large skillet. As soon as bacon cools, crumble into crockpot with shredded cheddar cheese &amp; stir. (Add a little water or milk if soup is too thick). Serve &amp; enjoy!
