<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Hand Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425ยบ. In a medium bowl.  Whisk together pumpkin,  both sugars, spices, 1 egg and cornstarch.  Stir till well mixed..
			
			
		</li>
