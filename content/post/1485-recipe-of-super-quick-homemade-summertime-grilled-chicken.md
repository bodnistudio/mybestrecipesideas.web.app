##### The ingredients needed to make Summertime Grilled Chicken:

1. Get  pineapple marinade
1. Get 1 cup pineapple juice
1. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1. Take 1/3 cup soy sauce
1. Take 1/3 cup teriakyi sauce
1. Make ready 5 piece chicken breasts
1. Get  BBQ sauce
1. Take 4 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1. Get 3 tbsp brown sugar, packed
1. Make ready 1/2 cup ketchup
1. Make ready 1/3 cup pineapple

##### Steps to make Summertime Grilled Chicken:

1. Heat all the marinade ingredients in a sauce pan. Remove from heat before it starts to boil. Let set 5-10 before pouring over chicken. Cover and place in fridge for at least 30 mins. I tend to leave it for a couple hours.
1. Place on grill and brush with BBQ sauce repeatedly til done. Cooking time will vary depending on thickness of chicken. I usually cook mine 20 mins.
