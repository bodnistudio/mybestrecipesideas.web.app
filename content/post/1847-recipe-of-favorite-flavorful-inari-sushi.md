##### The ingredients needed to make Flavorful Inari Sushi:

1. Take 6 Aburaage or sushiage
1. Make ready 12 inari sushi Hot cooked rice
1. Prepare 300 ml Dashi stock
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Get 4 tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin
1. Get 1 tbsp Sake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Flavorful Inari Sushi:

1. Pour hot water over the abura-age to drain excess oil.
1. When the aburaage has cooled down, use a chopsticks to roll against the aburaage to open up the slit (skip this step if using sushi age).
1. Slice the abura-age in half. (Triangle or square - either is fine! I usually cut it into squares.)
1. Simmer dashi soup and condiments. Add the abura-age and simmer until the broth is reduced (don&#39;t burn it!).
1. Prepare the rice filling. (In the photo above, I used shiso leaves, kelp, and shirasu).
1. When the abura-age has cooled down enough, stuff in the rice to finish.
