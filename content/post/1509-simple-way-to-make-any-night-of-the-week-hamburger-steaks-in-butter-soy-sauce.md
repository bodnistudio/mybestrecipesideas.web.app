##### The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Steaks in Butter-Soy Sauce:

1. Prepare 400 grams Ground beef and pork mix
1. Make ready 1 large Finely chopped onions
1. Make ready 60 grams Panko
1. Get 1 Egg
1. Make ready 1 pinch Oregano or nutmeg
1. Make ready 1 fair amount Salt and pepper
1. Get 40 ml Milk
1. Take  For the sauce;
1. Get 120 cc Red wine
1. Get 20 grams Butter
1. Get 2 tsp Sugar
1. Take 20 ml Soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp Oyster sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Hamburger Steaks in Butter-Soy Sauce:

1. In a bowl, mix the ingredients for the hamburgers. Mix until the meat and ingredients have become well incorporated.
1. Divide the ingredients into 4 equal portions and form into round patties as shown in the picture. Make sure they&#39;re an even thickness. Each patty should weight about 200g.
1. Heat a pan with oil and brown the patties on both sides. Turn off the heat, transfer them back to a plate, and microwave for 4 minutes without using plastic wrap.
1. The hamburgers should be thoroughly cooked once the juices run clear. If the bottom of the pan is burnt, scrape it off with a spatula, add red wine, and bring it to a boil.
1. Turn down the heat to low and add butter, sugar, and soy sauce, return the hamburgers and meat broth to the pan, coat both sides with the sauce, boil briefly, and it&#39;s done.
1. Transfer to plates and pour the sauce over them. You can combine them with anything.
