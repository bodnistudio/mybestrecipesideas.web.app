<h3>Steps to make Chocolate Pumpkin Pudding Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Mix together dry chocolate pudding mix, 3/4 cup of milk, &amp; 1 cup of the whipped topping. Whisk until blended. Set aside..
