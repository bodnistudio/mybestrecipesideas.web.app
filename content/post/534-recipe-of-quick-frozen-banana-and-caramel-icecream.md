##### Instructions to make Frozen Banana and caramel icecream:

1. Cut the bananas into slices and put them into a ziplock bag.
1. Put the ziplock bag into the freezer for a minimum one hour. (you can put it for a longer time as well)
1. After an hour or so take out the bananas from the freezer and churn them with the help of the grinder.
1. Put 2 tbsp milk to smoothen it.
1. Add 1 tbsp of honey for extra sweetness.
1. For caramel, heat up 50 g sugar in a vessel until the sugar turns light brown.
1. Take the sugar off from the stove and let it chill for 30 seconds.
1. Meanwhile pour the churned bananas into an icecream container. Pour the caramel on top of it.
1. Let it freeze for at least 2 hours.
1. Enjoy your health yet delicious icecream.
