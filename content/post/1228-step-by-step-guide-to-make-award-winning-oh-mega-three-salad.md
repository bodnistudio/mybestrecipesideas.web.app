##### Steps to make Oh Mega Three Salad:

1. Preaheat oven to 180C (360F)
1. Place salmon fillet on foil, rub on pinch of salt, place on slice or two of lemon, place slice of butter, sprinkle some chopped spring onion (optional). Wrap tight and place it in the oven. Cooking time - 30-35 mins for 350g fillet.
1. While salmon is cooking, chop up salad ingredients.
