<h3>The ingredients needed to make Mini Pecan Pie Tarts:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of Chopped Pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tbsp of Melted Butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 4 tbsp of Brown Sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of Large Eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tbsp of Honey. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 pinch of Cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 packages of Filo Tart Shells. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Mini Pecan Pie Tarts:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Set aside tart shells and pecans. 2 packages of tart shells yields 30 tarts and can be found in the freezer section of your grocery store..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Blend all other ingredients together in a medium mixing bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add pecans to mixture and mix well.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Arrange tart shells on baking sheet and fill with mixture.
