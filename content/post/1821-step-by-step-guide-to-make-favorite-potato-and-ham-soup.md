##### The ingredients needed to make Potato and Ham Soup:

1. Make ready  Diced potatoes to your liking
1. Prepare 1 chopped onion
1. Get 2 chicken bouillon cubes
1. Prepare  Water to cover potatoes
1. Prepare 1/4 cup butter (half stick)
1. Make ready  Ham diced to your liking
1. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
1. Get 2 cup milk
1. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Potato and Ham Soup:

1. Cube potatoes as many as you want. Cut onion and add chicken boullion, ham, salt &amp; pepper. Cook til tender.
1. In a separate sauce pan make white sauce (melt butter and whisk in flour until smooth. Add milk and whisk over medium heat until thickens
1. Add white sauce into the cooked potatoes and ham and stir over medium heat until soup thickens
1. Serve plain or with shredded cheese on top
