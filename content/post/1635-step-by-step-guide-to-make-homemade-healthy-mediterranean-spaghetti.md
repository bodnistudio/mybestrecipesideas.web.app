##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Mediterranean Spaghetti:

1. Take 3 tbsp Garlic
1. Prepare 2 stick Green onions
1. Make ready 1/2 can Diced tomatoes
1. Take 4 tbsp EVOO
1. Make ready 2 Chicken bouillon cubes
1. Prepare 1/2 box Whole grain spaghetti
1. Get 1/4 packages Frozen spinach
1. Get 2 tbsp Butter
1. Prepare 1 cup Water
1. Make ready 1 dash Pepper
1. Prepare 1 cup Low sodium chicken broth (instead of water and chicken bouillon if prefer)
1. Make ready 1/2 packages Frozen shrimp (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Healthy Mediterranean Spaghetti:

1. Cook spaghetti following box instructions.
1. Drain cooked spaghetti, add butter and 1 tsp EVOO, cover and let sit.
1. Add the rest of EVOO to a pan, garlic and  chopped onion, and cook for about 2 min.
1. Add diced tomatoes to pan, cubes of chicken bouillon, spinach, pepper and water. Also add shrimp if using. Simmer until lightly reduced.
1. Mix sauce with spaghetti and serve.
