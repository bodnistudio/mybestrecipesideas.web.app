##### The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Brownie Sundae (without sugar):

1. Prepare 1 cup Whole wheat flour
1. Get 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda
1. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder
1. Take Pinch salt
1. Get 1/4 cup Cocoa powder
1. Get 2 teaspoon Instant coffee powder
1. Get 1/4 cup Curd
1. Prepare 1/4 cup jaggery
1. Take 1/2 cup Oil
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Chopped walnuts
1. Prepare  Vanilla ice-cream (refined sugar free, preferably homemade)
1. Get  Chocolate sauce(refined sugar free preferably homemade)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Whole Wheat Brownie Sundae (without sugar):

1. First of all to make the Brownie mix wheat flour, salt, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder and coffee powder and sieve well. In another bowl add oil, jaggery, curd and whisk well till well combined and in this add the dry ingredients mixture in parts. Mix well.
1. Now pour the batter in a greased tin (or cake mould) and bake it till it is done.
1. After baking the Brownie loaf cool it down little bit in room temperature and cut in pieces.
1. Place the pieces in serving bowl. Place 1-2 scoops of ice-cream on Brownie pieces.
1. Add chocolate sauce on it.
1. Add chopped walnuts now and Brownie crumb if leaft.
1. And your healthy, without refined sugar, whole wheat brownie sundae is ready. Enjoy!
