##### The ingredients needed to make Twisted Chilli Garlic Pasta with Chicken:

1. Prepare 1 packet twisted macaroni (boiled)
1. Get 200 gm chicken breast cubes
1. Get 1 cup National chilli garlic ketchup
1. Get 4 tbsp soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp worstershire sauce(optional)
1. Prepare to taste Salt
1. Make ready to taste Black pepper powder
1. Make ready 4-5 garlic cloves crushed
1. Get 1/4 cup oil
1. Get 1 capsicum julienne cut
1. Make ready 1 carrot julienne cut
1. Take 3/4 cup cabbage shredded
1. Take 1/4 cup spring onions julienne cut
