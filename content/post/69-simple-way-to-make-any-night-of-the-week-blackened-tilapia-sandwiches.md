##### The ingredients needed to make Blackened Tilapia Sandwiches:

1. Prepare  Seafood
1. Get 1 1/2 lb Tilapia Fillets
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Old Bay Blackening Seasoning
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
1. Take 4 to 6 hoagie rolls, split,buttered,and toasted
1. Prepare 1 packages Shredded Lettuce
1. Get 1 Tomato, thinly sliced
1. Prepare 1 **Super-Duper Sauce Below**
1. Make ready 1 cup Mayonnaise
1. Take 1 tbsp Minced Green Onion
1. Get 2 tsp Fresh Lemon Juice
1. Prepare 2 tsp Sweet Pickle Relish
1. Get 2 tsp Hot Sauce
1. Prepare 1 tsp Creole Seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Blackened Tilapia Sandwiches:

1. Sprinkle tilapia with Old Bay Blackening Seasoning
1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add tilapia, and cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
1. Spread cut sides of rolls with desired amount of Super Duper Sauce. Top bottom halves of rolls with shredded lettuce, sliced tomato, and Blackened Tilapia. Cover with top halves of rolls. Serve with a smile.
1. **Super Duper Sauce** In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, green onion, lemon juice, relish, and Creole seasoning.
