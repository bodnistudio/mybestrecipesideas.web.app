##### The ingredients needed to make Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables:

1. Get 12 oz Potatoes (cut into 1 1/2-inch chunks)
1. Get 1 small Zucchini (sliced into rounds)
1. Prepare 2 Bell Peppers (chopped into 1-inch squares)
1. Take 1 cup Red Onion (sliced)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Olive Oil
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
1. Take 1/2 tsp Basil
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Chives
1. Prepare 4 Garlic Cloves (minced)
1. Get 1 Salt and Pepper to Taste

##### Instructions to make Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables:

1. Preheat oven to 425℉.
1. Prepare a shallow roasting dish with nonstick cooking spray.
1. Combine potatoes, zucchini, both bell peppers, and the red onion slices and place in roasting dish.
1. Whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, oregano, chives, garlic and salt and pepper.
1. Drizzle sauce over the mixed vegetables and toss to coat.
1. Roast vegetables until tender, about 45 minutes. Stir halfway through the cooking time.
