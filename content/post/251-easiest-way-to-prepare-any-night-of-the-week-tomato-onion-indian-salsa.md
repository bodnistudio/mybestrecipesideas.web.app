##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Onion Indian Salsa:

1. Make ready 3 Tomatoes
1. Make ready 1.5 Onion
1. Prepare 3 Shallots
1. Get 3 cloves Garlic(optional)
1. Take 1 Lemon for juicing
1. Make ready 2 tsp Chilli powder
1. Make ready 0.5 tsp Pepper powder
1. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
1. Prepare to taste salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Tomato Onion Indian Salsa:

1. Chop tomato into big size pieces
1. Chop onions, shallots, garlic into small pieces.
1. Mash all the 4 in a bowl using your fingers.If you find it difficult to mash using fingers, use back side of a steel tumbler.Mash well so that the tomatoes become pasty.
1. Add chilly powder, pepper powder(according to your taste), sugar, salt, and lemon juice to the above mixture and mash well.
1. Chop coriander leaves and garnish.
1. Mix well and keep aside for 15 minutes before serving.
