##### The ingredients needed to make Grandma&#39;s Simple Inari Sushi:

1. Prepare 5 pieces  Thin aburaage
1. Take 300 ml ○ Water
1. Get 3 tbsp ○ Sugar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ○ Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ○ Mirin
1. Make ready 1 approximately 225 grams  Sushi rice:  https://cookpad.com/en/recipes/188111-our-easy-reduced-sodium-sushi-rice

##### Steps to make Grandma&#39;s Simple Inari Sushi:

1. Remove the excess oil from the aburaage (by pouring hot water or with a paper towel). Roll a chopstick over them, slice in half, then use the tip of the chopstick to peel the layers to form pockets.
1. Place all the ○ ingredients into a large pot and once it boils, add the aburaage. Alternate low to high heat while boiling (7 to 8 minutes).
1. When most of the broth has evaporated, remove from heat to cool the aburaage. Lightly squeeze out the excess cooking liquid and pack with sushi rice.
1. If you have leftover aburaage, top on a bowl of udon or soba noodles.
