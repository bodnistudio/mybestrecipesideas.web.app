##### The ingredients needed to make Andouille Pasta:

1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Get 2 clove garlic, minced
1. Prepare 2 andouille sausage
1. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
1. Prepare 12 oz penne pasta
1. Make ready 3 cup chicken stock
1. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
1. Take 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
1. Take 1 tbsp chopped parsley
1. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Make ready 1/4 cup heavy cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Andouille Pasta:

1. Cut Andouille sausage into bite size slices.
1. Heat olive oil to a large soup pot.  Add sausage, garlic, and onion to oil and saute until just brown.
1. Add pasta, diced tomatoes, chicken stock, milk, parsley, salt and pepper to the pot and stir.  Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and continue to simmer until pasta is cooked.
1. Once pasta is cooked add cream and stir.  Let stand for several minutes to allow sauce to thicken.
