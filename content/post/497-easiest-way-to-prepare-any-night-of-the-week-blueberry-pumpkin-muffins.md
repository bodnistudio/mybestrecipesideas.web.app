##### The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Pumpkin Muffins:

1. Get 1 tsp baking soda
1. Make ready 2 1/2 cup AP flour
1. Make ready 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1 cup raw honey
1. Make ready 2 eggs
1. Take 2 cup or a 15 oz can pumpkin puree
1. Make ready 1/2 cup applesauce
1. Prepare 1 cup fresh blueberries




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Blueberry Pumpkin Muffins:

1. Preheat oven 350°F
1. Mix all dry ingredients.   Add the rest, except berries and mix partially.  Fold in berries.  Do not over mix.   Spoon into lined muffin tins.
1. Bake 18-20 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.
1. Let rest in pans for 15 minutes then transfer onto cooling racks and cool completely.  Store in an air tight container.
