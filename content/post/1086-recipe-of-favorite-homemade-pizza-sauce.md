##### Steps to make Homemade pizza sauce:

1. Heat a nonstick tawa, add oil. - Add garlic and chilli, sauté for two minutes.
1. Add the tomato purée. Sauté well and add the tomato paste. - Add salt. Mix well.
1. Add the dried herbs and chilli flakes. - Sauté well.
1. Now add the basil leaves and cook for two minutes. - Your pizza sauce is ready.
