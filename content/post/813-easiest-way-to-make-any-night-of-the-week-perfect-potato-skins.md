##### The ingredients needed to make Perfect Potato Skins:

1. Prepare 3 large Potatos
1. Take 1 Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1. Get 8 slice Bacon
1. Get 1 Olive oil
1. Take 1 pinch Salt &amp; pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Perfect Potato Skins:

1. Preheat oven to 450°F
1. Wash potatoes
1. Cut each in half. Then scrape out insides, leaving about a cm of potatoes each.
1. Cover the outside &amp; insides of the potatoes with butter, then olive oil, then salt &amp; pepper.
1. Place in the over on rack faced down for 12 - 15 mins.
1. While thats cooking, in a skillet on a medium high heat, cook the bacon until almost crispy.
1. Cut bacon up until it looks like bacon bits.
1. Take out potatoes sprinkle with more salt &amp; pepper. Especially on the skin part. Then fill each potato with shredded cheese &amp; bacon.
1. Place potatoes back in the oven for 3 mins. Or until the cheese melts.
1. Take them out &amp; sprinkle with extra salt &amp; pepper &amp; bacon, if you want.
