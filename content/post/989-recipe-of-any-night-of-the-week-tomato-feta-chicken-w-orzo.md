##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato-Feta Chicken w/ Orzo:

1. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Take 3 chicken breasts
1. Take 1 medium onion, chopped (~1 cup)
1. Get 2 clove garlic, minced
1. Get 2 tbsp cilatnro or parsely, chopped
1. Prepare 1 tsp dried oregano
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp pepper
1. Take 28 oz stewed, sliced tomatoes (undrained)
1. Prepare 3/4 lb orzo pasta
1. Take 1/4 cup feta cheese crumbles
1. Prepare 1/2 cup white wine or cooking sherry




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tomato-Feta Chicken w/ Orzo:

1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Heat 1 tbsp oil in large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until done, then remove from pan.
1. Meanwhile, boil water and cook orzo according to package instructions. Cover cooked pasta to keep warm.
1. Add remaining 1 tbsp oil to skillet, and cook onions until starting to brown, ~ 6 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, ~ 30 seconds more.
1. Stir in wine, tomatoes, pepper, herbs and cooked chicken. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat, COVER and simmer for 5-10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
1. Serve over hot pasta, and sprinkle with feta.
