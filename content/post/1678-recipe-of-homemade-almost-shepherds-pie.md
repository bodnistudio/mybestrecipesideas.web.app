<h3>The ingredients needed to make Almost Shepherds Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 can of Cream of mushroom soup. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 can of Cream of chicken soup. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 envelope of onion soup mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 lb of ground beef. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 4 cup of mashed potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of Course ground black pepper. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cup of frozen mixed vegetables. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Almost Shepherds Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350°.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Brown ground beef in skillet.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix soups, onion mix, salt, pepper, and milk in casserole dish.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Boil vegetables according to pkg directions then add to casserole dish.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix well.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add cooked beef to casserole.   Mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top with mashed potatoes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese, bake 5 minutes until cheese is melted..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
