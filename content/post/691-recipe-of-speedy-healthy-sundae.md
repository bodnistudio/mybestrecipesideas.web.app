##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Sundae:

1. Get 1 scoop Chocolate/Butterscotch Icecream
1. Get 1 teaspoon Biscuit Crumbs
1. Make ready 1 teaspoon Roasted and Crushed peanuts
1. Take 1 teaspoon Chocolate Sauce for garnish
1. Make ready 1 piece Wafer Biscuit




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Healthy Sundae:

1. Put chocolate sauce on the walls of the glass from inside. Keep rotating the glass while doing so.
1. Put 1 tbsp Muesli in the glass.
1. On it put 1 scoop of Vanilla Ice cream, then crushed biscuits and some more muesli.
1. On this put 1 scoop of Chocolate chip ice cream then crushed peanuts.
1. Drizzle Chocolate sauce and serve.
