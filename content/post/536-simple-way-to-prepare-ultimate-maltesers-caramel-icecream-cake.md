##### The ingredients needed to make Maltesers Caramel Icecream Cake:

1. Get 20 choco balls
1. Make ready 2 cups Whipping cream
1. Take 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
1. Take 1 cup Condensed milk
1. Take 1/2 cup Caramel syrup
1. Take 10 Choco biscuits
1. Prepare 3 tablespoons Butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Maltesers Caramel Icecream Cake:

1. Mix condensed milk with vanilla extract and then whip the cream till the soft peak forms.
1. Combine the condensed milk and vanilla mixture with the cream mixture. Fold it gently with some crushed malteser balls.
1. Grind the biscuit with butter in a processor. In a loaf pan line the biscuit mixture and make it even using a spatula.
1. After that pour the icecream mixture and keep it in freezer for atleast 6 hours.
1. Drizzle the caramel syrup on the ice cream and garnish it with maltesers choco balls..
