##### The ingredients needed to make Saucy BBQ Skillet Pork Steaks:

1. Prepare 3 large pork steaks
1. Take 2 cup sweet baby rays hickory and brown sugar bbq sauce
1. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
1. Get 1 dash onion powder, and salt per steak




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Saucy BBQ Skillet Pork Steaks:

1. Season pork steaks with onion powder and salt, rub as much brown sugar as you want on each steak.
1. Rub sweet baby rays on each steak.
1. Put in large bowl and put in fridge to marinate for at least a hour.
1. Put remaining sweet baby rays BBQ sauce in another bowl with lid, set aside for later.
1. In large preheated skillet add one steak and cook till done, flipping occasionally so it doesn&#39;t blacken real bad.
1. Remove pork steak from skillet and put in bowl with BBQ sauce, put lid on and shake till the pork steak is fully covered.  Add extra BBQ sauce as needed to make steaks as saucy as you like!
