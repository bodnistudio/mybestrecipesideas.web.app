##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Easy Green Pepper Pork:

1. Get 2 Green Bell Peppers
1. Prepare 8 oz Pork Tenderloin
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Ginger (see picture)
1. Take  Oil for cooking (we used vegetable oil)
1. Get  Soy sauce (we used 4 tbsp low sodium)
1. Prepare  Cooked noodles (we used spaghetti, cooked to package directions)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chinese Easy Green Pepper Pork:

1. Chop the ginger
1. Slice the pork into strips
1. Slice the green peppers into strips
1. Add oil, ginger, and pork to the pan on medium high
1. After pork is cooked, add green peppers
1. Add 4 tbsp of low sodium soy sauce and toss
1. Done!
1. Serve over noodles.
