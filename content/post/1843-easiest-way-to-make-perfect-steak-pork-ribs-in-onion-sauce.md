##### The ingredients needed to make Steak Pork Ribs in Onion Sauce:

1. Get  cut pork ribs (enough for 3)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp steak powder
1. Get 1 tsp garlic salt
1. Make ready 1 dash pepper
1. Prepare 3/4 tbsp salt
1. Make ready  corn starch (enough to coat meat evenly)
1. Take 1 tbsp chicken granules from aah! BISTO
1. Take 2 medium onions
1. Take 4 cloves garlic
1. Make ready  water (to taste)
1. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp Oyster sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Steak Pork Ribs in Onion Sauce:

1. Marinade pork ribs with garlic salt, a dash of salt, pepper, steak powder, corn starch. Set aside for 30 mins in fridge
1. Deep fry the pork ribs or air fry them until brown. If air frying, cover meat with a bit of oil before air frying. Set aside.
1. Over stove, separately saute onion until soft, add garlic and continue cooking for about 2 minutes
1. Add water, granules, any other seasoning u want, salt, pepper, sugar, soy sauce, oyster sauce and stir until thickened
1. Add deep fried pork ribs into the mix and coat the pork with sauce.
1. Serve!
