##### The ingredients needed to make Teri&#39;s Cheesy Salsa Chicken:

1. Get 4 Chicken Breasts, boneless &amp; skinless, cut into small cubes
1. Make ready 2 packages Taco Seasoning Mix
1. Make ready 1 cup Salsa, mild, med, or hot which ever you like best
1. Make ready 1 1/4 cup Cheddar Cheese, shredded
1. Get 1 dollop of sour cream per serving




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Teri&#39;s Cheesy Salsa Chicken:

1. Serves 4
1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. Lightly grease a 9 ×13 baking dish
1. Rub taco seasoning onto all cubes of chicken breasts
1. Place seasoned chicken breasts in baking dish
1. Pour salsa over all chicken
1. Bake at 350° for 30 - 35 minutes or until jucies from chicken run clear
1. Sprinkle cheese over chicken and bake for 4 - 7 minutes more
1. Garnish with a dollop of Sour Cream
1. enjoy
