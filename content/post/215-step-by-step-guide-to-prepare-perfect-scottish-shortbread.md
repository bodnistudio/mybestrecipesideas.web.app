##### The ingredients needed to make Scottish Shortbread:

1. Get 1 lb butter softened
1. Take 1 cup sugar
1. Prepare 4-4 1/2 cups flour (approximately)
1. Get Pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Scottish Shortbread:

1. Cream butter and sugar
1. Sift flour and salt
1. Add about 4 cups of flour to butter mixture a 1/2 cup or so at a time mixing thoroughly between additions until dough forms a ball adding more flour as needed
1. Roll out into a rectangle or press evenly into a 9 by 12 baking pan (I use a glass baking pan)
1. About 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick, score with a knife into approximate 1 1/2 x 3 inch pieces and dock with a fork
1. Bake in a 325 Degree oven for 60 to 75 minutes until lightly browned
1. 
1. Cool in pan for 5 minutes and cut along score marks while still warm, remove from pan and cool on wire rack
1. Yield 24 biscuits
