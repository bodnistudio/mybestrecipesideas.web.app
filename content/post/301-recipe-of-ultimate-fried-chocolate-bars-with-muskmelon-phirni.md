##### Steps to make Fried chocolate bars with muskmelon phirni:

1. For phirni, add 1 bowl chopped muskmelon to a mixer and blend well. Keep aside.
1. Boil 1 cup milk in a pan. Add 1 cup cooked samo rice. Mix well and remove all the lumps.
1. Add 4 tbsp powdered sugar, pinch of salt, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder, 1 tbsp almond &amp; pistachio flakes and mix well.
1. Add the blended muskmelon, mix well and let it thicken. Muskmelon phirni is ready.
1. For fried chocolate bars, mix 1 cup maida, 1 tsp cornflour, 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder, pinch of salt, 1/2 tsp rose essence, 1/2 tsp baking soda and water to form a thick batter.
1. Heat oil in a pan. Dip and coat the chocolate wafer bars in the maida batter and deep fry them.
1. Once fried, arrange them in a serving plate. Sprinkle 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tbsp castor sugar and pour 2 tbsp chocolate syrup. Serve fried chocolate bars with muskmelon phirni.
