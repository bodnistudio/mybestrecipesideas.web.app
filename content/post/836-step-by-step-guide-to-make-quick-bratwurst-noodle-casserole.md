##### The ingredients needed to make Bratwurst Noodle Casserole:

1. Make ready 2 lb ground bratwurst
1. Get 1 packages noodles
1. Get 1 cup Frozen peas
1. Prepare 2 can Cream of mushroom soup
1. Take 1/2 can milk
1. Get 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Get 1 mozzarella cheese if desired




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Bratwurst Noodle Casserole:

1. preheat oven to 400°
1. cook noodles according to package directions
1. cook bratwurst until done.  (I use ground bratwurst so if using links,  remove casings)
1. In 9x13 pan, combine noodles, bratwurst, cream of mushroom soup, peas and milk. mix together. salt and pepper to taste.
1. top with mozzarella cheese if desired
1. bake for 30 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted
