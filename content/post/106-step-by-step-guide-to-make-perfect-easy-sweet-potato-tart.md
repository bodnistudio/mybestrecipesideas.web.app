##### Steps to make Easy Sweet Potato Tart:

1. Combine all of the tart ingredients in a bowl.
1. With a spatula, press the mixture into the bottom of the pan and pierce small holes in the bottom to prevent the crust from puffing up.  Bake for 10 minutes at 180℃.
1. Cut the sweet potatoes into 2cm cubes. Steam the potatoes with a steamer or a microwave.
1. Combine all the ingredients for the filling in a bowl and mix in the sweet potatoes.  Take the crust out from the oven and pour the filling into it.
1. Bake again at 180℃ for 20 minutes. When the surface becomes golden brown, it is done .
