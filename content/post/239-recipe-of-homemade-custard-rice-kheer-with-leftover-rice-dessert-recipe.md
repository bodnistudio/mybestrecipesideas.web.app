##### Steps to make Custard Rice Kheer With Leftover Rice – Dessert Recipe:

1. Mix custard powder with 1/4 cup cold milk and keep it aside. - Now boil 2 cups of milk in a deep saucepan and when it comes to boil. - Add custard milk mixture and stir continuously. Cook on medium flame for 10 minutes. - After 10 minutes add sugar and cook until sugar melts.
1. Now milk will become little thick then add cooked rice and mix them well. - Cook for 5 minutes on medium-low flame.
