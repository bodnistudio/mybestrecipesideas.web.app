##### Steps to make Chocolate brownie ice cream:

1. In a bowl add chopped dark chocolate. Melt it. Make sure there are no lumps.
1. In another bowl add cream. Whip till soft peaks. Add vanilla essence, cocoa powder and condensed milk. Mix everything well.
1. Now add melted chocolate. Mix well. Whip it for 2-3 minutes.
1. Trasfer into a airtight container.
1. Take some brownie pieces. Crumble and add in chocolate cream mixture. Mix everything well.
1. Garnish with some brownie crumbs. Cover it with cling wrap.
1. Cover the lid and set for7-8 hour or overnight.
