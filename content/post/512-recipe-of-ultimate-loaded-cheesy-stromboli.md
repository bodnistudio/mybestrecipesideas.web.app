##### The ingredients needed to make Loaded Cheesy Stromboli:

1. Make ready 1 tube refrigerated crusty french loaf
1. Make ready 6 oz sliced pepperoni
1. Prepare 6 oz sliced mozzarella
1. Get  Diced onion, mushrooms or whatever filling you would like
1. Take  Pizza sauce for dipping




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Loaded Cheesy Stromboli:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Unroll dough into rectangle on greased cookie sheet
1. Place pepperoni on dough leaving 1/2 inch around edges
1. Evenly place toppings and cheese
1. Roll like a jelly roll, pinch seams tuck ends under. Place seam side down.
1. Cut several deep slit in top of dough
1. Bake 20-30 minutes or until golden brown.
1. Slice and serve with pizza sauce.
