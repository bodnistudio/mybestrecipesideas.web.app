<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie Rice Pudding:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get  of uncooked minute rice. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of 12 oz evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of canned pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of water. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of packed dark brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of vanilla extract. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie Rice Pudding:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Combine rice, evaporated milk,  pumpkin, water, brown sugar, pumpkin pie spice and salt in a medium saucepan..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cook over medium heat 20 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Let stand for 5-10 minutes before serving..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Garnish with a dusting of pumpkin pie spice, whipped cream or crushed pecans if desired..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
