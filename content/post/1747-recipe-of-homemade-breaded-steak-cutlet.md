##### The ingredients needed to make Breaded Steak Cutlet:

1. Prepare 3 pounds beef steak (the type of meat of your choise, cut into thin steaks)
1. Take 1/2 tablespoon garlic salt coarse ground with parsley
1. Make ready 4 eggs
1. Make ready  Necessary amount of :
1. Prepare  Bread crumbs italian style
1. Get  Oil or cooking spray




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Breaded Steak Cutlet:

1. Ingredients:
1. In a bowl beat the eggs
1. Add the garlic salt, mix and reserve
1. Place the breadcrumbs in another glass dish, set aside
1. Remove all surplus fat from the meat
1. Submerge the steak in the egg
1. Place the steak on the breadcrumbs
1. Press gently on both sides so that the bread sticks
1. Do the same with each of the steaks
1. They can be cook in the pan or in the oven:
1. In the pan:
1. Put a pan with oil on a medium-high heat, when it is hot, put the breaded steak until it is golden brown both sides, do the same with each bread steak, put it in a plate with paper towels to reduce the surplus of oil
1. In the oven:
1. Preheat the oven at 400 degrees temperature, spray a baking sheet with cooking spray, arrange the steak in it, spray the breaded steak on top with cooking spray and cook it for 20 minutes or until they are golden brown both sides
1. I prepared them in the pan
1. Accompany it with tomato, lettuce and sweet chili
