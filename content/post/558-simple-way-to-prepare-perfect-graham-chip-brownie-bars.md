<h3>The ingredients needed to make Graham Chip Brownie Bars:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 of 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 cup of finely crushed graham cracker crumbs, about 32 full size rackers. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of baking powder. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 tbsp of melted butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 tsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/8 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of mini semi sweet chocolate chips. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of raisins, optional. </li>
	
</ol>

<h3>Instructions to make Graham Chip Brownie Bars:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spray a 8 by 8 inche baking pan with non stivk spray. Line the bottom with parchment paper, spray paper with non stick spray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a bowl combine all ingredients with a spoon. Combine well, batter will be thick. Spread into prepared pan evenly..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake about 25 minutes until toothpick comes out clean. Cool on rack 5 minutes, run a sharp small knife around edges to free edges and finish cooling on rack..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut into squares..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
