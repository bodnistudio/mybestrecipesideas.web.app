##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin and banana smoothie:

1. Make ready 1 banana, frozen
1. Prepare 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
1. Prepare 1 cup almond milk
1. Take 2 tbsp yogurt
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
1. Take 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
1. Make ready 4 tbsp oats
1. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1. Prepare 1 tbsp honey


For this smoothie you can use frozen or fresh bananas. Frozen bananas will create a more frothy texture, which I prefer.
