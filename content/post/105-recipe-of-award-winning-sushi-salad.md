##### The ingredients needed to make Sushi Salad:

1. Prepare 1 cup shredded lettuce

##### Steps to make Sushi Salad:

1. Divide shredded lettuce and Sliced cucumber between two bowls.
1. Slice tomatoes and avocado and divide between the two bowls
