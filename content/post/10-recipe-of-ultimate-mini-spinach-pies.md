<h3>The ingredients needed to make Mini Spinach Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of Pillsberry Uncut Croissant rolls. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 lb of Italian sausage. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 4 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 10 oz of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 8 oz of mozzarella cheese, shredded (not the pre-shredded kind, we know you were thinking it). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of 2% milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of 4% cottage cheese. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 tsp of pepper. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/3 tsp of garlic powder. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Mini Spinach Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Cook and break apart sausage to a crumble. Drain grease and set aside over a paper towel..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add frozen spinach, cheese, milk, cottage cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder, mix together. Mix in sausage..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Layout croissant dough and cut into 48 squares *Pro-Tip 2 4x6 * Grease pan and stretch out the dough a little and place it into each cup. Fill all the cups and fill with mixture..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake 350 degrees for 18 minutes or until golden brown..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
