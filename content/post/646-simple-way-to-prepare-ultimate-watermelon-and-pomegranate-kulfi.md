##### The ingredients needed to make Watermelon and Pomegranate Kulfi:

1. Make ready 2 cups watermelon juice
1. Take 1 cup pomegranate juice
1. Get 1/4 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp chaat masala
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp lemon juice
1. Get 1/2 cup sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Watermelon and Pomegranate Kulfi:

1. First mix both juices.Mix well.
1. Then mix salt, chaat masala and lemon juice.
1. Then pour it in kulfi mould. Keep them in freezer for 7-8 hours.
1. Now demould them and serve.
