##### The ingredients needed to make Minchee (Chinese Beef &amp; Potato Hash):

1. Take 1 lb 80/20 Ground Beef
1. Make ready 1 Russet Potato
1. Make ready 1/4 White Onion
1. Take 1 Bay Leaf
1. Take 1 tsp Canola Oil
1. Make ready 1 tsp Grated Ginger
1. Prepare 4 clove Grated Garlic
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Low Sodium Worcestershire Sauce
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
1. Take 1 dash Salt
1. Make ready 4 Green Onions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Minchee (Chinese Beef &amp; Potato Hash):

1. Dice potato and white onion into 1/4-inch cubes.
1. Heat canola oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add white onion, bay leaf, and salt; cook 3-4 minutes. Add grated garlic and ginger; stir and cook an additional 1 minute.
1. Add beef to the saucepan and break up into small pieces. Cook about 2 minutes until broken and just beginning to brown.
1. Add diced potato, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce and mix with beef/onions. Cover and cook over medium heat for 20 minutes.
1. Serve over rice and top with diced green onions.
