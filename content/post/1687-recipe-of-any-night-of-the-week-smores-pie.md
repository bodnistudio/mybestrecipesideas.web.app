##### The ingredients needed to make Smores Pie:

1. Take 1 graham cracker crust
1. Get 1 cup heavy cream
1. Get 1/2 cup milk
1. Prepare 10 oz semisweet chocolate chips
1. Take 2 tbsp sugar
1. Take 1/4 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. Take 1 bag large marshmallows




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Smores Pie:

1. Preheat oven to 325.
1. Combine milk and cream in saucepan over low heat. Once simmering, remove from heat and add chocolate chips. Stir until melted, then whisk in sugar and salt.
1. In a bowl, beat eggs and slowly add to chocolate mixture.
1. Pour filling into pie crust and bake 45 minutes. Once chocolate is firm, remove from oven.
1. Top with marshmallows and broil on low until golden.
1. Let cool then refrigerate 1-2 hours before cutting.
