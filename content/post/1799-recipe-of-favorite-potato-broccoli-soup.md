##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Broccoli Soup:

1. Prepare 3 cups diced broccoli heads
1. Get 3 carrots peeled and diced
1. Prepare 6 medium potatoes peeled and cubed into small pieces
1. Get 1 small onion, chopped
1. Prepare 8 cups Chicken Broth
1. Make ready 4 tablespoons butter
1. Prepare 1/3 cup all purpose flour
1. Make ready 3 1/2 cups milk
1. Prepare 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
1. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon garlic pepper
1. Make ready 8 slices bacon cooked and chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Potato Broccoli Soup:

1. In a large pot, combine chicken broth, carrots, potatoes, and onion. Bring to a boil, cover and simme for about 10 minutes.
1. Add broccoli, cover and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
1. While simmering, melt butter in a large sauce pan. Whisk in the flour and cook for another minute (or until golden brown). Whisk in milk and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
1. Add cheese and stir until it is all melted. Pour cheese sauce into the large pot and stir until well combined.
1. Add salt and garlic pepper. Add bacon pieces to the soup.
1. Serve warm. Enjoy.
