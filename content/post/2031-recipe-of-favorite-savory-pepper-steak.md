##### The ingredients needed to make Savory Pepper Steak:

1. Prepare 1 1/2 lb round steak, cut into 1/2 in. strips
1. Get 1/4 cup flour
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
1. Take 1 onion (small)
1. Prepare 4 clove garlic
1. Take 1 green pepper, diced
1. Get 1 red pepper, diced
1. Get 1 can 16 oz. Italian style tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 tbsp beef bouillon
1. Make ready 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1. Prepare 1 tbsp steak seasoning
1. Make ready 1 tbsp steak sauce

##### Steps to make Savory Pepper Steak:

1. Toss steak in salt, pepper, flour.
1. Place in gallon-sized freezer bag.
1. In a medium bowl, mix together the rest of the ingredients. Add to bag.
