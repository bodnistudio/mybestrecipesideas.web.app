##### The ingredients needed to make Warm Vegetable Soup:

1. Prepare 6 big carrots
1. Prepare 5 small potatoes
1. Make ready 350 grams frozen peas
1. Get 300 grams sweet corn
1. Take 500 grams crushed tomatoes
1. Take 2 clove garlic
1. Prepare 1 yellow onion
1. Get  Seasoning
1. Get 1 pinch sugar
1. Take 1 curry powder, salt, pepper, parsley, smoked paprika, basil, rosemary, cayenne pepper
1. Get 1 stock cube
1. Make ready 1 bay leaf




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Warm Vegetable Soup:

1. Wash, peel and roughly chop carrots, onions, garlic and potatoes
1. Heat oil in a big pot over medium high heat. Sauté onion and carrots until fragrant
1. Next, add garlic, the bay leaf and potatoes. Sauté for a minute or so before adding enough water to submerge vegetables. Bring to a boil
1. Cover with a lid and let simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring every now and then
1. Then, add crushed tomatoes, peas, corn and seasoning. Simmer, covered, until potatoes and carrots are fork tender
1. Serve warm with a splash of tabasco
