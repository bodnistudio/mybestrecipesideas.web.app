##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Sweet Potato Cake Using Only 20 g of Butter!:

1. Prepare 1 Egg
1. Take 50 grams Sugar
1. Make ready 100 ml Milk
1. Get 20 grams Butter
1. Take 100 grams Cake flour
1. Prepare 5 grams Baking powder
1. Get 1/2 medium Sweet potato




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Sweet Potato Cake Using Only 20 g of Butter!:

1. Chop the sweet potato into 1 cm cubes, then soak in water.
1. Melt the butter in the microwave, then mix in the sugar, eggs, and milk. Preheat the oven to 180째C.
1. Sift the flour and baking powder and fold into the mixture. (Sifting is optional.)
1. Pour the batter into a pound cake mold, put the well-drained sweet potatoes on top of the batter (or press them down into it), and bake for 40 minutes at 180째C.
