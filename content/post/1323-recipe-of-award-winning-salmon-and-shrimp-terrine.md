##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon and Shrimp Terrine:

1. Make ready 300 g salmon cooked      /  10   oz .
1. Get 200 g shrimp prawn cooked     /     /  7 oz .
1. Get 200 g Greek yogurt low fat  (2%)  plain      /  7   oz .
1. Prepare 100 g tomato flesh   /  3½   oz .
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons tomato purée
1. Take 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
1. Prepare 2 teaspoons onion powder
1. Get 1 teaspoon garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 teaspoon aspartame

##### Steps to make Salmon and Shrimp Terrine:

1. To Skin The Tomatoes: Place in boiling water for 1 minute. Remove the tomatoes and the skin should peel off easily. Cut the tomato in half and de-seed keeping only the flesh. Continue until enough flesh has been harvested to make up the weight required.
1. Put all the ingredients into a food processor and blitz until smooth. Divide the mixture between four ramekin dishes (about 10 x 6cm).
1. Refrigerate overnight or, if needed sooner, put in the freezer for half an hour then refrigerate until served.
