##### Instructions to make Szechuan Shrimp:

1. Combine soy sauce, sherry, sugar, ginger, and cayenne in a small bowl.
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over high heat.
1. Stir fry the shrimp until pink and firm, about 2 minutes.
1. Remove shrimp and set aside covered to stay warm.
1. Add the garlic to the skillet and stir fry for 1 minute.
1. Add the soy sauce mixture and cook stirring continuously until bubbly.
1. Return the shrimp to the pan and heat 1 minute more.
1. Serve with hot cooked fried or plain rice.
