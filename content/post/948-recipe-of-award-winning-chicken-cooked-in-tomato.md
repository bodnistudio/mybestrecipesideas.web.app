##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Cooked in Tomato:

1. Prepare 1 canned tomato
1. Prepare 300 g chicken breast
1. Get 1 onion (peeled, sliced thinly, stir fried well until golden browned)
1. Prepare 1/3 carrot (cut into thin slices)
1. Prepare 4-5 cabbage leaves (cut into bite sized pieces, boiled lightly)
1. Get 10 cm celery (cut into thin sticks)
1. Prepare 1 bouillon cube
1. Make ready 50 cc tomato juice
1. Make ready 50-100 cc water
1. Take 3 tbsp white wine
1. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce
1. Prepare 1-2 tbsp sugar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Take 30 g flour
1. Make ready  salt &amp; pepper

##### Steps to make Chicken Cooked in Tomato:

1. Cut the chicken breast into bite sized pieces, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dust them with flour and shake off any excess.
1. Heat the olive oil in a pan, sear the chicken on both side until browned, fragrant and crisp.
1. Add all vegetables, chicken, ingredients in a large pot and stew over medium-low heat.until cooked. Season with salt and pepper.
