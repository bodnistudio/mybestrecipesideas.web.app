##### The ingredients needed to make For an Appetizer or Bento Dish Wasabi &amp; Cheese Chinese Cabbage Meat Rolls:

1. Make ready 1/8 Chinese cabbage
1. Take 8 slice Thinly sliced pork
1. Take 2 Processed cheese
1. Take 1 Wasabi paste (tubed)
1. Prepare 1 dash Salt and pepper
1. Take 1 tbsp Sake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make For an Appetizer or Bento Dish Wasabi &amp; Cheese Chinese Cabbage Meat Rolls:

1. Roughly cut the Chinese cabbage to about 2 cm wide pieces, sprinkle with a little bit of salt (not listed), and rub it in gently. Set aside. When it has become tender, rinse with running water and wring it out.
1. Spread out the pork and use a spoon to spread on some wasabi. Cut the cheese into 4 pieces and place on the pork. Place some cabbage on top of the cheese and roll up the meat.
