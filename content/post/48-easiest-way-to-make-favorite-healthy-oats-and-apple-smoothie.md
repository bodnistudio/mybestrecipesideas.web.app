##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Oats and Apple Smoothie:

##### Steps to make Healthy Oats and Apple Smoothie:

1. Take little amount of milk in a bowl and soak oats and de seeded dates in it for 10 mins
1. Grind the soaked oats,dates,honey,milk and cut apple in the mixer to a smooth paste
1. Add milk as per required consistency not making it very thin
