##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Crockpot Potato Soup:

1. Prepare 1 30 oz. bag of frozen diced hash browns
1. Take 1 32 oz. box of chicken broth
1. Make ready 1 10 oz. can of cream of chicken soup
1. Take 1 8 oz. package of cream cheese (not fat-free)
1. Make ready 3 oz real bacon bits
1. Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1. Prepare 1 Salt &amp; pepper to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Crockpot Potato Soup:

1. Put the hash browns in the crock pot.  Add the chicken broth, cream of chicken soup and half of the bacon bits.  Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
1. Cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
1. One hour before serving, cut the cream cheese into small cubes and stir into soup.  Once it is melted, it&#39;s ready to serve. Top with cheddar cheese and additional bacon bits.  Even better if served in a bread boule!
1. Enjoy!
