##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pancake Oatmeal Muffins:

1. Get 1 can pureed pumpkin
1. Prepare 1 cup instant pancake mix
1. Make ready 1 cup quick cooking oats
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup milk
1. Make ready 1 tbsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pumpkin Pancake Oatmeal Muffins:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray 18 muffin cups with baking spray.
1. Mix all ingredients together in large mixing bowl
1. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter into each muffin cup
1. Bake for 25 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center of muffin comes out clean.
1. Allow to cool on counter before removing muffins from pan. Drizzle with honey, top with icing, or eat as is
