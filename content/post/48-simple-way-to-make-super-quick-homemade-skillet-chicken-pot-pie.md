<h3>The ingredients needed to make Skillet Chicken Pot Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 lb of cooked diced chicken breast. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tbs of olive oil. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tbs of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of chopped onion. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 of carrots diced. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 stalks of celery diced. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of dried thyme. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 cloves of minced garlic. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare to taste of Salt and pepper. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 cups of chicken broth. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 cup of heavy cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of frozen peas. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 cup of fresh parsley. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 8 of Pillsbury grands biscuits. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Skillet Chicken Pot Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In cast iron skillet heat oil and melt butter medium heat. Add onion, celery, carrots and thyme and cook until veggies are soft..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add garlic and cook about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Blend in flour and cook another minute..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Whisk in chicken broth and heavy cream stirring constantly until thickened..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Stir in chicken, bring to a boil then simmer for 5 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Remove from heat. Add peas and parsley and mix well. Top with biscuits and cook in oven at 400 for about 20 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
