##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta Rigatoni:

1. Make ready 4 cup rigatoni pasta(uncooked)
1. Get 1/2 kg ground beef
1. Make ready 1 tbsp garlic,minced
1. Get 1/2 cup bell pepper,chopped
1. Get 1 can sausage,sliced or cubed
1. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
1. Prepare 1/2 cup tomato paste/sauce
1. Make ready 1 can whole tomatoes,peeled
1. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 cup water
1. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped parsley
1. Make ready 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pasta Rigatoni:

1. Cook pasta as directed on package and drain. Meanwhile,saute onion and garlic in large nonstick skillet until caramelized.
1. Add ground beef and cook on medium heat until evenly browned.(reduce oil if necessary
1. Add 1/2 cup of water,sugar,sausage,bell pepper and tomatoes, cover and cook (15-20 minutes) stirring occasionally.
1. Add tomato paste or tomato sauce. cover &amp; simmer (5-10 minutes)
1. Add pasta and parsley. Mix lightly on very low heat. (20-30 seconds.)
1. Turn the heat off.Serve and top with cheese
