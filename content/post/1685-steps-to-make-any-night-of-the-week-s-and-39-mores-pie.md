<h3>The ingredients needed to make S&#39;mores Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 packages of Graham Crackers. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of Unsalted Butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 1/4 cup of Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips, approx. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of Sweetened Condensed Milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/2 cup of Mini Marshmallows, approx. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make S&#39;mores Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Pre-heat oven  to 350°.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a 9in pie pan....
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Finely crush graham crackers, melt butter, and mix together. Press down evenly into pie pan..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread chocolate chips over graham cracker mix in an even manner. Drizzle the sweetened condensed milk evenly over the chocolate chips, making sure there are no pools of milk..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 10 minutes. remove from oven and turn oven to 500° or broil..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top with Marshmallows evenly, in a double layer. Return to oven on highest rack for 3 minutes or until marshmallow tops are golden brown..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool for about 10 minutes and then edge the pan or it will be very difficult to get the &#34;pie&#34; away from the edges of the pan. Cool completely..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Is a bit difficult to cut because of the sticky marshmallow, but will come out like a piece of &#39;pie&#39;.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			*optional...add coconut before the chocolate chips and or nuts on top of the chips*.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
