##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Mia! -prima-:

1. Prepare 350 g flour
1. Take 30 g olive oil
1. Make ready 180 ml water
1. Take 4 g yeast
1. Take 100 g tomato sauce
1. Take 200 g cheese (mozzarella)
1. Take  toppings (ex. olive&amp;anchovy, gorgonzola&amp;onion)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pizza Mia! -prima-:

1. Mix flour, olive oil, water and yeast all together to make dough.
1. Stay the dough at a cool place for about one hour.
1. Put the dough in a pan and make it flat.
1. Put tomato sauce on the dough. Then on the tomato base, put mozzarella cheese.
1. Put toppings as you like on top. This time, I will make half and half pizza with olive&amp;anchovy, gorgonzola&amp;onion;)
1. Bake it in the oven for 20 minutes at 200℃.
