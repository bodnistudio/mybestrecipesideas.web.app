##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Dough:

1. Get 2 Cups All-purpose Flour
1. Prepare 1 Tsp Sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 Tsp Salt
1. Prepare 1 Tsp Yeast
1. Take 2 Tbsp Milk Powder
1. Take 2 Tbsp Melted Butter
1. Take  As Required Water (Lukewarm) (To knead Dough)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pizza Dough:

1. Add Salt, Yeast, Sugar, Milk Powder in All-purpose Flour. Mix well.
1. Now Gradually add Water and Knead the Dough.
1. Add Melted Butter and Knead again.
1. Cover the dough with Plastic Wrap. Let it Rise for 2 Hours on Room Temperature.
1. Pizza Dough is Ready. Give it a few Bunches and then make Pizza with it.
1. Enjoy your meal.
