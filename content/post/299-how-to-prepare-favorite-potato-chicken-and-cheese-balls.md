##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Chicken and Cheese Balls:

1. Make ready 500 Grams Boiled Potato
1. Make ready 250 Grams Boiled and Shredded Chicken
1. Make ready 250 Grams Breadcrumbs
1. Take to taste Salt
1. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon White Vinegar
1. Get 4 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
1. Make ready 2 Teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
1. Get 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce
1. Take 1 tablespoon Chaat masala
1. Take  Oil for frying
1. Prepare  Cheese as required
1. Prepare 1 Egg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Potato Chicken and Cheese Balls:

1. Take a bowl and add all the ingredients into it except cheese, egg and oil. Mix it very well.
1. Now take some potato mixture and make a ball. Now press the ball with the help of your hands.
1. Add some cheese and make a ball again.
1. Dip this ball in egg and then coat it with breadcrumbs. Make all the balls with the same process.
1. After coating one time keep the coated balls in refrigerator for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Now repeat the coating process.
1. Heat oil in a pan. Make sure the oil should not be very hot. Otherwise the balls will break.
1. Fry until it is golden brown in colour.
