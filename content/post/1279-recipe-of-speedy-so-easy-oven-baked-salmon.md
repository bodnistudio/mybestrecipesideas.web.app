1. Prepare 1 tsp Soy sauce

##### Steps to make So Easy! Oven Baked Salmon:

1. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
1. Put all the ingredients except the salmon into a small bowl and mix. I used blanched and minced spinach in the recipe, but you can use parsley or other green herbs or vegetables for colour. My young child doesn&#39;t like parsley.
1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and lay the salmon filets. Spoon the sauce from Step 2 on top of the salmon.
