##### The ingredients needed to make Hand-Rolled Sushi:

1. Prepare 540 ml White rice
1. Get  Sushi Vinegar:
1. Take 100 ml Rice vinegar
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Take 4 tbsp Sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Hand-Rolled Sushi:

1. Cook the rice. I think it tastes nice if cooked with a little bit of dashi konbu. Combine the ingredients for the sushi vinegar and mix into the rice. When mixing, line a wooden sushi tub with a bit of sushi vinegar to stop the rice sticking to the sides.
1. Quickly warm both sides of the toasted nori over a flame.
1. Top with watever you like: omelet, cucumber, radish sprouts, pickled daikon radish, tuna, salmon, shiso leaves, canned tuna, prawn, squid etc.
