##### Instructions to make Tandoori Chicken Soup:

1. boil gallon water in large pot
1. add chicken carcass and dripping to boiling water and boil for 2 hours on medium heat until meat falls from bone
1. carefully remove bones. allow stock to cool if need be before removing bones
1. add all spices to stock and simmer for 1 hour
1. add remaining ingredients to stock and simmer covered for and additional hour
1. allow to cool, then serve
