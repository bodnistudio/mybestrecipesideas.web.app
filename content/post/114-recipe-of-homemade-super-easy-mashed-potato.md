##### Steps to make Super Easy Mashed Potato:

1. Put chopped potato into a pot of cold water. After boiling, add 2 tsp salt and turn to low heat continuing cook for 20 minutes.
1. Discard the white foam on the surface. Scoop the potato out by slotted spoon. Use a fork to break potato roughly then add in unsalted butter till combine well. Then add milk and half &amp; half continuing stir clockwise till smooth. Seasoning with salt and pepper or your favorite seasoning salt. Enjoy~~
