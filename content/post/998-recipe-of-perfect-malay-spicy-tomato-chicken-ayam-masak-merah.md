##### The ingredients needed to make Malay Spicy Tomato Chicken (Ayam Masak Merah):

1. Make ready 350 g Chicken meat (skinless, deboned)
1. Prepare  Blended Paste;
1. Take 4 cloves (40 g) Shallots
1. Make ready 3 cloves (20 g) Garlic
1. Make ready 1/2 inch (10 g) Turmeric root
1. Take 1 inch (20 g) Galangal
1. Make ready 1 inch (20 g) Ginger
1. Make ready 1 stalk (20 g) Lemongrass
1. Take 6 pc Dried Chillies (soak in warm water)
1. Make ready  For Braising;
1. Get 1/3 cup Tomato Puree
1. Make ready 1 cup Coconut Milk
1. Make ready 2 pc Tamarind slices
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp Palm sugar
1. Take 1 tsp Salt (to your taste)
1. Take 1 stick Cinnamon
1. Get 2 pc Star Anise
1. Take 3 pods Cardamom
1. Make ready 3 Tbsp Cooking Oil
1. Get 2 Tbsp Ghee (optional)
1. Make ready  Optionals;
1. Get 1 Tomato (quartered)
1. Make ready 1 Onion (Sliced)
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp Ketchup
1. Get 1 Tbsp Chili Sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Malay Spicy Tomato Chicken (Ayam Masak Merah):

1. Heat the oil (and Ghee if you like) in the wok at high heat. Fry the cinnamon, star anise and cardamom until fragrant (10 seconds). Then add the blended paste and chicken. Mix well and fry until the oil separates.
1. Once the oil separates, turn down the heat and add the coconut milk, palm sugar, tamarind slices, tomato puree and salt. Cover the wok and cook between 10 to 20 minutes (depends on how you cut your chicken), stirring occasionally every 5 minutes until sauce thickens. Chicken is ready to serve.
1. OPTIONAL; in step 2 you can also add tomatoes, onions and if you like you can also add ketchup and chilli sauce.
