##### Steps to make Chinese Orange Chicken:

1. Coat the chicken slices in the egg white mixture then deep fry. Set aside after frying.
1. In a separate pan, heat 3 cups of orange juice.
1. Add the rice wine or cooking wine to juice.
1. Add an amount if grounded ginger, orange zest and brown sugar. Add more sugar if needed. Bring to a boil.
