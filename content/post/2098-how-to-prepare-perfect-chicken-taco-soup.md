##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Taco Soup:

1. Make ready  Soup
1. Take 16 oz Kidney Beans, drained
1. Get 15 oz Black Beans, drained
1. Take 15 oz Sweet Corn, drained
1. Make ready 8 oz Tomato Sauce
1. Prepare 20 oz Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1. Take 12 oz Water
1. Prepare 1 packages Taco Seasoning
1. Prepare 3 medium Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
1. Prepare  Toppings (Optional)
1. Make ready 1 Shredded Mexican Cheese
1. Take 1 Sour Cream
1. Prepare 1 Tortilla Chips




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken Taco Soup:

1. Turn the crock pot on low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours.
1. Drain and rinse off the kidney &amp; black beans, put them in the cock pot with the sweet corn, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes and water.
1. Add the taco seasoning and completely mix it together.
1. Rinse off the chicken breasts, placing them in the crock pot. Gently push them down so they are completely covered.
1. Once the chicken is done according to your crock pot settings, take it out and let it cool. Shred the chicken with 2 forks (or your hands which works easier), placing it back in the crock pot, stirring completely.
1. Serve plain or top with shredded cheese, sour cream and crushed tortilla chips.
