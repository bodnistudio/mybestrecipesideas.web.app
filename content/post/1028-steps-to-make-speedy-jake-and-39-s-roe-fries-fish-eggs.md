##### The ingredients needed to make Jake&#39;s Roe fries (fish eggs):

1. Get  Roe (i used sardine)
1. Take  Kashmiri chilly powder (can be substituted with 3 tsp paprika and 1 tsp cayenne pepper)
1. Prepare  turmeric powder
1. Get  salt (you can add as per your taste)
1. Take  pepper powder
1. Prepare  few curry leaves
1. Make ready  garam masala(optional)
1. Prepare  Oil to fry




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Jake&#39;s Roe fries (fish eggs):

1. Mix the Roe with kashmiri chilly powder , turmeric powder, salt and garam masala (dont add pepper) and let it stay for 5 min
1. Heat oil in a frying pan and add curry leaves. Then add roe and fry on low flame for 15 min. Then add pepper powder. Stir occassionally and fry both sides till its crunchy
1. Towards the end garnish with cillantro or mint leaves (optional) and serve
1. Warning : use low flame for safety..
1. Can be had as snacks or with rice.. Taste excellent!
