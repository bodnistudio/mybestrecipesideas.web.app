##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Salmon steak:

1. Make ready 2 cuts of salmon steak or fillet
1. Take 300 g Sweat pea
1. Get  Others; salt &amp; pepper, olive oil, butter, lemon




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simple Salmon steak:

1. Pea version: cut off the tips and peel the ”spine” of the snow pea. Defrost or prep your salmon if fresh.
1. Bring a shallow pan of water up to a gentle boil. Add 2 tablespoons of salt to the hot water. 5 Min in the hot water, simmering or gentler.
1. Drain the water and remove the peas. Then add a stick of butter into the same pan then stir mix with the snow pea. Once done you may remove or plate out straight.
1. In the same pan with hot (MDM heat) olive oil now, place the salmon skin side down first. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top to taste. Grill both sides until golden brown or until desired cooked level (rare, well done etc.).
1. Squeese a slice of lemon if desired.Plate and enjoy!
