##### The ingredients needed to make Pan Seared T-Bone Steak:

1. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
1. Take 1 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1. Prepare 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
1. Get 1 1/2 lb. T-Bone (steak)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pan Seared T-Bone Steak:

1. Sprinkle salt and fresh ground pepper to both sides of the steak.
1. Drizzle the EVOO on a medium-high cast iron skillet. Make sure that you begin to see smoke and then place the steak on the skillet.
1. Pre-set oven to 350°F and sizzle steak on medium-low for 4 minutes on each side. Place steak in oven for 6-8 minutes.
1. (An instant-read thermometer inserted perpendicularly into the steak registers 125 degrees F for medium rare.)
