##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Fresh Corn &amp; Tomato Salad:

1. Make ready 2 Tablespoons olive oil (1 for sauteing corn, 1 for dressing)
1. Prepare 2 medium beefsteak tomatoes (heirlooms are great if you can get &#39;em), cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1. Prepare 1/4-1/2 small sweet onion, minced
1. Take 5-6 leaves basil, chiffonaded/chopped (fresh mint, parsley, and cilantro also work well)
1. Get 2-2.5 Tablespoons white or red wine vinegar or fresh lemon or lime juice
1. Make ready 1/4-1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1. Get 1 pinch (about 1/8 teaspoon) sugar
