##### Steps to make Masala chai kulfi:

1. Take a pan, add milk and let it boil. Add chai masala, tea powder, sugar and boil for 4-5 minutes in a low flame.
1. Strain in a bowl and let it cool.
1. Add fresh cream, condensed milk and chopped dry fruits to the masala chai. Mix well.
1. Pour the mixture in kulfi mould. Cover and refrigerate.
1. Once they are half set, insert a stick in between them.
1. Freeze till set. Lastly garnish with some roasted dry fruits.
