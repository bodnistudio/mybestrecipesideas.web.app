##### Instructions to make Fried ice cream:

1. Start with lining a plate with parchment paper or foil paper can be used
1. Scoop ice cream and place on lined plate. Pop in freezer for 2 hrs or until solid
1. Now make a thick batter of refined flour by adding water and keep aside.batter must be of flowing consistency not thick nor thin
1. Crush cornflakes
1. Take out ice cream from freezer &amp; roll in the batter first and then coat in cornflakes evenly from all sides, you have to work quickly it can start melting.
1. Now return back to freezer for 2 hrs or until solid
1. Heat oil in a wok on medium to high heat
1. Once oil is hot take out coated ice cream balls from freezer and put in hot oil carefully and fry for 10 - 15 seconds only.dip each ice cream ball in hot oil one by one.once done take out from oil
1. Be careful not to fry for too long otherwise your ice cream will start melting in oil
1. Take out in serving plate and serve immediately with chocolate sauce
