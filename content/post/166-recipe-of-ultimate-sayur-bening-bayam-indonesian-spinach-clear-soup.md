##### The ingredients needed to make Sayur Bening Bayam (Indonesian Spinach Clear Soup):

1. Make ready 2 bunches (300 gr) spinach, cut, washed
1. Prepare 150 gr corn kernel
1. Take 4 cloves shallots, thinly sliced
1. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1. Get 1 lt chicken broth
1. Get 1,5 tsp salt or to taste
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp mushroom bouillon
1. Get 1 medium tomato, cut into wedges




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sayur Bening Bayam (Indonesian Spinach Clear Soup):

1. Boil chicken broth.
1. Add garlic and shallots followed by corn kernel. Keep boiling until the corn kernel softened.
1. Add spinach. Season with salt, white pepper powder, and mushroom bouillon.
1. Add tomato. Mix well. Remove from the heat.
