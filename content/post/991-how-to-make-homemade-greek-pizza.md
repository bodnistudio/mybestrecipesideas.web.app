##### The ingredients needed to make Greek Pizza:

1. Take 1 pre-baked pizza crust
1. Get 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1. Take 4 clove minced garlic
1. Get 1 cup feta
1. Get 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes, chopped
1. Take 1 tbsp flavored olive oil
1. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped kalamata olives
1. Take 1 tsp dried oregano
1. Prepare 2 cup baby spinach
1. Take 1/2 small Red onion,  thinly sliced




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Greek Pizza:

1. Heat oven to 450°F.
1. Mix together yogurt, garlic and half of the feta.  Spread over pizza crust.
1. Top crust with tomatoes,  olives and oregano.
1. Bake for 10 minutes.
1. Toss spinach and onion with flavored oil.  (I used garlic hot pepper.   You can also use the oil that comes in the sun dried tomatoes. )
1. Top hot pizza with spinach mixture and rest of feta.
1. Bake for 2 minutes.   Let set for 2 minutes before slicing.   Enjoy! :-)
