<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Bread:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 1/2 cups (420 g) of all purpose flour (I use cake flour). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 cups (300 g) of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 tsp (7.5 g) of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 teaspoons of (10g)baking soda. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 tsp (15 g) of pumpkin pie spice (or 2 tsp cinnamon, 3/4 tsp nutmeg, 1/8 tsp cloves, 1/8 tsp ground ginger)). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 (15 oz) of can (424 g) of pure pumpkin puree (about 1 3/4 cup). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 5 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup (240 ml) of canola oil. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup (120 ml) of water (can use Coconut milk, almond milk, soy milk...). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Optional. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup (240 ml) of raisins. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup (240 ml) of Toasted Pumpkin Seeds. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup (240 ml) of walnuts. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Bread:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350F (176C) degrees. Grease 2 loaf pans (23 cm x 13 cm)..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, pumpkin spices)..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix wet ingredients (egg, pumpkin puree, oil, water..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine the dry into the wet, 1/3 at a time, being careful not to over mix! (Or you will have tough bread!) Add raisin and mix..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour batter into two loaf pans. Top one with walnuts and top the other with Pumpkin seeds..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, about 50- 60mins..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool in rack..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Make a fantastic hostess gift if you’re traveling to someone’s house for Thanksgiving..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
