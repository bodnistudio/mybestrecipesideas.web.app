##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Crunch Cake:

1. Get 1-15 OZ can pumpkin puree
1. Take 1-12 OZ can evaporated milk
1. Get 4 eggs
1. Get 1 1/2 cups sugar
1. Make ready 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1/2 ish box of yellow cake mix
1. Take 1 Cup pecans
1. Get 1 Cup (2 sticks) melted butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pumpkin Crunch Cake:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with non stick spray.
1. In a large bowl beat together pumpkin, milk, eggs, sugar, and spices until well combined. Pour into prepared dish.
1. Sprinkle the top with yellow cake mix. I used half a box but if you prefer a more cake like texture you can use more.
1. Sprinkle with chopped pecans.
1. Pour the melted butter evenly over the top.
1. Bake for 60-70 minutes (I did 70) or until the top is lightly browned and the custard is set.
1. Serve warm or cold with ice cream (optional). Store in the refrigerator.
