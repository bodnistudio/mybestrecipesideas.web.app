##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious Meat cubes Soup:

1. Make ready 1 kg beef, round, &#34;mawzat&#34;, cut into cubes
1. Take 1/2 cup white rice, washed and strained
1. Make ready 1 small onion, thinly chopped
1. Take 1 garlic, crushed
1. Get 1/2 lemon, squeezed
1. Take 2 tablespoons fresh coriander, chopped
1. Take 2 cinnamon sticks
1. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1. Get 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1. Prepare  pepper to taste
1. Get  salt to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Delicious Meat cubes Soup:

1. In a pressure cooker, fry onion with ½ tbsp of vegetable oil until golden and soft. Add the meat cubes and stir for 2 minutes. Add 2 litres of water, rice, cinnamon sticks, ground cinnamon, salt, pepper, and close pressure cooker cover securely. Cook for 45 minutes at highest pressure (achieved when steam starts escaping). Remove from heat and let pressure drop before opening cooker cover (achieved when all steam escapes).
1. In a skillet, fry crushed garlic and fresh coriander with ½ tbsp of vegetable oil, and then add garlic mixture and lemon juice to meat soup and stir over high heat for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and serve hot.
