##### The ingredients needed to make Inari Sushi:

1. Get 450 grams White rice
1. Take 8 sheets ★Aburaage
1. Take 200 ml ★Water
1. Take 4 tbsp ★Soy sauce
1. Make ready 4 tbsp ★Sugar
1. Get 4 tbsp ★Mirin
1. Get  Sushi vinegar
1. Take 3 tbsp ◎Vinegar
1. Take 3 tbsp ◎Sugar
1. Take 1 tsp ◎Salt

##### Steps to make Inari Sushi:

1. Pour boiling water over the aburaage to cut excess oil. Slice into half, and open up to make little pockets.
1. Add the ★ ingredients and the aburaage from Step 1 into a pot, and simmer for 10 minutes. Mix the ◎ ingredients in a bowl to make sushi vinegar.
1. Add ◎ mixture to freshly cooked rice, and mix with a cutting motion. Divide the rice into 16 portions.
1. Squeeze out the excess water from the aburaage pockets, and stuff with sushi rice, and they&#39;re done.
