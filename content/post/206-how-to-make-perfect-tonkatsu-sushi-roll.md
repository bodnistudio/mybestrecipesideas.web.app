##### The ingredients needed to make Tonkatsu Sushi Roll:

1. Get 1 Tonkatsu (breaded deep fried pork cutlet)
1. Get 20 to 30 grams ☆ Cabbage (thick julienne)
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp ☆ Mentsuyu
1. Make ready 1 tsp ☆ Mayonnaise
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ☆ Ground sesame seeds
1. Get 1 Tamagoyaki (flavored as you like)

##### Instructions to make Tonkatsu Sushi Roll:

1. Make a tamagoyaki. Let cool and julienne.
1. Put the ☆ ingredients in a bowl and mix.  Brush the tonkatsu sauce on the tonkatsu.
1. Put the nori seaweed sheet on a sushi rolling mat. Top with the sushi rice, julienned cabbage, and the sliced tonkatsu (sauce side down), with the sliced tamagoyaki on one side, and roll.
1. Leave for a while with the cut side down. Moisten a kitchen knife by running it under water or wiping it with a wet kitchen or paper towel, and slice.
