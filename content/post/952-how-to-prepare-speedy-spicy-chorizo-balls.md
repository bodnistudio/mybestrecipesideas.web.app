##### Steps to make Spicy Chorizo Balls:

1. 1: Cut the potatoes in slices and boil until they are soft. -  - 2: Drain and put them back in the cooking pot. Add a 1 tbsp of butter, 75ml of milk and 100g of grated cheese. Mash it all up until thick and creamy. -  - 3: Finely chop the chorizos and green pepper making tiny cubes. -  - 4: Fry the chorizo and green pepper for 5 minutes on medium heat. Crush the chillies and add it whilst frying. -  - 5: Prepare a bowl of flour. -  - 6: Prepare a bowl of bread crumbs.
1. 7: Prepare a bowl for the eggs which you have to beat. -  - 8: Add what you have in the frying pan to the mashed potatoes and mix well. -  - 9: Leave to cool down for 30 minutes. -  - 10: For making the balls you can use latex gloves to prevent the mix from sticking to your hands too much. -  - 11: With a tablespoon grab some mix and put it in the flour and roll with both hands to make a ball. -  - 12: Place the ball in the egg so that it is all covered.
1. 13: Place the ball in the bread crumbs so that it is fully covered with them. -  - 14: Repeat 11-12-13 until you have used up all the mix. -  - 15: Heat up a pan with plenty of oil and add 2 or 3 balls at a time, frying them on high heat until they are golden brown. -  - 16: Serve hot or cold. -  - 17: Enjoy…
