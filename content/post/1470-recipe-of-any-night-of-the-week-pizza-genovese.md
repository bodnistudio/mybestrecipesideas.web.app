##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Genovese:

1. Prepare 280 g flour
1. Take 150 ml water
1. Prepare 15 ml olive oil
1. Make ready  salt
1. Get 4 g yeast
1. Take 30 g genovese sauce
1. Take 100 g cheese (hopefully mozzarella)
1. Make ready  toppings you want (this time, paprika, anchovy and eggplant)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pizza Genovese:

1. Mix flour, water, yeast, salt and olive oil with hands to make dough.
1. Stay the dough for at least 30 minutes.
1. Flatten the dough and put Genovese sauce on top.
1. Put toppings on top.
1. Put it in the oven for 14 minutes at 250℃
1. Buon Appetito!
