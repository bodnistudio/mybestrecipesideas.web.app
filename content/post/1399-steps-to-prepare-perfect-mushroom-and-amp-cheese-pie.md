##### Steps to make Mushroom &amp; Cheese Pie:

1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease 9&#34; pie plate.
1. Combine chex, oregano, &amp; melted butter until well mixed.  Press onto bottom &amp; sides of pie plate. Bake for 8 minutes. Cool for at least 5 minutes.
1. Spread mushrooms in bottom of shell.
1. Combine eggs, half &amp; half, salt, pepper, &amp; onion powder. Pour over mushrooms. Sprinkle cheese evenly over top.
1. Bake for 35 minutes.  Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
