##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Simmered Chinese Cabbage and Turnip:

1. Get 4 leaves Chinese cabbage
1. Get 1 Turnip (or daikon radish)
1. Prepare 1 Aburaage
1. Make ready 2 tbsp White soy sauce (or dark)
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Beet sugar (or regular sugar)
1. Take 1 Ichimi spice (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Vegan Simmered Chinese Cabbage and Turnip:

1. Remove the core of the Chinese cabbage from the leaves. Chop into pieces (about 4 x 3 cm in size).
1. Peel the turnip and cut into long, thick strips. Cut the fried tofu into the same size.
1. Put the Chinese cabbage core, turnip, fried tofu, and Chinese cabbage leaves into a pot, in that order.
1. Add in the beet sugar and white soy sauce, cover with a lid, turn the heat to low, and let it be for 20 minutes. Then it&#39;s done!
1. You can change it up by adding leftover white sauce!.
