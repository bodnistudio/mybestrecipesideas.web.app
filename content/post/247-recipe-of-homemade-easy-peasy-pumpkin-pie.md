<h3>The ingredients needed to make Easy Peasy Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Pumpkin pie filling (sweetened). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2/3 cup of sweetened condensed milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 of eggs beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 9 of in raw pie shell. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1-2 tsp of nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1-2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tsp of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take Dash of ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Whipped Cream (optional). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Easy Peasy Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat to 425°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix beaten eggs, easy pumpkin pie mix (i used libbys, the already mixed stuff), sweetened condensed milk, and seasonings.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into raw pie shell.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 425°F for 15 mins.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Turn temperature down to 350°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 60 mins.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool for a bit.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add whipped cream if desired.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
