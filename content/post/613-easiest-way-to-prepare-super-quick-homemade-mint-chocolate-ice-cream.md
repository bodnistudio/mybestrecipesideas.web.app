##### The ingredients needed to make Mint Chocolate Ice Cream:

1. Prepare 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh mint leaves
1. Make ready 1 cup Heavy Cream
1. Get 1/2 cup Milk
1. Get 1 cup chocolate chips
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Sugar
1. Take Pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Mint Chocolate Ice Cream:

1. In a saucepan, combine together the chopped mint leaves, heavy cream and milk.
1. Stirring constantly, bring to a simmer. Remove from heat and let rest, while stirring occasionally.
1. After about half an hour of stirring, pour this mix into serving bowls and let it freeze overnight or for about 4-5 hours.
1. Garnish with mint sprigs and serve chilled.
