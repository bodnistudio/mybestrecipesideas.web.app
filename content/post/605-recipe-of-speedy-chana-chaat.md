##### The ingredients needed to make Chana Chaat:

##### Steps to make Chana Chaat:

1. Soak chana for 6 to 7 hours. Boil it by putting a pinch of salt and a pinch of baking soda in it and give 7 to 8 whistles.
1. Assemble all the ingredients so that it is easy while making.
1. Mix all the vegetables and masalas with the boiled chana. Add lemon juice and coriander leaves and give it a good mix. Chana Chaat is ready to be served. It is a very less time consuming recipe, do try making chana chaat.
