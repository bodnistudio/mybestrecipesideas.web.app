##### The ingredients needed to make Flank Steak In Sauce On Hoagie:

1. Make ready 3 clove Garlic
1. Take 3 each Scallions
1. Make ready 1 tsp Dry mustard
1. Make ready 1/2 each Anaheim pepper
1. Take 1/4 cup Sherry
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Worcestershire
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Ground pepper
1. Get 1 cup Ketchup
1. Get 1 cup Beef stock with bay leaves and thyme-bay leaves removed after 5 min cooking
1. Get 2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 lb Flank steak - sliced against the grain, very thin
1. Make ready  Canola oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Flank Steak In Sauce On Hoagie:

1. Put all 10 ingredients together in a food processor. Buz until all smooth. Put in a pot and cook down for 30 minutes.
1. In a pan with canola oil, wait until smoking hot, add thinly slice steak and cook, stirring constantly for 4 minutes.
1. Add sauce to steak as preferred to toasted hoagies with arugula
