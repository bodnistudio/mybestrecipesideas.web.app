##### The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Swedish Steaks:

1. Get 1 lb Salisbury Steak (cubed steak)
1. Prepare 8 oz Mushrooms (sliced)
1. Take 6 oz Fat-Free Evaporated Milk
1. Get 10 1/2 oz Cream of Celery Soup
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Sour Cream
1. Make ready 3 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Seasoned Salt
1. Get 1 tsp Onion Powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Slow Cooker Swedish Steaks:

1. To a medium mixing bowl, add all ingredients except steaks and mushrooms. Stir until completely combined. I&#39;d advise mixing in a blender to fully incorporate them. Otherwise, the solids tend to separate during the cooking process.
1. Place steaks in bottom of a slow cooker. Layer mushrooms on top. Pour over bowl of mixed ingredients.
1. Cover and cook in slow cooker on high for 4 hours.
1. Serve with mashed potatoes or egg noodles.
