##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious Marinaded Skirt Steak:

1. Prepare 4 skirt steaks
1. Take 1/2 cup soy sauce
1. Prepare 2 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
1. Take 1/4 cup your favorite steak sauce (optional)
1. Make ready 1/3 cup olive oil
1. Make ready 4 garlic cloves, minced
1. Get  salt &amp; pepper
1. Take 1 small yellow or white onion
1. Make ready 4-6 tbsp butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Delicious Marinaded Skirt Steak:

1. Marinade: Put steaks in a big zip lock bag. Pour soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, olive oil, steak sauce, garlic, salt and pepper in bag. Seal and shake to cover all sides of the steaks. Marinade in refrigerator anywhere from 2-24 hours
1. Melt some of your butter in a pan over medium high heat and add steaks
1. Add in the cut onions and cook with steak. Make sure to add more butter and spoon over steaks while cooking
1. Cook until desired temperature (For the medium rare in this photo I cooked them about 2 1/2 minutes per side)
1. Let set before serving.
