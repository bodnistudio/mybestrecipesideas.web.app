##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Aloo Tiki:

1. Take 1 bowl boiled potato
1. Get 2 tbsp chilli ginger paste
1. Take to taste Black salt
1. Prepare 1 cup chopped coriander
1. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped mint
1. Make ready 1/4 cup aara flour
1. Get 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
1. Take 1/4 cup grated carrot
1. Prepare 1/4 tbsp garam masala
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp amchur powder
1. Make ready 1/4 tbsp lemon juice
1. Make ready Fot garnishing:
1. Get As required Sev, onion, garlic chutney, coriander and pomegranate

##### Instructions to make Spicy Aloo Tiki:

1. Take a bowl and add potatoes and mash them and add chilli ginger paste, coriander, capsicum, mint, black salt, garam masala, amchur powder, lemon juice and carrot.
1. Mix it softly and add aata flour and mix it again.
1. Now make small balls out of it and then press it and it will get shape of tikki.
1. Now shallow fry it and then serve it. Garnish it with garlic chutney, coriander, sev, onion and pomegranate.
