##### The ingredients needed to make Beef and Pork Hamburger Steaks With Salt-Leek Sauce:

1. Take 200 grams Mixed ground beef and pork
1. Make ready 100 grams Pork (thickly cut boned rib or shoulder meat)
1. Make ready 1/2 Onion
1. Take 1 tsp ◎Sesame oil
1. Get 1/2 tsp ◎Chicken soup stock granules
1. Prepare 1 clove ◎Garlic (grated)
1. Get 1 to ◎Black pepper, salt and pepper
1. Prepare  Leek sauce:
1. Make ready 1 The white part of a leek (or green onion)
1. Take 1 tbsp ●Sesame oil
1. Take 2 tsp plus ●Roasted sesame seeds
1. Make ready 1/5 tsp ●Salt
1. Take 1 ●Black pepper, chicken soup stock granules
1. Get 1 optional ＊Lemon  ＊Slow boiled egg or fried egg

##### Steps to make Beef and Pork Hamburger Steaks With Salt-Leek Sauce:

1. Finely chop the onion, put into a heatproof container and microwave (1 1/2 minutes at 500W). Transfer the onion to a sieve and leave to cool.
1. Chop the pork up roughly so that there&#39;s still some texture (about 5mm pieces). Compare with the egg.
1. Make the leek sauce: Finely chop the white leek, and combine with the ● ingredients to season. If you aren&#39;t fond of raw leek, soak it in cold water first. You can mix it half and half with green onion, or just use all green onion.
1. I used teppanyaki (or yakiniku) pork this time. Choose pork rib, pork shoulder or any part that has some fat.
1. Put the ground meat and chopped up pork from step 2, plus the onion from step 1, into a bowl. Add the ◎ marked seasoning and mix well.
1. Knead well until the ground meat has turned white (this is the key), and form into patties while eliminating any air bubbles.
1. I made 3 burgers this time. If you make 2 they&#39;ll be pretty big ones that are about 150 g each. You can make a lot of little ones too.
1. Put a little oil in a frying pan and pan fry the burgers. When they are nicely browned, flip them over.
1. Turn the heat down, and cook through to the middle thoroughly. When they are cooked they will puff up.
1. Transfer to serving plates, top with plenty of the leek sauce from step 3, and serve. Top with a slow-boiled egg or a fried egg for more volume. It goes well with lemon and black pepper too.
1. I tried making them small and dainty to serve to guests. They may be cute topped with fried quail eggs.
