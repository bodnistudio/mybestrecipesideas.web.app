##### Steps to make Strawberry-banana smoothie bowl:

1. Take a banana. slice it. Put into a air tight box and freeze for about 10-20 mins
1. Slice the strawberries and freeze them too.
1. Take out the frozen fruits and put them in a blender
1. Add About 2 tbs of milkmaid
1. Add 4-5 drops of vanilla essence
1. Blend it all into a smooth paste
1. Take the smoothie out into a bowl
1. Add toppings now. You can add granola, fresh strawberries and shredded pineapple.
1. Enjoy your healthy and very tasty strawberry banana smoothie!
