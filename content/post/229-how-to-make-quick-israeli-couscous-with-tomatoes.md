##### The ingredients needed to make Israeli Couscous with Tomatoes:

1. Take 2 cups Israeli couscous
1. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 3 1/2 cups chicken broth (divided)
1. Get to taste Salt and pepper
1. Take 1 pound cherry tomatoes
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp fish sauce
1. Make ready 1/2 lemon (juiced)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Israeli Couscous with Tomatoes:

1. Heat 2 Tablespoons olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add couscous and toast until fragrant, 5-6 minutes. Add 2 ½ cups chicken stock and a pinch of salt. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
1. To a large skillet, add 1 Tablespoon olive oil and tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until they become saucy, 5-6 minutes.
1. Add remaining chicken broth (1 cup) and fish sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer. Cook until the sauce is slightly thickened but still brothy, 3-5 minutes.
1. Pull it off heat, stir in cooked couscous and lime juice and season with salt and pepper. Let sit a few minutes to allow broth to absorb. Season with additional salt and pepper and serve.
