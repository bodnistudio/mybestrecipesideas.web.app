##### The ingredients needed to make Chorizo Hash with Over Easy Eggs:

1. Make ready 1 lb good quality chorizo from a local meat market
1. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
1. Get 1 cup chopped cilantro
1. Take 4 medium Yukon Gold Potatoes
1. Prepare  salt and pepper
1. Prepare  olive oil, extra virgin
1. Prepare  over easy eggs




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chorizo Hash with Over Easy Eggs:

1. Cook chorizo in a good heavy bottom pan.
1. Add the onion and cilantro. Cook until the onion is half done.
1. Dice the potatoes. Add the potatoes with the salt and pepper.  And cook until done.
1. Cook your eggs in a seperate pan to how you like them.
1. Top with a sauce, salsa, fresh cilantro or chives, etc....if desired.
1. You may add summer squash to the recipe if desired.
