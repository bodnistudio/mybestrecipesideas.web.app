##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Tartare:

1. Make ready 8 oz boneless, skinless salmon fillet (sushi grade)
1. Prepare 1/4 cup finely diced cucumber
1. Take 1 Tbsp lime juice
1. Make ready 1.1 /2 tsp minced chives
1. Prepare 1.1 /2 tsp minced cilantro
1. Get 1.1 /2 tsp vegetable or olive oil
1. Prepare 1.1 /2 tsp minced, seeded jalapeno
1. Get 1.1 /2 tsp minced shallot
1. Prepare 3/4 tsp minced ginger
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp sesame oil
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp lime zest
1. Make ready  Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Salmon Tartare:

1. Place salmon on a plate and freeze for 20 minutes until firm
1. Cut salmon into 1/8 cubes and place into a bowl
1. Combine remaining ingredients and season with salt and pepper
1. Serve with chips
