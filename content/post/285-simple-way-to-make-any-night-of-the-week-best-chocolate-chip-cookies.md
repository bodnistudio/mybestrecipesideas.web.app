1. In a large mixing bowl beat the shortening and butter with an electric mixer on medium to high speed for 30 seconds.

1. Beat in eggs and vanilla until combined, use big kitchen spoon or sturdy spatula to hand mix flour in(optional add half teaspoon of salt) Mix until completly combined. Mix in chocolate chips.
