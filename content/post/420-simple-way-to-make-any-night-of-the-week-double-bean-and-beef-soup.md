##### The ingredients needed to make Double Bean and Beef Soup:

1. Prepare 6 cups chopped cabbage

1. Prepare 1 small onion diced
1. Take 1 stick butter
1. Get 1-1/2 quart beef broth
1. Take 2 teaspoon summer savory
1. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
1. Take 15 ounces canned Cannellini beans
1. Prepare 15 ounces canned black beans drained
1. Prepare 1-1/2 cups English peas
1. Make ready To taste Valentina hot sauce optional
