##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Cellophane Noodle Salad:

1. Get 40 grams Cellophane noodles
1. Take 1 Cucumber
1. Take 1 Egg for usuyaki tamago
1. Take 4 slice Ham
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ● Soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp ● Vinegar
1. Take 2 tbsp ● Sugar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ● Sesame oil
1. Prepare 1 tsp ● Chicken stock (granules)
1. Make ready 1 White sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chinese Cellophane Noodle Salad:

1. Julienne the cucumber and ham.
1. Also julienne an egg crepe.
1. Rehydrate the cellophane noodle according to the package instruction, and cut into bite sizes.
1. Mix the ● ingredients.
1. Put all the ingredients prepared in Steps 1-4 in a bowl.
1. Add white sesame seeds and mix well. Leave in the fridge for about 30 minutes and let the flavors meld.
1. Done.
