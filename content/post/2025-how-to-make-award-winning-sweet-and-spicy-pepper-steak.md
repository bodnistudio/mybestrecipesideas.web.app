##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Spicy Pepper Steak:

1. Get 1 Red Bell Pepper
1. Take 1 Yellow Bell Pepper
1. Get 1 Green Bell Pepper
1. Make ready 1 White Onion
1. Take 1 1/2 cup beef stock
1. Prepare 2 Packs of Beef Chuck (thinly sliced)
1. Take 1 tbsp sruracha sauce
1. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
1. Prepare  Creole Seasoning
1. Make ready 2 tsp Minced Garlic
1. Take 1 packages White mushrooms
1. Make ready 1/3 cup Cornstarch (for thickness)

##### Instructions to make Sweet and Spicy Pepper Steak:

1. Season meat to your liking and add to the bottom of your crock pot.
1. Cut peppers into cubes, slice onion and add on top of the meat along with mushrooms. Add beef stock.
1. Set on high for 4 hrs. Add cornstarch and stir 30 mins prior to being done to thicken gravy.
