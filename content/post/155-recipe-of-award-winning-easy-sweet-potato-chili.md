##### Steps to make Easy Sweet Potato Chili:

1. Peel and cut sweet potato into roughly diced chunks
1. Dice onion and bell pepper
1. Add olive oil into a stock pot over medium high heat then add potatoes, onion, and pepper.
1. Add a cup of water to pot. Cook and continue to stir until sweet potato is slightly tender, about 5-7 minutes
1. Add ground chicken and seasonings to pot. Cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink.
1. Add diced tomatoes and black beans to pot. (Add more water if necessary)
1. Reduce heat and simmer on low for about 10 minutes. You&#39;re done! Serve in a bowl or eat over rice.
