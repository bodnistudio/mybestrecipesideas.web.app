<h3>The ingredients needed to make Smores Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of graham cracker crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of heavy cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 10 oz of semisweet chocolate chips. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tbsp of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 bag of large marshmallows. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Smores Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 325..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine milk and cream in saucepan over low heat. Once simmering, remove from heat and add chocolate chips. Stir until melted, then whisk in sugar and salt..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a bowl, beat eggs and slowly add to chocolate mixture..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour filling into pie crust and bake 45 minutes. Once chocolate is firm, remove from oven..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top with marshmallows and broil on low until golden..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Let cool then refrigerate 1-2 hours before cutting..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
