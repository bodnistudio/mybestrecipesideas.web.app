##### The ingredients needed to make Double Trouble Double IPA hot sauce:

1. Prepare 6 Carolina reaper peppers
1. Get 3 habanero peppers
1. Make ready 5 cloves garlic
1. Prepare 1/2 sweet onion
1. Prepare 1 c. pineapple
1. Make ready 1 c. apple cider vinegar
1. Take 1 1/2 c. Double IPA style beer
1. Take 1 Tbsp cayenne powder
1. Get 2 Tbsp basil
1. Prepare 2 tsp lime juice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Double Trouble Double IPA hot sauce:

1. Preheat grill to 400 deg. On a grill pad (or pan/baking sheet) lightly char peppers, garlic, onion and pineapple for 12-15 min until light brown. Keep an eye on these. You don&#39;t want them to burn, and watch out for any fumes.
1. Add all ingredients to a pan and bring to a simmer for 15 min. stirring occasionally.
1. Pour into a blender or food processor and blend to desired consistency. For very smooth sauce you can push the sauce through a strainer or cheesecloth, or use a food milk to really strain it.
1. Let cool. Pour into bottles and refrigerate for 24 hrs to let the flavors meld.
1. Enjoy on your favorite foods
