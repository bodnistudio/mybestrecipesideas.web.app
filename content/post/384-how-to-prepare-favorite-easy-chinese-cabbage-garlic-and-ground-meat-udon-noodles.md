##### Instructions to make Easy Chinese Cabbage, Garlic and Ground Meat Udon Noodles:

1. Slice the garlic thinly, and cut the Chinese cabbage up into 2 cm wide pieces.
1. Put the ground meat into a pan with thick sides and stir-fry. When the color of the meat changes, add the garlic and Chinese cabbage.
1. When the cabbage has wilted, add the water and bring to a boil. Add the sake, sugar, soy sauce and vinegar in that order. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper.
1. Add the frozen udon noodles and simmer until warmed through. You&#39;re all done!
