##### The ingredients needed to make Colourful Fruit salad:

1. Take 1 banana
1. Prepare 1 Apple
1. Make ready 1 mango
1. Prepare 1-2 slices payaya
1. Make ready 1/4 bowl fresh pomegranate seeds
1. Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice
1. Make ready Few Mint leaves
1. Get 1/2 tsp chaat masala

##### Instructions to make Colourful Fruit salad:

1. Take a serving bowl.Spread pomegranate seeds.Put all the fruits over it.
1. Add lemon juice and Sprinkle chaat masala.
1. Garnish it with mint leaves.
