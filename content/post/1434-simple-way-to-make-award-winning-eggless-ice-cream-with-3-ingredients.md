##### The ingredients needed to make Eggless Ice Cream with 3 ingredients:

1. Get 2 cups Heavy Cream Milk(or half and half)
1. Take 1 cup Condensed milk
1. Take 1 tbsp vanilla essence




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Eggless Ice Cream with 3 ingredients:

1. Whisk cream milk with hand blender/ mixer or with a whisk till stiff peaks forms.
1. Add condensed milk and vanilla and mix gently so that all mix together using fold and cut method.(not over mix)
1. Shift it to a air tight container and freeze it for 7-8hrs.
1. Serve it. Enjoy this scoop of ice cream on your fav dessert. Here is the pic with Scoop of ice cream on brownie.
1. *Note: Make sure to use chilled cream milk and chilled condensed milk.
1. Note;**Can make any flavored ice cream by justing replacing vanilla extract with melted chocolate, cocoa powder or peanut butter or as per your choice
