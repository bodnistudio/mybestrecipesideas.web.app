##### Instructions to make Beef steaks platter:

1. In a bowl add ginger garlic paste, add chilli flakes, black pepper, salt, meat tenderizer.
1. Now add soya sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce and oil in it and mix well.
1. Hammer the beef steaks well and flattend them.Apply the prepared marination mixture to beef.
1. Cover it well and let it marinate for 4 hours.
1. Apply oil in grill pan and heat it. Place marinated steaks and cook from both sides. Press them in between to get perfect colour.grill them till they get tender.
1. For stir vegies:
1. Cut the vegetables (carrot, capsicum in strips.) And dip them in cold water+ 1 tbsp white vinegar) for 20-30 mins at least.
1. Saute them in 1 Tsp oil at high flame.Season with black pepper and salt while they are on stove and set aside.
1. Mashed potatoes
1. Mash the boiled potatoes and add them in pan along with butter. Add black pepper and salt and mix.
1. Start adding milk in small quantity and mix so they get absorbed in potatoes.
1. Note: Don&#39;t over mix. It will make mashed potatoes sticky.
1. Arrange the platter with grilled steaks, vegies, fries and mashed potatoes.
