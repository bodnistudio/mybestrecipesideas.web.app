##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken and Mashed Potatoes:

1. Get 8 chicken breasts/thighs
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour
1. Take 2 tbsp Lawry&#39;s salt
1. Get 8 potatoes
1. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
1. Make ready 6 cup milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fried Chicken and Mashed Potatoes:

1. pour olive oil in skillet and heat
1. mix flour with Lawry&#39;s salt in a bowl
1. wash chicken, pat dry with paper towels, then cover with flour mix
1. fry chicken (cook time varies with chicken thickness)
1. wash potatoes, chop, and boil them
1. mash potatoes, adding 2 cups milk and half stick of butter
1. add remaining flour mix to leftover oil in skillet, stir, and add milk as necessary to make gravy
