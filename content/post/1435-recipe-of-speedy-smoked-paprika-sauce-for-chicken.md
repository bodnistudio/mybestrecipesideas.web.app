##### Instructions to make Smoked Paprika Sauce for Chicken:

1. Put oil in saute or large frypan and get hot
1. Chop onion to a dice and fry off for minute
1. Add Smoked Paprika and stir continuously for another minute or so
1. Stir in flour for 45 seconds
1. Add Chicken Stock and stir well then lower heat to a simmer and cook covered for 15 minutes, stirring half way through
1. Pour in cream and stir then simmer again for 10 minutes
1. Stir in Lemon juice and seasoning and pour over grilled or roasted chicken breasts
