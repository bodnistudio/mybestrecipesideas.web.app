##### The ingredients needed to make Loaded Potato and Chicken Casserole:

1. Make ready 4 each russet potatoes diced (small)
1. Get 1 lb chicken diced (medium)
1. Get 1 lb bacon cooked and crumbled
1. Take 1 1/2 cup shredded cheese
1. Take 1/2 cup heavy cream
1. Make ready 4 oz cream cheese
1. Make ready 4 tbsp butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Loaded Potato and Chicken Casserole:

1. preheat oven 350
1. Melt butter, whipping cream and butter on stove
1. layer half of the potaoes on the bottom in an 8x8 casserole dish
1. place half the chicken on top of the potatoes
1. salt and pepper
1. sprinkle half of the bacon
1. sprinkle half of the cheese
1. repeat all layers
1. Pour cream cheese, whipping cream and butter over top
1. bake covered for 90 minutes
