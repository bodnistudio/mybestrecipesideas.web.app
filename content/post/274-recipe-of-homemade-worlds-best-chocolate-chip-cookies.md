##### Instructions to make Worlds best Chocolate Chip cookies:

1. Preheat Oven to 375°F
1. In a small bowl pour in the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Then set it off to the side.
1. In a large bowl mix together the butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix ingredients for 2 minutes or until creamy.
1. Then in the same bowl as the butter mixture add the eggs one at a time blending after each addition.
1. Then you take the bowl with the dry ingredients and gradually add it to the butter mixture until blended.
1. Then scoop tablespoon size balls of the batter onto a NON GREASED cookie sheet.
1. Finally bake for 9-11 minutes.  And cool on a wire cookie rack.
