##### The ingredients needed to make Patti Labelle Famous Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Prepare 3 Large orange-fleshed sweet potatoes(scrubbed)
1. Make ready 8 tbsp (1)Stick butter,melted
1. Prepare 3/4 cup Packed Light Brown Sugar
1. Take 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
1. Make ready 2 large Eggs, beaten
1. Get 1/4 cup Half - Half
1. Make ready 3/4 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1. Prepare 1 tsp Ground Nutmeg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Patti Labelle Famous Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Bring a larg pot of lightly salted water to boil ova high heat.Add sweet potatoes &amp; reduce heat to medium.Cook until the sweet potatoes are tender when pierced with a knife,about 30 mins.drain &amp; run under cold water until cool enough to handle.Peel the sweet potatoes &amp; place in a medium bowl.Mash with an electric mixer on medium speed until very smooth.Measure 3 cups mashed sweet potatoes,keep any extra for another use &amp; set aside
1. 9 inch Ready made Pie Crust if you don&#39;t have time to make Basic. Homemade Pie Crust..I&#39;ll put that recipe up later
1. Uncover pie shell &amp; brush the interior with some melted butter.Sprinkle 1/4 cup brown sugar over the bottom of pie shell
1. Using an electric mixer on low speed, mix the mashed sweet potatoes, the remaining melted butter &amp; 1/2 cup brown sugar, the granulated sugar, eggs,half-half, cinnamon &amp; nutmeg.Then spread into pie shell, smoothing the top
1. Bake on 350°f until a knife inserted in the cente of filling comes out clean, or crust is golden Brown.....you will enjoy it
