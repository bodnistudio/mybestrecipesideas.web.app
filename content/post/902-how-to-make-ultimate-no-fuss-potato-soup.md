##### The ingredients needed to make No-Fuss Potato Soup:

1. Take 6 cup red potatoes, diced
1. Get 5 cup water
1. Take 2 cup red onion, diced
1. Prepare 1/2 cup celery, diced
1. Make ready 1/2 cup carrots, chopped
1. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
1. Make ready 4 tsp chicken bouillon granules
1. Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1. Prepare  season salt
1. Get  garlic powder
1. Prepare dash hot sauce
1. Get 12 oz evaporated milk
1. Get 3 tbsp parsley
1. Take 1 lb bacon- chopped, cooked and drained
1. Get 8 oz shredded Velveta
1. Take  flour or cornstarch - to thicken




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make No-Fuss Potato Soup:

1. Combine all ingredients except milk, cheese, bacon, hot sauce, season salt and garlic powder in slow cooker.
1. Cover and cook on high 7-8 hours or until vegetables are tender.
1. Stir in remaining ingredients and stir until cheese is melted and heated through. If it needs thickened you can use flour/ cornstarch and water mixture to do so.
