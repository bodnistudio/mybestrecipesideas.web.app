##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Sushi:

1. Get 4 pieces white bread
1. Get 1/2 cup mawa or khoya
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon sugar powder
1. Take 4 finger shape Mango slices
1. Make ready 4 tablespoons chocolate sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sweet Sushi:

1. In a bowl take mawa and mix powder very well.
1. Remove the edges of white bread.
1. Roll it softly to make it slightly thin.
1. Apply mawa mixture on it and place mango slice on one side.
1. Roll it along mango side to form cylinder.
1. Now apply chocolate sauce on all sides and cut into two and serve.
