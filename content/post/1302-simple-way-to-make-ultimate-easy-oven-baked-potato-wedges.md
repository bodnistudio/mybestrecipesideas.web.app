##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Oven Baked Potato Wedges:

1. Take  large russet potatoes
1. Prepare  Vegetable oil
1. Get  Salt and pepper (optional)
1. Make ready  Garlic powder (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Oven Baked Potato Wedges:

1. Preheat oven to 400° F.
1. Scrub and wash potatoes.
1. Cut potatoes in half and then into thick slices, about a half inch.
1. On a baking sheet pour a bit of vegetable oil to thinly cover the bottom of the baking sheet.
1. Place wedges on baking sheet in single layer.
1. Place in oven for about 15-20 minutes or until bottom of wedges are golden brown.
1. Turn wedges over and continue baking for about 10 minutes.
1. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste and enjoy!
