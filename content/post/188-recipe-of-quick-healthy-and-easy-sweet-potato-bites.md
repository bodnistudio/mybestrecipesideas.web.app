##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy and Easy Sweet Potato Bites:

1. Get 1 Asian sweet potato
1. Get 2 Egg yolks
1. Take 50 grams Honey (or sugar)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Milk
1. Make ready 20 grams Margarine
1. Take 3 drops Vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Healthy and Easy Sweet Potato Bites:

1. Peel the potato, cut into 1 cm slices, and soak in water. Drain and place in a heatproof plate and cover with plastic wrap. Microwave until softened.
1. White the potatoes are hot, mash with a pestle until there are no lumps left.
1. Add all the ingredients, reserving 1 tablespoon of egg yolk, and combine well.
1. Shape as you like. If the paste sticks to your hands, chill the mixture in the refrigerator before working them again.
1. Dilute the reserved egg yolk with a little bit of water and brush on the potato bites.
1. Bake in the 220℃ oven for about 20 minutes.
1. Round shapes are cute too.
