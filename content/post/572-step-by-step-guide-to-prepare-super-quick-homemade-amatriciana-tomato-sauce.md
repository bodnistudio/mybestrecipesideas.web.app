1. Take 1/2 cup water
1. Make ready 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
1. Take  Salt

##### Instructions to make Amatriciana (Tomato Sauce):

1. Add tomatoes to blender and puree. Put aside for later use.
1. In a large Dutch oven cook onion and garlic in olive oil until onions are translucent.
1. Add bacon and water. Cook until water is evaporated and fat has rendered.
1. Add tomato paste and red pepper flakes and a few teaspoons of salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
1. Add wine and cook until reduced by half.
1. Add tomato puree and bring to a low boil. Reduce heat, cover partially and simmer for at least 45 minutes.
1. Serve with pasta noodle of your choice. Corkscrew or penne work best.
