##### The ingredients needed to make Temari Sushi:

1. Take 700 grams Plain cooked rice
1. Take 1 as stated on the packet Sushi vinegar
1. Get 4 servings Assorted sashimi pack
1. Prepare 1 Salmon roe preserved in soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 Daikon radish sprouts
1. Prepare 1 Nori (dried seaweed sheet)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Temari Sushi:

1. Make the sushi rice. I used Top Valu sushi vinegar.
1. Once the rice has cooled, use some cling film to shape into bite sized balls.
1. With a clean sheet of cling film, wrap the topping and rice ball together and use it to shape the sushi into balls.
1. Once shaped, serve them on a plate and decorate with salmon roe and radish sprouts.
