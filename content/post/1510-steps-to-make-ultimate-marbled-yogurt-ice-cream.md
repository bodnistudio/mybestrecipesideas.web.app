##### The ingredients needed to make Marbled Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. Make ready 200 ml Heavy cream
1. Make ready 200 grams Plain yogurt
1. Make ready 50 grams Sugar
1. Make ready 1 roughly 3 tablespoons Blueberry jam (or any jam)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Marbled Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. Whisk heavy cream and sugar in a bowl until soft as yogurt.
1. Add yogurt and whisk for another minute (30 seconds when using an electric mixer.)
1. Pour into a container. Cover to freeze.
1. After a while (about an hour), mix lightly and continue to freeze. After some time, mix, and freeze... repeat 2 or 3 times.
1. When it&#39;s all airy and fluffy like the picture above, drop spoonfuls of jam.
1. Mix everything lightly for a marbled effect. Freeze again.
