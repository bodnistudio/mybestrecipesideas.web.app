##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Potato Soup:

1. Get  Beef and Taters
1. Prepare 1 1/2 lb beef stew or your favorite cut
1. Prepare 2 russet potatoes
1. Get  Juice
1. Take 5 cup beef broth
1. Prepare 4 tsp liquid smoke flavor
1. Take  Spices
1. Get 5 tbsp celery seed
1. Make ready 10 tbsp garlic powder
1. Make ready 5 tbsp paprika
1. Prepare 3 tbsp ground parsley




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Beef Potato Soup:

1. Lightly stir-fry beef stew until medium rare (should take about 4 minutes).
1. Dice your russet potatoes into 1/2 square inch cubes and stir-fry for 1 minute.
1. Place beef and potatoes in crock pot and pour your liquids (broth and liquid smoke) into the same pot. Let it sit for roughly 8 minutes.
1. Add spices to the mix, stir around, and set the crock pot temperature to 180°F. Let sit for 4 and a half hours.
1. Enjoy the goodness that is my spiced beef and potato soup!
