##### Instructions to make Grilled Fresh Broccoli:

1. Make basting sauce
1. In a bowl combine melted butter, olive oil, lemon and lime juice, garlic, srirahca, pepper, salt snd romano, stir to blend
1. Cut woody ends of broccoli but keep heads intact
1. Slice broccoli down center of stalk , all the way through. Each head will become two with one flat side.
1. Blanch broccoli in either chicken broth or salted water for 2 minutes
1. Brush with basting sauce all over, if you have time place broccoli in a ziplock bag with the basting ssuce and marinate it, refrigerated up to overnight
1. Grill  over direct hest untl brown in spots and tender
