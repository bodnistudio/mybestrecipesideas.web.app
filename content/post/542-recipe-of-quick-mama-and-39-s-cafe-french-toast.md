<h3>The ingredients needed to make Mama&#39;s Cafe French Toast:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 loaf of of bread ( your desired kind, we have used texas toast, plain white, french bread, and wheat.). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 of Eggs  ( we estimate 1 egg per person eating.). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 pinch of of salt and a dash of pepper.. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of Flavored coffee Creamers In the Dairy Section ( We have tried many kinds, however our favorite ones are French Vanilla, Pumpkin Spice, Gingerbread, any of the holiday varieties.). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 stick of of butter for greasing the griddle ( The whole stick is not all used unless making multiple flavors. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of Aluminum foil to cover plate to keep them warm while the rest cook. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Mama&#39;s Cafe French Toast:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Heat a griddle to 375 or use a frying pan if you don&#39;t have a griddle..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			While griddle is heating up beat the eggs, salt and pepper and the flavored coffee creamer of you choosing with a whisk or a fork until thoroughly combined..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Grease the hot griddle with the butter well. Then dip bread on both sides into the egg mixture and then place it on the griddle..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cook until golden brown on one side and then flip over. The second side will cook sightly faster than the first..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Set them on to a plate or serving dish and serve!.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			** to make multiple flavors, simple repeat the steps mixing the different flavors in separate bowls then cooking only one flavor at a time to avoid confusion.**.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			** To freeze let them cool completely and place in freezer bags and label them with the flavor on bag.** This makes a great morning breakfast even on the crazy school mornings..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
