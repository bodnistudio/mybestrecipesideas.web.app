##### The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Breakfast:

1. Prepare  sausage links (12ounces each)
1. Get  refrigerated shredded hash browns (20 ounces)
1. Get  chopped green onions
1. Prepare  sweet red bell pepper, chopped
1. Take  diced mild green chilies (4 ounce)
1. Take  shredded cheddar cheese
1. Prepare  eggs
1. Take  milk
1. Take  salt
1. Get  pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Slow Cooker Breakfast:

1. Cook sausage according to package; cut into 1/4 inch slices, set aside
1. In a bowl combine green onions, red pepper, chilies; set aside.
1. Spray the interior of a 5-6 quart slow cooker with veg. cooking spray. Layer one third of hash browns, green onion mixture and sausage and cheese into crock. Repeat layers twice.
1. In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper, pour over layered ingredents.
1. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or until themometer inserted in center reads 160°F. Serve.
1. Cooking this over night makes a great ready meal in the morning. Also makes great breakfast burritos. You can add any meats you wish.
