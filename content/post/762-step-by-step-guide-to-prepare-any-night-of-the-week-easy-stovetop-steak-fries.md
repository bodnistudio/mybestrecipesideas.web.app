##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Stovetop Steak Fries:

1. Prepare 3 large russett potatoes, cleaned
1. Prepare 4 tbsp oil
1. Take 1 tsp salt to sprinkle over fries.




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Stovetop Steak Fries:

1. Microwave or bake your potatoes until tender but not too soft. Microwave can take up to 10 minutes. Baking will be in a 425 oven for 45-60 minutes.
1. When potatoes are cool enough to handle cut them length wise into quarters.
1. In a hot large skillet, heat oil on medium heat.
1. Add potatoes and leave cooking on each side until you reach desired brown color and crispness.
1. This usually takes 3-4 minutes a side.
1. Sprinkle with salt when finished cooking and serve.
