##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Desserts:

1. Get 1cup Nice cream frozen

1. Take as needed mango for garnish

1. Take 30 almonds soaked overnight
1. Take 3 cups water
1. Take 1/2 cup dates desseeded
1. Take  For mango milkshake
1. Take Half mango chopped
1. Take 1 glass Almond milk
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sabja seeds

##### Steps to make Vegan Desserts:

1. First we shall make almond milk. Soak almond overnight add to a juicer mixer grinder pulse it with one cup water first then let it incorporate then add two more cups of water. Strain the mixture and put it back to the juicer jar then add the dates and blend well. Almond milk with natural sweetness is ready.
1. For nice cream. In a blender add ripe banana with 4 teaspoon of almond milk (mentioned above) Then transfer to a bowl and add chopped mango and Sabja seeds for garnish and protein aspect.
1. For Mango milkshake. In a juice jar add chopped mango the strained almond powder, that was made while making the almond milk and then add almond milk and blend well. Serve it a tall glass and garnish with Sabja seeds. Protein got from almonds and Sabja seeds.
1. Note please do not add extra sweetners mango and ripe banana are already sweet and dates gives an additional sweetness.
