##### The ingredients needed to make Made Simply in the Microwave--Juice Gummies:

1. Prepare 100 ml Your choice of juice
1. Get 15 grams Gelatin
1. Take 3 tbsp Honey
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon juice (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Made Simply in the Microwave--Juice Gummies:

1. Put the juice and gelatin in a heat resistant bowl and allow the gelatin to soak for about 10 minutes.
1. Microwave the bowl until the gelatin has completely dissolved. (Heat for about 1.5 minutes at 700W.)
1. Add the honey and lemon juice to the gelatin mixture and combine. Pour the contents of the bowl into your choice of molds, then chill them in the fridge for about 2 hours to set. All done.
1. I made toy block shaped gummies.
