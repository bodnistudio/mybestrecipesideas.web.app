##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato and Smoked Beef Rice:

1. Take 1 cup basmati rice
1. Prepare 4 smallish tomato, diced
1. Get 1/2 onion, sliced
1. Make ready 100 gr smoked beef, sliced
1. Get 2 cup beef broth
1. Prepare 1 pinch salt
1. Get 1/2 tsp crushed black pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tomato and Smoked Beef Rice:

1. Heat oil in a pot. Add smoked beef and onion, stirring occasionally, for a couple of minutes.
1. Add tomato and stir.
1. Add basmati rice (be sure to wash it beforehand) and stir for a couple of minutes. Add broth, salt, and black pepper. Stir.
1. At this point, wait until the water is boiling. After it boils, use the lowest setting and cover the pot.
1. After 15 minutes, it should be ready. If not, cover and wait for a couple of minutes more.
