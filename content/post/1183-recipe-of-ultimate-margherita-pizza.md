##### The ingredients needed to make Margherita Pizza:

1. Get 1 readymade thin-crust pizza base
1. Get 2 tablespoon readymade Schezwan Chutney
1. Make ready 2 tablespoon Butter
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon chopped Olive
1. Make ready 3 cube Cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Margherita Pizza:

1. First of all apply butter on the pizza base.
1. Then spread Schezwan Chutney on it.
1. Apply shredded cheese all over the base.
1. Put Olives as topping on the base.
1. In a pre-heated Oven,bake it for around 15 mins. on 180°.
1. Now cut and serve with ketchup.
