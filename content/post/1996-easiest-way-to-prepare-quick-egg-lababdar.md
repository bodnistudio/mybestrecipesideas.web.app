##### Instructions to make Egg lababdar:

1. Now heat oil in a kadhai and add bay leaves, onions,green chillies and saute till onions become light brown.Add the dry masalas mix it well
1. After a while add tomato cashew paste,kasuri methi and salt. Now saute the mixture till oil separates from it.
1. Add water to adjust consistency as it starts boiling add 4 eggs cut into half,grate 1 egg white upon it cover it and cook for 10minutes on low flame.
1. Add coriander leaves and cream, cook for another 5 minutes and then serve with hot chapatis.
