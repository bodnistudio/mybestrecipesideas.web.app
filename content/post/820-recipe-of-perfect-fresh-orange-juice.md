##### The ingredients needed to make Fresh Orange Juice:

##### Instructions to make Fresh Orange Juice:

1. Drain the orange slices, arrange in two tall glasses.
1. Combine orange juice, water, salt, sugar, the syrup from canned orange slices. Pour into two glasses.
1. Stir well. Add li hing mui. Steep for 10 minutes or so. Add some ice, and enjoy!
