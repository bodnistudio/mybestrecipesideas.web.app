##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Pan-Fried Salmon in Olive Oil:

1. Prepare 2 filets Raw salmon
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive oil
1. Take 1 clove Garlic
1. Prepare 1 Red chili pepper
1. Take 1 tsp Salt
1. Get 1/2 tsp Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Pan-Fried Salmon in Olive Oil:

1. Sprinkle both sides of the salmon filet with salt and pepper.
1. Add olive oil, garlic, and chili pepper in a pan, and turn the heat to low. When fragrant, turn the heat up to medium-low. Add the salmon, and pan-fry for 3 minutes.
1. Turn the salmon over, turn down the heat to low, and cook for 3 more minutes. When cooked 90% through, it&#39;s done.
