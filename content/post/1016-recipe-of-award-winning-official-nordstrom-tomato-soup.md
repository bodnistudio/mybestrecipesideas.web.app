##### Steps to make Official Nordstrom Tomato Soup:

1. Add tomatoes with their juice, water and chicken base. Stir to dissolve chicken base and let simmer for 60 min.
1. Turn off heat and blend with a hand mixer until smooth - about 3-4 min on high speed.
