##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Potato Bake:

1. Get 6 medium Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs
1. Prepare 1 large Yellow Onion, sliced in wedges
1. Take 5 medium Red Potatoes, quartered
1. Prepare 1 Lime, sliced in half and juiced
1. Get 1/3 cup Olive Oil
1. Get 7 clove Garlic (skin on and crushed)
1. Get 1/4 cup Paprika
1. Get 3 tbsp Honey
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon Pepper Seasoning
1. Take 3 tbsp Oregano
1. Take 1 Salt and Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken and Potato Bake:

1. Mix all of ingredients in a large mixing bowl (including lime and lime juice)
1. Marinate the chicken, veggies, and lime mixture in the fridge for at least an hour. (Overnight is best)
1. Preheat oven to 400°F Fahrenheit
1. Grease a baking dish and lay bed of potatoes, onion, garlic, and lime haves. Add chicken on the top.
1. Bake for 60 minutes, or until the chicken and potatoes are cooked through
