##### The ingredients needed to make General Tsao Chicken:

1. Get 4 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast cut into 1 inch pieces
1. Prepare 2 teaspoons salt
1. Get 1 1/2 cups plus 4 tablespoons cornstarch divided
1. Make ready 1 teaspoon pepper
1. Prepare 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
1. Get 1 cup brown sugar
1. Get 1 cup soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 cup pineapple juice
1. Take 2/3 cups rice vinegar or white vinegar
1. Take 8 cloves garlic minced
1. Make ready 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon dried Ginger
1. Prepare  Green onions for garnish
1. Make ready 1/2 cup cold water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make General Tsao Chicken:

1. Coat chicken pieces in remaining 1 1/2 cups corn starch. Pan fry in oil or deep fry for 2 -3 minutes per batch.
1. In a saucepan add soy sauce, pineapple juice, vinegar, salt, pepper, oil, garlic, sugar, cayenne pepper, dried ginger. Bring to a slow boil. Dissolve 4 tablespoons cornstarch in cold water. Add cornstarch and water to saucepan. Stir till thickened.
1. Pour sauce over chicken and toss.
1. Keep warm in oven or slow cooker until ready to serve and garnish with green onion.
