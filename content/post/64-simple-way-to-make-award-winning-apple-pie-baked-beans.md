<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Baked Beans:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 48 oz of jar great northern beans (or your choice) (drained). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 4 tbs of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 of large baking apples - peeled and diced. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of white sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of apple pie spice (or nutmeg). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/8 tsp of red pepper flakes (optional). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of ketchup. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1-2 tbs of molasses. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of apple cider vinegar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 4-5 slices of bacon. </li>
	
</ol>

</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Apple Pie Baked Beans:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 400F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spray a 2-quart casserole dish with cooking spray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In large skillet, melt butter, add diced apples and diced onion then cook until tender (about 5 minutes)..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, red pepper flakes, salt &amp; pepper, stir until well combined..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cook until sugar is melted..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Stir in ketchup, molasses, vinegar. Stir well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add beans and mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place bacon slices over the top..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour mixture into the prepared 2 quart casserole..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake in a 400F oven for about 45 mins-1 hour..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Crock Pot Instructions:
Spray the crock pot with cooking spray.
Sautee apples and onions in a large skillet then place all ingredients in a crock pot or slow-cooker. Cook on high 2-3 hours or low 4 hours..

</ol>
