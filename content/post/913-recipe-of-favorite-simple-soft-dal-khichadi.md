##### The ingredients needed to make Simple-Soft Dal Khichadi:

1. Prepare  Khichadi Rice
1. Take  yellow lentil (Moong Dal)
1. Make ready  salt
1. Make ready  turmeric powder
1. Prepare  water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simple-Soft Dal Khichadi:

1. Take rice and dal in a bowl. Wash it thoroughly. Soak for 5 mins in 2 glasses of water.
1. Meanwhile, take a cooker add 2 glasses of water. Put on high flame. Add salt and turmeric water. Get water boiled. Now add rice and dal with soak water.
1. If required add more water. Mix it well. Close the lid of the cooker. Get 3 whistles. Let the cooker cool completely.
1. Khichadi is ready to serve. I served with Gujarati Kadhi and salad.
