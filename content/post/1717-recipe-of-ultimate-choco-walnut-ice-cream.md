##### The ingredients needed to make Choco Walnut Ice-cream:

1. Prepare 500 grams whipping cream
1. Get 250 grams condensed milk
1. Get 1/2 cup walnuts
1. Take 250 grams dark chocolate bar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Choco Walnut Ice-cream:

1. Put whipping cream into a large bowl and add condensed milk to it.
1. Whip at medium speed for couple of minutes and mix the ingredients.
1. Now add melted chocolate, walnut powder.
1. Mix the ingredients well.
1. The Ice-Cream mixture is ready to be refrigerated.
1. Ice-cream can be refrigerated in an air tight container
1. Pour the mixture in the container.
1. Level the mixture.Cover the container tightly.
1. Refrigerate for 7-8hours or overnight.
1. Serve it with chocolate sauce and perk on top
