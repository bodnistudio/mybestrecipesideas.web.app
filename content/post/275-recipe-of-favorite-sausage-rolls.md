##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage Rolls:

1. Make ready 500 grams Sausage mince
1. Get 500 grams Lean pork mince
1. Make ready 1 medium onion, finely chopped
1. Make ready 2 medium carrots, grated
1. Take 1 Zucchini, grated
1. Get 1 tbsp Minced garlic
1. Make ready 7 Sheets puff pastry (makes 14 large sausage rolls)
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sausage Rolls:

1. Really easy- mix mince, carrot, zuchini and garlic together in large bowl.
1. Spoon mixture onto puff pastry sheet, spread and then roll almost to the end.
1. Brush some milk on the end of sheet and roll the end to seal.
1. Trim both ends, slice in half and place on a prepared baking tray. Repeat until mixture is used up.
1. Brush rolls with milk and place in oven at 160*C for 45 to 60 mins. Mine took 60 mins on low fan.
1. Serve with a nice salad. - Its so easy and ofcourse you can tweak it in many ways- different sizes, flavours, ingredients. Better than shop bought for sure.
