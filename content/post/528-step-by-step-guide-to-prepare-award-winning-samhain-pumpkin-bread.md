<h3>The ingredients needed to make Samhain Pumpkin Bread:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 3/4 cup of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3/4 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tsp of baking soda. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 1/2 tsp of cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of allspice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 15 oz of pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/3 cup of water, juice, or pumpkin spice coffee cooled. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 5 of drops almond extract (optional). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of vegetable oil. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts or nut of your choice (optional). </li>
