##### The ingredients needed to make Easy! Kuri Kinton (Chestnut and Sweet Potato Paste) Made with Annoh-Imo Potatoes:

1. Make ready 660 grams Sweet potatoes (Anno-imo variety)
1. Take 10 pieces Chestnuts in syrup (store-bought)
1. Prepare 2 Gardenia seeds
1. Make ready 50 ml ※ Candied Japanese chestnut syrup
1. Make ready 50 ml ※ Mirin
1. Take 50 ml ※ Water from boiling the sweet potatoes




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy! Kuri Kinton (Chestnut and Sweet Potato Paste) Made with Annoh-Imo Potatoes:

1. Peel the potatoes rather thickly and soak in water for 10 minutes.
1. Wash the potatoes well under running water and put in a pan. Fill with a water to cover 1 cm above. Next, add crushed gardenia seeds put into an empty tea bag.
1. Boil until they can be poked through easily with a skewer. Reserve the broth in another container and discard the gardenia seeds.
1. Blend the still-hot potatoes and the ingredients marked ※ in a food processor until smooth.
1. Soften the mixture to your liking by adding potato broth. Mix in the candied chestnuts and it&#39;s done.
1. I used this in my Osechi (the New Year&#39;s feast) (see picture).
