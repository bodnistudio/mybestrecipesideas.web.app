##### Instructions to make Flaky Pie Crust (easily removable):

1. Add the sifted flour, sugar, and salt into a bowl. Lightly mix together and then add the cubed butter.
1. Cut in the butter using a rubber spatula or scraper to efficiently mix it in.
1. When it has become crumbly, add the egg and lump it together.
1. Cover with plastic wrap for 30 minutes or more. If you have time, leave it in the refrigerator for half a day.
1. Roll it out, then fold it, roll it again, and fold it up. Repeat this about three times.
1. Pack into a tart pan and pierce holes with a fork.
1. Place parchment paper in the tart pan and fill with pie weights. Bake for 25 minutes at 180°C, remove the weights and bake for another 5 minutes. (The photo is a 12 cm crust).
