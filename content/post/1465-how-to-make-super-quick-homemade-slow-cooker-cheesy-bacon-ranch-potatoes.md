##### The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Cheesy Bacon Ranch Potatoes:

##### Instructions to make Slow Cooker Cheesy Bacon Ranch Potatoes:

1. Cook bacon till brown and crispy. Let cool before crumbling; set aside.
1. Line a slow cooker with aluminum foil, leaving enough to overhang to wrap the potatoes on top, and coat with nonstick spray. Place a layer of potatoes evenly into the slow cooker. Top with cheese, ranch seasoning and bacon, repeating 2 more times and reserving 1/2 cup cheese.
1. Cover potatoes with aluminum foil and cook on low  heat for 7-8 hours or high heat for 3-4 hours. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Cover and cook until melted, about 1-2 minutes.
1. Serve immediately, garnished with chives.
