##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Ogura (Adzuki Bean Paste) Ice Cream:

1. Make ready 1 can Boiled and canned aduki beans
1. Make ready 200 ml Heavy cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Ogura (Adzuki Bean Paste) Ice Cream:

1. Beat heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks. Chill the bowl in ice water for best results.
1. Add boiled and canned adzuki beans into Step 1 and mix completely.
1. Freeze to harden as is, or transfer to a metal tray.
1. Before it hardens completely, stir the mixture once or twice. This way, the adzuki beans won&#39;t sink to the bottom and will be scattered throughout the ice cream.
