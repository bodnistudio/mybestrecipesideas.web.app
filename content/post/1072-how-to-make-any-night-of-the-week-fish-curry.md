##### Steps to make Fish curry:

1. Mix grated coconut, tomato, onion, garlic, coriander seeds, red chilli powder (gives that fish curry colour) and turmeric in a mixer (add little water to ease mixing).
1. Pour oil in a heated vessel and add onion (This is not a fodni. So no rai, jeera)
1. Add few cups water to the mixture.
1. Once onion is little fried, Add the diluted mixture to the vessel.
1. Add salt and kokam (4-5 pieces)
1. Once it starts boiling, lower the gas and add the cut fish pieces.
1. Keep low gas and let it cook. Add coriander to taste.
