##### The ingredients needed to make Flank Steak:

##### Instructions to make Flank Steak:

1. Mix dry ingredients together and rub the flank steak
1. Pour liquid smoke over the steak and let it sit room temperature before cooking. (To cook evenly)
1. Place on grill 4 mins on each side to get nice marks. Move to cutting board and let it rest.
1. Top with jalapeno chimi churri.
