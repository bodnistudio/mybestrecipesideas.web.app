##### The ingredients needed to make Ms. B&#39;s Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Get 1 premade uncooked pie crust
1. Take 3 oz cream cheese (room temp)
1. Take 1 1/2 cup sweet potato (mashed)
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp nutmeg
1. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1/4 cup half &amp; half or milk
1. Get 2 large eggs
1. Take 1 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 cup butter (softened)
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Ms. B&#39;s Sweet Potato Pie:

1. preheat oven to 325
1. place pie shell in pie pan all the way to top.
1. in a bowl blend cream cheese and sugar. set aside.
1. in another bowl combine rest of ingredients.
1. blend cream cheese mix in with sweet potato mix.
1. pour into pie shell to almost the top.
1. bake for about 1 hour or until knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
1. let cool and serve or serve a little warm.
