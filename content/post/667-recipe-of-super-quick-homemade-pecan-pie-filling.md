##### Steps to make Pecan pie filling:

1. preheat oven to 350°F.
1. combine eggs, corn syrup, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. mix well.
1. line a 9 inch pie plate with pastry.
1. line pastry shell with all the pecans.
1. place pan on oven rack. carefully pour the filling into the pastry shell.
1. to prevent overbrowning, cover edge of pie with foil. bake in oven for 25 minutes. remove foil.
1. bake for 20-25 minutes more or until knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
1. cool on wire rack. cover and refrigerate within 2 hours.
