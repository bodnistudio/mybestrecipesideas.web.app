##### The ingredients needed to make Simmered Meat and Potato (Nikujaga):

1. Make ready 4-5 potatoes (approx. 500 g)
1. Prepare 3 onions
1. Make ready 200 g thinly sliced pork or beef (if any, otherwise minced pork)
1. Make ready 200 g shirataki (konnyaku noodles)
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp oil
1. Get 6 tbsp soy sauce
1. Take 4 tbsp sugar
1. Get 1 tbsp butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simmered Meat and Potato (Nikujaga):

1. Cut the potatoes into bite-size chunks. Cut the onions into wedges. And cut the carrot into random pieces. Boil the konnyaku noodles for about 5 minutes and drain water. Cut them into bite-length.
1. Heat the oil in a pot and fry the onion for 2 minutes. Add the carrot and the potatoes and keep frying. Put the meat and the konnyaku noodles over them. Add the soy sauce and the sugar. Then, cover with a lid. Don&#39;t mix them now.
1. Turn down the heat to medium-low heat and simmer for 15 minutes with the lid. 15 minutes later, open the lid and mix all ingredients in the pot.
1. Simmer with the lid again for 8 ~ 10 minutes over medium-low heat until the potatoes are cooked. Open the lid and add some butter.
