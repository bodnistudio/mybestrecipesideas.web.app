<h3>The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Jell-O Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of box strawberry jello. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of cold water. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of hot water. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of tub cool whip (lite can be used; not fat free though). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Approx. 5-7 strawberries for garnish. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of sliced strawberries for filling. </li>
	
		<li>{Take  of (8 or 9in.) graham cracker pie shell. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Strawberry Jell-O Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In a bowl, make jello according to package directions..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place into fridge for about 15-18 minutes or until jello just slightly sets..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			With a whisk or hand mixer, add entire tub of cool whip into the thickened jello &amp; blend well until there are no lumps..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Fold in chopped up strawberries..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour mixture into pie shell &amp; place into fridge to set a minimum of 4 hours or overnight..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top with the whole strawberries &amp; enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
