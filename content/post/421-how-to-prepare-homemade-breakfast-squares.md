##### The ingredients needed to make Breakfast squares:

1. Take 1 lb ground sausage
1. Take 6 large eggs
1. Make ready 6 slice bacon
1. Make ready 1/4 tbsp salt
1. Prepare 1/4 tbsp pepper
1. Take 1/3 cup shredded mozz
1. Make ready 2 packages crescent rolls
1. Make ready 1 tsp melted butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Breakfast squares:

1. preheat oven to 350
1. spray baking sheet with even layer of cooking spray
1. scramble eggs with salt and pepper, then set aside
1. cook sausage and set aside
1. crisp bacon and set aside
1. mix eggs, bacon, and sausage together in a bowl.
1. unroll crescents into 8 squares, seeking the perforated seems
1. add 4 tbsp of breakfast mixture to each square and fold corners up
1. brush each folded square with melted butter to preference.
1. place on baking sheet(s) and cooking for 20-25 mins or until golden brown on top
