##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Smoothie:

1. Make ready 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1. Prepare 3 tbsp avocoto
1. Prepare 1/4 pineapple
1. Prepare 1/3 lemon
1. Take 1 banana
1. Take tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1/4 stalk of celery
1. Make ready 1/4 cucumber
1. Prepare tbsp peanut butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Healthy Smoothie:

1. Blend in 1 cup of milk along with the 1/4 pineapple and 1 banana.
1. Add in 1/4 celery, 1/4 cucumber and 3 tablespoons of avocoto and blend.
1. Then add in a teaspoon cinnamon and a tablespoon of peanut butter and blend it for one last time.
1. Enjoy!
