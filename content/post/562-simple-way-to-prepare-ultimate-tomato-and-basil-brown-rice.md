##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato And Basil Brown Rice:

1. Take 1 cup Brown Basmati rice
1. Take 2 &amp; 1/4 cup Water
1. Make ready 2 tsp Salt
1. Take 1 cup Tomato,deseeded and chopped
1. Prepare 1 cup Basil leaves chopped
1. Get 1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
1. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
1. Get 1 tbsp Pizza sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp Chilli flakes
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tomato And Basil Brown Rice:

1. Wash and soak rice for one hour.
1. In a pan add water and salt.Bring to a boil on high heat and add the rice.Turn heat to simmer and let cook until the rice is cooked through.Turn heat off and keep aside.
1. In a bowl whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar,pizza sauce, chilli flakes and sugar.
1. Add the balsamic vinegar dressing to the rice and stir through.
1. Add the tomatoes and basil leaves.
1. Serve hot.
