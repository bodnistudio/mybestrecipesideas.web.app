##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Steak &amp; Kidney Pie:

1. Make ready 800 g steak and kidney, proportions according to taste
1. Prepare 1 large onion, roughly chopped
1. Prepare 850 ml beef stock
1. Make ready  Salt &amp; pepper
1. Prepare 2 tbsp beef gravy granules
1. Take 500 g Jus-Rol chilled shortcrust pastry




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Quick Steak &amp; Kidney Pie:

1. Put the meat, onion and gravy into the pressure cooker, bring to pressure and then cook for 25 minutes. Allow to cool.
1. Grease a pie dish. Divide the pastry one third/two thirds. Pre-heat oven time Gas Mark 5 or electric equivalent, probably 180C but 160C on my Circotherm.
1. Roll out the larger piece of pastry and use it to line the dish, including rim.
1. Season meat to taste, then use a slotted spoon to separate it from the gravy and place it on the pie.
1. Roll out the remaining pastry and use to cover the pie, crimping the two layers of pastry edges together. Make three small nicks in the lid of the pie to allow steam to escape.
1. Put the pie into the pre-heated oven for 40-45 minutes or until it looks cooked.
1. When the pie is nearly ready, heat the gravy in a saucepan and add beef gravy granules to thicken.
1. Serve with new potatoes and vegetables of your choice. Carrots and something green go well.
