##### Steps to make Vickys Sweet Wild Rice, GF DF EF SF:

1. Cook the rice in boiling water according to the pack instructions, adding the peas during the last 5 minutes of cooking time
1. Meanwhile, dry fry the almonds in a frying pan until golden and fragrant
1. Fluff the rice and add in the ground almonds, toasted almonds reserving some for garnish and the currants. Season with pepper and toss together gently
