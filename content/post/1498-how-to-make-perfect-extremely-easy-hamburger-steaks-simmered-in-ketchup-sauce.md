##### The ingredients needed to make Extremely Easy! Hamburger Steaks Simmered in Ketchup Sauce:

1. Prepare 250 grams Minced meat (with minimal fat)
1. Prepare 1/2 cup panko (I used sliced bread crusts)
1. Get 100 ml Milk
1. Prepare 1 medium Onion
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Take 1 medium Onion
1. Prepare 1 packages Mushrooms (Shimeji, Maitake, etc.)
1. Take 100 ml Ketchup
1. Make ready 380 ml Water (Vegetable Juice if you have it)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Extremely Easy! Hamburger Steaks Simmered in Ketchup Sauce:

1. Add milk to the panko to soften.
1. Add the microwaved onions, ground meat, and eggs to a bowl and knead. Add salt and pepper, and nutmeg if you have.
1. Fry on both sides in a frying pan. Wipe out the excess grease in the pan with a paper towel.
1. Add the onions and mushrooms. You don&#39;t need to saute them.
1. Squueze in the ketchup.
1. Add the water (or vegetable juice if you have it... this makes the flavor richer).
1. Cover with a lid without stirring and slowly boil over low-medium heat.
1. The moisture that comes out of the vegetables will turn the sauce into a tomato sauce. Simmer to reduce it down to your desired consistency.
1. I added coffee cream as the finishing touch.
