##### Instructions to make Quick and Easy Vegan Stir Fry:

1. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes. Stir occasionally to stop sticking and to cook evenly.
1. Bring a pan of water to boil and add rice noodles. Simmer for a few minutes until soft. Strain.
