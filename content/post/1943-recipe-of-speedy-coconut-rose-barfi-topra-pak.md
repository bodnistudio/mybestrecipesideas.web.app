##### The ingredients needed to make Coconut Rose Barfi (Topra Pak):

1. Take 2 cup desicated coconut
1. Make ready 1 cup milk
1. Make ready 1/2 cup milk powder
1. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
1. Take 1 tbsp ghee
1. Get 4 tbsp rose syrup
1. Get 2-3 drops pink food colour




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Coconut Rose Barfi (Topra Pak):

1. Heat a pan add ghee and roast desicated coconut for 2 minutes over a medium flame. Be careful to not burn it.
1. Add milk,sugar, rose syrup and pink food colour mix it well and cook till the mixture gets cooked well and all the moisture evaporates then add milk powder mix it well for 2 minutes.
1. Grease a plate with ghee. Spread the coconut mixture evenly and sprinkle some slice almonds on it and allow to set.
1. Let it cool completely and then cut into desire shape and transfer onto a serving plate.
