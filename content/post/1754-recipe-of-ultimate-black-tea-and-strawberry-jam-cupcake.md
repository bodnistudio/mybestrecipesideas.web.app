##### Instructions to make Black Tea and Strawberry Jam Cupcake:

1. Combine the pancake mix, leaves from the tea bag, egg, sugar, honey, and milk in a bowl with a whisk.
1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the butter in the microwave at 600 W for about 30 seconds. Mix into Step 1.
1. Use your finger to use the butter leftover in the bowl from Step 2 to grease the cupcake molds.
1. Fill the molds rom Step 3 halfway with batter from Step 2. Add some strawberry jam and use a toothpick to create a marbleized pattern.
1. Pour the remaining batter over the jam from Step 4. Top with a bit of strawberry jam.
1. Bake in an oven preheated to 170℃ for 15 minutes.
