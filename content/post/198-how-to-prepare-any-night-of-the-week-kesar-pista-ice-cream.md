##### Instructions to make Kesar pista ice cream:

1. Transfer this icrecream mixture to a airtight container. Add few tutty fruity on top if required. Cover with plastic wrap to avoid getting ice formed. Now cover with airtight lid and keep in freezer for 8-10 hours.
