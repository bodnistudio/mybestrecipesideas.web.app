##### The ingredients needed to make Triple Decker Tortilla Pie:

1. Make ready 1 Rotisserie Chicken
1. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1. Get 1 can green chile enchilada sauce
1. Get 3 flour tortillas
1. Get 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded lettuce or spinach
1. Take 1/2 diced tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 diced avocado




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Triple Decker Tortilla Pie:

1. In medium bowl, mix shredded meat from roasted chicken, cilantro, and enchilada sauce. Place 1 flour tortilla in 10- to 12-inch skillet. Top with half of chicken mixture and 1/3 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese; repeat layering, then top with 1 final tortilla. Sprinkle with remaining 1/3 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese; bake at 450°F 8 to 10 minutes, or until heated through and cheese has melted.
1. To serve, top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and diced avocado.
