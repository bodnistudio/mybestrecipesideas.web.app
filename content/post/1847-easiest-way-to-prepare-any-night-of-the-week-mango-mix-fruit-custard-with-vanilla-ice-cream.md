##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Mix Fruit Custard with Vanilla Ice Cream:

1. Make ready 1 Cup Chopped Mixed Fruits (banana,apple,mango and grapes)
1. Get 4 Scoops Vanilla Ice Cream
1. Make ready  For garnishing
1. Prepare 1 tbsp chocolate sauce
1. Get  Some Chocolate Vermicelli
1. Prepare 4 Chocolate Pieces
1. Prepare  Some Mango Pieces for garnishing
1. Prepare  For Mango Custard
1. Take 1 1/4 Cup Milk
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Sugar
1. Take 2 tbsp Custard Powder
1. Get 4 tbsp Mango Pulp




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Mango Mix Fruit Custard with Vanilla Ice Cream:

1. Take 1 cup Milk and add sugar to it boil this milk.
1. Now in the remaining cool milk add custard powder and add this mixture to the milk.
1. Now Mix it well and try to avoid Lumps.Once custard thickens remove from the stove.
1. Once the custard cools down add mango pulp to it and mix well.
1. Now add 1 cup Chopped Mixed fruits to the custard and let it chill in the fridge for a while.
1. Once it is fully chilled,take serving bowls and pour the mix fruit mango custard to half level.Now place a scoop of vanilla Ice Cream over each serving glass.
1. Now pour Chocolate sauce over the ice cream,sprinkle some chocolate Vermicelli and place some chocolate bars alongside Chopped mango.
1. Serve it chilled!Now your yummy Mango Mix Fruit Custard with Vanilla Ice Cream is ready!
