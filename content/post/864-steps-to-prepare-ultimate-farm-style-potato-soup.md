##### The ingredients needed to make Farm Style Potato Soup:

1. Take 6-8 potatoes, washed, peeled, chopped
1. Make ready 4 tablespoons butter
1. Take  Milk
1. Take  Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Farm Style Potato Soup:

1. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes. Drain the water away and return to the pan.
1. Pour milk till the potatoes are covered. Place over medium heat.
1. Add butter and stir till melted.
1. Season to taste with pepper, bacon pieces, green onions, etc. Cook to a simmer for 10 minutes.
