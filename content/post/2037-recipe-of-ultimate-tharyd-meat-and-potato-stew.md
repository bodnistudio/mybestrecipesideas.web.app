##### The ingredients needed to make Tharyd: Meat and Potato Stew:

1. Get 1 kg leg of lamb, cut in cubes
1. Make ready 2 onions, chopped
1. Get 2 large tomatoes, peeled and diced
1. Make ready 1 kg potatoes, peeled and cut into 4 pieces
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1. Make ready 1 clove garlic, crushed
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons tomato paste
1. Prepare  baharat mix
1. Take  salt to taste
1. Make ready 1 pita bread, deep-fried cut into pieces
1. Prepare  parsley for garnish
1. Take  pickles for serving




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tharyd: Meat and Potato Stew:

1. In a deep pot, fry onions in the vegetable oil on medium heat, until they are golden.
1. Raise heat, add meat and stir.
1. When meat browns, add the baharat mix, garlic and salt.
1. Add potatoes, tomatoes and tomato paste. Cover, and simmer on medium-low heat for 2 hours.
1. To serve, place the fried bread in a serving dish and top with meat and potato stew, then sprinkle with the chopped parsley.
