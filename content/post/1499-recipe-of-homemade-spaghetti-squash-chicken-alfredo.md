1. Take 2 Spaghetti Squash
1. Get 1/2 Cup Water
1. Take 2 Grilled Skinned and Deboned Chicken Breasts, cut into cubes
1. Get 1 Jar Garlic Parmesan Sauce
1. Make ready 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1. Make ready 1 Can Sweet Peas, drained
1. Take 1 Tbsp. Garlic Powder

1. Prepare 1 Tbsp. Old Bay Seasoning

##### Instructions to make Spaghetti Squash Chicken Alfredo:

1. Wash dirt off squashes. Score squashes like u do a Baked Potato before cooking it. Add to Crock Pot. Pour in water. Cook on High 4 hours. Remove from crock pot an slice in half. Remove seeds and shredded pulp with a fork (look like tiny spaghetti noodles). Dump water out of crock pot and add the shredded squash back into the crock pot. Add rest of ingredients.
