##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Pizza Dough in 5 Minutes:

1. Prepare 80 grams Bread (strong) flour
1. Make ready 20 grams Cake flour
1. Take 1 pinch Salt
1. Get 1/2 tsp Dry yeast
1. Make ready 65 ml Lukewarm water
1. Get 1 tsp Olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Pizza Dough in 5 Minutes:

1. Combine all the ingredients except the olive oil.
1. Once it comes together, add the olive oil and knead it.
1. Microwave on low for 30 seconds.
1. Roll it out with a rolling pin into your preferred pizza shape.
1. Cover it with a tightly wrung damp cloth and let it rise while preparing the toppings.
