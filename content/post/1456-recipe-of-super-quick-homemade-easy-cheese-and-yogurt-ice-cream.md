##### The ingredients needed to make Easy! Cheese and Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. Get 60 grams Cream cheese
1. Prepare 250 grams Yogurt (sweetened or non-sweetened)
1. Get 1 Jam of your choice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy! Cheese and Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. For the yogurt, use non-sweetened that comes in a large 500 g container, or sweetened ones in portions.
1. Bring cream cheese to room temperature, or microwave for 20 seconds to soften. Use a spoon to knead and make smooth.
1. Add yogurt to the cream cheese. Whisk well.
1. Pour into a flat tupperware container and freeze to harden. After 2 hours, mix everything until smooth. Freeze again to harden.
1. It will be just right after 3-4 hours. Serve with jam or any other sauce with fruits.
1. In the picture above, I served the ice cream with &#34;Strawberry Sauce with a Microwave&#34;.
