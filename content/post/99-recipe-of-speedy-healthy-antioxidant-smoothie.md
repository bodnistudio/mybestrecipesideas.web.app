##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Antioxidant Smoothie:

1. Make ready 1/2 cucumber, skin on and roughly chopped
1. Get 3 carrots, roughly chopped
1. Take 1.5 inch ginger root, peeled and sliced
1. Take 1 inch tumeric root, peeled and chopped *I recommend you use a surface that does not stain for this, because tumeric will leave everything orange and yellow, including your fingers.*
1. Get 1 dash freshly ground black pepper (for tumeric absorption)
1. Prepare 1 baby bok choy, chopped (or 2 cups fresh broccoli, chopped)
1. Get 1/2 avocado, scooped out, throw away the pit
1. Take 1 orange, peeled and sections separated
1. Prepare 1 cup spinach/kale/carrot greens or whatever dark leafy greens you have. However, I learned the hard way not to use arugula, because it makes the smoothie taste like lawn clippings
1. Get 1 cup any fresh berries (I used blueberries for this one.)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp flax seed oil/walnut oil
1. Take 2 cups filtered water, or more as needed for consistency
1. Make ready 1 large and mighty blender in which to cram it all. It doesn&#39;t have to be an expensive one, just big enough to hold all of this, even if you have to hold the lid down




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Healthy Antioxidant Smoothie:

1. In the order which the ingredients are listed above, cram it all into the blender and start it at its lowest setting to pulverize the heartier ingredients at the bottom. If you have to hold the lid down (as I have on occasion), this will help the contents go down so you can secure the lid and let go.
1. After 2 minutes turn it up to its highest setting and let the whole thing puree for 3-5 more minutes.
1. If it&#39;s too thick, add more filtered water and puree.
