##### Instructions to make Mango Ice cream:

1. In a bottom, vessel pan add milk as it gets boil add sugar stir it and cook it for 10-15 mins. Meanwhile, in a separate bowl add milk powder and 2 tbsp(cold milk) and make a paste of it. After 15min add this mixture into the milk and cook it for 5min and turn off the gas. Let the milk cool down completely.
1. After milk gets cooled in a mixer jar add mango puree, milk and malai beat them transfer this mixture into a container and cover it with foil paper freeze it for at least 3 hours.
1. After 3 hrs blend it again and then transfer it in an airtight container freeze it for at least 6-8 hrs. In a serving bowl add 2 scoops of ice cream and garnish it with dry fruits.
