<ol>

		<li>{Take 2 1/2 cup of light brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 gallon of apple juice. </li>

<h3>Steps to make Dave&#39;s Apple Pie Moonshine:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Pour apple cider and apple juice and 4 cinnamon sticks into a large pot and begin heating on highest setting. Once the mixture is hot stir in white and brown sugar. Bring to a rapid boil and remove from heat. Cover and allow to sit until it reaches room temperature. Once cool, stir in Everclear. Put remaining cinnamon sticks into quart mason jars.
