##### Steps to make Easy, Tender Steak Every Time:

1. Age your steaks for a couple of days in the fridge. When ready to cook, hand rub with olive oil and salt and pepper lightly.
1. Rub grill grates with peanut oil. (Stand up to high heat) Preheat grill to 400 degrees. Place steaks on grill for 3 minutes and 30 seconds. When timer goes off, quarter turn and cook another 3:30. Timer goes off, turn over for 3:30, then turn a quarter turn for the final 3:30. (Excellent grill marks) Remove and let rest for 5 minutes. Perfect medium rare.
1. For rare use 3 minute intervals, For shoe leather, use 4 minute intervals.
