##### Instructions to make Chocolate ice-cream:

1. Switch off the flame and whisk it nicely to make Chocolate ganache
1. Allow it to cool.
1. Add the ganache to whipped cream and beat nicely
1. Now transfer in a air tight container and keep it in freezer for 6-8 hours or overnight
