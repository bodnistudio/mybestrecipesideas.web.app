##### The ingredients needed to make Sheik&#39;s Garden Fresh Vegetable Cream Cheese:

1. Prepare 1 (8 oz.) package of cream cheese
1. Get 1/2 cup finely chopped sweet onions
1. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped red peppers
1. Get 1 tsp. Grill Mates Garden Vegetable Seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sheik&#39;s Garden Fresh Vegetable Cream Cheese:

1. Bring boxed/wrapped cream cheese out and set on counter for about 15 minutes. We want to get the chill out of it so we can thoroughly mix it effortlessly.
1. Unwrap cream cheese and drop into a mixing bowl. To this, add the prepared onions and red peppers. What I like to do is to actually pour the veggies over the cream cheese and cover it completely. And then throw the seasoning on top and walk away for 5 minutes. The water and juice from the onions and peppers will seep into the cream cheese and further break it down for the mix.
1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
