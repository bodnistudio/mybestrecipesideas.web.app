##### The ingredients needed to make Loaded Potato and Chicken Casserole:

1. Make ready 4 each russet potatoes diced (small)
1. Make ready 1 lb chicken diced (medium)
1. Prepare 1 lb bacon cooked and crumbled
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup shredded cheese
1. Make ready 1/2 cup heavy cream
1. Make ready 4 oz cream cheese
1. Get 4 tbsp butter
