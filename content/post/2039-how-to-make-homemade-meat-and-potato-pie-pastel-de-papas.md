##### The ingredients needed to make Meat and Potato Pie (Pastel de Papas):

1. Take  Potato puree:
1. Make ready 2.2 pounds white potatoes
1. Make ready 8 tablespoons butter (4 oz.) (room temperature)
1. Make ready  Salt and pepper (to taste)
1. Get 100 ml milk (room temperature)
1. Get 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1. Take 2 egg yolks
1. Get 1 egg
1. Prepare  Meat Filling:
1. Make ready 1.8 oz ground beef
1. Take 2 big onions chopped
1. Prepare 1 big red bell pepper chopped
1. Take 2 garlic cloves chopped
1. Take 1 tablespoon paprika
1. Take 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
1. Get 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1. Make ready 1 cube beef bouillon
1. Make ready 8 tablespoons crushed tomatoes
1. Get 4 boiled eggs chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Meat and Potato Pie (Pastel de Papas):

1. Potato puree ingredients:
1. Preparation:
1. Peel the potatoes, wash and cut in fourths
1. In a cacerole put water, potatoes and salt, cook it in high temperature until boils, lower the heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes or until tender, put the potatoes in a colander to drain the water
1. Place it in a large bowl
1. Add butter
1. Mash the potatoes until you achieve the consistency of a puree
1. Add salt and pepper to taste, mix
1. Add milk, mix
1. Add nutmeg
1. Eggs
1. Mix well, set a side
1. Meat Filling ingredients:
1. Preparation:
1. In a small pot, boil the eggs, let it cool and chop, set a side
1. Put oil in a large skillet and heat it to a medium temperature
1. Add onions, red bell peppers and cook it until onions are translucent
1. Add garlic cloves cook it for 2 minutes
1. Add paprika, ground cumin, cayenne pepper
1. Beef bouillon cube (crush), mix well
1. Add the ground beef
1. Mix well, cook it for 10 minutes
1. Add the crushed tomatoes
1. Mix, cook it for 5 minutes, taste to adjust the salt
1. Mix the chopped eggs with the meat filling
1. To arrange the Pie:
1. Preheat the oven at 375 degrees fahrenheit
1. In an oven dish, sprayed with cooking oil
1. Lay 1 cm layer of potato mixture
1. Empty all the meat filling onto the potato layer, spread the mixture evenly
1. Cover it with another layer of potato, wich should be almost equal to the layer of beef filling
1. Make waves on the top of the potato layer with a fork so that it toasts a little
1. Place it in the oven and cook it for 30 minutes on bake and then 4 minutes on broil
