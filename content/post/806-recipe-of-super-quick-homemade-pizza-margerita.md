1. Take maida in a bowl,add salt and yeast mixture. Combine well. Knead to a soft dough. Apply oil on dough and cover. Leave for 60-70 minutes in a warm place.
1. After 70 minutes, dough is fermented well and doubled to it&#39;s size. Take out and punch to remove air. Knead once.

1. Bake in a preheated oven at 200 °c for 25 minutes.
