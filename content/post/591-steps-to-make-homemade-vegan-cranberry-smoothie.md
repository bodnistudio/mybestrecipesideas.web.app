##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Cranberry Smoothie:

1. Make ready 1 Ripe Banana
1. Get 1.5 Tsp Honey (optional)
1. Get 5 soaked and peeled Almonds
1. Prepare 2 Tbsp Dried Cranberries
1. Take 1 tetra pack of soy milk(unsweetened)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vegan Cranberry Smoothie:

1. In a blender jar, put together all ingredients- banana, honey, almonds, cranberries and soy milk.
1. Blend nicely until smooth.
