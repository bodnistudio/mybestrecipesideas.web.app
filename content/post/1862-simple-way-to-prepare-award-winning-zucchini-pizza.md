##### Instructions to make Zucchini Pizza:

1. Grate the 3 zucchinis and then squeeze out the excess juice. I use a paper towel to soak it all up.
1. Salt the zucchini and mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
1. Spray a baking pan and spread the mix in the shape of a circle.
1. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
1. Brush the top with olive oil and broil for 5 minutes.
