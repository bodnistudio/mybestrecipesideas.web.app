##### The ingredients needed to make Tea Cake Cookies:

1. Get 3 cup All-purpose Flour
1. Take 1 1/2 cup Sugar
1. Prepare 1 pinch of Salt
1. Make ready 2 Eggs
1. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract
1. Take 2 tsp Baking powder
1. Get 1 cup Butter, melted




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tea Cake Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
1. Mix all dry ingredients into a large bowl.
1. Melt butter and mix into dry ingredients. Add vanilla and eggs.
1. Mix dough and kneed with hands until well combined and forms ball of dough. (Add more flour if needed)
1. Roll out onto a flour dusted surface. Cut circles out of dough with small coffee cup or any desired shape of cookie cutter.
1. Place on cookie sheet and bake in preheated oven for 8-12 minutes. Or until slightly brown.
1. Decorate if desired after cooling. Store or serve.
