##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken Rolls:

1. Take 400 grams whole wheat flour
1. Make ready 1 chicken sausages/salami
1. Prepare 1 capsicum (cut into small thin strips)
1. Get 1 large onion (cut into thin strips)
1. Make ready 2 inch piece of ginger cut into thin strips
1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
1. Make ready 1/4 garam masala
1. Get 1 tsp Green chilli sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli sauce
1. Take 1 salt to taste
1. Make ready 1 water for dough




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spicy Chicken Rolls:

1. Make a soft dough with whole wheat flour and water.
1. Heat oil in a pan and add onion strips .cook till light pink
1. Add ginger and capsicum to the pan. Cook  for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Add spices and sauce ...add salt. Mix well
1. Chop salami/sausages coarsely and add to the pan...mix and cook for 5min .
1. Roll out the dough and cook slightly on a pan ..now add the filling .roll it up ..cook with little olive oil.
