##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Sashimi on Rice:

1. Get  rice (as much as you want)
1. Prepare  any sashimi (your favorite one-example, tuna, salmon) (cut into slices)
1. Prepare MARINADE: 
1. Get 3 Tbsp soy sauce
1. Take 2 Tbsp mirin
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp sake
1. Make ready TOPPING: 
1. Get to taste wasabi paste (as much as you want)
1. Get to taste grated ginger (as much as you want)
1. Make ready  shiso leaves for topping




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Marinated Sashimi on Rice:

1. To make the marinade, combine mirin and sake in a saucepan, bring to a simmer and then add soy sauce, bring it to boil and immediately turn off the heat. Let it cool.
1. Soak the fish in the marinade for 15~20 mins.
1. Arrange the rice in a bowl and place the shiso leaves over the rice, top with the marinated sashimi slices. Pour the sauce over the rice and sashimi. Garnish with the wasabi paste and grated ginger.
