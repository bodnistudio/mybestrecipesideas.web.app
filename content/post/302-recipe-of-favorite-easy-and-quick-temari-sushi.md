##### The ingredients needed to make Easy and Quick Temari Sushi:

1. Prepare 100 grams Pickled sea bream
1. Take 100 grams Smoked salmon
1. Prepare 1 Cucumber
1. Prepare 450 ml Sushi rice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy and Quick Temari Sushi:

1. Cool the sushi rice and season with whatever you like. For a spalsh of colour, I&#39;ve added some finely chopped cucumber.
1. Cut the salmon and sea bream into bite sized pieces.
1. Place a piece of salmon or seabream on top of a piece of cling film, then add a heaped tablespoon of cooled sushi rice on top.
1. Wrap the sashimi and rice in cling film and mould the sushi. Repeat this step until all of the ingredients are used up.
1. Serve on a plate to finish!
