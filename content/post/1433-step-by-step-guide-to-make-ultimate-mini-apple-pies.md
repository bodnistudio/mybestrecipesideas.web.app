<h3>The ingredients needed to make Mini Apple Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of green apples. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tbsp of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tbsp of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of boxed pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Mini Apple Pies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Make pie crust as stated on box. You can also use pillsbury premade pie crust. Roll out with rolling pin and cut out 20 circles. Big enough for muffin pan.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Peel, core, and cut apples into small pieces..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix apples with flour, sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Grease muffin pan. Lay one circle of pie crust in 10 slots. Put even amount of apples in each section. Lay another circle on top of each. Press together. Slit top crust and bake in preheated 425Â° oven for 15-18 mins..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
