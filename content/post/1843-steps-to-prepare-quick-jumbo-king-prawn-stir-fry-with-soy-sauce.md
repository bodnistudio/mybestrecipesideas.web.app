##### The ingredients needed to make Jumbo King Prawn Stir-fry with Soy Sauce:

1. Make ready 6 jumbo king prawns
1. Get 1/2 tsp corn oil
1. Prepare 1-2 Tbsp light soy sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Jumbo King Prawn Stir-fry with Soy Sauce:

1. Clean and snip off the sharp points.
1. Dry the prawns with a paper towel.
1. Place a fry pan on the hob and heat until it is hot, then turn the heat to low, add the jumbo king prawns and cook for 3-4 minutes. Turn to the other side cook for 3-4 minutes as well.
1. Add the corn oil and fry both sides about 30 seconds, then put in the light soy sauce in it and mix well. Serve at once.
