##### Steps to make My Cucumber and Tomato Pepper Salad:

1. Slice the cucumber &amp; tomato.
1. Slice the white onion into strips.
1. Arrange the salad in a dish
1. In a bowl add the virgin oil &amp; white wine vinegar and mix
1. Sprinkle the salt all over the salad
1. Add the pepper to the oil and stir, then pour all of the mix slowly onto the Salad
1. Put the piece of crisp bread in a bag. Crush it in the bag with a rolling pin or a heavy object.
1. Then sprinkle it over the salad, cover and leave for an hour before serving.
