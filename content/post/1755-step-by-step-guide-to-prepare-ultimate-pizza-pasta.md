##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Pasta:

1. Take 1 lb. hamburger
1. Prepare 1 yellow onion, diced
1. Get 1 Can black olives, sliced
1. Take 1 Tablespoon onion powder
1. Get 1 Tablespoon garlic powder
1. Make ready to taste salt and black pepper,
1. Prepare 1 Pkg. sliced pepperoni, diced
1. Prepare to taste grated parmesan / romano cheese
