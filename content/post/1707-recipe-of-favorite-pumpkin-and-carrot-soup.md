##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin and Carrot Soup:

1. Prepare 250 gms Pumpkin skin removed and chopped
1. Take 2 carrots peeled and chopped
1. Get 5-6 pods garlic
1. Make ready 1/2 inch ginger
1. Make ready 2-3 spring onions chopped
1. Prepare 2 tomatoes chopped
1. Make ready 1/2 inch turmeric root (optional)
1. Prepare 1/4 cup coconut cream
1. Make ready 2-3 tbsp pumpkin seeds/ mix seeds
1. Make ready 2 tbsp coconut flakes for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pumpkin and Carrot Soup:

1. Pressure cook all the vegetables with salt and turmeric root for up to 2 whistles and 10 minutes on low flame.
1. Dry roast the seeds. Keep aside.
1. Now, when the veggies are done and cool, Remove the tomato skin and blend everything to a smooth puree.
1. Now heat again, add coconut cream and pepper.
1. Garnish with roasted seeds, coconut flakes and Serve hot.
