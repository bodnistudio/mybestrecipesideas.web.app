##### The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Lo Mein:

1. Get 1/2 cup chicken broth
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1. Take 2 tablespoons sesame oil
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons cornstarch
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1. Prepare 2 cloves minced garlic
1. Get 2 tablespoons minced ginger
1. Get 1 cup mushrooms sliced
1. Take 1 cup onions sliced
1. Prepare 1 cup carrots sliced
1. Get 1 cup snap peas halved
1. Prepare 1 box cooked noodles (I used linguine)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vegetable Lo Mein:

1. In a medium bowl combine broth, sesame oil, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Mix well and sit to the side
1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet.
1. Add garlic and ginger to the pan
1. Add mushrooms, carrots, onions, and peas; stir fry until tender
1. Add noodles and sauce until well blended
1. Enjoy!!
