##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Inarizushi:

1. Take 5 Aburaage
1. Prepare 200 ml Dashi stock
1. Make ready 2 1/2 tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 2 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp Sake




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Inarizushi:

1. Cut the aburaage in half.
1. Put the dashi stock, sugar, soy sauce, sake and the cut aburaage into a heatproof bowl, and microwave for 2 minutes 30 seconds at 700 W.
1. Flip the aburaage over, and microwave for another 2 minutes. Leave to cool naturally, ideally for at least 30 minutes (you can make it in advance and leave it to rest overnight).
1. Add beni-shouga red pickled ginger to sushi rice. This is the standard version of inari sushi.
1. We often add a topping to the inari sushi. I added quail eggs, gingko nuts, ginger, yama-gobo (pokeweed root) pickles here. Great in bentos too.
