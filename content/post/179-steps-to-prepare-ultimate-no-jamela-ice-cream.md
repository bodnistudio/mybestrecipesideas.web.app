##### Instructions to make NO JAMELA Ice Cream:

1. Take bananas, chop them and freeze them for 3 hours.
1. Remove from the fridge, put in the mixer grinder and grind, just add little milk and castor sugar as per your taste and churn again.
1. Do not over churn else it will form liquid.
1. Serve with chopped nuts.
1. Variation: Oreo biscuits can be added, milk instead of plain, add little kesar and then add, coffee can be an option too.
