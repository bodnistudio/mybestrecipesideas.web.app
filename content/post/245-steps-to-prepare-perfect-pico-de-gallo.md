##### The ingredients needed to make Pico de Gallo:

1. Take 3-4 Roma tomatoes, seeds and pulp removed, chopped into 1/4&#34; pieces, about 1.5 cups total
1. Make ready 1/2-1 medium onion (white, red, or sweet yellow all work great), chopped into 1/8&#34; pieces, 3/4 to 1 cup total
1. Get 1/2 cup chopped jalapeño or serrano chilies (Jalapeños are generally milder and a little juicier.)
1. Get 1.5-2 Tablespoons fresh lime juice
1. Prepare 3/4-1 teaspoon kosher salt
1. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
1. Take 1/4-1/3 cup loosely packed fresh chopped cilantro
1. Prepare  Optional:
1. Make ready 1-2 teaspoons lime zest - a zippy and refreshing addition. Make sure to zest your limes before juicing them
1. Prepare 1 very small clove garlic, grated (about 1/4 teaspoon) for that extra savory oomph
1. Prepare 1 Tablespoon vegetable oil (unconventional, but gives extra body and helps keep all the flavors in suspension)
1. Get 1/2 teaspoon to 1 teaspoon dried oregano or 1 Tablespoon finely chopped fresh

##### Steps to make Pico de Gallo:

1. In a mixing bowl (or other some such large bowl - you want room to toss the ingredients together), combine all the ingredients except oil and gently fold until ingredients are thoroughly combined and evenly distributed. Adjust seasoning if needed before adding oil and giving a few more gentle folds.
1. Enjoy! :)
