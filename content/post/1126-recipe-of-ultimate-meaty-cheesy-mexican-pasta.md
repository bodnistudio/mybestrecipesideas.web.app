##### The ingredients needed to make Meaty Cheesy Mexican Pasta:

1. Take 1 medium onion, small dice
1. Make ready 1 green bell pepper, small dice
1. Get 1 lb sausage
1. Make ready 1 can Rotel
1. Take 1 can (15 oz) whole tomatoes
1. Get 1 can (15 oz) whole kernel corn, drained
1. Get 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
1. Prepare 2 tsp Cumin
1. Prepare 2 tsp garlic
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp chili powder
1. Get to taste Salt &amp; pepper
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups shredded Pepperjack cheese
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups Monterey Jack cheese
1. Prepare 1 lb box of elbow type macaroni, cooked reserving 1 cup pasta water
1. Prepare 2 Tbsp vegetable oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Meaty Cheesy Mexican Pasta:

1. Dice onion and pepper. Heat large skillet with sides on medium high, add 2 Tbsp vegetable oil, diced onions and peppers. Sauté 5 -6 minutes or until soft.
1. While veggies are cooking, slice sausage in 1/2&#34; pieces. Add to skillet stirring into veggies. Add spices, stirring to mix.
1. Next add in the Rotel and canned tomatoes (breaking them up) juice and all, stir to remove brown on bottom of skillet. Add drained black beans and corn, stir.
1. Turn to medium low heat and simmer while pasta is cooking.
1. Cook pasta according to directions. Drain reserving 1 cup pasta water. Add pasta and water to skillet stirring to incorporate.
1. Add in all the cheese, stirring until mixed and creamy. Enjoy!
