<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Peel the pumpkin and cut in to small pieces..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Take oil in a pan and add pumpkin pieces to it and mix well. Cover with a lid and let it cook for almost 1:30 hours. You have to saute it after every 10 mins..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Now add sugar to pumpkin and mix well. Now pumpkin will leave it&#39;s water and let it dry. Switch off the flame..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
