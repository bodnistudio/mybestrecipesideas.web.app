##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage Balls:

1. Get 1 lb sage sausage
1. Take 1 1/2 cup baking mix (such as Bisquick)
1. Take 2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

##### Instructions to make Sausage Balls:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Knead until all flour and cheese is incorporated into sausage
1. Roll into balls (slightly smaller than a golf ball) &amp; place on ungreased cookie sheet
1. Bake for 20-25 mintues (until sausage is done)
