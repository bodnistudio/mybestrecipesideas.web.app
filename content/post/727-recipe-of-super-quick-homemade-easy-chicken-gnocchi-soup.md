##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Gnocchi Soup:

1. Prepare 10 oz Frozen chopped spinach
1. Take 1/4 cup chopped onion
1. Take 1 can Cream of celery soup
1. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 lb Dry gnocchi
1. Prepare 1 cup matchstick carrots
1. Make ready 2 5 oz cans of chicken
1. Prepare 1 can Half and half
1. Get 1 quart chicken broth




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Chicken Gnocchi Soup:

1. Place spinach, carrots,  onion and cans of chicken into  large saucepan.
1. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a slight boil.
1. Add gnocchi and cook for 2 minutes
1. Add garlic powder and can of celery soup.
1. Fill soup can with half and half and turn pot down to slow simmer.
1. Soup is ready when carrots are tender and gnocchi is cooked.
