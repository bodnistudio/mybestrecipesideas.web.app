##### Instructions to make Easy Pan Roasted Potatoes:

1. Slice potatoes in half or quarters - this will determine your cooking time.
1. Heat a pan on med heat.  Coat with oil (EVOO).  When hot (shimmering) add salt.  Add potatoes to heated oil and salt.  Cook for approx. 10 mins
1. Cover and lower heat.  Cook an additional 10 mins.
