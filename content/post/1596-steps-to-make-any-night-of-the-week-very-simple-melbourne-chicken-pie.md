<h3>The ingredients needed to make Very Simple Melbourne Chicken Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of chicken breast. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of chopped onion. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of sliced button mushroom. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of diced potato. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tbsp of cooking oil / olive oil. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 of egg yolk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3 packages of readymade pastry. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 can of Campbell mushroom soup. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Very Simple Melbourne Chicken Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			preheat oven to 200&#39;C.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine onion and potato in the pan and cook for few minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add chicken breast and let it cook for few minutes morr.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add sliced mushroom and Campbell mushroom soup with pinch of salt and let it cool down for a while.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place ready made pastry in the pan and pour the cooked ingredients and cover the top with another pastry and brush egg yolk on top.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 20 minutes in 200&#39;C.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
