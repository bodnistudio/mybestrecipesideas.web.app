##### The ingredients needed to make Super Simple Chicken Steak:

1. Get 2 large skinless boneless chicken breast
1. Get 1 pinch salt and pepper
1. Get 5 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
1. Prepare 1/4 cup oyster sauce
1. Prepare 1/4 cup sweet soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp oregano
1. Prepare 1 tsp freshly grinded black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Super Simple Chicken Steak:

1. Clean chicken under running tap water
1. Rub chicken with salt, pepper and lemon juice. Lemon juice is optional
1. Mix sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce, and herbs
1. Marinade the chicken. An hour is enough. No more than 6 hours
1. Cook the chicken with the marinade in a stickless pan until caramelized
1. Serve with rice or potato and salad
