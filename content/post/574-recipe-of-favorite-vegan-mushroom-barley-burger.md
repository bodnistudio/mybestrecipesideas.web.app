##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Mushroom Barley Burger:

1. Prepare 2 cups minced mushrooms
1. Take 1 minced red onion
1. Prepare 1/2 cup minced parsley
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups cooked pearl barley
1. Take 2 tablespoons miso paste
1. Get 2 tablespoons tomato paste
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups flour
1. Get 2 tablespoons minced garlic
1. Get to taste Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Vegan Mushroom Barley Burger:

1. Preheat oven to 400 while you prep ingredients.
1. Mix ingredients, form into burgers, and place on generously sprayed baking sheet.
1. Bake for 20 minutes at 400, then finish on a hot skillet in vegan butter to get that seared crust.
1. Dress with your favorite burger accoutrements.
