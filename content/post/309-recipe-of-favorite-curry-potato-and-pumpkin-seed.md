##### The ingredients needed to make Curry Potato And Pumpkin Seed:

1. Take  POTATO AND PUMPKIN SALAD
1. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
1. Take 1/2 tbsp cumin seed
1. Prepare 1 large onion dice
1. Prepare 1 tbsp curry powder
1. Get 10 curry leaf fine cut
1. Take 5 medium potato cut in small cube
1. Get 1/2 cup ready to eat pumpkin seed
1. Prepare 2 tsp salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Curry Potato And Pumpkin Seed:

1. in pan with oil put cumin seed low heat till it start to fragrant and crack
1. add onion , curry leaf and curry powder sautee for 2minute
1. add potato and pumpkin seed mix well then add salt in low heat
1. add butter and mix well then cover for 1minute stiring every now and then prevent burn
1. serve hot or cold
