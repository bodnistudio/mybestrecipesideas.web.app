##### The ingredients needed to make My Asian coleslaw salad:

1. Get 1 (16 oz) bag coleslaw
1. Make ready 1/4 large purple onion, thin sliced
1. Get 1 pkg chicken flavored Ramen noodles (3oz)
1. Take 1/3 C apple cider vinegar
1. Take 1 splash rice vinegar
1. Prepare 1/2-1 C sugared holiday pecans (recipe in my recipes), chopped
1. Make ready 1/2 C sunflower oil
1. Make ready 1-2 Tbsp dark brown sugar

##### Instructions to make My Asian coleslaw salad:
