1. Get 2 tbsp mayo

##### Instructions to make Tuna Mayo Boats:

1. Cut the pepper through the natural grooves to create boat shapes. You&#39;ll need to remove the top and discard the seeds. You can make big boats, or cut each pepper section in half to make smaller boats.
1. In a bowl, mix the tuna with the mayonnaise and season the mix with salt and pepper.
1. Spoon the tuna mayo into the boats.
