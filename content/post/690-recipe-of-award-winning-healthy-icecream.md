##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Icecream:

1. Take 1 Banana
1. Get 1 tsp Cinnamom
1. Get 1/4 tsp Olive oil
1. Make ready 15-20 Almonds
1. Take  Honey as per taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Healthy Icecream:

1. Roast almonds.
1. Grind almonds and make fine powder.
1. Same time add oil slowly and keep churning.
1. Now almond butter is ready.
1. Slice banana and keep in a bag.
1. Keep bag in freezer for an hour.
1. After one hour add chilled banana slice in a blender.
1. Make creamy texture and add 1 Tbsp almond butter, cinnamon powder,honey.
1. Run blender again.
1. Healthy Ice cream is ready to serve!!
