##### The ingredients needed to make Gobber Pea Stew/Soup:

1. Make ready 1 pound green/raw peanuts
1. Make ready 2 quarts water
1. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery
1. Make ready 1 medium onion chopped
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon salt
1. Make ready 1 large red bell pepper
1. Prepare 1 cup peanut butter
1. Prepare 1-1/2 pound chicken thighs skin on and bone in
1. Get 15 ounce diced tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1. Take 1 tablespoon minced garlic
1. Make ready 1/4 cup chicken broth cooled
1. Make ready 1 Tablespoon cornstarch




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Gobber Pea Stew/Soup:

1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
1. Add water, chicken, and spices to a pot. Bring to a boil.
1. Shell the peanuts
1. Chop the celery, onion, and bell pepper
1. Add the peanuts to the chicken.
1. When the chicken is done remove the chicken.
1. Take out the bones and skin.
1. Spread the chicken skins out on a pan add some salt to taste. Bake in oven till crispy.
1. Let cool
1. Slice the skins into bits
1. Add peanut butter and stir in well
1. When 20 minutes have past mix the cornstarch and chicken broth and add to soup to thicken.
1. Simmer 10 minutes stirring occasionally then let rest for 10 minutes.
1. Serve garnish with crisp chicken skins. I hope you enjoy!
