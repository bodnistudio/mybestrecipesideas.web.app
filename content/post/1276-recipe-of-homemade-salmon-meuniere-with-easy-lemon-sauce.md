##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Meunière with Easy Lemon Sauce:

1. Take 2 pieces Salmon
1. Take 1 Salt and pepper
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Flour
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Butter
1. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
1. Make ready 1/2 lemon (1~11/2 tablespoons Lemon juice (pokka Lemon)
1. Take 2 tbsp White wine




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Salmon Meunière with Easy Lemon Sauce:

1. Season the salmon with salt and pepper (Set aside for 10 ~ 15 minutes, if possible.)
1. Coat the salmon evenly with flour.
1. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan. Shake off the excess flour and fry the salmon until golden brown on one side.
1. Turn the salmon over, cover with a lid, and cook for 2 minutes over medium to low heat.
1. Take out the salmon, add the wine and lemon juice to the liquid left in the pan, and bring to a boil to make the sauce.
1. Place the salmon on serving plates, pour the sauce over it, and it&#39;s ready.
1. A user told me that it&#39;s also good to use katakuriko instead of flour.
