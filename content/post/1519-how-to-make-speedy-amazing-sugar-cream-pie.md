<h3>The ingredients needed to make Amazing Sugar Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3/4 cup of white sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 cup of half-and-half cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of whipping cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 stick of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Amazing Sugar Cream Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In sauce pan combine first 4 ingredients. Bring to boil..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In another sauce pan combine brown sugar and cornstarch..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Gradually whisk in hot mixture into brown sugar mixture..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add butter..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cook over medium heat, whisk constantly, 5-10 minutes or until thick..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Simmer 1 minute and stir in vanilla..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into par baked pie shell  and sprinkle with cinnamon..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 375 for 25 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
