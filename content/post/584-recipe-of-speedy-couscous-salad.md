##### The ingredients needed to make 🍝Couscous Salad🍝:

1. Get 1 cup dried couscous
1. Get 1 cup boiling water
1. Make ready 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper or to taste
1. Make ready 2 cloves garlic (grated)
1. Prepare 3 tbsp lemon juice
1. Make ready 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1. Get 1 cup grated carrot
1. Take 1 cup canned chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
1. Take Handful fresh parsley (chopped)
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1. Prepare 1/2 cup cucumber (chopped)
1. Prepare 1/2 cup fresh pomegranate




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make 🍝Couscous Salad🍝:

1. In a large bowl whisk the salt, black pepper, grated garlic, lemon juice and olive oil with the boiling water.
1. Pour over the dried cous cous and. Cover with the plate and leave to stand for 15 minutes. Once soaked and cooled slightly, fluff up the grains with a fork.
1. Add the chickpeas, grated carrot, cucumber,pomegranate and fresh parsley give everything a good stir and serve.
