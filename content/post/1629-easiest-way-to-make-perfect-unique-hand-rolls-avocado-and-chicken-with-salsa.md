##### The ingredients needed to make Unique Hand Rolls Avocado and Chicken with Salsa:

1. Take 350 grams Uncooked white rice
1. Take 2 tbsp Sushi vinegar
1. Make ready 1 fillet ● Chicken (thigh or breast)
1. Make ready 1 dash ● Salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ● Sweet chilli sauce
1. Get 1 Avocado
1. Make ready 1 tsp Lemon juice
1. Prepare 1 Thin stripped cheese
1. Take 1 Mayonnaise
1. Get 1 Toasted nori seaweed
1. Make ready  To make the salsa sauce:
1. Make ready 1 Tomato, finely minced
1. Get 1/6 Finely chopped onions
1. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Take 1 pinch Black pepper
1. Take 1 tsp Ketchup
1. Prepare 1 tsp Honey
1. Get 2 tsp Olive oil

##### Instructions to make Unique Hand Rolls Avocado and Chicken with Salsa:

1. Put the ● ingredients in a plastic bag, then rub in and let sit for 1 hour (or overnight) in the refrigerator. See. -  - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/148319-sauteed-chicken-marinated-in-sweet-chili-sauce
1. Cook the rice to use as sushi rice. Mix in the sushi vinegar to make sushi rice.
1. Put the salsa ingredients in a bowl, then mix.
1. Place chicken skin side down in a heated frying pan. Pan fry until crisp, turn it over, reduce heat to low, then cover and cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Remove the lid, turn up the heat, pan fry both sides, then remove from the pan. When it cools, chop into bite-sized pieces.
1. Slice the avocado into 1-cm sticks, then squeeze lemon juice over the top.
1. Place the sushi rice, chicken, avocado, and drained salsa sauce on a piece of roasted nori seaweed, sprinkle with cheese or mayonnaise to taste, then roll it up.
