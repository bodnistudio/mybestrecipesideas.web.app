<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Crunch Cake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1-15 OZ of can pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1-12 OZ of can evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 4 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 cups of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tsp of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 of ish box of yellow cake mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 Cup of pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 Cup (2 sticks) of melted butter. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Crunch Cake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with non stick spray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large bowl beat together pumpkin, milk, eggs, sugar, and spices until well combined. Pour into prepared dish..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle the top with yellow cake mix. I used half a box but if you prefer a more cake like texture you can use more..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle with chopped pecans..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour the melted butter evenly over the top..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 60-70 minutes (I did 70) or until the top is lightly browned and the custard is set..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Serve warm or cold with ice cream (optional). Store in the refrigerator..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
