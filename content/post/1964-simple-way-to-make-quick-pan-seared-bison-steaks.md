##### The ingredients needed to make Pan Seared Bison Steaks:

1. Get 2 Bison Steaks
1. Get 1 Sea Salt
1. Take 1 Fresh Cracked Pepper
1. Make ready 1 Canola Oil
1. Get 1 unsalted butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pan Seared Bison Steaks:

1. Take steaks out of refrigerator 30 min before cooking. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
1. Pat the steaks dry with a paper towel
1. Coat the steaks with sea salt and peper.
1. Place enough canola oil in the bottom of your stainelss steel pan to coat the bottom, and place the pan on medium high heat.
1. Sear steaks on each side for one minuet. Once seared, melt a small amount of unsalted butter and seasonings in the side of the pan and let melt. Then spoon the butter mixture onto the steaks.
1. Place pan directly into oven and cook for 4 to 5 minuets.
1. Once finished cooking, let steaks rest for 10 minuets before cutting into the steaks. (Rest means to let them sit - so that the juices do not run out of the seaks and they become dry)
