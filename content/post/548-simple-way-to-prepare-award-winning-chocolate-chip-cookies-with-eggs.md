##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies (With Eggs):

1. Prepare 1/2 Cup Butter/ margarine
1. Get 1/2 Cup Icing sugar (sifted)
1. Prepare 1 Cup Self raising flour
1. Prepare 1/4 Cups Chocolate chips
1. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
1. Get 1 Egg lightly beaten




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chocolate Chip Cookies (With Eggs):

1. Mix sugar with butter/margarine. I used melted to quicken the process.
1. Add egg. I used half on it - then vanilla essence.
1. Fold in your flour.
1. Add chocolate chips.
1. If you find it too soft set it in the fridge for about 10 -15mins - other proceed to shape as desired.
1. Bake for 15-18/20mins till brownish.
1. Let me cool and serve!
