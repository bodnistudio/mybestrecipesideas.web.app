##### Steps to make Spanish vegan tortilla:

1. Heat 1 tbsp of the olive oil in a large medium pan and add the chopped onions, chopped red bell pepper and potatoes. Cook on a low heat for about 20 minutes until the potatoes are cooked through
1. Meanwhile, mix the chickpea flour and water together in a bowl with the salt and pepper
1. Once the potatoes and onions are cooked, add them to the chickpea mix and stir gently to combine.
1. Add the chickpea mixture and cook on a medium heat for 5-8 minutes until the bottom is cooked.
