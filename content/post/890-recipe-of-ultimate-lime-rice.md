##### The ingredients needed to make Lime Rice:

1. Take 4 cups cooked rice (preferably long grain or basmati rice)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil (preferably sesame seed oil)
1. Make ready 1 tsp mustard seeds
1. Get 1 pinch asafoetida
1. Make ready 3 green chilies, thinly sliced
1. Take 2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)
1. Get 1/4 cup curry leaves
1. Take 1/2 c freshly squeezed lime juice
1. Prepare 1 tbsp lime zest
1. Make ready 1 inch cinnamon stick
1. Take 1 tsp cardamom powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1. Prepare 1 inch long fresh ginger, thinly sliced
1. Get 1 cup cilantro chopped
1. Take 1 cup cashew nuts (dry roasted)
1. Prepare to taste salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Lime Rice:

1. Pop mustard seeds in the hot oil in the skillet over medium heat, about a minute or two. Add asafetida, fennel seeds green chilies, garlic and ginger; continue cooking 1-2 minutes over medium heat. Remove from the stove.
1. Add the lemon zest, juice, turmeric, cinnamon, and cardamom. Salt to taste. Add the cooked rice. Mix thoroughly with a wooden spoon. Let the rice sit at room temperature prior to serving for 30 minutes.
1. Garnish with cilantro and nuts. Serve at room temperature. Garnish with cilantro and nuts. Serve at room temperature. - Serve with vegetable chips and pappads
