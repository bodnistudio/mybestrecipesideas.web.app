##### Instructions to make Steak &amp; Ale Pie:

1. Cut the brisket into large chunks and fry in a hot pan until caramelised and put to one side
1. Cut the onion &amp; garlic and add to the same pan, fry on a low heat for 5 minutes. Add the beef back into the pan &amp; add the flour. Stir until the flour has coated the pan.
1. Add the Guinness, stock pot, thyme &amp; season with salt and pepper. Bring to the boil. Transfer to slow cooker on low heat for 8 hours or high heat for 5 hours. Alternatively add to oven at 140 for 3 hours. Cook until the beef is tender and falls apart with a fork.
1. Separate the beef &amp; cooking liquid. Pull the beef, leaving a mixture of large chunks &amp; smaller chunks. Add half the liquid to the pulled beef.
1. Let the mixture cool before adding the pastry. Whisk up an egg with a fork in a cup then brush the pastry. Add to a preheated over at 180 degrees and cook for 40 minutes or until pastry is golden brown.
