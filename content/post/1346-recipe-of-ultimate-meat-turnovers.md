##### The ingredients needed to make Meat Turnovers:

1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
1. Get 1 envelope (1 oz) onion soup, dry mix
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1. Make ready 1 package refrigerated crescent rolls




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Meat Turnovers:

1. Fry the ground beef, drain the fat.
1. Add the dry onion soup mix and cheese. Mix well.
1. Cut crescent rolls in half lengthwise.
1. Spoon meat mixture into the cut crescent roll. Pinch roll dough over the meat mixture.
1. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350°F for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
