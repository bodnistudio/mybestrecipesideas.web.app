##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies:

1. Get 125 gm White flour
1. Prepare 1 tbsp gram flour
1. Get 2 tbsp Sugar powder
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking powder
1. Prepare 2/4 cup Butter or ghee
1. Get 1/4 cup Milk
1. Get as needed Almonds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cookies:

1. Take a bowl and add white flour and gram flour in it,now add baking powder salt it it and mix it
1. Now take another bowl and add butter or butter and sugar powder in it and mix it well.
1. Now mix both mixtures together and add milk and make dough.
1. Now you can use cookies cutter to shape it and garnish with almonds
1. Now take a pan and preheat for 10min.
1. Take a plate grease it well and put the cookies on plate and put the plate in pan and cover it with lid and bake it at medium flame
