##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Artichoke Pie:

1. Make ready  container of cottage cheese
1. Get  garlic, herb spreadable cheese
1. Get  eggs, lightly beaten
1. Get  chopped canned artichokes
1. Take  fresh mushrooms

##### Instructions to make Spinach and Artichoke Pie:

1. grease a 9 inch pie pan with cooking spray
1. preheat oven to 375
1. Wring spinach dry
1. in large bowl, whisk together the ricotta cheese, garlic herb cheese, and eggs.
1. add spinach, artichokes, and mushrooms. salt and pepper to taste. stir to combine.
1. pour into prepared pan and bake until golden brown. about 45 minutes.
