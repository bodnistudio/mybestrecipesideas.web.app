##### The ingredients needed to make Chivito Canadiense (Uruguayan steak sandwich- Canadian style):

1. Get 1/4 lb flank steak pounded into one thin slice
1. Prepare 2 slice mozzarella cheese
1. Prepare 2 slice ham
1. Prepare 2 slice bacon
1. Prepare 2 lettuce
1. Take 2 slice ripe tomatoes
1. Take 1 egg
1. Prepare 1 soft sandwich or burger bun
1. Take 1/2 white onion




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chivito Canadiense (Uruguayan steak sandwich- Canadian style):

1. Pound the steak until its as flat as possible then season with a little salt.
1. Fry the steak in a large pan with vegetable oil for about 3 minutes each side (add the ham and cheese in the last 2 min of cooking).  In the same pan fry the bacon and egg. Fry the onion until it&#39;s caramelized (about 4 min)
1. Open the bun add mayonnaise or mustard then add the lettuce and tomato then the steak with ham and cheese finally topping with the onion, egg and bacon.
