##### The ingredients needed to make Coffee Cookie:

1. Take 2-3 oz sugar
1. Prepare 4-5 oz butter
1. Prepare 12 oz flour
1. Prepare 6 gram coffee
1. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
1. Get 1 egg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Coffee Cookie:

1. Mix flour and butter until it cream perfectly, around 15 minutes
1. Add flour, coffee, baking soda and egg. Mix perfectly
1. Make ball from the dough
1. Put to freezer, for a night
1. Wait 20 minutes after taking it out from freezer and before baking
1. Bake for 40 min at 170 C
