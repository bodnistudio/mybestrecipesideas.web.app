##### Instructions to make Oreo Ice cream:

1. Very important step, if you are not using whip cream than keep Amul fresh cream, mixing bowl and whisking blades inside the fridge at least for 30mnts
1. Firstly crush the Oreo biscuits into small parts in a ziplock baad. Keep 2-3 biscuits for garnishing. Now whip the cream till stiff peaks. Add condensed milk and again whip for a second to incorporate it. Finally add the crushed Oreo biscuits and mix gently.
1. Pour the mixture in a rectangular or any bowl. Garnish with the broken chunks of Oreo biscuits. Tap the bowl to remove any air bubbles formed. Cover on top with cling or aluminium foil and keep in a freezer for at least 6hrs or overnight
