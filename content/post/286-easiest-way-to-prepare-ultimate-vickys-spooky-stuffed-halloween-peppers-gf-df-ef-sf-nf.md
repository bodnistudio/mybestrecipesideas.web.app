##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Spooky Stuffed Halloween Peppers, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Get 4 yellow bell peppers
1. Get 180 grams dry quinoa
1. Prepare 150 grams chopped onion
1. Make ready 100 grams chopped mushroom
1. Make ready 2 clove minced garlic
1. Get 2 tbsp chopped parsley
1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 4 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
1. Make ready 480 ml vegetable stock
1. Make ready  salt &amp; pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Vickys Spooky Stuffed Halloween Peppers, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Preheat the oven to gas 4 / 180C / 350째F
1. Cut a neat lid off the peppers and carefully deseed keeping the peppers whole, then carve a pumpkin face out
1. Brush the inside with oil, sprinkle with salt &amp; pepper and bake for 25 - 30 minutes
1. Meanwhile, fry off the onion until almost translucent before adding the mushrooms and cooking a further 2 minutes
1. Stir in the garlic, then add the parsley, quinoa and stock, seasoning to taste
1. Reduce heat and simmer gently until quinoa is light and fluffy
1. When the peppers are ready, stuff them with the quinoa and put their lids back on
1. You can use small squash for this too. Bake at gas 5 / 190C / 375째F for an hour for them
