<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Cranberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of apples(Thinly sliced). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/2 cup of chopped cranberries. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready Tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tbsp of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of hot water. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 (9 inch) of ready made pie crust or you can make your own. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of My crumb topping recipe. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Apple Cranberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Stir into a pot, over medium heat, the apples, cranberries, sugar, cinnamon, flour and water. Cook for about 15 minutes together..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			As it is cooling, stir in the vanilla extract. Allow to cool for about 15 to 20 minutes. After cooling, pour the pie mix into a pie crust..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put the crumb topping on the pie, then put the pie into the oven for 30 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place on a cooling rack and allow to cool for about 10 to 15 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
