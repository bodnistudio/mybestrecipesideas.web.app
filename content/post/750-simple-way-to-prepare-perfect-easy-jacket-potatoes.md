##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Jacket Potatoes:

1. Make ready 4 large potatoes, washed
1. Take 1 medium brown onion, peeled-1/2&#39;d &amp; sliced
1. Make ready 1 cup Sliced bacon
1. Take 3/4 cup Sour cream
1. Make ready 3/4 cup Grated cheddar cheese
1. Take 1 Sprinkle garlic salt
1. Get 1 Cracked black pepper
1. Prepare 1 Olive oil
1. Get 1 stick Green onion, sliced




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Jacket Potatoes:

1. Pre-heat oven to 180*C.
1. In a large mixing bowl- throw in spuds and coat with olive oil and garlic salt. Transfer to oven and bake until skin is crunchy and starts to wrinkle and centre is soft. Remove from oven.
1. Meanwhile- fry onion and bacon until bacon is cooked and onion is slightly caramelised. Remove and set aside.
1. Now slice spuds lengthways (not all the way through) so you can add your filling. Bacon and onion first- then sour cream- grated cheese on top and seasoning.
1. Return to the oven until cheese has melted. Sprinkle with sliced green onion and serve. Goes really well as a side with pork chops- or a nice piece of beef steak. Enjoy!
1. Serves 4 sides. More people over for dinner? Just cook a few more spuds and up the ingredients.
