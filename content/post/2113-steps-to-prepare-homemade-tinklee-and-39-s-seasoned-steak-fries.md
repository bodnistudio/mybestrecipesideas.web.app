##### The ingredients needed to make Tinklee&#39;s Seasoned Steak Fries:

1. Prepare 1 packages frozen steak fries
1. Take 2 tbsp vegetable oil
1. Make ready 1 envelope lipton&#39;s savory herb &amp;garlic seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tinklee&#39;s Seasoned Steak Fries:

1. preheat oven to 375F; line baking pan with liner if you use them (I use Reynolds new baking liner, comes in a roll like the foil does, saves wear on your pan and faster/easier clean up)
1. place steak fries in a pile on the baking sheet
1. pour the oil over the fries and mix them all around until completely coated, every single one
1. open seasoning packet and shake over fries, remix until all fries are coated
1. spread out flat on baking sheet, and bake for 22-27mins
