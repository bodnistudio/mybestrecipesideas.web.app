##### The ingredients needed to make Beer Can Chicken:

1. Make ready  4-3-2-1 Rub
1. Make ready 4 tbsp Kosher Salt
1. Take 3 tbsp brown sugar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp sweet Paprika
1. Make ready 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
1. Take 4 lb chicken
1. Get 1/2 can beer




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Beer Can Chicken:

1. Combine all dry ingredients to form the rub.
1. Wash your chicken &amp; cavity. Pat dry.
1. Rub that bird.
1. Place half full beer can in the cavity. Cook on medium-high indirect heat. Cook until internal temp reads 165. Let the chicken rest 10 mins before serving.
