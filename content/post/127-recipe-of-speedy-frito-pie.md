##### The ingredients needed to make Frito Pie:

1. Make ready 1 lb. hamburger
1. Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
1. Get 2 (15.5 oz) cans pinto beans
1. Take 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
1. Make ready 1 (14.5 oz) can petite diced tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 package chili seasoning or do it yourself:
1. Get  chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper cumin
1. Take 1 bag Frito chips
1. Get  Cheddar cheese and sour cream for topping




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Frito Pie:

1. Drain the beans and tomatoes; set aside.
1. Brown hamburger along with onions until thoroughly cooked; drain and return to pot.
1. Add in canned ingredients and seasonings. Simmer on medium low for 5 minutes.
1. Make a bed of Fritos. Top with chili mixture followed by cheese and sour cream. Enjoy!
