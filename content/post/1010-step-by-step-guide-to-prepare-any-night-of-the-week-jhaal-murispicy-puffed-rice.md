##### Steps to make Jhaal Muri(Spicy Puffed Rice):

1. Take a mixing bowl. Add in tomato, cucumber, coconut, onion, green chilli and coriander leaves.
1. Next add salt, red chilli powder, amchur powder, chaat masala and lemon juice. Give them a good stir.
1. Now add puffed rice, chanachur and mustard oil. Toss them all.
