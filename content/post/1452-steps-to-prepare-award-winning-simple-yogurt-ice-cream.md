##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. Take 100 ml Heavy cream
1. Prepare 200 grams Plain yogurt
1. Take 120 grams Your favourite jam




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simple Yogurt Ice Cream:

1. Whip heavy cream with a hand mixer until peaks form. In the summer, place the mixing bowl into a larger bowl with water and ice to keep cool.
1. Add yogurt and jam to Step 1, and mix well, then taste. Mix with a beater from the hand mixer by hand to reduce the amount of washing up later.
1. Pour the mixture into a container, and freeze until hardened.
1. Serve in a bowl, and it&#39;s done.
