##### The ingredients needed to make Millet,dry fruits and chocolate icecream:

1. Prepare 100 grm millet flour
1. Take 250 ml fresh cream
1. Make ready 1/2 cup condensed milk
1. Get 2 tbsp already melted chocolate
1. Prepare 4 tbsp dry fruits chopped(optional like almond,cashew,walnuts)
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Chocolate sauce

##### Steps to make Millet,dry fruits and chocolate icecream:

1. First roast millet flour in a pan till pleasant smell
1. Whisk cream till soft peak
1. Take condensed milk in a bowl add roasted millet flour and 2 tbsp already melted Chocolate,half of dry fruits and mix well and prepare a mixture
1. Now add gradually condensed milk mixture in whisked cream and mix lightly,now transfer in air tight container and keep in deep freeze to set
1. After 30 minutes take out the box from freezer churned the and keep again in freezer,.Do this process atleast 2 times for better result
1. After 2 churned finally keep the icecream box in zip lock pouch and place in deep freezer for 4 to 5 hours to set
1. When completely set serve with remaining dry fruits and pour chocolate sauce over it
1. Now millet and chocolate icecream is ready to serve
