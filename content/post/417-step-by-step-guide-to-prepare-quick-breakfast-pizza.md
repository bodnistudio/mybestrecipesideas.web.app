##### The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pizza:

1. Take 1 Pillsbury Pizza Dough
1. Prepare 1 Bob Evans Sausage Roll
1. Prepare 12 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1. Take 2 medium Russet Potatoes
1. Prepare 6 large Eggs
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Milk
1. Prepare 4 tbsp butter
1. Make ready 1 can White Gravy




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Breakfast Pizza:

1. Preheat Oven to 415
1. Shred potatoes, squeeze to remove moisture
1. Fry pototoes in large pan with salt and pepper, cook until crispy
1. Brown sausage in frying pan
1. Scramble eggs, keep them easy and wet
1. Spread pizza dough, sauce with gravy
1. Chop hash browns and spread over entire surface of pizza and cover lighly with cheese
1. Chop scrambled eggs into small bits and cover pizza
1. Chop sausage and cover pizza
1. Cover pizza to edges with cheese
1. Bake for 14 minutes
