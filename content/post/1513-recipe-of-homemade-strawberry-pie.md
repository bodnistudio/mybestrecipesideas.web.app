<h3>The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Crust. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 1/4 cup of plain flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tablespoon of castor sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 8 tablespoons of chilled butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tablespoon of apple cider vinegar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of ice water. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Strawberry filling. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 250 gram of strawberry. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tbsp of cornstarch. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 3-4 tablespoons of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of Butter and milk wash. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tablespoon of soft butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tablespoon of milk. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Strawberry Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			In a bowl add flour, salt and sugar and mix with a fork..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add chilled butter. Mash butter into the flour with a fork, and also keep mixing, till the flour looks like crumbs..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add apple cider vinegar and mix roughly. Add ice water and bring the dough together. Don&#39;t add all water immediately. Keep adding teaspoons only if required..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cling wrap the dough and refrigerate for atleast 1 hour..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Till the dough is getting chilled, let us prepare for the filling. Clean the fruit and slice into equal size pieces..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add salt, sugar, cornstarch mix and set aside..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			After 1 hour or so, remove dough from fridge. Take on lightly floured work surface and roll a circle out of it..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Lightly grease a 17cm Pie tray. Preheat the oven @190C..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place the rolled circle gently over the pie-tray and press it to the bottom. Also gently press against the sides of the tray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			With the help of a rolling pin, cut out the excess dough from edges..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pinch and roll a small circle with the extra dough and cut ribbons.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put the strawberry filling in the pie-tray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cover the tray as you like. I like basket pattern..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Prepare butter and milk wash and apply on the edges and on the ribbons..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake in preheated oven for 40-50 minutes, till filling is bubbling and the crust looks brown and crunchy. Refer below picture for half done pie.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Once done let it sit for few seconds in the tray..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Keep over a can. Gently pull the pie tray down..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Remove pie on serving plate. Serve warm with dollop of strawberry ice-cream or whipped cream. My family loves as is..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
