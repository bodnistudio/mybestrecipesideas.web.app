##### Steps to make 3 Fish Stew:

1. Cube the fish into about 1&#34;
1. Pour the water into a saucepan and add about 1/3 of the fish cubes. Boil for about 10 minuts
1. Brown the bacon, and onion,when crispy cube and add the potato. Brown well
1. Add the fish in the water to the bacon/onion/potato bring to a simmer and add the rest of the fish. Simmer for about 10 minuts
1. After 10 minutes add the cream, evaporated milk, salt, pepper, and shrimp. Bring to just below a boil and com for 1 hour.  Note: it is important not to let this boil your sauce will break and while it will still be tasty, visually it will get really ugly.
