##### Steps to make Sukka Bhel (Chatpata Puffed Rice Dry Mix):

1. To make Sukka Bhel in Mumbai street food style, mix together all the above mentioned ingredients in a large mixing bowl, using just half a cup of sev initially.
1. Adjust the green chillies, black salt, chaat masala and lemon juice as per preference.
1. Immediately serve in paper cones just like it is done in the streets of Mumbai.
1. Garnish with some more sev and toss slightly and serve with a crisp poori alongside.
