##### The ingredients needed to make Easy-Peasy Cheesy Chicken:

1. Make ready 1 packages Ranch dressing mix
1. Take 1/3 cup olive oil
1. Make ready 3 chicken breasts, diced into 1 inch pieces
1. Make ready 4 medium russet potatoes, washed and diced into 1 inch pieces
1. Make ready 3/4 lb cheddar or desired cheese, grated
1. Prepare 1/2 lb Bacon, diced and cooked (or precooked bacon bits for an easier meal)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy-Peasy Cheesy Chicken:

1. Preheat oven to 375°, grease large baking dish, set aside.
1. Put chicken, potatoes, oil and ranch packet all into a gallon-sized plastic bag. Shake until all is coated evenly. Dump into pan and spread out evenly.
1. Bake for 45-60 mins, stirring every 15-20 minutes to prevent it from sticking. Stab your potatoes with a fork to see if they are done.
1. Once it&#39;s cooked, pull pan out, top with cheese and bacon, and place back in oven for 2-5 minutes to melt the cheese.
1. Serve and enjoy! You could also add green onions to the top, sour cream served on top, ranch dressing drizzled on it, anything you like! We loved it just the way this is though. :)
