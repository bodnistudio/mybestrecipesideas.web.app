##### The ingredients needed to make Frozen Curd Ice-Cream:

1. Take 1 cup Hung Curd
1. Take 3/4 cup Condensed Milk(Little less than)
1. Take 1 tbsp Vanilla Essence
1. Get as required Chocolate Sauce for Garnishing
1. Take as required Sliced Almonds, Pistachios and Cashews for Garnishing




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Frozen Curd Ice-Cream:

1. Take curd in a mixing bowl. Beat the curd very well. Add condensed milk and vanilla essence. Mix all well and beat everything very well.
1. Transfer the mixture in a container and keep in freezer for 2 hours. After 2 hours, remove container from freezer and again beat very well.
1. Keep the container back in freezer for 3 to 4 hours to set.
1. Drizzle some chocolate sauce and garnish with sliced almonds, pistachios and cashews and serve chilled.
