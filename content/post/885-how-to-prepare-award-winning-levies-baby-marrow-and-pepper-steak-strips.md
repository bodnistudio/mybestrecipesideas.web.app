##### The ingredients needed to make LeViés Baby Marrow and Pepper Steak Strips:

1. Take  Baby Marrows (2 packets)
1. Take 500 g Tenderized Steak (Strips)
1. Prepare Tablespoon Garlic
1. Make ready Tablespoon Black Pepper
1. Make ready 1 Packet Spaghetti Bolognais Sauce
1. Make ready 1 Packet Oxtail Soup
1. Get 100 ml Cooking Oil
1. Prepare 1 Onion Chopped
1. Prepare  Spicy spice to taste (Optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make LeViés Baby Marrow and Pepper Steak Strips:

1. In your pan fry your onion, garlic, salt and pepper to taste. You can add a spicy spice if you want to. Next add Steak strips and fry till all the juices is gone. Take half a packet Bolognais sause and half oxtail soup and mix with the steak. Do not add water. Before dishing you can add a little bit of water if you prefer a runny sauce.
1. Separately prepare your Baby Marrows. Fry in a little bit of oil to tender but still crunchy. Add salt to taste. I sommer used aromat. When all ready dish and enjoy. Bon Appetit!
