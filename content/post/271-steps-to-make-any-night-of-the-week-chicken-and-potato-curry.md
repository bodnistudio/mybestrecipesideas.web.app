##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Potato Curry:

1. Take Half Chicken
1. Prepare  Panch Phoran (optional - an asian 5-spice blend)
1. Take  Dalchini powder (cinnamon bark)1tspn
1. Get  Cardimond powder 1 tspn
1. Make ready  Salt 2 tbsp + adjust if needed
1. Take  Water
1. Get 2 tbsp mixed curry powder
1. Get  Garlic and ginger paste 1tspn each
1. Get  Potatoes
1. Take  Coriander to garnish
1. Prepare 3 onions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken and Potato Curry:

1. Add 3 tbsp of oil, 3 onions, chicken, 2 tbsp salt, cardimond powder and dalchini powder and let onions soften and chicken turn white.
1. Add 2 tbsp of mix curry powder, and 1tspn ginger and garlic paste. Let cook.
1. Add peeled potatoes on top of the curry base and close lid. Let cook for 6-7 mins. Low heat.
1. Once base reduced, add boiling water up untill it nearly covers the potatoes.
1. Mix and let simmer. Check salt and add if needed.
1. Garnish with chopped corriander
1. Enjoy!
