<h3>The ingredients needed to make Bourbon Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 3 of eggs, large. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 1/2 oz of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 6 oz of golden syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 4 tbsp of butter, melted and slightly cooled. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 1/2 tbsp of bourbon. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 10 oz of pecans (8 oz chopped/2 oz whole). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>
