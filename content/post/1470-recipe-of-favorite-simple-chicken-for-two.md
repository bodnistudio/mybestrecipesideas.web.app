##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Chicken for two:

1. Get 2 large Skinless chicken breasts
1. Prepare 1 large Baker potato (skin on - cut into chunks)
1. Prepare 1 cup Baby carrots - whole
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Freshly chopped chives
1. Get 2 tbsp Freshly chopped rosemary
1. Get 1 tbsp Freshly chopped thyme
1. Take 1 dash Smoked paprika (for each breast)
1. Prepare 1 tsp Dry rosemary
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Red pepper flakes
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Course ground fresh cracked pepper
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Low sodium chicken stock
1. Make ready 1/2 stick unsalted butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Simple Chicken for two:

1. Pre heat oven to 350°F
1. Place chicken breasts in a 7½ x 11&#34; baking dish and surround them with carrots and potato chunks
1. Sprinkle all herbs and spices evenly over entire dish
1. Slice butter into ¼&#34; slices and place throughout dish.
1. Gently pour chicken stock into dish making sure not to wash off seasoning.
1. Cover with tin foil and bake for 50 - 70 minutes
