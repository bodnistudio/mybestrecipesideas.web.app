<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 (29 ounce) of can pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1.5 cups of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 4 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare .5 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 (12 ounce) of cans evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 of deep dish raw pie crusts. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine pumpkin, sugar, eggs. Stir in salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves. Add evaporated milk while stirring. Pour into pie shells..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake on cookie sheet for 15 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Reduce oven temp to 350, bake for 45 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Makes 2 pies..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
