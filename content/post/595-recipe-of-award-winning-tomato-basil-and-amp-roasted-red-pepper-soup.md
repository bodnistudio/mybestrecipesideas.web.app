##### The ingredients needed to make tomato, basil, &amp; roasted red pepper soup:

1. Get 2 can diced tomatoes, undrained
1. Get 1 can tomato sauce (15 ounces)
1. Prepare 1 cup vegetable broth
1. Take 1 tsp sugar
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
