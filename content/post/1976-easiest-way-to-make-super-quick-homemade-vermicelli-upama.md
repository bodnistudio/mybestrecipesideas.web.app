##### The ingredients needed to make Vermicelli Upama:

1. Prepare 1 no. potato chopped
1. Make ready 2 nos. onions chopped
1. Take 1 tbsp chopped ginger
1. Make ready to taste Salt
1. Prepare 2 tsp sugar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ghee
1. Take 2 tbsp oil
1. Make ready  Curry leaves few
1. Get 1/4 tsp asafoetida
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
1. Get 1/4 tsp urad dal
1. Take 1/4 tsp chana dal
1. Make ready 1 cup semolina
1. Take 1 cup vermicelli
1. Make ready 1 chopped tomato
1. Get 1 chopped green chilli
1. Make ready 8 nos. cashew nuts




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Vermicelli Upama:

1. Heat oil in a kadai add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, chopped chilli, when it splutters then add asafoetida, curry leaves, ginger, onion chopped and tomato chopped, cook for 3 minutes on low flame.
1. Then add cashew nuts and potato chopped, cover and cook for some time. Then add vermicelli stir it continuously, then add salt to taste, sugar and 3 cups of water.
1. When water starts to boil then add semolina slowly stir it continuously until the mixture is thick and water is absorbed, add ghee mix well. Serve hot with tomato sauce.
