##### Steps to make Easy Cheesy Potato Nuggets #potatocontest:

1. In a bowl add mashed potatoes along with the dry ingredients. (cornflour,gramflour,salt,oregano, turmeric,chilliflakes)
1. Grate your favourite cheese on top of the mixture,and knead the mixture using your hand and refrigerate for 30min.
1. The mixture should resemble a a kneaded dough.if its still wet and sticky,you can add more flour.
1. Grease your palms with oil and make small balls of the mixture and set aside in a plate. NB//Greasing your palms helps to easily handle n shape the ball without sticking.
1. COATING... Take 3 separate bowls for plain flour,milk and breadcrumbs.
1. Dip first in milk,then plainflour,then milk and finally breadcrumbs.
1. Shallow fry both sides till golden brown and pat in paper towel to drain excess oil.
