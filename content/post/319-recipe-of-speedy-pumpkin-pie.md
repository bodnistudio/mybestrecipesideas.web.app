<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 4 large of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of Libby&#39;s 100% Pure Pumpkin (29 oz). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 can of Carnation 2% Evaporated Milk (12 fl. oz. each). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2 of unbaked 9-inch deep dish pie shells. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In small bowl, mix sugar, salt, cinnamon,  ginger,  and cloves.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In large bowl, beat eggs.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into pie shells.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 15 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Reduce temperature to 350°F and bake for 40-50 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool on wire rack for 2 hours.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
