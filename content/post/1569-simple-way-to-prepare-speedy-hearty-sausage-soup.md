##### The ingredients needed to make Hearty Sausage Soup:

1. Get 1 lb turkey or chicken sausage (removed from casing)
1. Get 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Make ready 2 clove garlic, minced
1. Get 2 large carrots, diced
1. Take 1 large red potato, diced
1. Get 2 tsp Italian seasonings
1. Get 2 can chicken broth (14 oz. each)
1. Get 1 can diced tomatoes
1. Get 1 can white beans




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Hearty Sausage Soup:

1. Saute onions and garlic with sausage until browned.
1. Add carrots, potatoes, and seasonings and cook 3 minutes.
1. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
1. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
1. Season with salt and pepper.
1. Serve with grated cheese and crusty bread.
