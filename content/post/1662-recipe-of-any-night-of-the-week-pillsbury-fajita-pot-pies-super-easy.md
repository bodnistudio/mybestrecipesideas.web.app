1. Take 1 each onion
1. Get 1 1/4 bunch cilantro
1. Take 1 cheese-cheddar or mozarella
1. Get 1 salt to taste

##### Instructions to make Pillsbury Fajita Pot Pies (Super easy):

1. Open Pillsbury flakey biscuits and flatten each biscuit.
1. Grease muffin pan and place each flattened piece of dough in the pan
1. you can begin preheating the oven if you like, but you still have to cook the chicken and stuff.

1. Spoon the chicken, onions, and bell pepper mix into each biscuit.
