##### The ingredients needed to make Boiled Potato Jalebi:

1. Get 1 big boiled potato
1. Get 1/4 cup milk
1. Get 2 tbsp curd
1. Make ready 1/2 cup arrowroot powder
1. Get 2 tsp cardamom powder
1. Prepare 1 pinch turmeric powder or yellow / red food color
1. Take 1 cup Sugar
1. Make ready  Water half cup
1. Make ready  Kesar 3 4 strands
1. Get  Ghee for trying




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Boiled Potato Jalebi:

1. Blend boiled potato and milk and keep aside for 10 min. Strain the mixture and add all other ingredients to make smooth batter.
1. Heat ghee in a padhai and make jalebis using a ziplog bag or a small hole plastic bottle.
1. 3. Make one string consistency syrup.
1. 4. Add jalebis in syrup and keep them inside for a min. Flip them once and take them out.
1. 5. Serve hot or cold.
