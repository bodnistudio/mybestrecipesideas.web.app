##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Steak With Leftover Sashimi:

1. Make ready 1 Katakuriko

1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp ● Ketchup
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp ● Japanese Worcestershire-style sauce
1. Prepare 1 Japanese leek

##### Instructions to make Tuna Steak With Leftover Sashimi:

1. Finely shred the white part of a Japanese leek and put into a bowl of water.  Cut the tuna into bite sized pieces, and coat with katakuriko.
1. Heat some olive oil in a frying pan and stir fry the tuna.
