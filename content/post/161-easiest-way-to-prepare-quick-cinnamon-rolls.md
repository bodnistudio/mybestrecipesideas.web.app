##### The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Rolls:

1. Get  In order to make the dough, you’ll need;
1. Make ready  · 2 cups of all-purpose flour
1. Prepare  · 3 teaspoons of baking powder
1. Take  · 2 tablespoons of sugar
1. Make ready  · ½ teaspoon of salt
1. Take  · 3 tablespoons of butter/ Margarine
1. Get  · ¾ cup of milk
1. Get  For the filling;
1. Get  · 3 tablespoons of butter
1. Take  · ¾ cup of brown/white sugar
1. Get  For the glaze;
1. Get  · 3 teaspoons of cinnamon
1. Make ready  · ½ cup of powdered sugar
1. Make ready  · ¼ cup of milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cinnamon Rolls:

1. Directions; - Step 1: In a bowl, mix together flour sugar baking powder and salt.
1. Step 2: Cut in softened butter/ margarine and mix it using your hands.
1. Step 3: Add milk as you continue mixing with your hands till you form soft dough. You can take out the dough from the bowl and continue mixing on a lightly floured surface to achieve the desired texture of the dough.
1. Step 4: Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into a rectangle that is about ¼ inch thick.
1. Step 5: In a small bowl mix the brown/white sugar and cinnamon.
1. Step 6: Spread the butter on the rolled out dough, followed by the crumbly mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
1. Step 7: Roll up the rectangle and slightly pinch the edges to seal it, then with a sharp knife slice into 12 rolls. If you want them a little smaller you can make up to 16 rolls.
1. Step 8: Place your rolls on a non-stick baking tray or grease it with butter/ margarine if it is non- stick then bake for 20-25 minutes at 200 degrees Celsius.
1. Step 9: For the glaze, combine powdered sugar and milk in a bowl and stir until smooth.
1. Step 10: Once the rolls are done drizzle the glaze while they are still warm so that the glaze will melt into the cracks making every bite full of deliciousness.
