<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 3/4 cup of white sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 15 oz of canned pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 12 oz of evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425°F. Whisk together sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Beat eggs in mixer bowl, then add pumpkin puree. When well combined, stir in evaporated milk and dry ingredients..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour mixture into premade pie crust. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes, then turn oven down to 350°F for 40 to 50 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Allow to cool for 1 to 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
