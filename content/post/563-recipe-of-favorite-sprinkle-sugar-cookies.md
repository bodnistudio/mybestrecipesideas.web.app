##### The ingredients needed to make Sprinkle Sugar Cookies:

1. Get 2 cups flour
1. Prepare Half cup sugar (adjust to taste)
1. Take Half teaspoon baking powder
1. Get 125 grams magarine
1. Get 1 large egg
1. Take  Sugar sprinkles




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sprinkle Sugar Cookies:

1. Preheat oven
1. Cream magarine and sugar 3minutes
1. Add the broken egg and whisk for 2 minutes
1. Add the flour mixed with baking powder
1. Mix the dough to get a stiff dough
1. Roll out small balls of dough until the dough is exhausted
1. Flatten on your pan
1. Mesh the flattened cookie inside the sugar sprinkle
1. Repeat process for all the dough balls
1. Line them in an ungreased pan and bake for 25 minutes
