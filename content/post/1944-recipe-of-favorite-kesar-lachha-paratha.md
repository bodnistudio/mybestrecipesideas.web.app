##### The ingredients needed to make Kesar Lachha Paratha:

1. Take 1 Tbsp Ghee
1. Take 1/4 Cup Milk /Water Approximately
1. Get 1/2 Cup Maida
1. Get 1/2 Cup Wheat Flour
1. Prepare 1/2 Tsp Kesar
1. Make ready 2 Tbsp Sugar Powder
1. Get Pinch Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Kesar Lachha Paratha:

1. Mix all ingredients and Make a soft dough.
1. Add water little by little and make a dough and let it rest for 15 minutes.
1. Divide dough in equal portions and take one portion and roll to make a big round.
1. Apply ghee and sprinkle dry flour and cut thin strips.
1. Gather the strips and roll and make a lachha paratha.
1. Place on tawa, apply ghee and cook on low flame from both sides till crisp and serve hot.
