##### Instructions to make Rice Matka Kulfi:

1. Take 1 cup rice. Roast it in 1 tbsp butter.
1. Add some dryfruits in pan and roast it.
1. Grind rice and dryfruits in grinder. It should not be in powdered form.
1. Take 400 ml milk in a pan. Boil it on medium flame.
1. Add grinded rice and dryfruits mixture in milk.
1. Cook it till it reduces to half the quantity.
1. Add saffron milk in it. Cook for 5 min.
1. Now add 1/2 cup sugar in milk and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame and let it cool at room temperature.
1. Now put it in cups and refrigerate for about 7-8 hours.
1. It&#39;s ready to be served cold. Enjoy.
