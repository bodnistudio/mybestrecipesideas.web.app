##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Club Pizza:

1. Prepare 1/4 cup mayonnaise
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Ranch Dressing, divided
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup shredded triple cheddar cheese
1. Get 1 1/2 cup finely chopped cooked chicken or I use rotisserie chicken
1. Get 8 slice bacon cooked and crumbled, or I use precooked bacon pieces in a bag
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
1. Take 1 tomato finally chopped
1. Get 1 (13.8 oz.) tube of refrigerated pizza crust




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Club Pizza:

1. preheat oven to 400
1. unroll pizza dough on to greased cooking sheet. bake for 10 minutes.
1. mix mayonnaise and 1/3 cup of ranch dressing. spread on to crust. tap with half of cheese, chicken, remaining cheese and bacon.
1. bake 5 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted. top with lettuce and tomato drizzle with remaining dressing
