1. Make ready 50 gr corn flour
1. Get 10 gr finely shredded cheddar cheese

##### Instructions to make Caterpillar Cookies (Gluten Free Cookies):

1. Mix butter/margarine with condensed milk (just use the spatula)
1. Mix with the corn flour. Add pandan essence until you reach the colour you want. (Pls note that the colour will be lighter after you bake the cookies)
1. Add shredded cheddar cheese and mix well. Cool the dough in the fridge for 30-60 minutes.
1. Shape the cookies using clean comb (pls refer to this video: https://youtu.be/TAlqKr2OomA). Made double recipes to get this amount of cookies.
1. Bake it at 130-150 degree celcius until the base of the cookies turn bit brownish in colour. About 20-25 minutes. Pls note that the cookies will expand after baking. The colour will turn lighter also :) happy baking!
