##### The ingredients needed to make Matcha Ice Cream:

1. Get 1/2 Cup Condensed Milk
1. Take 1 Cup Heavy Cream
1. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Matcha Powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Matcha Ice Cream:

1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl. Pour in the heavy cream and the condensed milk and mix all the ingredients on high speed in a stand mixer or a hand mixer until soft peaks form.
1. Put all of the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for at least 8 hours or overnight.
1. Enjoy your homemade, no machine matcha ice cream!
