##### Steps to make Cheesy Tomato Pasta Sauce:

1. In saucepan over medium heat, melt butter &amp; heavy whipping cream until smooth.
1. Add pepper, oregano, salt (&amp; onion if desired). Simmer 5 minutes.
1. Add Tomato puree, combine well, then add Mozzarella cheese and heat until melted and smooth, stirring often.
1. Serve over pasta and enjoy!
