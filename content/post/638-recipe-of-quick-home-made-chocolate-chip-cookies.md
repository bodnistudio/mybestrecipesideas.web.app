##### The ingredients needed to make Home Made Chocolate Chip Cookies:

1. Get 3 stick butter
1. Prepare 1 cup white sugar
1. Prepare 1 cup brown sugar
1. Get 2 eggs
1. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
1. Take 4 tsp real vanilla extract or 6 tsp. immitation
1. Get 2 tsp baking soda
1. Take 4 cup flour
1. Make ready 12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Home Made Chocolate Chip Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
1. In large bowl, mix together butter, both sugars, eggs, baking soda, and vanilla extract
1. Add 2 cups of flour to the mixture and stir in
1. Add the rest of flour and mix together
1. Mix in the chocolate chips (I prefer to use my hands for this part)
1. Spoon on to a cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown on edges and tips and slightly lighter in the center
1. Never bake these when in a bad mood or they will NOT taste as good
