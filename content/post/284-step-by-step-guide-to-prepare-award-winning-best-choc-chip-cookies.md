##### The ingredients needed to make Best Choc Chip Cookies:

1. Take 125 grams unsalted butter
1. Prepare 1/3 cup caster sugar
1. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup plain flour
1. Take 1 tsp baking soda
1. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla
1. Take 1 egg
1. Get 1/2 cup chocolate chips




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Best Choc Chip Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 180°F
1. cream butter and sugar together with an electric mixer on high for 2-3 minutes
1. add vanilla and egg and mix till combined
1. slowly add flour and baking soda and mix with a spoon till completely combined.
1. add chocolate chips and mix
1. roll a tablespoon of dough into a ball and place in lined baking tray.
1. bake in oven for 6-8 minutes or barely golden.
1. take out and leave to cool for 5 minutes.
1. then serve and enjoy :)
