##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Salmon Meunière with Tartare Sauce:

1. Take 2 pieces King salmon, fresh salmon or cod
1. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper
1. Take 1 Flour (cake flour)
1. Get 1 tbsp Oil for pan frying (grapeseed, vegetable etc.)
1. Take 5 to 10 grams Butter
1. Prepare 1 Tartar sauce
1. Prepare 1 Boiled eggs
1. Take 1/8 of an onion Onions (finely chopped)
1. Prepare 1 just 1 dash for color!! Parsley (dried parsley is fine)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Take 1 tsp Lemon (or Italian dressing)
1. Make ready  Side vegetables
1. Prepare 5 cm Carrot
1. Make ready 10 to 15 pods Snow peas
1. Take 1 Boiling water
1. Take 5 grams Butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Salmon Meunière with Tartare Sauce:

1. Salt and pepper and the salmon, and dust lightly with flour.
1. Spread the oil in a frying pan, and put in the flour dusted salmon.
1. Cover with a lid, and cook over low heat until the salmon is browned on one side.
1. Turn the salmon over, and brown the other side. Drop the butter into the pan. That&#39;s it. I believe in keeping cooking easy and delicious!!
1. To make the tartare sauce, finely chop the boiled egg, onion and parsley, and mix with mayonnaise and lemon. Use as much as you like!
