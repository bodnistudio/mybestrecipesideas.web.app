##### The ingredients needed to make Awesome Pasta Salad:

1. Take 1 packages Spiral Pasta  16 oz
1. Take 3 cup Cherry Tomatoes
1. Make ready 1/2 lb Provolone Cheese - Cubed
1. Make ready 1/2 lb Salami - Summer Sausage Cubed
1. Get 1/4 lb Pepperoni
1. Prepare 1/2 Green Bell Pepper
1. Make ready 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper
1. Get 1 can Black Olives - 10 oz Drained
1. Make ready 1/2 Red Bell Pepper
1. Get 1 jar Pimientos - Drained
1. Prepare 8 oz Italian Salad Dressing




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Awesome Pasta Salad:

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Then drain.
1. In a large bowl, combined pasta with tomatoes, provolone, salami, pepperoni, peppers, olives and pimientos.
1. Pour in salad dressing and toss to coat.
