##### The ingredients needed to make Organic Vegan Basil Pesto Pasta:

1. Get  Pesto Sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
1. Get 2 cups Fresh Basil
1. Get 1/2 cup walnuts
1. Prepare 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
1. Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice
1. Get 1/4 tsp sea salt
1. Take 4 tbsp water (plus more as needed)
1. Make ready  Pasta
1. Prepare 1/2 pack spaghetti or any kind of pasta
1. Take 1/2 cup green peas
1. Make ready 1 cup mushrooms
1. Take 1/2 cup minced onion




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Organic Vegan Basil Pesto Pasta:

1. Boil water and cook the pasta, set aside
1. Add all the Pesto sauce in the blender for 2 minutes
1. Add 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan and sauté mushrooms and onion
1. Add green peas and cook for another 3 minutes
1. Add cooked Pasta
1. Add Pesto Sauce
1. Mixed well, add salt and pepper to taste
