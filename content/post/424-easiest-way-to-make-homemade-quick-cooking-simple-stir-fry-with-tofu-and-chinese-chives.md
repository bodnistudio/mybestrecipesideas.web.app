##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Cooking! Simple Stir-fry with Tofu and Chinese Chives:

1. Get 300 grams Firm tofu
1. Make ready 1 bunch Chinese chives
1. Prepare 7 cm length Japanese Leek
1. Make ready 1 Red chilli (sliced)
1. Make ready 1 tsp Sesame oil
1. Take 2 tsp ○Sake
1. Get 1 teaspooon plus ○Sugar
1. Prepare 1 tbsp ○Soy sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Quick Cooking! Simple Stir-fry with Tofu and Chinese Chives:

1. Wrap the tofu in paper towels and microwave at 500 W for 3 minutes. Heat sesame oil in a pan and fry the chopped leeks. Add the sliced chili to taste.
1. Cut the tofu as illustrated in the photo and fry.
1. Season the tofu with the ○ ingredients. Add the chives cut into 3 cm and stir. Serve.
