##### The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Pomegranate Juice:

1. Take 2 cup pomegranate seeds
1. Get 6 strawberry washed and chopped. chop one strawberry into slices for garnishinh
1. Get 1 tsp rock salt sanchal (optional)
1. Get 1/2 tsp cumin seed powder roasted (optional)
1. Take 1/4 cup water if required to blend into juice
1. Get 10 ice cubes if you want to have chilled juice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Strawberry Pomegranate Juice:

1. In a bowl take the seeds of pomegranate and strawberry. Put it into a mixer/grinder or blender. Blend to smooth adding water if required.
1. Strain the juice through the strainer in a bowl. Press and strain. Discard the seeds. Add the rock salt and cumin seed powder. Mix well. (If you want to have plain juice, skip adding rock salt and cumin seed powder).
1. Garnish the serving glasses with the strawberry slices. Pour the juice in them and serve. Chill the juice for sometime if you want to have it chilled or add some icecubes.
