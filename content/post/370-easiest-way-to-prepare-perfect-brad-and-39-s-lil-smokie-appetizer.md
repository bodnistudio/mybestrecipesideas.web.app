##### Instructions to make Brad&#39;s Lil smokie appetizer:

1. Cut each rectangle into 4 strips lengthwise. Then cut those strips in half width wise.
1. Roll each little strip around a little smokie. Place on a greased baking sheet. Leave enough room in between for dough to rise.
