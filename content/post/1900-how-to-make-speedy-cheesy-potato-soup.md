##### Instructions to make Cheesy Potato Soup:

1. Combine potatoes, onion, and water in a large saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer until soft (20-30 min).
1. In skillet melt butter. Add flour. Cook and stir until mixture bubbles. Remove from heat and stir in milk. Return to heat and stir until thickened.
1. Stir milk mixture slowly into potatoes. Cook and stir mixture until thick.
1. Add cheese and seasonings.
1. Serve with chopped chives and enjoy!
