##### The ingredients needed to make Sour Chinese stir fry marinade:

1. Make ready 60 ml light soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp Mirin
1. Take 1.5 tbsp Chinkiang vinegar
1. Get 1 tbsp fish sauce
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
1. Get 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
1. Take 1 tbsp corn starch
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp garlic, minced
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
1. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
1. Prepare  Cilantro for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sour Chinese stir fry marinade:

1. Mix all ingredients
1. Add meat of choice and marinade for 30-60 minutes
1. Stir fry vegetables with a tbsp of oil, and then remove from pan.
1. Remove meat from marinade and reserve the marinade. Cook meat with 1/2 tbsp of oil. When meat is almost done, add vegetables and the rest of marinade. Stir fry until meat and marinade are safely cooked.
