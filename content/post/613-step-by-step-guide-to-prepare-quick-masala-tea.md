##### Instructions to make Masala Tea:

1. In a sauce pan or a small pan with a handle, heat half cups water. - Let the water come to a boil add 1 inch ginger crushed.
1. Add sugar as per taste. I usually add 1 teaspoon raw sugar. You can add more or less. - Now add half tspn tea. Boil for a minute. This boiling time depends on how strong you want your tea. If you prefer a strong intense tea, then boil for a few more minutes. - Add milk. After adding milk boil for 2 to 3 minutes. - If using chilled milk, then boil with tea for 2 to 3 minutes. If using hot milk, then add the milk and switch off the flame. - Pour the tea through a tea strainer directly in the cup.
1. Serve masala chai hot with some indian snacks or indian cookies or biscuits.or rusk.
