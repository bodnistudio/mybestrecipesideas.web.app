##### The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Spicy Fruits Salad:

1. Take 1 large raw manggo
1. Prepare 1 large Grany Smith apple
1. Get 1/4 pineapple
1. Take 60 grams palm sugar
1. Get 2 Thai chilli
1. Prepare 6 grams tamarind
1. Get 1 pinch shrimp paste
1. Take 2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1 cup water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Indonesian Spicy Fruits Salad:

1. Peel all the fruits. Shredded manggo and apple into the big bowl. Cut the pineapple into small and thin cutting. Mix all the fruits.
1. Grounded sugar, chili, tamarind,  salt,  shrimp paste,  water together.
1. Pour grounded sugar use striner into the shredded fruits. Mix it well, put in the fridge. Serve it cold.
