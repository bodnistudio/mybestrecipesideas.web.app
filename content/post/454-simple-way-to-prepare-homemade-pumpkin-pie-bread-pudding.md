<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie Bread Pudding:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of egg. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of (60ml)milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 Tbsp of pumpkin pureé. </li>
	
		<li>{Take pinch of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready pinch of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 slice of bread. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 Tbsp of unsweetened apple sauce. </li>
	
		<li>{Take pinch of cinnamon. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie Bread Pudding:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Place egg, milk, pumpkin pureé, pumpkin pie spice, and salt in a mug or bowl. Mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut the bread into 1/2 inch dices and add to the egg mixture. Mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Microwave 1 min and sprinkle with apple sauce and cinnamon..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Serve hot :).
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
