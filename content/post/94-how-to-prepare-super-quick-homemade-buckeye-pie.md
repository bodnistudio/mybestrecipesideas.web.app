<h3>The ingredients needed to make Buckeye Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 20 of oreos, crushed. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 tbsp of butter, melted. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of butter, room temp. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 2/3 cup of creamy peanut butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/4 cup of powdered sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of Kosher salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of pure vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 cup of chocolate chips. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3/4 cup of heavy cream. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Buckeye Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Combine Oreos with melted butter, stir until all crumbs are moistened. Press and shape into pie dish.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine room temp butter, peanut butter, powdered sugar, salt, and vanilla. Stir until smooth and creamy. Spread mixture over pie crust and place dish in freezer while you make ganache..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Place chocolate chips in a heat proof bowl. In a small sauce pan over medium heat, heat heavy cream until it begins to simmer. Pour over chocolate and let sit 5 minutes before stirring until smooth..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour ganache over peanut butter and smooth. Refrigerate until set, about one hour..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Serve!!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
