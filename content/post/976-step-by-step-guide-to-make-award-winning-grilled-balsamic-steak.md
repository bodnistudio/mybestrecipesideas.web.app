##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Balsamic Steak:

1. Make ready 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
1. Get 1/4 cup soy sauce
1. Take 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1. Prepare 1/2 onion, chopped
1. Take 1 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Take 1 1/2 lb flank steak




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Grilled Balsamic Steak:

1. Mix marinade ingredients and pour into gallon sized zip lock bag and as steak.
1. Marinade 30 + min or overnight
1. Remove steak from marinade and shake off extra. Save marinade and brush on a few times while grilling.
1. Grill on hot grill for approx 20 min for medium- well. Instant read thermometer should read 150°
1. Let rest a few min before eating
