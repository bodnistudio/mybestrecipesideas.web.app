##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Muesli Smoothie:

1. Get 1 Mango
1. Prepare 1 greek yoghurt  pack(or simple yoghurt )
1. Prepare 2 tbsp muesli (with nuts and figs)
1. Get 1 Tsp soaked chia seeds
1. Take 1 cup milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mango Muesli Smoothie:

1. In a blender jar, add chopped pieces of Mangoes.
1. Now add muesli to it. Prefer adding muesli with nuts and figs in it for extra healthy bunch.
1. Now add soaked chia seeds to it. Best to soak it overnight else minimum 1 hour soaking is required.
1. Now to bring the flavours together, add a small pack of greek yoghurt if you are health freak else normal yoghurt you can add. Make sure it isn&#39;t flavoured except Mango flavour.
1. Now lastly add Chilled milk to it for consistency check. Add in as per desired consistency of smoothie.
1. Now blend everything together in a blender jar until smooth.
1. Add Honey as sweetner if required.
