##### The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef and Cabbage:

1. Prepare 3-4 lb corned beef brisket, with spice packet
1. Prepare 10 small red potatoes
1. Prepare 4 large carrots, cut into matchbox sticks
1. Get 1 onion, chopped into bitesized  pieces
1. Prepare 4 cup water
1. Make ready 6 oz beer
1. Make ready 1/2 head cabbage,  coarsely chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Corned Beef and Cabbage:

1. Place the carrots, potatoes, and onion into the bottom of a slow cooker, pour in the water, and place the brisket on top of the vegetables. Pour the beer over the brisket. Sprinkle on the spices from the packet, cover, and set the cooker on High.
1. Cook the brisket for about 8 hours. An hour before serving, stir in the cabbage and cook for 1 more hour.
