<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 180ºC.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Dice pumpkin into cubes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			(Optional) Bake pumpkin for 40min till soft (to be mashed).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put baked pumpkin into a bowl (if not baked - into blender), mash it with a fork.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add to pumpkin eggs, flours, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, baking powder, olive oil - mix ingredients.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into baking mould and put into the oven..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 180ºC for about 30-35 minutes (check with toothpick or knife: it should be dry).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			(Optional) Pour icing: mix sourcream or youghurt, lemon and tangerine zest, lemon juice and honey..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Enjoy this mild flavoury pie! :) Recipe based on Jamie Oliver&#39;s squash muffins..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
