##### The ingredients needed to make Sour Potatoes:

1. Make ready 1 kg potatoes
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup curd hung for half hour
1. Get 1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds
1. Make ready 2 green chillies
1. Get 1 tsp oil
1. Take 1 salt to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sour Potatoes:

1. Boil potatoes in little water. Peel and cut in large chunks.
1. Blend chillies and sesame together until crumbly. Add yoghurt and blend a little just for mixing.
1. Brush a non stick pan with oil. Medium flame.
1. Add sesame paste and stir-fry for few seconds. Add salt and then potatoes. Mix well.
1. Raise flame to high and cook until moisture evaporates.
1. Enjoy!
