##### Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie:

1. Cut the kabocha squash and remove the skin. Put the kabocha squash and one tablespoon each of water and sugar (not listed in ingredients) in a bowl. Cover with plastic film, and microwave for 5 minutes.
1. Mash with a rolling pin and strain through a sieve (optional).
1. Put the mixture in a pan, and add the sugar, butter and milk. Simmer over low heat until the mixture is thickened and slightly firm.
1. I used casserole dishes for this recipe.
1. Roll out the puff pastry. Grease the dish with butter, and line the puff pastry snugly on the dish. Put the kabosha squash filling on top.
