##### The ingredients needed to make Banoffie Pie:

1. Prepare 1 packages Oreo cookies
1. Get 1 Banana
1. Prepare 1 1/4 tbsp Caramel sauce
1. Take 1/3 cup Whipped cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Banoffie Pie:

1. Blend Oreo cookies in the food processor until smooth.
1. Cut banana into pieces.
1. Add the oreo crumbs in the bottom of the cup. Take it to the fridge.
1. Add the banana into the cup and pour the caramel sauce.
1. Top with whipped cream.
