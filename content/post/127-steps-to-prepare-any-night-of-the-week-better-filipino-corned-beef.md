##### The ingredients needed to make Better Filipino Corned Beef:

1. Take 1 can Ox &amp; Palm corned beef or equal quality
1. Get 3 red potatoes (diced)
1. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic (diced and crushed)
1. Take 1 small, white onion (diced)
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cayenne powder (optional)
1. Get 1/2 tsp salt
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground pepper
1. Prepare 1/2 cup water
1. Take 3 tbsp cooking oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Better Filipino Corned Beef:

1. Heat oil in pan on medium heat
1. Add garlic and sauté until almost golden brown
1. Add onions, and continue to sauté for about 30 seconds
1. Add corned beef and potatoes
1. Break-up corned beef, and make sure all ingredients are well-combined
1. Add salt, pepper, cayenne, and water
1. Allow dish to simmer until potatoes are tender, occasionally stirring
1. Serve. Typically enjoyed with steamed Jasmine rice
