##### Instructions to make Ice-Cream Cake:

1. Grind biscuits and sugar in a mixer grinder. Add milk and mix well to make a thick flowing consistency batter. Lastly add baking soda and stir well.
1. Pour the cake batter into a greased cake tin and microwave for 2 minutes.
1. Let the cake cool completely. Divide the cake horizontally into two slices from center.
1. In the same cake container, place a plastic sheet and keep a base on it. Now spread the ice cream evenly on the 1st base and place the 2nd base on ice cream. Cover the plastic and deep freeze for 2 hours.
1. Till then make the ganache. For the ganache, take equal quantity of dark chocolate and cream and microwave for 1 minute. Add butter and mix well. The ganache is ready.
1. Remove the cake from freezer and unmould the cake by pulling out the plastic. Now place the cake on a wire stand and pour the ganache over the cake. Decorate the cake and serve immediately. You can keep the cake deep freezed and remove 10 minutes before serving.
