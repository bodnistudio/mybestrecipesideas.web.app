##### The ingredients needed to make Kids Simple Breakfast:

1. Get 50 g-60g Salmon
1. Make ready 4 Pieces Broccoli
1. Get  Salt
1. Take  Kids Portion Rice
1. Prepare 1 Whole Eggs
1. Make ready  Sunflower Oil
1. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Dice Onions
1. Make ready 1 Tablespoons Chop Garlic




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Kids Simple Breakfast:

1. Nicely season Salmon with Salt. Sear Salmon and bake at 180 Degrees for 10 Minutes.
1. Heat up Water till boiling point. Blanch Broccoli for 90 seconds. Strain them and season with Salt.
1. Heat up pan under medium heat. Pour in 2 Tablespoons Sunflower Oil. Once hot, put in onions and sauté. Put in Garlic once onions is about translucent. Crack eggs and cook it. Put the rice in and mix them well. Once cook, season with Salt and ready to serve.
