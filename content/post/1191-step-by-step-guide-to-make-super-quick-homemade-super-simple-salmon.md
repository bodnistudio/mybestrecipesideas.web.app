##### The ingredients needed to make Super Simple Salmon:

1. Make ready 1 tbsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp dried basil
1. Get 1/2 tbsp salt
1. Get 2 tbsp butter
1. Take 1 lemon
1. Get 6 oz salmon




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Super Simple Salmon:

1. mix together the garlic powder, Basil, and salt in a small bowl; rub equal amounts onto the salmon fillets.
1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook salmon in the butter until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
1. Squeeze lemon over salmon then serve.
