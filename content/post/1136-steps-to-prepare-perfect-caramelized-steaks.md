##### The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Steaks:

1. Get 2 (16 oz) Steaks
1. Make ready 1 tbs Olive Oil
1. Take  Steakhouse Seasoning
1. Take 3 1/2 tbsp Course Black Pepper
1. Get 2 tbsp Kosher Salt
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Fine Pink Salt
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp Onion Powder
1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Garlic Powder
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Season Salt
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Turbinado Sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Adobo Powder (w/pepper)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Smoked Paprika
1. Get 1/2 tbsp Meat Tenderizer
1. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp Cayenne
1. Take  Brown Butter Sauce
1. Prepare 1 stick Butter
1. Make ready 6 clove Garlic minced
1. Take 3 tbsp Brown Sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Caramelized Steaks:

1. Coat steaks with olive oil then add steakhouse blend &amp; let sit
1. After the steaks have sat for about 15-30 minutes begin to cut the butter into slices &amp; start melting in a pan with the minced garlic on low
1. After the butter has been melted for 10-15 minutes start grilling the steaks &amp; add the brown sugar to the butter as it continues to cook down on low
1. Once there is a good sear on each side of the steaks paint the butter sauce on the steaks making sure to do both sides, doing this will add moisture, tenderness &amp; caramelization to your steak
