##### The ingredients needed to make Soy Bean Hamburger Steaks:

1. Prepare 200 grams Cooked soy beans
1. Get 1/4 Onion
1. Take 3 cm Carrot
1. Prepare 5 Shiso leaves
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Katakuriko
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 3 cm knob Grated ginger
1. Take 1 tbsp Ground sesame seeds
1. Take 1 tsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1 dash Salt and pepper
1. Take 1 dash Nutmeg
1. Take 1 tsp Japanese dashi stock powder
1. Prepare  Japanese-style mushroom sauce
1. Prepare 1 bunch Mushrooms of your choice
1. Prepare 150 ml Dashi stock
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar
1. Take 1 tbsp Katakuriko dissolved in water
1. Prepare 1 Green onions or scallions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Soy Bean Hamburger Steaks:

1. Crush the cooked soy beans with your pestle &amp; mortar or in a food processor.
1. Chop the onion and carrot finely and microwave for two minutes. Julienne the shiso leaves.
1. Put all the ingredients except the sauce ingredients into a bowl and mix together.
1. Shape the mixture into small balls and fry in a frying pan with oil until crispy and golden on both sides.
1. In the same frying pan stir-fry the mushroom quickly and add the dashi stock, soy sauce, sugar and mirin.
1. Check the taste and if it is alright, add the katakuriko dissolved in water to thicken the sauce. Sprinkle with chopped green onion and pour over the burgers.
