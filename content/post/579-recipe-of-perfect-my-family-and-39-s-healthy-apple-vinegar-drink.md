##### The ingredients needed to make My Family&#39;s Healthy Apple Vinegar Drink:

1. Take 2 and 1/2 or 3 apples Apple
1. Get 400 grams Rock sugar (or crushed brown cane sugar, or honey)
1. Get 500 ml Vinegar (grain vinegar, aged black vinegar, wine vinegar, etc.)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make My Family&#39;s Healthy Apple Vinegar Drink:

1. Rinse the apples, then blot excess water with a paper towel. Chop into 8 sections, then core and cut into thirds leaving on the peel.
1. Add 1/2 of the chopped apples, then 1/2 of the amount of rock salt, the rest of the apples, then the rest of the rock salt. Then, pour in the vinegar.
1. Close the lid, then keep in a cool, dark place for 2 weeks. (During the summertime, store in the refrigerator.) After removing the apples, it will keep in the refrigerator for up to a year.
1. Since the large jar takes up space in the fridge, I transfer it to a 900 ml empty vinegar bottle to store.
1. Dilute with 5 to 7 parts ice water, hot water, milk, soy milk, or carbonated water (or add 2 tablespoons to a cup, fill halfway with diluting liquid, then adjust to taste.)
