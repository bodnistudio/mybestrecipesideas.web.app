##### The ingredients needed to make Milk Chocolate ice-cream:

1. Take 1/2 ltr milk(full fat)
1. Take 7 tbsp sugar
1. Get 1 +1/2 cornflour
1. Make ready 1+1/2 GMS powder
1. Get 1/8 tsp CMC powder
1. Prepare 1/4 cup cocoa powder
1. Prepare 60 gm dark chocolate
1. Get 1 cup cream or fresh malai
1. Make ready  Chocolate sauce for decoration




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Milk Chocolate ice-cream:

1. Take gms, cmc, cocoa and cornflour in a bowl. And add little milk to it and make it into a smooth paste, set aside.
1. Take milk and sugar in a sauce pan and bring it to a boil now simmer for 5 mins.
1. Now add in the mixed cornflour mix and dark chocolate in this And keep mixing. It will thicken. Now boil this for 1 or 2 mins.
1. Now cool this and pour in freezer safe container and freeze for 3-4 hours.
1. Now it out and use electric beater and beat for 5mnt and add cream and again beat for 5mnt.
1. Creamy and soft ice-cream is ready.
1. Serve with chocolate and sauce.
