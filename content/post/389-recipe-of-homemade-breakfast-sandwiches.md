##### Steps to make Breakfast sandwiches:

1. Use ingredients on the back of the yeast roll box. Once all the yeast is mixed(usually after 5 min) set aside in a bowl covered with a towel, while  you cook your breakfast sausage. Once sausage is thoroughly cooked, add your eggs and jalapeños to the mixture. After the eggs are cooked, combine the egg/ sausage mixture in a bowl and add the jalapeño cream cheese, cottage cheese, and pepper jack shredded cheese. Spray your baking sheet, and begin to lay out the dough evenly in the sheet. Now fill your mixture in the dough. Lastly, top off the mixture with a layer of dough, be sure to pinch the ends in so they cook together. Combine some melted butter, cinnamon and honey, brush the mixture on the tops of dough.(after cooking them, I wished I brushed the dough on the inside before I put in the mixture). Follow the directions on the roll box to bake.
