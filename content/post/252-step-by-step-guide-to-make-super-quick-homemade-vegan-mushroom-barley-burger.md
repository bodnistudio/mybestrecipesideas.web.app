##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Mushroom Barley Burger:

1. Get 2 cups minced mushrooms
1. Prepare 1 minced red onion
1. Get 1/2 cup minced parsley
1. Take 1 1/2 cups cooked pearl barley
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons miso paste
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato paste
1. Take 1 1/2 cups flour
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons minced garlic
1. Prepare to taste Salt

##### Steps to make Vegan Mushroom Barley Burger:

1. Preheat oven to 400 while you prep ingredients.
1. Mix ingredients, form into burgers, and place on generously sprayed baking sheet.
