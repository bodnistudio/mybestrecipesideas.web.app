##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Corn Soup:

1. Make ready 350 grams Pumpkin cut into cubes
1. Get 150 gms Boiled corn
1. Take 2-3 Garlic finely chopped
1. Get 1 Onion Finely chopped
1. Prepare 1 tspn olive oil or butter
1. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
1. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
1. Take 4 Cups vegetables stock
1. Prepare 1 Cup coconut milk
1. Prepare 1 tsp Pumpkin seeds for garnishing
1. Take 1/2 tsp Cream for garnishing




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pumpkin Corn Soup:

1. Heat 1tsp butter or olive oil in a non-stick pan, then add chopped garlic and saute, after few seconds add chopped onions and fry them until it&#39;s light golden brown in colour.
1. Now add pumpkin cubes and boiled corn and mix it very well. Then add salt and pepper powder. Fry it for 2-3 minutes. Then add 4cups vegetable stock, mix it well and cover it with a lid for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes then add 1 cup coconut milk. Then cover it again and simmer until soft.
1. Now switch off the flame and use a hand blender and mix until smooth. Pumpkin soup is ready. Garnish with some pumpkin seeds and fresh cream.
