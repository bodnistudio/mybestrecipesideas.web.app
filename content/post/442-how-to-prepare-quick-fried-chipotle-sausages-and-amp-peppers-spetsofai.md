##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Chipotle Sausages &amp; Peppers (Spetsofai):

1. Make ready 4 Greek/Italian or Chipotle Sausages (chopped)
1. Prepare 3 Bell Pepper (Red &amp; Yellow &amp; Green (the last one is optional)
1. Prepare 1 large Onion (sliced)
1. Take 1 teaspoon Tomato Paste
1. Take 3 Fresh Tomatoes (sliced)
1. Take 2 Bay leaves
1. Take 1/4 cup Olive Oil/Vegetable Oil
1. Take 1 tablespoon Sugar
1. Prepare  Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Fried Chipotle Sausages &amp; Peppers (Spetsofai):

1. Slice onions and place into a large skillet,
1. Along with the sliced peppers and olive/vegetable oil. You can use which ever oil you prefer, in the recipe. The sausages already have fat in them so to make this a little lighter I used vegetable oil for frying.
1. Cut sausages into smaller pieces and toss into skillet and cook on medium heat for about 10 minutes along with the peppers.
1. Add in the tomatoes, and bay leaves and mix.
1. Lastly, add in the tomato paste and cook for another 15 to 20 minutes. :)
