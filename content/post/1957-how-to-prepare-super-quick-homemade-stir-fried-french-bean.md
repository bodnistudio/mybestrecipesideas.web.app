##### The ingredients needed to make 清炒四季豆 Stir-fried French Bean:

##### Steps to make 清炒四季豆 Stir-fried French Bean:

1. 四季豆切小段，红萝卜切丝 Cut french beans into small sections, cut carrot into thin slices
1. 热油，加入虾米和蒜米炒香 Heat the oil, add dried shrimp and minced garlic
1. 加入红萝卜，四季豆翻炒 Add carrot and french beans, stir-fry
