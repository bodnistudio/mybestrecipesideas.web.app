##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Cheese Soup:

1. Take 4 1/2 medium potatoes; finely chopped
1. Prepare 2 medium carrots; finely chopped
1. Get 1/2 medium onion; finely chopped
1. Prepare 1 1/2 stalks celery; finely chopped
1. Make ready 1 tsp seasoned salt
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1 cup instant mashed potatoes
1. Prepare 1/2 lb Velveeta, cubed




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Potato Cheese Soup:

1. Cook potatoes, carrots, onion, and celery with seasoned salt, garlic powder, and pepper in 1 1/2 quarts water. Bring to boil and simmer 45 minutes
1. When vegetables are tender, add Velveeta and stir constantly until dissolved.
1. Add instant potatoes a little at a time until Soup is desired consistency. (Add more instant potatoes if needed.)
