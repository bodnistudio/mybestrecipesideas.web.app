##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Tomato Instant Chutney:

1. Get 5 Green tomato
1. Take 2-3 tsp Bengal gram
1. Get As needed Coriander leaves
1. Prepare 1 tsp Jeera
1. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin seeds
1. Prepare 6-8 Curry leaves
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
1. Take 2 tsp Ginger garlic paste
1. Make ready to taste Green mirchi
1. Make ready To taste Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Spicy Tomato Instant Chutney:

1. Take a pan, pour oil &amp; add jeera, cumin, few Bengal gram seeds, curry leaves, green mirchi. Fry them up on frying up gently, add little garlic-ginerpaste. Cut the green chillies into half, as well as tomato. Add them in pan with required salt &amp; place the lid. Leave it for cooking 6-8mins.
1. Open the pan lid &amp; add finely chopped coriander leaves. Turn off the stove &amp; let it cool for 8-10mins. You can now take the cooked material into mixer grinder to grind it finely.
