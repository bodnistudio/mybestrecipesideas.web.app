##### Steps to make Coffee &amp; chocolate cake with coffee &amp; chocolate sugar syrup:

1. Prepare 2 8&#34;by 4&#34; round pan by lightly greasing with butter or oil, &amp; coating with flour. Preheat oven.
1. In one large bowl, sift in 4cups of flour, &amp; 2tbsps baking powder together, add 2cups sugar &amp; mix thoroughly. Add 4eggs, 2cups milk, 2/3cups oil, 2tsps vanilla &amp; mix till smooth.
1. In a smaller bowl, sift in the remaining flour, cocoa powder, baking powder &amp; baking soda together, add sugar &amp; mix thoroughly. Add egg, 1/4cup oil, 1/2cup milk &amp; 1tsp vanilla &amp; mix till smooth &amp; well mixed. Add the 1/2 cup boiling water last.
1. In the first bowl, add the 1/2cup of hot coffee &amp; mix till combined.
1. Pour both batters in each pan, alternating both to form a swirl of both cake batters in each pan. Bake for 45minutes on medium heat, or till a toothpick inserted in the centre of both comes out clean. Remove from heat &amp; run a knife round the edges &amp; allow to cool a bit before removing from the pans, poke holes in the cake using a skewer &amp; pour the chocolate &amp; coffee syrup over the cakes, &amp; then allow to cool.
1. For the syrup, dissolve 3tsps of coffee in 1tbsp of hot water, add 2tbsps cocoa powder, &amp; 1/2-1cup of icing sugar, mix &amp; place on low heat, stirring till desired texture is achieved.
