##### The ingredients needed to make Cheddar Chicken Casserole:

1. Prepare 12 oz good quality extra sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1. Make ready 1 1/3 can cream of chicken soup
1. Prepare 3/4 cup mayonaisse
1. Get 3/4 cup sour cream
1. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
1. Prepare 1 pepper to taste
1. Prepare 3 cup cooked white rice
1. Make ready 2 1/2 lb cooked chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cheddar Chicken Casserole:

1. Preheat oven to 350, spray 13&#34;by 9&#34;casserole with cooking spray
1. Add rice to casserole dish
1. In medium bowl, mix soup, mayo, sour cream,lemon juice, pepper, and half the cheese. yes, this dinner is going right to our butts :P stir to combine
1. Mix in chicken, stir to coat. spread evenly over rice.
1. Sprinkle with remaining cheese
1. Cook about 40 minutes until cheese is golden brown.
1. Note- this is one of those things that tastes even better the next day, but we rarely leave any!
