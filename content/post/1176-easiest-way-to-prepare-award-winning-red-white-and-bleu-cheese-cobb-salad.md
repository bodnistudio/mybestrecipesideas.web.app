##### The ingredients needed to make Red, White and Bleu Cheese Cobb Salad:

1. Get 2 heads Romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
1. Prepare 3 large hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1. Take 1 small jicama, peeled and shredded
1. Get 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
1. Prepare 1 c grape tomatoes, halved
1. Get 1 c carrots, peeled and shredded
1. Take 1/4 c Bleu cheese, crumbled
1. Take 12 oz chicken breast, cooked and chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Red, White and Bleu Cheese Cobb Salad:

1. Prepare all ingredients and layer in four quart-sized jars.
1. LAYER: - chicken - tomato  - yellow pepper - carrots - jicama - eggs - cheese  - lettuce
1. Serve with your favorite dressing - just add the carbs!
