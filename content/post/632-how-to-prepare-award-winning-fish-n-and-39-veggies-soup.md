##### The ingredients needed to make Fish n&#39; Veggies Soup:

1. Take 1 pcs small Dourada (golden) fish/ trout
1. Take 30 gr Cauliflower
1. Take 5 pcs string beans
1. Get 1 Small tomato
1. Get 1 stalk celery
1. Make ready Half medium Onion
1. Take 1 shallot
1. Take 1 clove Garlic
1. Make ready 2 cm fresh Ginger
1. Take 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
1. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
1. Make ready 1 cup vegetable / Chicken stock
1. Prepare  Olive oil

##### Steps to make Fish n&#39; Veggies Soup:

1. Chop onion, garlic, shallot and ginger.. for the rest veggies roughly chop as you wish..
1. Cut the fish to be half.. wash and drain well.. squish of lemon just set aside
1. Heat Olive oil in the pan, saute the onion and shallot until give you aroma.. add ginger, chilli flakes, celery stalk and garlic fry for few minute.. add tomato saute for few minute until tomato soft..
1. Add the stock of vegetables or chicken.. add the rest vegetable ingredient and the fish..
1. Make sure the fish cook each side.. and season well..
1. Cook until the fish and veggies tender.. enjoy your meal!!
