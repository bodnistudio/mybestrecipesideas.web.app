##### The ingredients needed to make Super Bowl Brats &amp; Knocks in Beer:

1. Make ready 16 oz can of beer
1. Make ready 1 salt &amp; pepper to taste

##### Steps to make Super Bowl Brats &amp; Knocks in Beer:

1. In a small pot add the beer &amp; sliced onion &amp; simmer until onions are soft
1. Add the sausages &amp; salt &amp; pepper &amp; let simmer for 15 minutes
