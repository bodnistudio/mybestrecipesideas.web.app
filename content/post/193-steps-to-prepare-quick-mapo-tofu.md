##### The ingredients needed to make Mapo Tofu:

1. Make ready 2 gloves garlic minced
1. Prepare 1 inch ginger minced
1. Take 2 scallions sliced
1. Make ready 14 oz soft tofu cubed
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon canola oil
1. Make ready 1/2 pound ground pork
1. Make ready  Ingredients for sauce
1. Get 1 tablespoon tobanjan
1. Get 1.5 tablespoons gochujang
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons mirin
1. Make ready 1 tablespoon miso
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
1. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
1. Get 1 teaspoon sesame oil

1. Get 4 tablespoons water

##### Steps to make Mapo Tofu:

1. Combine all ingredients for sauce
1. Heat oil on medium high heat and add garlic and ginger. Cook until fragrant
1. Add ground pork and cook until no pink meat remains. Add sauce and stir thoroughly
