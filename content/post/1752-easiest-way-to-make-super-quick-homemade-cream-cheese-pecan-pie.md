<h3>The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese, softened. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of granulated sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 of egg, beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 2/3 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 10 inch of unbaked deep dish pie shell. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 1/4 cup of chopped pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3 of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of light corn syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Cream Cheese Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 375Â°F..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In large mixing bowl, cream together until smooth and creamy, the cream cheese, sugar, eggs, salt and vanilla..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread mixture over bottom of pie shell..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle pecans evenly over top of cream cheese layer..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Make topping by combining 3 eggs, corn syrup and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Pour over top of pie..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 35-45 minutes or until golden brown..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Let stand 10 minutes before serving..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
