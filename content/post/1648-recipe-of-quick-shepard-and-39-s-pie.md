<h3>The ingredients needed to make Shepard&#39;s Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 1 pound of Turkey. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of medium Eggplant. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 Tablespoons of diced onions. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 of diced mushrooms. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 cup of chicken gravy. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 package of frozen spinach. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 Tablespoons of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 8 of medium white potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 tablespoons of garlic salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready to taste of Black pepper. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Shepard&#39;s Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Brown turkey in a skillet.  While browning the turkey, peel and dice the eggplant.  Add to the skillet with the turkey..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Peel, dice, rinse and add the potatoes to boiling water.  Boil the potatoes for fifteen minutes or until they are easy to mash with a fork. Drain in a colander and replace in the pot along with the butter and  1 tablespoon of garlic salt and black pepper.  Mash with a hand mixer..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Defrost spinach in the microwave for 5 minutes on high.  Place spinach in a colander and press out the excess water..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a deep casserole dish place turkey mixture on the bottom. Scatter the onions, mushrooms and gravy over the turkey.  Next add a layer of spinach over it and Sprinkle garlic salt on top.  Lastly add the mashed potatoes over the layer of spinach..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
