##### The ingredients needed to make Baby/Toddler Fave Chicken and Rice Soup:

1. Prepare 1/2 cup uncooked rice
1. Make ready 1/2 small onion, small dice
1. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, minced
1. Take 1 cooked chicken breast, shredded and chopped
1. Get 1/4 zucchini, diced small
1. Take 1/2 carrot, diced small
1. Get 1/2 celery stalk, diced small
1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Prepare 4-4.5 cups low sodium chicken broth
1. Make ready 1.5 tsp original Ms. Dash Seasoning




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Baby/Toddler Fave Chicken and Rice Soup:

1. Heat oil in pot, add onions and cook until translucent
1. Add all the veggies and rice to the pot, cook additional 5 min.
1. Add your cooked chicken and the Ms. Dash seasoning and stir to combine (this is baby friendly so no added salt, but if you&#39;d like to add salt or pepper, now is the time to do it)
1. Pour in your chicken broth, bring to a boil then reduce to medium-low and let simmer until rice is cooked
1. Tip: you can add a squeeze of lemon when serving in bowl, adds a nice citrus zing♡
