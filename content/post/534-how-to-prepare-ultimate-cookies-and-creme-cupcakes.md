##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies and Creme Cupcakes:

1. Get 1   1/2 Cups All-purpose Flour
1. Make ready 1/2 Cup Cocoa Unsweetened      powder
1. Prepare 1   1/4 Cups Sugar
1. Prepare 3/4 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1. Make ready 2 Eggs
1. Take 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
1. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1. Make ready 3/4 Cup Milk
1. Get 3/4 Cup Water ,  hot
1. Make ready 24 Oreos
1. Prepare of   1 can whipped   icing




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cookies and Creme Cupcakes:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
1. Line cupcake tin with baking cups.
1. Break apart at least 12 cookies and place the broken pieces in the bottom of the cups.
1. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large mixing bowl using a wire whisk.
1. Add the eggs, oil, vanilla, and milk. Mix well until thoroughly combined. Add the hot water and mix until combined.
1. Transfer the batter (it will be very runny and liquidy) to a large measuring cup and pour batter into each baking cup so it&#39;s about 3/4 full.
1. Bake for 16-18 minutes.
1. Add about 2 teaspoons (or more if you&#39;d like) cookie crumbs to the can of frosting and mix.
1. Apply frosting and top with a cookie.
