##### Steps to make Chicken nugget plate:

1. Mix the crushed corn flakes and chopped cheese on a plate like trail mix.
1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add milk, paprika lemon pepper, salt, garlic pepper, parsley flakes, basil, and pepper.
1. Put all of the chicken pieces in the egg mixture and marinate for 30 minutes
1. Cover the chicken with corn flakes and cheese
1. Cook the chicken nuggets in a small oven with the butter for 9-10 minutes. Put 12 chicken nuggets onto the 2 plates.
1. Put 1 tsp of ketchup and mustard, 1 cup of rice, and 1/2 cup of tomatoes onto one of the 2 plates
1. Enjoy warm!
