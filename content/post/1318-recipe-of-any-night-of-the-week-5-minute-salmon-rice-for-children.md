##### The ingredients needed to make 5 Minute Salmon Rice for Children:

1. Prepare Handful frozen peas
1. Take 150 mls boiling water
1. Make ready 1/8 chicken stock cube
1. Get 1 tsp cornflour
1. Make ready 2 tsp soy sauce
1. Prepare 1 salmon fillet, skinned and sliced
1. Make ready 1 bag frozen microwave rice
1. Prepare 2 tsp tomato purée




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make 5 Minute Salmon Rice for Children:

1. Get the rice cooking in the microwave, according to the manufacturers instructions. The one I use takes 4 minutes.
1. While the rice is cooking, add the peas, stock cube and water to a small frying pan. Cook the peas in the stock for a couple of minutes.
1. Add the soy sauce, tomato purée and cornflour, bring it up to a boil and stir while it thickens up.
1. Add your salmon fillet slices to the sauce and stir gently for a minute or so until the salmon is cooked through (it really only takes about a minute). Nb. make sure you’ve checked carefully for bones before you add the salmon in.
1. Serve the salmon sauce on top of the rice, ta da! 5 minute salmon rice!
