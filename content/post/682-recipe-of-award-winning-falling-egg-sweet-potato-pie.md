##### The ingredients needed to make Falling Egg Sweet Potato Pie:

1. Take 4 sweet potatoes
1. Prepare 1 cup white sugar
1. Make ready 6 oz unsalted sweet cream butter
1. Take 3 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1 tsp nutmeg
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Take 1 cup evaporated milk
1. Take 2 eggs beaten
1. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Falling Egg Sweet Potato Pie:

1. preheat oven to 400°F
1. boil sweet potatoes about 45 min (or until fork goes through w/o getting stuck)
1. drain potatoes and peel under cold water
1. mash potatoes; add all ingredients (mixing after each)
1. pour into crust evenly
1. bake @ 400°F for 10 min
1. reduce temperature to 375°F; bake 1 hour
