<h3>The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Shepard&#39;s Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 lb. of hamburger. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of small onion, chopped. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 (15 oz) of can tomato sauce. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 (15.25 oz) of cans corn, drained. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 6 of medium red potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 3/4 cup of milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of sour cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Seasonings to taste: salt, pepper, garlic powder &amp; parsley. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Cheesy Shepard&#39;s Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cook hamburger along with onion and seasonings until browned; drain and return to skillet. Add tomato sauce. Simmer on low for 10 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Meanwhile, boil potatoes in water until tender..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Drain off water; return to pot and mash with milk, sour cream and salt &amp; pepper..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a 9x13 baking dish layer the ingredients as follows: start with the hamburger mixture..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Next top with the cans of corn..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread mashed potatoes evenly over the top..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			And top with the cheese last..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 30 minutes uncovered or until hot and bubbly. Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
