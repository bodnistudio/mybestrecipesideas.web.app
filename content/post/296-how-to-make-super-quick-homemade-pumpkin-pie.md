##### Instructions to make Pumpkin pie:

1. Preheat oven to 180ºC
1. Dice pumpkin into cubes
1. (Optional) Bake pumpkin for 40min till soft (to be mashed)
1. Put baked pumpkin into a bowl (if not baked - into blender), mash it with a fork
1. Add to pumpkin eggs, flours, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, baking powder, olive oil - mix ingredients
1. Pour into baking mould and put into the oven.
1. Bake at 180ºC for about 30-35 minutes (check with toothpick or knife: it should be dry)
1. (Optional) Pour icing: mix sourcream or youghurt, lemon and tangerine zest, lemon juice and honey.
1. Enjoy this mild flavoury pie! :) Recipe based on Jamie Oliver&#39;s squash muffins.
