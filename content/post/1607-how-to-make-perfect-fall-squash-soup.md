##### The ingredients needed to make Fall Squash Soup:

1. Make ready 3 each butternut squash
1. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
1. Get 1 medium onion, diced
1. Get 2 clove garlic, sliced
1. Make ready 1 tsp thyme, dried
1. Take 1 broth
1. Make ready 1 apple cider vinegar
1. Prepare 1 salt
1. Prepare 1 pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Fall Squash Soup:

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
1. Cut squash in half. Scoop out and discard seeds.
1. Place squash on a baking tray, cut-side down. Bake until tender (35-45 minutes).
1. Remove skin and stem. Don&#39;t be too meticulous about removing the skin. We&#39;ll pass the squash through a sieve later.
1. Heat oil in a pan. Saute onion until tender.
1. Add garlic and thyme. Cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant.
1. Transfer to a blender. Add squash. Add about 1 cup of stock. Pulse on low speed until pureed. You may need to work in 2 batches.
1. Adding broth to desired consistency, blend on high speed for about 45-60 seconds or until smooth.
1. Pour into a soup pot and heat gently, stirring often.
1. Add apple cider vinegar, salt and pepper to taste.
1. Garnish and serve.
