##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Fried Egg with Chinese Chives and Pork:

1. Take  Eggs
1. Make ready  Thinly sliced pork belly
1. Take  Chinese chives
1. Get  Mentsuyu (x3 concentrate)
1. Get  Water
1. Take  Chicken soup stock granules
1. Make ready  Vegetable oil, salt and pepper
1. Prepare  Ichimi spice, sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Fried Egg with Chinese Chives and Pork:

1. Cut the pork into bite sized pieces and chop the Chinese chive into 3-4 cm. Beat the eggs and mix the chicken stock.
1. Heat the pan with oil and saute the pork.  Lightly season with salt and pepper and add the Chinese chive. Turn up the heat to high and pour in the eggs.
1. Scramble with chopsticks and spread out the egg mixture evenly on the pan.  Turn heat to low and continue cooking the egg mixture to your desired firmness. I like semi-cooked eggs.
1. Serve on a plate. Drizzle a mixture of mentsuyu and water on top of the egg.  Season with sesame seeds and ichimi spice as you like.
