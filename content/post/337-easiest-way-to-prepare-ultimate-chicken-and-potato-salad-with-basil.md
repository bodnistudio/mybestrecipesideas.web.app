##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Potato Salad with Basil:

1. Get 6 Potatoes
1. Get 1 large Chicken breast
1. Make ready 100 grams Bacon (block)
1. Take 50 grams Red kidney beans
1. Prepare 50 grams Chickpeas
1. Make ready 6 leaves ◎Basil
1. Prepare 40 grams ◎Mayonnaise
1. Get 40 grams ◎Heavy Cream
1. Make ready 1 dash ◎Salt and pepper
1. Take 1 dash ◎Nutmeg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken and Potato Salad with Basil:

1. Cook the potatoes in the microwave for about 15 minutes at 500w.
1. Once the potatoes are cooked, peel and quarter them.
1. Boil the chicken and cut into bite-size pieces.
1. Cut the bacon into bite-size pieces.
1. Cook the bacon in a frying pan.
1. Finely cut the basil and combine with all the ◎ marked ingredients.  (You could also use a food processor.)
1. Combine the basil sauce with the potatoes and chicken.
1. Add in the cooked bacon, red kidney beans and chickpeas.
1. Finished.
