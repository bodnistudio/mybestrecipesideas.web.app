##### The ingredients needed to make Poblano Steak:

1. Get 2 6 Oz Steak
1. Make ready 1/2 cup mushrooms
1. Get pinch Kosher salt
1. Take dash ground pepper
1. Take 2 large poblano peppers
1. Get 1/4 stick butter
1. Make ready 1 bunch green onion
1. Prepare clove garlic




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Poblano Steak:

1. Dice poblano peppers and set aside. Cut butter into small slices. Dice green onions about 1/8 cup.
1. Season steaks with kosher salt and pepper.
1. Add steaks to hot pan with olive oil  along with peppers, green onions, and crushed garlic.
1. Add mushrooms after steaks are seared on both sides. Add butter to pan and baste for 1 minute.
1. Cook until medium well or your preference
