##### Instructions to make Cappuccino Coffee:

1. Take coffee in a bowl,add sugar and 2 tsp water.Blend with an electric blender for 2 minutes on low speed.You may blend by hand also.
1. Add more 2 tsp water and further blend on medium to high speed for 4-5 minutes.Coffee mixture will turn to creamy and colour will also lighten.
1. Meanwhile boil milk in a pan.Take 1.5 tsp coffee mix into a mug/cup.Add hot milk gradually to the mug.
1. Coffee mix floats on the upper surface.Gently mix the coffee with a spoon.
1. Take 1 tbsp chocolate syrup in a spoon,apply over coffee in circular motion.Make design with a toothpick.
1. You may store remaining coffee mix for 2-3days in a fridge.
1. Now your cappuccino coffee is ready to serve.
