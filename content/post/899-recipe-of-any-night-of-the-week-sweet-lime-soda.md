##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Lime Soda:

1. Get 5 sweet lime
1. Get 1 cup Soda
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp roasted cumin seeds powder
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp black salt
1. Make ready 1 tsp Sugar (optional)

##### Steps to make Sweet Lime Soda:

1. Take out the juice of sweet lime with the help of juicer or any other source you are comfortable with.
1. Use fresh juice and not canned.
1. Add 1 cup soda and ice cubes optional.
1. Add roasted cumin seeds powder and black salt.
1. You can add a teaspoon of sugar if you want it to be extra sweet.
1. Mix and serve immediately.
