<h3>The ingredients needed to make Chicken Ala King Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 cup of shredded cooked chicken breast. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 of whole onion finely chopped. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 pack of frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, corn and green peas). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 block of shredded quick melt cheese. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of all purpose flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of all purpose cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of melted butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 cup of mashed potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 of large egg beaten. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Chicken Ala King Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Sauté chopped onion in butter then add shredded chicken breast..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add frozen vegetables and sauté for another 2 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix all purpose flour and all purpose cream and add to mixture. (You can replace cream with chicken stock if you don’t want it too creamy).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add cheese and turn off the heat when the cheese melts..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Flatten the mixture in a butter greased baking dish..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Top the dish with the mashed potato completely covering the top..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Brush the mashed potato with egg wash and bake under 250 degrees for 10 to 15 mins or until the mashed potato turns golden brown..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
