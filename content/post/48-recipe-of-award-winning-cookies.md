##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies:

1. Prepare 3/4 cup All-purpose flour
1. Get Pinch Red colour
1. Take As required Butter
1. Get 1/4 tbsp Vanilla essence
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Milk
1. Make ready As required Powdered sugar
1. Take 1 packet Eno fruit salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cookies:

1. Take a bowl add butter and powdered sugar. Mix it well. Now add vanilla essence, and flour in it. Mix it well.
1. Now divide it in two parts in this ratio 2:1. Now add milk in each parts add prepare nice dough.
1. Add red colour in large part. Roll out it. Cut small round from it. Layer round one top of each other with light layer of water in between. Refrigerator it for 30 minutes.
1. Remove it from the refrigerator and cover it with white dough. Again put it in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
1. Now cut it. Preheat pan for 10 minutes. Now put a plate with paper.Now arrange all balls on it.
1. Cook it for 30 minutes on low flame or till it change it colour. Now cool down it completely.
1. Yummy and delicious Cookies are ready to serve. Enjoy it.
