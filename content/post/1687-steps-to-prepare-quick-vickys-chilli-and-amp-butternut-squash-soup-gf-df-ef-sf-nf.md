##### Steps to make Vickys Chilli &amp; Butternut Squash Soup, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Preheat the oven to gas 6 / 200C / 400F and line a roasting tin with foil
1. Spread the cubed squash over the tin, drizzle with the oil, season with salt &amp; pepper and roast for 20 minutes
1. Meanwhile, melt the sunflower spread in a large saucepan and gently cook the onion, garlic and one of the chillies for 5 - 10 minutes over a low heat until the onion is translucent
1. Add the cubed squash to the pan with the stock and bring to the boil
1. Use a stick blender to puree the soup completely smooth then stir in the cream. Alternatively puree in batches in a jug blender
1. Serve immediately with a swirl of extra cream and the remaining chopped chilli sprinkled on top
