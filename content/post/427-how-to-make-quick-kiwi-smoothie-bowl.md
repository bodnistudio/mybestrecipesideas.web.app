##### The ingredients needed to make Kiwi Smoothie Bowl:

1. Prepare 3 frozen kiwi
1. Take 1 cup chilled yogurt (curd)
1. Prepare 1 tsp honey
1. Take 4 tsp granola
1. Make ready 1 tsp chia seeds
1. Make ready as needed Pomegranate seeds, for garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Kiwi Smoothie Bowl:

1. Chop the frozen kiwis.
1. Take a mixer jar and add the frozen kiwis, yogurt and honey and blend.
1. Blend till smooth.
1. Pour the thick mixture in a bowl.
1. Garnish with granola, chia seeds and pomegranate seeds and serve immediately.
