##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Steak, Crunchy Potatoes &amp; Vegetables!:

1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Minced Garlic
1. Make ready 3 medium Carrots
1. Prepare 3 Corn Cobs
1. Get 400 grams Frozen Peas
1. Take 5 large Potatoes cubed
1. Get 1 tbsp Chives
1. Make ready 2 1/2 tsp salt
1. Make ready 250 grams Unsalted Butter
1. Prepare 3 large Rump Steak




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Garlic Butter Steak, Crunchy Potatoes &amp; Vegetables!:

1. 1- Slice Carrots &amp; Add Corn Cobbs, frozen Peas &amp; Carrots To steamer or pot &amp; cook for 15-20 minutes or until cooked.
1. 2- Preheat deep fryer to hot chips setting or fairly high. Whilst heating up, Season Cubed potatoes with chives &amp; salt. Once the fryer is hot Add potatoes &amp; cook until golden Brown.
1. 3- Heat Pan &amp; cook Steak Until Desired. Whilst steak is cooking mix Garlic &amp; Softened butter until smooth, scoop in to 3 separate ball piles &amp; put in the freezer to firm until the steaks are ready.
1. 4- When Everything is cooked serve &amp; Add a scoop of garlic butter on each steak.
1. 5- Sit Back &amp; Enjoy :)
