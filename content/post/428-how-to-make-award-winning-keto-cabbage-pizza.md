##### Steps to make Keto cabbage pizza:

1. Place the cabbage, green pepper and garlic in a food processor and grate until fine.
1. Remove and drain the water out with a clean kitchen cotton towel
1. Place in a bowl and add the eggs, parmesan cheese, baking powder, chilli and salt and pepper to taste.
1. Divide mixture in two. Place in a rectangular or round microwavable turperware and microwave on high for about six minutes or until firm
1. On a stove top, place a nonstick pan with a tablespoon of coconut oil. Place the firm pizza and fry for two minutes on each side
1. Top with your desired toppings and cheese
1. Serve hot and enjoy
