##### The ingredients needed to make Ham Steak with Orange Butter:

1. Make ready 2 slice Ham (for steak)
1. Make ready 1 dash Olive oil
1. Prepare 1 dash Garlic (finely chopped)
1. Make ready 1 Black pepper
1. Take 20 grams Butter
1. Take 100 ml ☆ Orange juice (100%)
1. Take 1 tsp ☆ Maple syrup (or honey)
1. Take 1/2 tsp ☆Consommé bouillon cubes
1. Prepare 1 tsp ☆ Whole-grain mustard
1. Make ready 1 tsp ※ Katakuriko slurry

##### Steps to make Ham Steak with Orange Butter:

1. Fry both sides of the ham steaks in olive oil and garlic. Sprinkle on black pepper and plate. Cook the ham to your desired degree of doneness.
1. Add butter to the same frying pan. When it&#39;s melted, add ingredients marked ☆ and lightly simmer while shaking the pan.
1. Add katakuriko, and when it has thickened, pour it over the ham. Done!
1. Sprinkle on parsley if you have it.
