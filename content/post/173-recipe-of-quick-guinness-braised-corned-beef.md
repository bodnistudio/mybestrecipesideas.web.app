1. Once the Guinness has formed a reduction, wipe it up with the biscuit, and then place brisket into a universal pan (or Dutch oven, or roasting pan)
1. Add another can of Guinness to the skillet and deglaze.
1. Peel and crush a whole garlic.
1. Add to skillet, then add another can of Guinness, once it has been reduced.
1. As some coarse salt, ground pepper, and Stone mustard to your brisket.
1. Turn off heat, deglazes and add liquid smoke.
1. Add the little stupid pack of seasoning that your brisket came with.
1. Pour on Guinness garlic mixture
