<h3>The ingredients needed to make Easy Shepherds Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 lbs of ground beef. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 Tbsp of garlic. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 Tbsp of cavendars. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 Tbsp of thyme. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 Tbsp of Rosemary. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 Tbsp of Worsteshire sauce. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 cup of red wine. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of chicken broth. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 bag of frozen mixed veggies. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 6 oz of can tomato paste. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 5 of large potatoes. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 of egg yolk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of Parmesan. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Easy Shepherds Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Prehate oven to 400. Boil potatoes and make mashed potatoes. Salt and pepper to taste.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In large skillet, cook ground beef through. Add garlic, spices, Worsteshire, wine, chicken broth. Simmer until most liquid evaporates.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add tomato paste and veggies. Simmer until veggies are thawed..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add two yolks and Parmesan to mashed potatoes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put meat mixture in baking dish and top with mashed potatoes. Sprinkle Parmesan on top. Bake for 20 minutes. Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
