##### The ingredients needed to make Soba Inari:

1. Take 2 x 90g Dry Soba Noodles
1. Get 12 pieces ‘Inari-zushi no Moto’ (Seasoned Abura-age)
1. Take 1 Egg *cooked into a thin omelette
1. Prepare 1/2-1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds
1. Prepare 1 Spring Onion *finely chopped
1. Make ready  Sauce
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Dashi Stock *OR 1/4 cup Water &amp; 1/8 teaspoon Dashi Powder
1. Prepare 1 &amp; 1/2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
1. Prepare 1 tablespoon Mirin




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Soba Inari:

1. Combine Dashi Stock, Soy Sauce and Mirin in a small saucepan, bring to the boil, and simmer for a few minutes. Then allow to cool.
1. Cook a thin omelette and cut into thin noodles.
1. Warm ‘Inari-zushi no Moto’ (Seasoned Abura-age) to soften, lightly squeeze, carefully open the bags.
1. Cook Soba Noodles as instructed. DO NOT add salt into the water. Drain, rinse well in COLD water, and drain well again.
1. Combine drained Soba Noodles, Egg, Toasted Sesame Seeds and the cooled sauce. Fill up Abura-age bags with noodle mixture. Discard the excess sauce.
1. Place some finely chopped Spring Onion on top of each piece.
