##### The ingredients needed to make BBQ Island Sauce:

1. Take 1/2 cup Original Bold BBQ sauce
1. Get 1/2 cup Ketchup
1. Take 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Pineapple Juice
1. Make ready 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
1. Take 1 tsp Ready to use Minced Garlic




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make BBQ Island Sauce:

1. Put 1/2 cup of ketchup in mixing bowl.
1. Put 1/2 cup of Original Bold BBQ sauce in same mixing bowl.
1. Add 2 tbsp of brown sugar to ketchup and BBQ sauce. Stir together.
1. Add in 2 tsp of Vanilla Extract to mixture and stir.
1. Put in 1 tsp of ready to use minced garlic and stir.
1. Voila! You have BBQ island sauce. This sauce can be put on pork, steak, chicken, almost every kind of meat.
