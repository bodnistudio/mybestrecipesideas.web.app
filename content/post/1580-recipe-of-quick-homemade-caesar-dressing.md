##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade Caesar Dressing:

1. Prepare TBS. anchovy paste
1. Take 1 large clove of garlic, minced
1. Take Tsp. salt
1. Take 3/4 tsp. black pepper
1. Make ready 1 egg yolk from a large egg
1. Get 2 TBS. lemon juice
1. Get Tsp. Dijon mustard
1. Prepare 1/2 cup vegetable oil
1. Prepare 1 handful grated Parmesan cheese
1. Take 2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1. Get 1 splash hot sauce (to your liking)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Homemade Caesar Dressing:

1. Mince the garlic and add to bowl.
1. Add anchovy paste.
1. Add salt, pepper and egg yolk.
1. Add lemon juice and Dijon mustard.
1. Add vegetable oil, Parmesan cheese, Worcestershire sauce and hot sauce and whisk until creamy.
1. Refrigerate until needed.
