<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3/4 cup of sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tbsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 tsp of ground ginger. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 tsp of ground cloves. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 large of eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 can of pumpkin (15 oz). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 can of evaporated milk (12 oz). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 425°F..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix sugar, salt, and spices in a small bowl. Beat eggs in a large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and spice/sugar mixture. Gradually add evaporated milk..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into unbaked pie shell..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake in a preheated 425°F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temp to 350, bake 40-50 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
