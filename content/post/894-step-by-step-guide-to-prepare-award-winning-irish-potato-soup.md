##### The ingredients needed to make Irish Potato Soup:

1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
1. Make ready 1 medium onion sliced thin
1. Get 3 leeks sliced
1. Get 3 large baking potatoes, peeled and cut into quarter inch slices
1. Prepare 3 can (14.5 oz) chicken stock
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
1. Get 1 toppings: Cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, sliced chives




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Irish Potato Soup:

1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat; stir in onion and leek. Cover and cook 20 minutes. Stir in potato; cover and cook 15 minutes.
1. Stir in broth, salt, and pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes or until potato is tender. Remove from heat, and cool slightly
1. Process soup in batches in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides; return to saucepan, and cook over medium heat until thoroughly heated. Serve with desired toppings
