##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Sandwich:

1. Get 1 kosher pickle
1. Prepare 2 slices swiss cheese I used tillamook
1. Get 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
1. Prepare 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1. Get 1-1/2 vine ripened tomatoes
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons sliced black olives
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
1. Make ready 1 hoagie bun
1. Take To taste salt and ground black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Tomato Sandwich:

1. Slice the tomatoes thin. Preheat the oven on hi broil. Add mayonnaise and dijon mustard to the hoagie bun. Add tomato slices on both sides of the bun. Season with salt and pepper. Toast in the oven 3 minutes.
1. Lay two slices of cheese on top of the tomatoes. Add pickles, olives, and parmesan cheese.
1. Put under the broiler again for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted well. I hope you enjoy!!!!
