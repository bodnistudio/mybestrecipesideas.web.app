##### The ingredients needed to make Organic Banana Peanut Butter Ice Cream:

1. Prepare 6 organic bananas
1. Get 1/2 cup organic peanut butter
1. Make ready 1/2 cup organic milk, chocolate milk, or almond milk
1. Prepare Handful chocolate chips




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Organic Banana Peanut Butter Ice Cream:

1. Slice bananas and freeze for at least 2 hours.
1. Place frozen bananas in food processor or blender and add peanut butter and milk.
1. Blend until ingredients are well mixed. Pulse in chocolate chips.
1. Pour into container and freezer for at least 4 hours.
