##### The ingredients needed to make My Aberdeen Angus Steak with Grilled Hallumi Cheese and Sauce:

1. Get 2 thick Slices of Hallumi Cheese

##### Steps to make My Aberdeen Angus Steak with Grilled Hallumi Cheese and Sauce:

1. Add the Steak Burgers to a dry Frying pan, then add 2 tsp Butter to stop the burger drying out
1. Cook until cooked medium for 5 - 10 minutes
1. When cooked add to a plate and then grill the hallumi cheese
1. Add the sauce to the top bun
1. Then add the salad to the top bun
1. Add the sliced Grilled Hallumi cheese to the steak burger
