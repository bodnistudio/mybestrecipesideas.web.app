##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Potato Soup:

1. Prepare 6 bacon strips, diced
1. Take 3 cup cubed peeled potatoes (about 3 medium)
1. Make ready 1 can 14 1/2 oz chicken broth
1. Take 1/3 cup grated carrots
1. Take 1/2 cup chopped onion
1. Take 1 tbsp parsley flakes
1. Get 1/2 tsp each celery seed, salt, &amp; pepper
1. Prepare 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
1. Get 3 cup milk
1. Make ready 8 oz velveeta cheese, shredded or cubed
1. Take 2 green onions, thinly sliced (optional)

##### Instructions to make Creamy Potato Soup:

1. In large sauce pan, cook bacon until crisp, drain. Same pot add potatoes, broth, carrots, onion, parsley, celery seed, salt and pepper.
1. Cover and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes on medium heat.
1. Combine flour and milk, slowly until smooth; add to soup. Bring to boil and stir for 2 minutes.
1. Add cheese, stir until melted and soup is heated through.
1. Garnish with green onions. (Optional)
