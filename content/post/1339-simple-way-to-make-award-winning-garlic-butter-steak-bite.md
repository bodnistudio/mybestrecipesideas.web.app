##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Steak Bite:

1. Get 350 gr beef diced
1. Get 5 cloves garlic
1. Get 4 tbsp butter
1. Make ready  parsley
1. Take  Marinade:
1. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp onion powder
1. Take 1 tbsp smoke paprika
1. Get 1 tbsp oregano
1. Make ready 1 tbsp thyme
1. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
1. Get pinch salt
1. Make ready 1 tbsp pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Garlic Butter Steak Bite:

1. Mix the beef with garlic powder, onion powder, smoke paprika, oregano, thyme, soy sauce, salt and pepper. Let marinate the beef for at least 30 minutes
1. Prepare the hot pan with butter and pan the beef just 1 minute for each side (medium well), set aside
1. In the same pan, put the butter and garlic, fry it about 2 minutes and then put the beef back to the pan, turn off the heat
1. Finish it with fresh parsley on top
