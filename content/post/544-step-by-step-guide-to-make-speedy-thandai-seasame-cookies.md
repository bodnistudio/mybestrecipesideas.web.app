##### The ingredients needed to make Thandai Seasame cookies:

1. Get 1 cup whole wheat flour
1. Make ready 1/3 cup sugar powder
1. Take 2 tbsp desi ghee
1. Make ready 2 tbsp unflavoured oil
1. Prepare 2 tbsp+1tbsp sesame seeds
1. Take 3 tbsp milk
1. Take 2 tbsp thandai paste
1. Prepare  Cardamon powder a pinch (optional)
1. Get 1 pinch Salt
1. Take 1/4 tsp Baking soda

##### Instructions to make Thandai Seasame cookies:

1. Add flour,sugar powder,salt,ghee, a part of sesame seeds,baking soda and oil to a bowl. Mix well with your fingers gently
1. Dilute the thandai paste in the milk. Preheat oven to 180째.
1. Add it to the flour gradually and gather to form a dough.
1. Pinch small lemon sized balls,roll and flatten on a plate of remaining sesame seeds.
1. Do it only for one side and place on a baking tray lined with parchment.
