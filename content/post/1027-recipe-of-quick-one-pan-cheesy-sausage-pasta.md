##### The ingredients needed to make One Pan Cheesy Sausage Pasta:

1. Get 8 oz Pasta
1. Make ready 2 Chicken Sausage Links
1. Take 1 can 10 oz Tomatoes
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
1. Take 1 can Chicken Broth (or 2 cups)
1. Take 1 cup Onion
1. Make ready 2 clove Garlic
1. Get 1 cup Mozzarella Cheese
1. Take  Salt and Pepper
1. Take 2 tbsp Olive Oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make One Pan Cheesy Sausage Pasta:

1. Heat onions and sausage in olive oil.
1. Mince the garlic and add to the onions and sausage.
1. Add can of tomatoes.
1. Then your cream.
1. Then the chicken broth.
1. Then your noodles.
1. Then the cheese
