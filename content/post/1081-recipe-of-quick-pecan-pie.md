<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take  of 3-1/4 oz Package Jell-O Vanilla Puddng &amp; Pie Filling. </li>
	
		<li>{Get  of Dark Corn Syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare  of Evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Egg Slightly Beaten. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Chopped Pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready  of Unbaked 8-in Pie Shell. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>

<h3>Steps to make Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Blend pie filling mix with corn syrup.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Gradually stir in evaporated milk and egg.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			add Pecans.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour into pie shell. Bake at 375 until top is firm and just begins to crack - about 40 minutes.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cool at least 3 hours. Garnish.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
