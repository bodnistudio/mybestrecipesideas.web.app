<h3>The ingredients needed to make 1-Pan Easy Chicken Pot Pie Bake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 3 cups of shredded deli rotisserie chicken. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2-10.5 oz of cans cream of chicken soup. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 cups of Colby jack cheese. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 cups of frozen veggies (corn carrots and peas). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tablespoons of melted butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 can of buttery grands biscuits. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare to taste of Salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder, and Italian seasoning. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make 1-Pan Easy Chicken Pot Pie Bake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease a large cast iron skillet or 13x9 baking dish..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix chicken, soup, cheese, veggies and spices in a mixing bowl. spread into pan..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut biscuits into quarters and toss in melted butter. Place on top the mixture in baking pan. Bake 20 minutes until biscuits are cooked fully through. Garnish with melted butter and herbs..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
