##### The ingredients needed to make Peach Buckle:

1. Make ready  Batter-----------------
1. Get 1-1/2 cup all purpose flour
1. Get 1 cup sugar granulated
1. Get 1 tablespoon baking powder
1. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
1. Prepare 2 large eggs
1. Prepare 2 stick butter room temperature
1. Prepare  Peaches--------------------
1. Get 3-4 large peaches
1. Take  Topping---------------------
1. Take 1 tablespoon brown sugar
1. Take 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1. Get To taste vanilla ice cream optional




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Peach Buckle:

1. Preheat the oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit
1. Peel the peaches and slice each into 8 wedges.
1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. In the bowl of beat together the butter and granulated sugar, until smooth. Beat in the eggs, one at a time. Add the flour mixture and mix until smooth, I used a hand mixer.
1. Spray nonstick spray in an oven safe skillet 8-9 inch one. Heat the pan till it&#39;s warm through. Add the batter to the skillet.
1. Push the peach wedges into the batter. Cover the whole bottom. This way the peaches are in every bite.
1. Sprinkle the cinnamon and brown sugar on top of the buckle.
1. Bake for an hour check towards the end in case it&#39;s browning too soon. If it is cover and finish baking.
1. Let rest for about 20 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
