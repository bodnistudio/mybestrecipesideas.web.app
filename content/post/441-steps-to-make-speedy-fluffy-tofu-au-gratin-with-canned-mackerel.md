##### The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Tofu au Gratin with Canned Mackerel:

1. Get 1/2 Canned mackerel
1. Make ready 2 stick Asparagus
1. Make ready 1/4 pack Shimeji mushrooms
1. Make ready 1/4 Onion (sliced)
1. Make ready  Tofu Sauce
1. Make ready 1/2 block Tofu
1. Take 1 Egg (beaten)
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Take 1 dash Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Fluffy Tofu au Gratin with Canned Mackerel:

1. Make the tofu sauce. Beat the egg into a bowl and stir in the mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Add the tofu and crumble as you mix it in.
1. Scrape off the hard parts of the asparagus skin and cut into 4 cm pieces. Remove the root from the shimeji mushrooms and shred. Thinly slice the onion.
1. Break up the canned mackerel a bit and place in a casserole dish with the sauce from the tin as well.
1. Top with the asparagus, shimeji mushrooms, and onion from Step 2.
1. Pour over the tofu sauce from Step 1 and bake in a 230°C oven, or in the toaster oven for 15-20 minutes.
