##### Instructions to make Meat Sauce Doria (Rice Gratin) with Leftovers:

1. Finely chop the carrot and onion. Coat a frying pan with oil and stir-fry the carrot and onion until wilted.
1. Quickly mix in the butter, then add the rice, and stir-fry.
1. Lightly season with salt and pepper. Put the rice mixture in a gratin dish, then top it with the meat sauce.
1. Sprinkle on some melting cheese or Parmesan cheese, and bake in the oven or toaster oven until golden.
