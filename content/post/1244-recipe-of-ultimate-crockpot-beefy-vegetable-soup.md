##### Instructions to make Crockpot Beefy Vegetable Soup:

1. Season meat with spices and add to bottom of crockpot. You can brown meat before adding to crockpot if u like. I didnt because I was in a rush to get it cookin!
1. Drain all veggies except tomatoes and add to crock pot
1. Add diced potatoes
1. Pour in juice until it covers all veggies. Add sugar on top if u choose to use. Turn on low cook for 6 to 8 hours. Stirring occasionally
1. Serve with crackers, cornbread or rolls! Enjoy! Also u could use fresh veggies what ever floats your boat!
