##### The ingredients needed to make Summer Steak Salad:

1. Make ready  Steak
1. Prepare 6 oz steak
1. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
1. Take 1 tsp Montreal steak seasoning
1. Make ready  Salad
1. Take 2 cup sweet baby leaves
1. Make ready 1 pinch walnuts
1. Prepare 6 slice watermelon
1. Prepare  Topping
1. Make ready 1 Gorgonzola cheese crumbles, to taste
1. Make ready 1 raspberry vinagarette, to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Summer Steak Salad:

1. Preheat grill to 425°.
1. Coat steak (I used filet mignon) in garlic powder and steak seasoning.
1. Grill steak; I cooked for about 15 minutes for medium-rare.
1. Plate salad ingredients while steak is cooking.
1. Slice steak and plate on top of steak.
1. Add toppings and serve.
