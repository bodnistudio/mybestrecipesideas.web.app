##### The ingredients needed to make Brocolli Potato Soup:

1. Get 1 meduim potato,cubed
1. Prepare 2 cups brocolli,chopped
1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 1 meduim onion
1. Take 1 cup cooking cream
1. Take 3 cups water
1. Get  Labneh for garnish
1. Prepare  Salt and black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Brocolli Potato Soup:

1. In a cooking pot,heat olive oil then add potato and brocolli cover the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Then add the rest except labneh,cook for another 25 minutes or until potato tenderizes.
1. Stir some labneh and mix in the soup and the left put for garnish.
1. Put in bowl then garnish with laneh and cumak then serve.
1. Enjoy.
