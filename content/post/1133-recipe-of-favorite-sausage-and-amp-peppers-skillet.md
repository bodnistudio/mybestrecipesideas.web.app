##### Instructions to make Sausage &amp; Peppers Skillet:

1. Now while that cooks, you need to get the pasta started. I salt my water to prevent sticking. Also, slice up the chicken &amp; sausage. Don&#39;t forget to occasionally stir the onion &amp; peppers!!
1. Now add the meats to the peppers &amp; enjoy the heavenly aroma! Heat can be raised to med-med/high. U need that chicken cooked thoroughly!
1. Once pasta is boiling &amp; almost done, scoop out the water (unless you decide to use broth!) Pour desired amount into a bowl with bullion cubes &amp; mash until dissolved. Add to skillet with meat &amp; peppers.
1. Add the heavy cream to skillet &amp; stir to blend well. Then add parsley. This will thicken slightly
1. Drain pasta &amp; add to skillet. Gently toss &amp; fold in.
1. Once served, you may add the tomatoes but I chose to leave those out. Enjoy!!!
