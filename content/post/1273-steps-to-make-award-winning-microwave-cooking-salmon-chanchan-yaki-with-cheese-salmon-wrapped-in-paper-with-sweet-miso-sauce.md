##### The ingredients needed to make Microwave Cooking: Salmon Chanchan-yaki with Cheese (Salmon Wrapped in Paper with Sweet Miso Sauce):

1. Make ready 2 pieces Raw salmon
1. Make ready 3 leaves Cabbage
1. Make ready 1/2 Onion
1. Get 4 tbsp Pizza cheese
1. Get 2 tbsp ○Miso
1. Take 3 tbsp ○Sugar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp ○Mirin




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Microwave Cooking: Salmon Chanchan-yaki with Cheese (Salmon Wrapped in Paper with Sweet Miso Sauce):

1. Cut the cabbage into bite sized pieces. Slice the onion thinly. Put the cabbage, onion and salmon in that order on 2 sheets of parchment paper.
1. Mix the ○ ingredients together in a bowl and put on top of the salmon. Add the cheese.
1. Wrap up both paper parcels, and twist closed the ends like wrapped candy.
1. Microwave for about 8 minutes, and it&#39;s done.
