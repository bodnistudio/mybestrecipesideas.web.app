##### The ingredients needed to make No Bake Pumpkin Spice layered Dessert:

1. Get 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1. Make ready 1/4 cup melted butter
1. Take 1 can pumpkin (15 oz)
1. Get 2 pkg.3.4oz each jello vanilla instant pudding divided
1. Take 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1. Prepare 1 8 oz. tub cool whip thawed - divided
1. Get 1 1/2 cup plus 1 Tbsp. cold milk divided
1. Get 8 kraft carmels




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make No Bake Pumpkin Spice layered Dessert:

1. Mix graham crumbs and butter. Press onto bottom of 13 x9 dish.
1. Whisk pumpkin 1 pk. Pudding mix and spice in large bowl until blended. Stir in 2 cups cool whip spread over crust.
1. Beat remaining pudding and 1 and 1/2 cups milk in medium bowl with wisk 2 min. Spread over first layer. Cover with remaing cool whip. Refrigerate 4 hours.
1. Microwave carmels and remaing milk on high 30 seconds. Stir until carmels are completely melted and blended. Drizzle over before serving.
