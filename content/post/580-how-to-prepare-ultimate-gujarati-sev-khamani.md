##### The ingredients needed to make Gujarati Sev Khamani:

1. Get 1 cup gram flour (besan flour)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp semolina (rava)
1. Prepare 1 tbsp curd
1. Take as per taste Salt
1. Take 1/2 tsp ginger-chilli paste
1. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1. Prepare Pinch asafoetida
1. Take 1 tsp sugar
1. Prepare 1 tsp oil
1. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
1. Take 3/4 cup water or as required
1. Get 1 tsp/sachet eno
1. Get for tempering-
1. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1. Make ready Pinch asafoetida
1. Make ready 1 tsp sesame seeds
1. Make ready 7-8 curry leaves
1. Prepare 1 chilli slit
1. Get 1 tbsp powder sugar
1. Take 2-3 tbsp water
1. Prepare For garnishing-
1. Take Handful pomegranate seeds
1. Take as required Sev
1. Take As required chopped coriander leaves

##### Steps to make Gujarati Sev Khamani:

1. Sieve the gram flour and semolina in a bowl. Add all the ingredients except eno and lemon juice. Whisk the batter for 5 minutes making sure the batter is lump free. Keep aside the batter for 15 minutes.
1. Add eno and lemon juice to the batter and mix gently. Pour the batter into a greased container and steam for 15-20 minutes.
1. After 15-20 minutes check if its cooked using a toothpick or knife.
1. Let the dhokla cool then break it coarsely and keep aside.
1. Now for tempering, heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let it splutter. Then add hing, cumin, curry leaves, chilli, sesame and water.
1. Add the dhokla and powder sugar and mix well. Cook for a minute and turn off the flame.
1. Lastly, garnish with coriander leaves, pomegranate and sev. Serve hot.
