##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Ham &amp; Bean Soup:

1. Make ready 2 c cooked ham, chopped

1. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
1. Make ready 3 carrots, chopped
1. Get 3 stalks celery, chopped
1. Take 4 c chicken broth
1. Make ready 4 cans cannelinni beans
1. Make ready 1 tsp chilli powder

1. Get 1 bay leaf
1. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, minced

##### Instructions to make Quick Ham &amp; Bean Soup:
