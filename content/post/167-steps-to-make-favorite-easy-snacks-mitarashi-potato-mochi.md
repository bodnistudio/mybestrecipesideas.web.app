##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Snacks Mitarashi Potato Mochi:

1. Prepare 5 Potatoes
1. Take 4 tbsp Katakuriko
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ★Soy sauce
1. Get 2 tbsp ★Sugar
1. Get 1 tbsp ★Mirin
1. Prepare 1 Margarine




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Snacks Mitarashi Potato Mochi:

1. Boil, drain and mash the potatoes. Mix in the katakuriko.
1. Roll into slightly flat ovals ( I made 16 bite-sized pieces).
1. Grease a frying pan with margarine and cook the potato bites. When the katakuriko has browned, flip over, cover with a lid and steam. Mix all of the ingredients marked with ★ together to make the sauce, pour over the bites and toss to finish.
