##### The ingredients needed to make Potato And Wolfberries In Essence Of Chicken Soy Sauce:

1. Make ready 4 cups potato
1. Take  oil
1. Get 3 tbsp dried Wolfberries (Kei Chi)
1. Make ready 2 tbsp dark Soy Sauce
1. Get 4 tbsp light soy sauce
1. Make ready 1/4 cup water
1. Make ready 1 bottle Essence Of Chicken or 1/4 cuany Chicken stock available
1. Prepare 4 clove garlic




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Potato And Wolfberries In Essence Of Chicken Soy Sauce:

1. With oil deep fry the potato for about 5 minutes then drain dry
1. With a tbsp of oil pan fry the garlic for 30 seconds then add in the cooked potatoes
1. Add in the light soy sauce, dark Soy Sauce and stir fry for few seconds then add in the esssence of chicken and water and mix well
1. Add in the dried Wolfberries and Let it simmer for 5 minutes with lid on then off heat and serve it up
