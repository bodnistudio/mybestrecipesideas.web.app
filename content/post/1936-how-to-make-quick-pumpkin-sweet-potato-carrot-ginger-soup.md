##### The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin, Sweet Potato, Carrot, Ginger Soup:

1. Make ready 290 grams Carrots
1. Make ready 795 grams Pumpkin
1. Get 175 grams Onion
1. Get 580 grams Sweet Potato
1. Take 10 grams Ginger Root
1. Take 20 ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1. Make ready 1 liter Water
1. Get 250 grams Plain Greek Yogurt
1. Get 2 packages Vegetable Broth
1. Get 10 grams Salt
1. Get 5 grams Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pumpkin, Sweet Potato, Carrot, Ginger Soup:

1. Chop all raw vegetables into similar sized chunks.
1. Add olive oil and onions into bottom of a pot and cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add water, carrots, pumpkin, ginger, sweet potato, salt, and pepper to the pot.
1. Let cook until all vegetables are thoroughly cooked.
1. Purée the mixture with a hand blender.
1. Add the yogurt and let cook for additional 10 minutes.
1. Enjoy!
1. Makes 22 servings of 150g each. **be careful of the amount of ginger you add**
