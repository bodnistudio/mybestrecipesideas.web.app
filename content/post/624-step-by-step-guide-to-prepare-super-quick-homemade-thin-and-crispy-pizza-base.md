##### Instructions to make thin and crispy pizza base:

1. preheat oven to 220c
1. combine water and sugar in small bowl. sprinkle with yeast and set aside till frothing.
1. sift flour and salt into large bowl, make well in middle. add yeast mixture and oil. use wooden spoon to bring together, then use hands to complete.
1. turn dough on lightly floured surface and on kneed for 6 minutes. divide into desired amounts and cover with clean damp towel, and set aside for 5 minutes.
1. roll each portion out to line grease tray. bake for 8 minutes or until golden.
