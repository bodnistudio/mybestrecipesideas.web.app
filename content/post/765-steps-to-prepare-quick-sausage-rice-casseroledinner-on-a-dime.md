##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage Rice Casserole(Dinner On A Dime):

1. Take 2 packages Rice pilaf
1. Make ready 2 lb Pork sausage
1. Take 6 Celery ribs, chopped
1. Make ready 4 medium Carrots, sliced
1. Take 1 can Condensed cream of chicken soup
1. Prepare 1 can Condensed cream of mushroom soup
1. Take 2 tsp Onion powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sausage Rice Casserole(Dinner On A Dime):

1. Prepare rice mixes according to package directions
1. In a large skillet, cook the sausage, celery, and carrots over medium heat until meat is no longer pink
1. Drain
1. In a large bowl, combine the sausage mixture, rice, soups, onion powder, garlic powder, and pepper
1. Transfer to 2 greased 11x7x2 baking dishes
1. Cover and freeze one casserole for up to 3 months
1. Cover and bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes or until veggies are tender
1. For frozen casserole thaw in fridge overnight remove 30 minutes prior to baking
