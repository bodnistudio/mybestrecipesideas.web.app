<h3>The ingredients needed to make Chia Planks:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of Chia Seeds. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup of old fashion oats. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/3 cup of Bran Bud cereal. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tbsp of pumpkin spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tbsp of honey. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of hot water. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 6 of pitted figs - minced. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tbsp of vanilla extract. </li>
	
</ol>

<h3>Steps to make Chia Planks:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Heat oven to 325 and line medium cookie sheet with parchment paper..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Combine dry ingredients in large bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix wet ingredients in separate bowl,   let sit 10 minutes so figs get soft..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour wet into dry and stir till well mixed. Pour out on to lined pan. Spread mixture about to about a quarter inch thick..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake 30 minutes. Rove from oven cut into planks, carefully turn planks over and bake 30 more minutes. Let completely cool on cookie sheet..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
