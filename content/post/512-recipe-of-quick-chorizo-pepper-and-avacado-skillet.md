##### Instructions to make Chorizo,  Pepper and Avacado  Skillet:

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
1. In baking pan, combine radishes, 2 T oil, salt and pepper.  Roast for 20 minutes.
1. Reduce oven heat to 350°F.
1. In ovenproof skillet, heat 2 T oil.  Cook garlic and onion.  Add sausage and pepper, cook until sausage is no longer pink.
1. Add spinach, cover and cook until wilted.  Stir in radishes.
1. With large spoon, make for depressions evenly spaced.  Crack an egg in each depression and bake for 10-15 minutes, until eggs set.
1. Remove from oven, add salt and pepper to taste.  Sprinkle avocado on top and serve.
