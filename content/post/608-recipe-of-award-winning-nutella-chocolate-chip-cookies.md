##### The ingredients needed to make Nutella Chocolate chip Cookies:

1. Take 150 grams All purpose flour
1. Prepare 240 grams Nutella or any chocolate spread
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. Make ready 1/4 cup chocolate chips + additional to garnish




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Nutella Chocolate chip Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 180 and line baking tray with parchment paper
1. Mix Nutella, flour, 1/4 cup chocolate chips and egg until well combined in a bowl
1. Take 2 tbsp of Dough. Roll like ball between palms and place on baking tray. Gently flatten it with back of spoon or palms. Do this until dough is completed. Leave a gap of 3 cm between each Cookies.
1. This is optional step. Add few chocolate chip on top of each cookies. This will make it look beautiful
1. Bake for 8-10 minutes until they are cooked at edges and slightly soft at centre
1. Allow to cool completely. Serve or store in airtight container
