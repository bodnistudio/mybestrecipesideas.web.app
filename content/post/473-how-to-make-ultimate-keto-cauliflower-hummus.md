##### The ingredients needed to make Keto Cauliflower Hummus:

1. Take 250 g Chopped Cauliflower
1. Prepare 3 Tbsp Almond Butter
1. Take 5 Tbsp Olive Oil
1. Prepare 1 Tbsp Erythritol
1. Take 3 Tbsp Water
1. Prepare  Juice of half a Lemon
1. Get Pinch Sea Salt
1. Get 1 Tsp Cumin
1. Make ready 1 Tsp Paprika




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Keto Cauliflower Hummus:

1. Preheat oven to 160°C
1. Bake the chopped cauliflower for 15 Mins or until soft and turning light brown.
1. Blend all of the ingredients together and top with a little extra cumin and paprika.
1. Refrigerate until ready to use.
