##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato pie:

1. Prepare 1/2 cup white sugar
1. Get 1/4 cup light brown sugar
1. Prepare 1 can evaporated milk
1. Take 1 tbsp cinnamon
1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp nutmeg
1. Make ready 2 eggs
1. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Sweet Potato pie:

1. preheat oven 350°
1. bake 3 yams for about an hour (this will make 2 pies)
1. once the potatos are cooked and soft peel skin and drop in mixing bowl.
1. add eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg,  and vanilla
1. add mixture to food processor and mix till smooth
1. add to pie crust and bake for 35 to 45 min.
1. let cool and serve.
