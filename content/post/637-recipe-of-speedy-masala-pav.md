##### The ingredients needed to make Masala Pav:

1. Get 4 bread/ pav
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
1. Make ready 2 spoons oil
1. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
1. Make ready 2 Chopped onions
1. Prepare 1 Chopped tomatoes
1. Take 1 tbsp pav bhaji masala
1. Take 1 capsicum
1. Get 1 green chilli
1. Take 1/4 spoon garam masala
1. Get 1/4 tsp cumin powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp coriander powder Cheese cubes
1. Take 1 tsp Green chutney
1. Take As per taste Tomato ketchup Coriander leaves Sev Bhujia Pomegranate




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Masala Pav:

1. Take a oil in a pan and heat it. Then add onion and ginger garlic paste. Fry it and then add capsicum.
1. Add chilli powder and sweet corn and cook for a few minutes. Then add tomatoes and cook.
1. Add all the dry spices and pav-bhaji masala. Cool the bhaji and add lemon juice. Garnish it with coriander leaves.
1. Now butter the bread(pav) and toast it for a few minutes in a pan. You can use oil or butter.
1. Squeeze the bread lightly and spread the prepared masala. Add shredded cheese on top. You can also garnish it with finely chopped onions.
