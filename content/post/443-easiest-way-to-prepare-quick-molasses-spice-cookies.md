##### The ingredients needed to make Molasses Spice Cookies:

1. Get  Wet
1. Get 3/4 cup butter
1. Prepare 1 cup sugar
1. Take 1/4 cup molasses
1. Prepare 1 egg
1. Prepare  Dry
1. Make ready 2 cups flour
1. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp cloves
1. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
1. Make ready 1 tsp ground ginger
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Molasses Spice Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
1. Melt butter on stove or in microwave
1. Combine butter, sugar and molasses
1. Set mixture aside, allowing it to cool a little in the fridge
1. In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
1. Remove wet mixture from fridge and stir in the egg
1. Add dry ingredients to the wet batter a little at a time until fully mixed.
1. Form dough in roughly golf-ball sized balls.
1. Optional: For sweeter, crisper cookies, roll the balls in sugar before baking.
1. Bake on greased cookie sheet for 9 to 10 minutes.
