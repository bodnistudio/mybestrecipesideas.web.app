##### The ingredients needed to make Primal Skirt Steak Marinade:

1. Make ready 1/4 cup lime juice
1. Make ready 1/2 cup olive oil
1. Take 1 zest of 1 lime
1. Take 2 clove garlic
1. Get 1 tsp cumin
1. Prepare 2 tbsp salt, dissolved in 2 ounces water
1. Get 2 tsp honey




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Primal Skirt Steak Marinade:

1. Mince garlic.
1. Heat 2 TBSP salt in 2 ounces water until dissolved.
1. Add honey to hot salt water.
1. Zest lime, then squeeze 1/4 cup lime juice into measuring cup.
1. Add olive oil, lime zest, garlic, cumin, &amp; honey salt water to measuring cup.
1. Pour into zip top bag with meat and squeeze out air.
1. Refrigerate for 1 hour no more.
