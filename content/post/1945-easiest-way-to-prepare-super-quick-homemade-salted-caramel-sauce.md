##### Instructions to make Salted Caramel Sauce:

1. In a medium sauvepan, combine butter, sugar and cream. Whisk until melted
1. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to an even simmer, whisking often until thickened 5 to 7 minutes
1. Turn off heat and add vanilla and salt and mix well. Caramel will continue to thicken as it cools
1. Pour into container, serve warm or cold.
