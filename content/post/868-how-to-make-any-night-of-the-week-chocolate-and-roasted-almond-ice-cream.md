##### The ingredients needed to make Chocolate and Roasted Almond Ice-cream:

1. Take 500 ml Full fat milk
1. Prepare 1/4 cup Misri powder
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Cornflour
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Roasted Almond  (chopped)
1. Take 3-4 Tbsp Cocoa powder
1. Make ready 1 tsp Coffee powder 1/2 tsp (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chocolate and Roasted Almond Ice-cream:

1. Mix cornflour in milk then put it on heat, add misri powder, cocoa and coffee powder.
1. Add chopped roasted Almond 2 tbsp and mix.
1. When the mixture becomes little thickened turn off the flame and keep it to come in room temperature then pour it in a airtight container.
1. Add more Roasted Almonds on top to decorate.
1. And keep it in deep fridge for 8 hours or until the Ice-cream is set and it&#39;s ready.
1. Enjoy your scoops.
