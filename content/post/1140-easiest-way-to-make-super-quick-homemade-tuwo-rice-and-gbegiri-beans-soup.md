##### Instructions to make Tuwo Rice and gbegiri (beans soup):

1. Put water in a pot bring to boil, wash Rice and add it to the boiling water cook till soft and torn till smooth.
1. Remove the back of the beans wash very well pour in a clean pot bring to cook.
1. Pound scotch bonnet and onion together add to the beans leave to cook till soft.
1. Add seasoning, oil, a little salt to taste then wisk with a broom then serve with tuwo rice and stew.
