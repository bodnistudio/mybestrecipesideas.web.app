##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Stirfry with Chow Mein Noodles:

1. Prepare 1 red onion - sliced
1. Take 8 oz button or cremini mushrooms - thinly sliced
1. Take 1 red pepper - sliced
1. Take 1 yellow pepper - thinly sliced
1. Make ready 2 celery stalks - thinly sliced on an angle
1. Get 1 head broccoli - chopped into med-large florets
1. Take 200 grams snow peas or snap peas - strings removed
1. Prepare 5 clove garlic - chopped or crushed
1. Make ready 1 thumb sized piece of ginger - grated
1. Make ready 1 packages blue dragon stirfry sauce (or your favorite)
1. Take 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
1. Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce + more to taste (as needed)
1. Take 1 tsp sesame oil
1. Get 1 packages fresh chow mein noodles




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chicken Stirfry with Chow Mein Noodles:

1. Get all the veggies washed and prepped.  Group the veggies in bowls in the order you will add them to your skillet/wok (see photo). Cube chicken and place in a ziplock bag.
1. Add hoisin sauce, soy sauce and sesame oil and about a half tsp of the garlic and ginger to the chicken and let it marinate for at least half an hour
1. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil and add chow mein noodles. Keep an eye on them and use a fork to help break them up gently.
1. Bring a large skillet or wok to med-high heat and pour the contents of the chicken bag in.  Sautee the chicken until just cooked through.  Remove to a bowl and keep warm.
1. Turn heat up to high and let your skillet/wok heat up again. Once it&#39;s super hot,  start adding your veggies. Remember to keep them moving! Mushroom and onions first for about 5 mins.  Then add peppers and celery for another 5 minutes.  Finally, add broccoli, snow peas,  garlic and ginger and about 1/4 cup water.  Cover to let the broccoli soften a bit.
1. Your noodles should be softened by now - strain them.
1. Turn off heat but leave your skillet/wok on the burner. Add noodles, stirfry sauce and chicken back to the pan and mix it all together (I find tongs easiest).  Add more soy sauce if needed - as desired.
1. Enjoy!!
