##### The ingredients needed to make Korma Soupra (vegan):

1. Make ready 1 onion
1. Prepare 1 tsp ground coriander
1. Take 1 tsp ground turmeric
1. Get 1 tsp ground cumin
1. Take 4 cloves garlic
1. Make ready 1 thumb sized chunk ginger
1. Get 4 big sweet potatoes
1. Make ready 2 cups uncooked rice
1. Get 1 handful fresh coriander leaf
1. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
1. Prepare 1 finger chilli
1. Get 1 can coconut milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Korma Soupra (vegan):

1. Chop the onion as finely as you can and fry it in a little oil or margerine with the ground spices. Also chop the garlic and ginger then add those for the last minute.
1. Peel and cube the sweet potato, then add it to the pot with the rice and enough boiled water to cover them. Leave to simmer for 15mins.
1. When you can easily smoosh the potato against the inside of the pan with a spoon, mash with a masher or blender if you prefer.
1. In a small frying pan, heat some oil and then drop the cumin seeds and chilli in there for about 30seconds, until the cumin seeds are jumping and popping about. Put them straight onto a piece of kitchen roll to soak the oil.
1. Add the thick part of the coconut milk, the cumin seeds and chilli, and the fresh coriander, then it’s ready to serve! If it’s too thick, then add a bit of the water from the can of coconut milk and stir it in.
