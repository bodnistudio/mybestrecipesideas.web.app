##### The ingredients needed to make Cheap Quick Ramen Noodle Meal:

1. Make ready 4 packages Pork flavor Ramen Noodles (or any flavor you prefer, I used pork as I thought it&#39;d go great with the pork sausage)
1. Prepare 2 cans green beans (cut, French, Italian, whole- your preference)
1. Get 1 can corn
1. Make ready 16 oz roll of breakfast sausage (I used Kroger brand Mild Pork)
1. Prepare  OPTIONAL** frozen diced green bell peppers and onions




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cheap Quick Ramen Noodle Meal:

1. Cook and then drain sausage, set aside.
1. While sausage is browning, in a separate pot, boil Ramen according to package instructions. (Will be mushy if noodles are over cooked)
1. Leaving some water in noodles, Drain Ramen and add flavor packets and stir.
1. Add cooked sausage, veggies and any other preferred seasoning, stir all together. (To avoid over salting, keep in mind that it already has the included Ramen seasoning) Continue cooking over medium low heat until all veggies are warm and cooked.
1. Ready to serve.
1. OPTIONAL, at this point, you can add to a casserole dish and bake at 325 apx 15-20 minutes to serve as a casserole. I kept it in the pot and served as a ONE pot recipe.
