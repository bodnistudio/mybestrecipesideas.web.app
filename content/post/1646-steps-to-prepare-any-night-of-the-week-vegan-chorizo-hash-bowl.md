##### Instructions to make Vegan Chorizo Hash Bowl:

1. Cook the 1 1/2 cup of frozen hash brown. Follow the instructions on package and set aside.
1. Pour 1 tbsp of oil in a skillet, add onion and sauté until translucent
1. Cut Chorizo sausage into pieces and add to onions. Now add the chopped bell pepper, chopped green Chile, garlic pepper, and salt.
1. Put Cooked hash brown in a bowl, add the chorizo mix on top.
1. Top with avocado, ketchup and hot sauce (I used Cholula)
