##### The ingredients needed to make Kai Lan with carrots:

1. Prepare  Main
1. Get 300 grams hong kong kai lan, cut into bite size, keep stems and leaves separate
1. Get 1 carrot, sliced thinly
1. Prepare  Other
1. Prepare 3 tbsp light soya sauce
1. Prepare 4 tbsp cooking oil
1. Make ready 4 tbsp water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Kai Lan with carrots:

1. Heat cooking oil in frying pan using low heat. Stir fry carrots and kai lan stems till almost done.
1. Add in kai lan leaves. Stir fry using medium heat till leaves almost done.
1. Add in water and light soya sauce. Stir fry till all well cooked.
