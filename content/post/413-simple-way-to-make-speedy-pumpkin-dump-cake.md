<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Dump Cake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 (15 oz) of can pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 (12 oz) of can evaporated milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of light brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 3 of large eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 3 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 box of yellow cake mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 cup (2 sticks) of butter melted. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of coarsely crushed graham crackers or pecans. </li>
	
</ol>
