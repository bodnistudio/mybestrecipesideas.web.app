##### The ingredients needed to make Chipotlé Steak Chili:

1. Take 1 lb stew meat
1. Get 4 can tomato sauce
1. Take 1 can tomato paste
1. Take 2 large tomatoes
1. Make ready 2 each green bell peppers
1. Make ready 1 large sweet onion
1. Get 2 each chopped canned chipotlé
1. Make ready 3 clove minced garlic
1. Prepare 2 tbsp paprika
1. Make ready 2 tsp black pepper
1. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil
1. Get 1 each lime,  juiced and zest
1. Get 2 tsp chopped fresh cilantro
1. Take 1 salt/pepper to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Chipotlé Steak Chili:

1. brown stew meat over medium a high heat in large skillet, cook to medium
1. drain grease then add to crock pot or dutch oven
1. add tomatoes, green pepper, onion, garlic and chipotles
1. add tomato sauce and paste
1. stir everything together
1. cover and heat. Crock pot: high 2+ hours, low 4+hours. Stove top: low-medium 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally
