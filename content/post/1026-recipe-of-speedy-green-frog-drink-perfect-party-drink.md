##### The ingredients needed to make Green Frog Drink (Perfect Party Drink):

1. Take 1 Big Bottle WKD Blue
1. Take 1 Big Bottle Smirnoff Ice Red
1. Make ready 1 Big Bottle Bacardi Breezer Orange
1. Get 6 pints Glasses
1. Take 1 bag Of Ice
1. Make ready 6 Straws




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Green Frog Drink (Perfect Party Drink):

1. Fill each pint glass up half way with ice
1. Grab your big bottle of WKD Blue and split the drink between the 6 glasses
1. Do the same with the big bottle of Smirnoff Ice Red
1. Last big bottle Bacardi Breezer Orange split again but this time pour very slowly this is because it forms a 2 tone drink of blue and yellow
1. Grab 6 straws wack one in each glass stir the drink carefully until the drink turns green and ENJOY!!!
