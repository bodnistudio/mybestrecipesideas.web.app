##### Instructions to make Teriyaki Sauce:

1. In a small saucepan whisk together the brown sugar, soy sauce, pineapple juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
1. In a small dish on the side whisk together corn starch and water until smooth.
1. Slowly whisk corn starch mixture into sauce in pan.
1. Bring sauce to a boil until it just starts to thicken.
1. Remove from heat and reserve about 1/4 for basting later.
1. Add teriyaki to the meat you are marinating and chill in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before cooking.
