##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Steak With Sauce:

1. Prepare 400 grams Beef (filet, sirloin, etc)
1. Make ready 50 ml ●Red wine
1. Prepare 1/2 ●Grated onion
1. Take 2 clove ●Grated garlic
1. Prepare 3 tbsp ★Soy sauce
1. Get 1 tbsp ★Balsamic vinegar
1. Make ready 3 tbsp ★Sugar
1. Make ready 3 tbsp ★Mirin
1. Make ready 15 grams Butter
1. Make ready 1 tbsp each Vegetable oil and butter
1. Get 1 Salt, pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Beef Steak With Sauce:

1. Marinate the beef in ● ingredients. If the meat is tough, pound the meat to tenderize.
1. Heat vegetable oil and butter in a pan. Pan-fry the meat seasoned with salt and pepper right before cooking. Fry the surface over high heat first, then adjust the heat according to how you like the meat cooked.
1. Remove the fried beef from the pan, and wipe it. Pour in Step one marinade and ★ ingredients, then simmer. Add the butter to finish the sauce!
1. Pour the sauce on top of the meat and enjoy. It is also delicious to eat it in a rice bowl.
