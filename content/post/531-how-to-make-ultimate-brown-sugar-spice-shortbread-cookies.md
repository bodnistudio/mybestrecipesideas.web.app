<h3>The ingredients needed to make Brown Sugar Spice Shortbread Cookies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of Soft butter (salted). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of Brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/4 cup of Heavy cream. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 tsp of Vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of White flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 tsp of Pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready pinch of Salt. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Brown Sugar Spice Shortbread Cookies:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 375. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix butter and sugar until well blended..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Blend in cream..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Blend in vanilla..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Mix dry ingredients until well blended..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut dry ingredients into wet ingredients until cookie dough forms. Should be moist but not wet and form a slightly flaky ball. If too wet add 2Tbls flour..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Roll into 1&#34; balls and press down on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake at 375 for 10-13 minutes..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
