##### The ingredients needed to make Chipotle-Pineapple Flank Steak:

1. Take 1 can crushed pineapple
1. Get 1 tbsp onion powder
1. Get 1 tbsp smoked paprika
1. Get 1 tbsp cumin
1. Take 2 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1 salt and pepper
1. Make ready 1 cup chipotle salsa

##### Instructions to make Chipotle-Pineapple Flank Steak:

1. Marinate steak in chipotle and pineapple for up to 48 hours
1. Wash off marinade and pat steak dry. Mix dry spices together. Season Steak.  Grill approximately 8 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
