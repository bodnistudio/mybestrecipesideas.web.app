##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Cookies:

1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup shortening
1. Take 2 cup brown sugar
1. Prepare 2 eggs
1. Make ready 1/2 cup honey
1. Make ready 4 1/2 cup flour, sifted
1. Make ready 4 tsp baking soda
1. Get 1/2 tsp salt
1. Prepare 2 tsp cinnamon




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Honey Cookies:

1. Mix all ingredients
1. Chill dough for at least an hour
1. Roll dough into walnut size balls
1. Roll balls in sugar
