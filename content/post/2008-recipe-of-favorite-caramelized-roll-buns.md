##### The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Roll Buns:

1. Get  Star shaped sugar candy for deco
1. Take  Oil for frying
1. Take  Dough wet ingrediants
1. Prepare 1 packet dry yeast
1. Take 2 tblspn condensed milk
1. Take 2 tblspn white sugar
1. Get 1 cup luke warm water
1. Get  Dough dry ingrediants
1. Prepare 2 tblspn butter
1. Get 1 tspn salt
1. Get 1 tspn baking powder
1. Prepare 4 cups flour
1. Get  Caramel sauce
1. Prepare 2 cups Brown sugar
1. Make ready 1/2 cup milk

##### Steps to make Caramelized Roll Buns:

1. In a small bowl, mix all the wet ingrediants. Allow the yeast to activate.
1. Meanwhile, in a another bowl, mix all the dry ingrediant well. Set aside.
1. Mix both wet &amp; dry ingrediants. Allow it to sit for 1 hour (covered with a clean kitchen towel in a bowl). Beat (box using your fist) the dough after it rises. Let it sit and then set it aside.
1. Make Dough into little roll (round shape) buns. Deep fry in a hot pan. Let it cool.
1. In another sauce pan, pour in all the sauce ingrediants until golden brown and thicken. Let it cool.
1. Once the fried dough &amp; caramel sauce cools, coat the dough with the caramel sauce. Or you can dip it in! Sprinkle the star sugar candy on top. And it is ready to serve.
