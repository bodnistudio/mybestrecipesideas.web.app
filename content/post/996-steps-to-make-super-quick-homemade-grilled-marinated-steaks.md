##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Marinated Steaks:

1. Take  Main Ingredients
1. Get 3 Steaks
1. Prepare 1 1/3 cup Red Wine Vinegar
1. Get 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Montreal Steak Seasoning
1. Get 1 tsp Lawrys Blackened Pepper Season Salt
1. Take 1 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Dijon Mustard




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Grilled Marinated Steaks:

1. Marinate for at least 2 - 24hrs before cooking on your grill.
1. Combine all ingredients including Steaks in a 1 gallon zip lock freezer bag. once you combine everything make sure you shake the bag so every steak looks evenly marinated.
1. When finished marinating cook your steaks inside on an iron grill, or outside grill.
1. When you are done please take a picture we would love to see how your steak turned out
1. Prep time less than 5 min.
