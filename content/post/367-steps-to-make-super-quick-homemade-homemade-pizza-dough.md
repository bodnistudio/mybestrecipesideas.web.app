##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade Pizza Dough:

1. Make ready 650 g strong white flour
1. Get 325 ml warm water at 36 degrees
1. Take 50 ml warm milk
1. Make ready 50 ml extra virgin olive oil
1. Make ready 7 g Instant dried yeast




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Homemade Pizza Dough:

1. Combine your wet ingredients together in a jug and dissolve your yeast into the mixture.
1. Add to the flour and combine.
1. Fold out onto a floured surface and knead for around 5 minutes to achieve and smooth elastic dough.
1. Place in a bowl and cover with a damp cloth to allow to double in size.
1. Once doubled in size. Divide into 6 individual doughs and rest.
1. These are now ready to be rolled out or frozen for a later date.
