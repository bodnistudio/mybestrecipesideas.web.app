##### The ingredients needed to make Steak Marinade Taco Style:

1. Take 5 oz Worcestershire Sauce
1. Prepare 1 envelope Taco Seasoning
1. Get 1 tbsp Season Salt
1. Take 1 tsp Garlic Powder
1. Take 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
1. Get 4 Steaks




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Steak Marinade Taco Style:

1. In a gallon sized zip lock bag, combine all ingredients except for the steaks.
1. Seal the bag and massage it until all ingredients are mixed thoroughly.
1. Open bag and add steaks.
1. Reseal bag and marinate in the fridge for at least 12 hours.
1. When finished marinating, cook the steaks however you prefer to cook them. It works great for grilling or frying on the stove top.
