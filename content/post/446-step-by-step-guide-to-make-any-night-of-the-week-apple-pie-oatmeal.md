<h3>The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Oatmeal:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 cup of oatmeal. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 cup of milk (could be more). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1/2 of apple (cut into dice). </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of raisins (I used golden raisins and less than 1/4 cup). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 cup of pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds (I used less than it). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 7 of almonds (chopped). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 tsp of ground nutmeg. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 pinch of ground cloves (optional). </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tbs of honey. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tbs of brown sugar. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Apple Pie Oatmeal:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Boil in medium low heat all the ingredients (except honey), stir them all and add more liquid such as milk or water if needed to reach the consistency that you want..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Simmer around 5 minutes (could be less or more) or until the apple a bit soften..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Garnish with ground cinnamon and a lil bit drizzles of honey (if you use honey for glazing the top, make sure to reduce the brown sugar).
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
