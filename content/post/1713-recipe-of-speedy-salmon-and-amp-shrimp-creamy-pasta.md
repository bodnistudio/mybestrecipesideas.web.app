1. Begin boiling the spaghetti in salted water for the amount stated on the package.
1. Add the salmon flakes to Step 1 and season with salt and pepper. Continue stir-frying until the liquid evaporates. Then add a little less than 1 ladleful of water from boiling the spaghetti.
1. Then add the dashi stock granules and the heavy cream. Once it begins to boil, stop the heat.
