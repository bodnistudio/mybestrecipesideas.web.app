1. Get 1 Sea Salt and Pepper
1. Get  Stuffing
1. Get 1 cup Ricotta Cheese (low fat for healthier version!)
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Parmesan Cheese - grated
1. Take 1 as needed Bacon crumbles! (FRESH is best)
1. Make ready 1 tbsp Fresh Basil (chopped)
1. Make ready 1 clove Garlic (minced)
1. Get 1 dash Of Oregano
1. Take 1 small Handful of Baby Spinach (chopped)

1. Preheat oven to 400
1. Combine all ingredients for stuffing in a bowl and mix!
