##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Fresh Lemonade:

1. Take 1 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
1. Get 4 tbsp white sugar
1. Make ready 6 cups water




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Fresh Lemonade:

1. Squeeze 1 cup of fresh lemon juice through a strainer to remove seeds/ pulp (I use 4 large Meyer lemons)
1. Fill a cup measure with 2 cups water, then add sugar. Microwave for 45 seconds, then stir until sugar completely dissolved.
1. Put lemon juice, sugar water, and additional 4 cups of water into a large carafe and refrigerate. Shake before serving.
