##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Rosemary Pork Steak:

1. Prepare 1 kg Pork Steak
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Soy sauce
1. Get 4 tbsp Honey
1. Make ready  Salt and pepper
1. Make ready  Dried rosemary
1. Make ready 3 clove Minced garlic




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Grilled Rosemary Pork Steak:

1. Rub salt, pepper, dried rosemary and minced garlic to the steak. Pour in 1/2 cup of soy sauce (adjust if too much). Spread honey on each side of the steak. Keep in the refrigerator. Not in the freezer. Marinate overnight.
1. Grill each side until golden brown.
1. Perfection!
