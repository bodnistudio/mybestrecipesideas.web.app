##### The ingredients needed to make Birthday Cake Cookies 🎉🎈:

1. Make ready 1 1/4 ap flour
1. Prepare 11/4 WHITE cake mix
1. Take 1 tsp bp
1. Take 1 tsp bs
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
1. Get 2 sticks butter room temp
1. Get 1 cup brown sugar
1. Get 1 cup white sugar
1. Prepare 2 eggs room temp
1. Prepare 2 tsp butter flavor vanilla. (Or just vanilla)
1. Make ready 1 heaping cup of sprinkles




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Birthday Cake Cookies 🎉🎈:

1. Preheat 350 whisk dry ingredients set aside
1. Cream together butter and sugars until smooth
1. Add eggs one at a time. Then add the vanilla
1. Add flour mix to the wet ingredients then add the sprinkles shortly after. Should be a nice dough. Wrap and refrigerate at least 30 min.
1. Line pan with parchment and weigh out 13g balls for mini cookies. Or 30g for regular size. Be sure to slightly flatten cookie ball before baking. Bake 8-9 mins for mini and 9-12 for bigger cookie. These cookie should stay soft and chewy for up to a week non refrigerated
