##### The ingredients needed to make Ice-cream Choco Bread-Bond:

1. Prepare 6 Bread slice
1. Prepare 2 tsp Choco-Chips
1. Get 2 tsp Ghee/ Butter
1. Prepare 2 cup Vanilla ice cream or/ Chilled whipped cream
1. Make ready 2-3 tsp Chocolate syrup/sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Ice-cream Choco Bread-Bond:

1. Take one bread, add half scoop ice cream or whipped cream.
1. Add some choco chips on it
1. Again cover with ice cream and put other bread slice on it
1. Press this slice little, now take cup or bowl and keep it on bread by correctly assuming where is ice cream filling
1. Now press it firmly and remove the sides of bread  - Check all side of the bread is stick together.
1. Now in small nonstick pan take little ghee and shallow fry both side
1. Garnish with chocolate sauce and choco chips and serve hot ice cream in new avatar.
