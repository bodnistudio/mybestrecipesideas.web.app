##### Steps to make Oreo Ice cream:

1. Take a zip lock bag and chopped the biscuits in it without making fine powder.
1. Then, Take a bowl and Pour 2 Cups of heavy whipping cream in it. And beat it a little it becomes foamy. Then Add icing sugar in it gradually. And then beat it until stiff peaks are formed.
1. Then add chopped Oreo Biscuits in it and mix well without beating. Then Take a Freezing Container and pour the ice cream batter in it.
1. Now, Put the butter paper on the top of the Ice cream cover it with plastic wrap or the lid of the container. And Freeze it for 6 to 7 hours in the Freezer.
