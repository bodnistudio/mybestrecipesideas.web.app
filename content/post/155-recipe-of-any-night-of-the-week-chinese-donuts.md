##### Steps to make Chinese Donuts:

1. Heat your oil in a pot just over medium.

1. Drop carefully into oil. After 30 second or so mix to flip with a metal slotted spoon.
1. When they&#39;re as dark as you&#39;d like, remove with the slotted spoon letting excess oil drain off the spoon before putting into sugar.
