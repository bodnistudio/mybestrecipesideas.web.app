##### The ingredients needed to make Caesar Salad:

1. Make ready 200 grams shaved (not powdered) parmesan cheese
1. Prepare 4 chicken breasts
1. Prepare 500 grams streaky bacon
1. Get 1/4 red onion
1. Prepare 2 ripe avocados
1. Get  salt
1. Get  ground black pepper
1. Make ready 1 tbsp sweet thai chilli sauce
1. Make ready 1 tbsp sirracha hot sauce
1. Make ready 1 bottle of caesar dressing
1. Get 2 tbsp lime juice
1. Make ready 1 french bread stick
1. Make ready 1 cup olive oil, extra virgin
1. Take 2 bags baby cos lettuce
1. Get 4 large tomatoes




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Caesar Salad:

1. Halve breadsticks lengthwise, and cover cut surfaces with oil and salt
1. Grill bread in oven cut-side up until crispy
1. Marinade chicken in oil, chilli sauce and sirracha, salt and pepper
1. Grill chicken breasts until golden
1. Finely chop red onion, tomatoes and avocado. Set in bowl with lime juice
1. Lay out bacon strips under grill until crispy
1. Slice grilled bread into croutons
1. Chop or shred grilled chicken
1. Add entire bottle of caesar dressing in with onions etc. Season with salt and pepper, and sirracha for a kick.
1. Chop grilled bacon into small pieces and add to chicken
1. On a plate, lay out the cos lettuce leaves, then a portion of chicken and bacon. Shaved parmesan goes next, followed by a scoop of the dressing. Finish with croutons!
