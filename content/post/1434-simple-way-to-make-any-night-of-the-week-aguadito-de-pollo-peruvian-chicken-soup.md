##### The ingredients needed to make aguadito de pollo. (Peruvian chicken soup):

1. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Get 1/2 cup olive oil
1. Take 1 tsp minced garlic
1. Prepare 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Prepare 1 tbsp seeded, minced serrano chilli
1. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1. Make ready 1 cup green peas
1. Get 1 cup corn
1. Prepare 1/2 chopped bell pepper
1. Make ready 10 cup chicken broth
1. Take 4 yukon gold potatoes, cut in half
1. Get 4 bonless skinless chicken breasts or 6 thighs, cut in half
1. Make ready 1 cup uncooked white rice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make aguadito de pollo. (Peruvian chicken soup):

1. Lightly season the chicken with salt and pepper.
1. Heat oil in a heavy pot (or dutchoven) over medium-high heat.
1. sauté onions, garlic and Serrano chillies until softened, 1-2 min, then add the chicken and brown lightly, 4-5 min.
1. add peas, corn, cilantro bell pepper, potatoes, rice and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked and the potatoes and rice are done, 30-40 min.
