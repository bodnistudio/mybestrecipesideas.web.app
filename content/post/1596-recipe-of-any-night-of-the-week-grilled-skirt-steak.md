##### Steps to make Grilled Skirt Steak:

1. Combine juices, herbs, garlic, oil and spices. Mix well.
1. Place marinade and steak into a large freezer bag. Be sure to get the meat nice and coated. Place in fridge for an hour. Flip bag after 30 minutes.
1. Place on a hot grill. The size of the steak will determine the cooking time. I cooked this for 7 minutes on one side and about three on the other side. When it looked like this, I determined it was done.
1. Let rest then slice across the grain of the meat. This turned out just fine for me. Juicy and tender.
1. Since it cooks so fast, I recommend it be one of the last items going on the grill. Enjoy.
