##### The ingredients needed to make Paprika Rose Potatoes:

1. Prepare 4-6 medium white rose potatoes
1. Get drizzle extra virgin olive oil
1. Get to taste salt
1. Make ready 1 tsp or to taste smoked paprika
1. Make ready as needed chicken broth to liquefy

##### Steps to make Paprika Rose Potatoes:

1. Wash, remove eyes, and slice potatoes into 1/4&#34; medallions.
1. Drizzle olive oil in cast iron skillet. Add potato slices. Sprinkle generously with salt and paprika. Brown on medium heat. Turn and continue cooking. Add some chicken broth to liquefy. Cover and cook until potatoes have softened and look slightly translucent.
