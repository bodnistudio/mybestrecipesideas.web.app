##### Instructions to make Spicy Sautéed Cockles (lunch):

1. Chop onions, garlics, tomato, and chillies
1. Heat some oil in a pan, stir fry onions, garlics and chillies over medium heat until it turns slightly brownish
1. Add in the cockles, pour 1/2 glass of water and put on the lid cover, leave it for about 10 mins to cook
