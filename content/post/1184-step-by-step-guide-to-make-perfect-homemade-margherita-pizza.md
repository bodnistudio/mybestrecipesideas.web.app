##### Steps to make Homemade Margherita Pizza:

1. This is a general recipe. A lot of pizza making is technical so adjust dough hydration, temp, and cook times to fit your oven.
1. Crush or lightly puree your tomatoes. In a stock pot, saute garlic a minute or two but do not brown
1. Add tomato puree and mix well, reduce heat to gentle simmer and let reduce so that it&#39;s thick and not soupy. Season to taste
1. In a bowl add basil leaves and a touch of olive oil to coat them. This will prevent them from drying out and burning.
1. Stretch your dough and put it on your peel, add sauce, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves.
