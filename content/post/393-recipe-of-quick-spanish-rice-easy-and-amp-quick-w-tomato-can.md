##### The ingredients needed to make Spanish Rice Easy&amp;Quick w/ Tomato can:

1. Get 1/4 cup olive oil
1. Take 1 onion
1. Prepare 1/2 red bell pepper
1. Take 1 clove garlic (or 1/2 tsp of minced garlic)
1. Take 1 can stewed tomato 14 oz
1. Make ready 1 tsp chili powder
1. Get 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1. Prepare 1 cup white rice
1. Make ready 2 cups water
1. Get to taste Salt and pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Spanish Rice Easy&amp;Quick w/ Tomato can:

1. Chop onion and red bell pepper. Mince garlic. Sauté in olive oil until onion is transparent.
1. Mix in remaining ingredients (tomato can, chili powder, Worcestershire sauce).
1. Add rice and water. Bring to a boil.
1. After boiled, tightly covered, simmer on lowest heat for about 20 minuets. Stir occasionally. (I normally simmer for 30 min, so rice is more tender.)
