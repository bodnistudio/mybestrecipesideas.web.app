##### The ingredients needed to make Mango Ice Cream:

1. Get 1 cup cream, chilled (Fresh Cream Or Whipping Cream Or Coconut Cream)
1. Get to taste Sugar according
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup mango pulp
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla essence (Optional)
1. Prepare as required Chocolate Sauce to top over (Optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Mango Ice Cream:

1. Take 1 large size mango. Peel and chop into cubes.
1. Add mango cubes, vanilla extract, and sugar to a blender to make a smooth puree.
1. Beat the whipped cream until soft peaks arrived using a hand blender or electric beater.
1. Add in mango puree and mix well.
1. Pour in a plastic container and cover it.
1. Keep in the freezer and when half set, remove it from the freezer.
1. Beat with beater one more time.
1. Pout the mixture in the same container. Cover with the lid and keep in the freezer to set overnight.
1. Top with chocolate sauce or sliced nuts.
1. Serve chilled.
