<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Dump Cake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Take 15 oz of Can Pumpkin. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 12 oz of Can Evaporated Milk. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 4 of Eggs. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 1/2 Cups of Sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 Tbsp of Pumpkin Pie Spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 Box of Yellow Gluten Free Cake Mix. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 Cup of Melted Butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 Cup of Chopped Pecans, Optional. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Pumpkin Dump Cake:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Line a 9x13 pan with parchment paper or spray with cooking spray. Preheat oven to 350 degrees..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large bowl, beat together the pumpkin, milk, eggs, sugar, and spices until well combined. Pour into the prepared baking dish..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle the top with yellow cake mix..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Sprinkle with the chopped pecans (Optional)..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour the melted butter evenly over the top of the cake..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake for 60-70 minutes or until the top is lightly browned and the custard is set..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
