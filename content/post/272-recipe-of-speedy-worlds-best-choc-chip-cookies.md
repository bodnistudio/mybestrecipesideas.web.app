##### The ingredients needed to make Worlds Best Choc Chip Cookies:

1. Make ready 1/3 cup White Sugar
1. Take 1 cup Brown Sugar
1. Prepare 1 cup butter, unsalted or salted
1. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
1. Prepare 2 large eggs, slightly beaten
1. Get 3 cup flour, if using self raising flour skip the baking powder
1. Make ready 3/4 tsp baking powder
1. Take 3/4 tsp baking soda
1. Take 1 pinch salt, if using unsalted butter add 3/4 of a teaspoon of salt
1. Take 3 cup chocolate chips, really depends on your taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Worlds Best Choc Chip Cookies:

1. Preheat oven to 180C or 350°F or Gas mark 4
1. In a large bowl sift the dry ingredents, set a side for the moment and do the wet ingredients
1. In a medium sized bowl combine sugars and butter,cream together. Add vanilla and eggs (one at a time) and mix well
1. Add the creamed mixture into the dry ingredients and mix well forming a dough. Dough will be a stiff mixture if it&#39;s to stiff for your liking add a dash of milk to smooth it out a little
1. Add choc chips, you can basically add anything you want to this cookie dough to make it your own =)
1. Line a cookie sheet, Drop dough on sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or 20 minutes for that extra crunch, once done once done place on a cooling rack
1. QuickTip: slightly dampen your hands to make it easier to work with the dough
1. Makes roughly 24 depending on size
