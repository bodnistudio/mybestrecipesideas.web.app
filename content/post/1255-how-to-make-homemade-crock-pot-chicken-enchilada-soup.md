##### The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Chicken Enchilada Soup:

1. Make ready 1 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1. Get 1 can whole kernel corn (15.25 oz.)
1. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes including juice (14.5 oz.)
1. Make ready 1 can chicken broth (14.5 oz.)
1. Make ready 1 can enchilada sauce (10 oz.)
1. Make ready 1 can green chilies
1. Prepare 1 white onion, chopped
1. Get 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1. Make ready 2 bay leaves
1. Make ready 3 clove garlic, minced
1. Take 1 tsp ground cumin
1. Prepare 1 tsp chili powder
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper, or to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Crock Pot Chicken Enchilada Soup:

1. Rinse and pat dry the chicken breasts, then place into the bottom of a crock pot.
1. Add the corn, tomatoes, chicken broth, enchilada sauce, green chiles, onion, cilantro, bay leaves, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
1. Cook on low for 6 hours.
1. Transfer the chicken to a large plate, shred the meat with two forks.
1. Return the chicken to the crock pot and continue cooking for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
