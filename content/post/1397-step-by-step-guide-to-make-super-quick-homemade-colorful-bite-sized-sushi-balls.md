##### The ingredients needed to make Colorful Bite-Sized Sushi Balls:

1. Get  Topping:
1. Make ready 20 cuts total Salmon, tuna, squid, sea bream, shrimp, etc
1. Prepare 1 Shiso, salmon roe (for white sashimi such as squid)
1. Get 1 Wasabi
1. Take  Sushi Rice:
1. Take 700 grams Freshly cooked rice
1. Get 3 tbsp Vinegar
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
1. Get 1 tsp Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Colorful Bite-Sized Sushi Balls:

1. Mix together the vinegar, salt, and sugar and add to the freshly-made rice. Mix together using cutting motions while flapping a fan over the rice.
1. Put the topping, wasabi, and rice on plastic wrap, and shape into a ball.
1. If putting squid or other white sashimi onto the rice, first put shiso or wasasbi onto the plastic wrap before the other ingredients, then top with some salmon roe after shaping.
