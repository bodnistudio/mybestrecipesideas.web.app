##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Mia -specialità-:

1. Make ready 150 g flour
1. Make ready 15 g olive oil
1. Make ready 80 ml water
1. Take 10 g yeast
1. Get 40 g cheese (mozzarella)
1. Make ready 5 pieces prosciutto
1. Take  toppings (ex. anchovy and gorgonzolla)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Pizza Mia -specialità-:

1. Mix flour, olive oil, water and yeast all together to make dough.
1. Stay the dough at a cool place for about one hour.
1. Put the dough in a pan and make it flat and round.
1. Make the edge and on the edge, put prosciutto.
1. Put mozzarella cheese and toppings as you like on top. This time, I put anchovy and gorgonzolla.
1. Bake it in the oven for 20 minutes at 200℃.
