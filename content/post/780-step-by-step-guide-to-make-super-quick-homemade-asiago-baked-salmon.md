##### The ingredients needed to make Asiago Baked Salmon:

1. Get 1 lb salmon filets
1. Make ready 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
1. Prepare 1 tbsp dried parsley
1. Get 3 tbsp grated asiago cheese
1. Take 1/2 tbsp chopped garlic
1. Prepare 1 tbsp chopped shallots
1. Take 1 salt &amp; pepper to taste




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Asiago Baked Salmon:

1. Preheat oven to 400.
1. Sprinkle salmon with salt and pepper.
1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, cheese, shallots, parsley and garlic together. Spread on top of salmon.
1. Bake until fish flakes about 15 minutes.
