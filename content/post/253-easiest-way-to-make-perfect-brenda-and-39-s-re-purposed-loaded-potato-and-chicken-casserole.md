##### The ingredients needed to make Brenda&#39;s Re-purposed Loaded Potato and Chicken Casserole:

1. Make ready 2 lb leftover cooked chicken, cut in 1/2&#34; cubes
1. Make ready 1 packages O&#39;Brian cubed potatoes
1. Get 1 can cream of mushroom soup
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp salt
1. Get 1 tbsp black pepper
1. Prepare 1 tbsp paprika
1. Get 6 tbsp hot sauce, or to taste (optional)
1. Get 2 tbsp garlic powder
1. Take  topping
1. Make ready 2 cup fiesta blend cheese
1. Prepare 1 cup crumbled bacon
1. Prepare 1 cup diced green onion
1. Make ready 1 sour cream, if desired




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Brenda&#39;s Re-purposed Loaded Potato and Chicken Casserole:

1. Preheat oven to 350°.
1. Spray a 9 x 13&#34; baking dish with cooking spray.
1. Bake the potatoes for 45 minutes, stirring every 10-15 minutes, until almost cooked through and starting to become crispy browned on the outside.
1. Mix together soup (thinned with a small amount of milk), seasonings, 1 cup of the cheese &amp; hot sauce. Add the cubed chicken and stir to coat.
1. Once potatoes are cooked, add chicken mixture and cook another 15 minutes.
1. Top with cheese, bacon &amp; green onions and return to oven till cheese is bubbly hot.
1. Serve with sour cream if desired.
