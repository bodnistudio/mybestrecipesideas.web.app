##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Potatoes:

1. Take 1/4 kg Baby Potatoes
1. Take 1/4 stick salted butter
1. Get 2 tbsp flour
1. Get 200 ml cold evaporated milk
1. Make ready 1 fresh parsley
1. Prepare 1 Salt
1. Take 1 Pepper
1. Prepare 2 cup cheddar cheese
1. Prepare 2 tbsp garlic powder




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Cheesy Garlic Potatoes:

1. Boil baby potatoes in water until half cooked. Do not over cook.
1. Slice potatoes in half. Set aside.
1. In a pan, melt butter.
1. Add flour. Mix well.
1. Pour in cold milk. Mix thoroughly for 3 minutes.
1. Add parsley. Mix.
1. Add garlic powder. Add more garlic powder if you want garlicky taste. Stir for 2 minutes.
1. Remove from heat. Add grated cheddar cheese and mix thoroughly until cheese melts. Add more cheese if desired.
1. Add salt and pepper to taste. If desired.
1. Stir in boiled baby potatoes. Serve.
