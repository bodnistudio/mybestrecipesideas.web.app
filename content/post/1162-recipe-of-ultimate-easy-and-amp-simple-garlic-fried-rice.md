##### Steps to make Easy &amp; Simple Garlic Fried Rice:

1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry until golden brown.
1. After the garlic has become golden brown, remove it to a plate.
1. Stir-fry the rice in the olive oil that the garlic was cooked in. Add the salt, pepper, and Chinese soup stock granules and continue to stir-fry.
1. Return the garlic to the pan and stir-fry until everything is mixed well. Add the soy sauce directly to the surface of the pan.
1. When the soy sauce is pleasantly fragrant, pile the chopped shiso leaves on top and it&#39;s done!
