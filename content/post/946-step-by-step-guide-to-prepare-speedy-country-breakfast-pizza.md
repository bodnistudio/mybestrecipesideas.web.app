##### The ingredients needed to make Country Breakfast Pizza:

1. Take 1 lb mild pork sausage
1. Prepare 1 can Pillsbury refrigerator pizza crust, thin
1. Take 1 1/4 cup milk
1. Take 2 1/2 tbsp flour
1. Get 1 black pepper to taste
1. Prepare 12 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1. Get 1/2 packages simply potatoes southern hash browns
1. Take 3 eggs




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Country Breakfast Pizza:

1. Begin by making the sausage gravy. In a deep frying pan, add the sausage and brown and crumble until no longer pink.
1. Without draining the grease, add the flour and milk and continuously stir to create the gravy. Season with pepper. Remove from heat.
1. On a cookie sheet unroll the pizza dough and fit to the pan. Preheat oven to 425째F.
1. Spread the gravy in a thin layer on dough.
1. Top with 3/4 of the cheese.
1. Sprinkle on the hash browns in an even thin layer.
1. Top with remaining cheese.
1. Place into oven and bake for 12 minutes.
1. Remove from oven and gently crack the 3 eggs onto the pizza. Reduce heat in oven to 395째F.  Put back in oven and bake until eggs are cooked (about 8 minutes or so).
1. Let set for 3 minutes to firm up. Serve and enjoy.
