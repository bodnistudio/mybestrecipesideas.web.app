##### The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef Dip:

1. Make ready 2 Packages cream cheese (8 oz box)
1. Take 1/2 containter to 1 full container of sour cream (your choice)
1. Get 4-5 green onions
1. Get 2 Packages Buddig corned beef (2 oz pkg)
1. Get 4 cloves garlic
1. Get  Optional - plastic wrap or leave in bowl




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Corned Beef Dip:

1. Let the cream cheese sit out for an hour to get warm and soft.
1. Using a food processor or cut very, very small, the corned beef, onions and garlic, put in a mixing bowl.
1. Add the sour cream and cream cheese to the bowl and use a mixer (I don&#39;t recommend mixing by hand).
1. Optional - Scoop dip onto a sheet of plastic wrap, wrap completely and form/shape into a ball.
1. Place dip into refrigerator for about 2 hrs to get cold.
1. Serve with crackers and enjoy.
