##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Make IceCream and Caramel:

1. Get 3 egg whites
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Icing sugar
1. Get 1/2 Milk
1. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla extract
1. Take  Caramel syrup




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Quick Make IceCream and Caramel:

1. Whisk egg whites until soft peak.
1. Add sugar and whisk until stiff peak. Set aside. Mix milk and vanilla and fold into egg whites meringue.
1. Scoop into a bowl and put in the refrigerator for 4 hours. Always check so it doesn&#39;t form ice crystals.
1. Serve and top with caramel syrup.
