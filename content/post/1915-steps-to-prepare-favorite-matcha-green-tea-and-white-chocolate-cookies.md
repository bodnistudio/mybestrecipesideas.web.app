##### The ingredients needed to make Matcha Green Tea and White Chocolate Cookies:

1. Take 100 g (3.5oz)unsalted butter
1. Prepare 50 g (1/4 U.S.cup)granulated sugar
1. Get 100 g (1/2U.S.cup)brown sugar
1. Make ready 1 pinch salt
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Take 150 g (5 and 1/4oz)all purpose flour
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp matcha green tea powder
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
1. Prepare 1/2 U.S. cup coarsely chopped white chocolate or white chocolate chips




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Matcha Green Tea and White Chocolate Cookies:

1. Preparation: Preheat the oven to 330F. Shift flour, green tea, and baking soda together.
1. Microwave butter until it&#39;s melted. Combine the sugar and butter together. Add the egg and salt, then mix.
1. Combine all powder ingredients with a spatula. Add the white chocolate.
1. Drop cookie dough in rounded tablespoonfuls onto the parchment paper. Leave a space between each piece of dough.
1. Bake 6 to 8 min. Don&#39;t over bake!
