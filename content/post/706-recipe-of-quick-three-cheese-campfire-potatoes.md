##### The ingredients needed to make Three Cheese Campfire Potatoes:

1. Take 3 large Potatoes (peeled and cut into 1in cubes)
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Parmesan Cheese
1. Get 1/2 tps Salt
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Bacon Pieces
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1. Take 1/2 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Three Cheese Campfire Potatoes:

1. In a heavy-duty foil (about 18in square) slice up butter
1. Add chopped potatoes and salt
1. Fold foil around potato mix and seal
1. Grill, covered, over medium heat for 15-18 min on each side until potatoes are tender
1. Carefully open foil and sprinkle bacon pieces and cheese over potato mixture
1. Grill 3-5 min longer until cheese is melted
