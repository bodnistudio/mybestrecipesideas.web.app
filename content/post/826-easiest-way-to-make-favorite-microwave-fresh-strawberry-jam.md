##### The ingredients needed to make Microwave Fresh Strawberry Jam:

1. Get 1 lb fresh strawberries-washed, hulled and sliced
1. Get 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
1. Take 3 tsp powdered pectin (Sure-Jel)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Microwave Fresh Strawberry Jam:

1. Crush sliced berries with potato masher or bottom of glass- leave them coarse with some large pieces.
1. Place berries into a 3 or 4 quart microwaveable bowl (nothing smaller as it can boil over).
1. Stir  sugar and powdered pectin into berries making sure you blended them in well.
1. Microwave on high 3-5 minutes until mixture reaches a rolling boil. Stir. Reduce power to 50% and microwave around another 5 minutes stirring occasionally until berry mixture begins to thicken to a medium syrup and coats back of spoon ( jam will thicken as it cools). Put into 2 clean 1/2 pint jars, or one pint jar. Refrigerate when cool.
1. This makes roughly one pint of strawberry jam. It&#39;s texture is more like a thick syrup--great on ice cream. If you want thicker, add an additional tsp.of pectin--but try it this way first.
