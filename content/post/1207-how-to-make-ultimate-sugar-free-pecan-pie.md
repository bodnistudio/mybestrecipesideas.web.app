<h3>Steps to make Sugar Free Pecan Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			preheat oven to 375°F.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			prrriick pie crust with fork and cover edges with foil to prevent over browning.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			mix eggs, agave, splenda, and flour in medium bowl.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			add pecans and butter , stir well.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			pour into pie shell.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			bake at 375°F for 60 minutes or until a knife comes clean when inserted.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			remove foil from edges,  bake an additional 10 to 15 minutes until edges are golden.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			cool, serve, and enjoy..
