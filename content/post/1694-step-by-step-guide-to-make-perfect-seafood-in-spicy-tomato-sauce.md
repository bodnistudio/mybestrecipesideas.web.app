##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood in Spicy Tomato Sauce:

1. Get 1 can chopped tomatoes
1. Take 4 fresh red chilli&#39;s, chopped
1. Get 1 onion, chopped
1. Get 50 ml white wine
1. Get  Hand full of fresh parsley, chopped
1. Prepare 6 Roasted garlic cloves, chopped
1. Make ready 300 g mixed seafood
1. Prepare 4 tbsp olive oil
1. Take to taste salt

##### Steps to make Seafood in Spicy Tomato Sauce:

1. In a saucepan, heat the olive oil.
1. Once the oil is heated. Reduce the heat to medium to low and add the garlic, parsley, onion and chili&#39;s. Fry for about 3-5 minutes or until the onions have softened.
1. Add the tomatoes, salt to taste, wine and simmer for about 5-8 min until most of the liquid has cooked down.
1. Add the seafood into the pan and cook. I did use already cooked seafood which literally just needed warming up but you can use raw seafood which will take no more than 5 minutes to cook as well.
1. Serve with warm crusty bread and enjoy.
