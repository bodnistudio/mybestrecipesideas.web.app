##### The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Steaks Simmered in Sauce:

1. Prepare  For the hamburgers (yields 5 large patties)
1. Take 600 grams Mixed ground beef and pork
1. Take 1 Onion
1. Make ready 300 ml Panko
1. Make ready 100 ml Milk
1. Take 6 tbsp ★ Sake
1. Make ready 1 tbsp ★ Grated garlic
1. Get 1 Beaten egg (large)
1. Make ready 1 dash ★ Salt and pepper
1. Get  For the sauce:
1. Prepare 1 can Demi-glaze sauce (canned, Heinz brand)
1. Prepare 200 ml Water
1. Make ready 1 cube ☆ Soup stock cubes
1. Take 50 ml ☆ Red wine
1. Prepare 5 tbsp ☆ Tomato ketchup
1. Prepare 1 ☆ Bay leaf
1. Take 1 dash ☆ Salt and pepper
1. Prepare  Accompaniment:
1. Take 1 Carrot
1. Prepare 1 Potatoes
1. Take 1 dash Heavy cream

##### Instructions to make Hamburger Steaks Simmered in Sauce:

1. Mince the onions, saute until softened, and transfer to a bowl to cool. Soak the panko in milk.
1. Mix 1 and the ★ ingredients into the ground meat and thoroughly knead.
1. Divide into 5 equal portions once it all sticks together. Remove the air by tossing each ball back and forth like playing catch, and mold into patties. (use 2 and store the rest in the freezer).
1. Heat up oil in a pan, and brown the hamburgers on both sides.
1. Add the demi-glaze and water to step to 4, bring to a boil, add ☆ and boil for 20-30 minutes (flip over occasionally).
1. While boiling, peel the carrots, and microwave. Cut the potatoes into bite-sized chunks and fry with the skins intact.
1. Arrange 5 and 6 on a plate, drizzle with fresh cream, and serve.
