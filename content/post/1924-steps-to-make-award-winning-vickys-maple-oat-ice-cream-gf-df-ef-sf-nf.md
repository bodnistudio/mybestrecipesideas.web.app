##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Maple Oat Ice Cream, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Get 90 grams gluten-free rolled oats
1. Get 240 ml boiling water
1. Get 480 ml full fat coconut milk
1. Prepare 100 grams soft brown sugar
1. Prepare 60 ml maple syrup
1. Get 1 tbsp cinnamon
1. Prepare 1 tsp xanthan gum
1. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Vickys Maple Oat Ice Cream, GF DF EF SF NF:

1. Add oats to boiling water and microwave on high for 1 minute
1. Add all other ingredients and mix well - do not drain any unabsorbed water
1. Allow to cool then churn in ice cream maker for 15 - 30 minutes
1. If you don&#39;t have an icecream maker, pour the mixture into a freezer container and stir it every 20 minutes until it reaches your desired consistency
