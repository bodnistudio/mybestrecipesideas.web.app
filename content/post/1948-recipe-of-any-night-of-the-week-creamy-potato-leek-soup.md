##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Potato Leek Soup:

1. Take 2 tbsp Butter
1. Take 1 1/2 large Leek, chopped
1. Get 1/2 large Onion, chopped
1. Get 3 medium Potatoes, cubed(small)
1. Take 4 cup Water
1. Take 1 cup Cooking Cream
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Garlic powder(really 1/8, didn&#39;t have that option)

##### Steps to make Creamy Potato Leek Soup:

1. In a large pot melt butter and sauté onion and leek for about 10 minutes.
1. Add the water and potatoes,  bring to a boil then let simmer for 25 to 30 minutes.
1. Let it cool enough so as not to crack your blender.  Place in blender and blend until most everything is smooth.
1. Place back in pot on low heat, add the cream, garlic, salt and pepper ( or to your taste)
