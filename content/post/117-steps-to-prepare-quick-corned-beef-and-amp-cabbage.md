##### The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef &amp; Cabbage:

1. Get 1 corned beef
1. Prepare 1 packages seasoning from the meat package
1. Get 1 water
1. Prepare 1 head cabbage
1. Prepare 3/4 lb baby red potatoes
1. Make ready 12 oz package of petite baby carrots




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Corned Beef &amp; Cabbage:

1. Put the corned beef in the crock pot sprinkle with  the seasoning that came with the meat. Fill the crock pot with water to cover the beef.
1. Wash then cut the cabbage into quarters. Put the cabbage into the crock pot.  If you prefer your cabbage not as soft you can allow your meat to cook for a few hours (about 2 or 3) then add your cabbage.  Set your crock pot on high. For 8 hours. 10 hours if you like the corned beef shredding.
1. When you have about 3.5 hours left add the potatoes and carrots.
1. Take everything out, slice the meat and serve. Salt and pepper as needed.  ENJOY
