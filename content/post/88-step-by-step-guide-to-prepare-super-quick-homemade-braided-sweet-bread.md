##### The ingredients needed to make Braided Sweet Bread:

1. Prepare 5 cups all-purpose flour
1. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
1. Take 1/3 cup refined oil
1. Make ready 1 tbsp dried yeast
1. Make ready 2 eggs
1. Get 1/4 tbsp salt
1. Make ready 1 cup water

##### Instructions to make Braided Sweet Bread:

1. Add sugar to 1 cup warm water and dissolve yeast into this. Leave it for 10 mins till the yeast is activated
1. Add flour, 1 egg, salt and oil to this and knead till a soft dough is formed.
1. Cover the dough with a wet cloth and leave it for 20 mins so that the dough raises.
1. After 20 mins, knead the dough once again. Roll into a strip and divide into three equal parts.
1. Roll each of the three parts into a long strip and make a braid with the three strips. Close the ends.
1. Generously pour 1 egg on this braided bread dough and sprinkle some sugar on top.
1. Line the baking tray with parchment paper and place the dough on it. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes till the bread turns golden brown. Enjoy it with butter or as is!
