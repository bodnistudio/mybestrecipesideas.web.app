##### The ingredients needed to make Oreo Cheese Cake in A Glass:

1. Prepare 5 oreos
1. Make ready 20 gr cheese
1. Make ready 3 tbsp maize flour/corn flour
1. Make ready 5 tbsp condensed milk
1. Get 2 tbsp sugar
1. Get 1 pinch salt
1. Take 1 glass water (200ml)
1. Take  NOTE!!! You can replace (cheese, maize flour and condensed milk) with only 1 glass (200gr) of Cream Cheese




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Oreo Cheese Cake in A Glass:

1. Remove cream (white part) from oreo cookies
1. Crush oreo cookies until fine crumbs. Use blender or zip lock or stone or hit ur oreos with a car/bus üòÅ or do whatever u can.
1. Boil water, add cheese, milk, oreos cream (white part of oreos), sifted maize flour/corn flour, sugar, salt. Then stir until thickened.
1. Pour thickened cream in a glass then add crashed oreos then add cream then add crashed oreos again
1. Put grated cheese as topping.
1. Put in a fridge then wait til 1 hour
1. Serve it
