##### The ingredients needed to make Kosambari Salad/ moong dal and anar salad:

1. Make ready 1 cup yellow moong dal
1. Take 1 cup cucumber
1. Take 1 cup anar/ pomegranate
1. Get 1/2 cup grated coconut
1. Make ready  as per taste Salt
1. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
1. Make ready  For tempering-
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp black mustard seeds
1. Get 1 tsp urad dal
1. Take 1 red chilli
1. Take 10-12 Curry leaves




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Kosambari Salad/ moong dal and anar salad:

1. Clean and dice the cucumbers and carrots and set aside.Peel the pomegranates extract the pearls and set aside.
1. Soak dal for an hour and mix it with everything.
1. Mix grated coconut with with moong mixture.Add salt and lemon juice. Mix the salad together.
1. In a small pan add the ingredients for tempering except the curry leaves.  As the mustard seeds splutter add in the curry leaves and take off the heat.  Set aside.
1. Now put tempering on top of salad and mix properly. Salad is ready to serve.
