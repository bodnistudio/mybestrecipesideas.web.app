##### Instructions to make Big Cream Pudding for a Party!:

1. &lt;Make Caramel Sauce&gt;
1. Put the sugar and water in the pot. Heat the pot at medium heat. Let it sit until the surface becomes lightly-colored. Swirl the pot and even out the sugar liquid.
1. Caramelize the sugar become brown, remove the pot from the heat. Quickly add the hot water in the pot.
