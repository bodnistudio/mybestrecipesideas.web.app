<h3>Steps to make Pillsbury Fajita Pot Pies (Super easy):</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Open Pillsbury flakey biscuits and flatten each biscuit..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Grease muffin pan and place each flattened piece of dough in the pan.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			you can begin preheating the oven if you like, but you still have to cook the chicken and stuff..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Cut the chicken into TINY ass pieces, cut the onions and bell peppers thin and long, then chop cilantro.  Add everything to a preheated pan with olive oil.  Next is pretty obvious,  Cook until chicken is done..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add however much salt, garlic salt, and pepper you want....to taste.  We added approx. 1TBS (of each) at first, then another  1 TBS of  after initial taste testing.  (we just shook each until it started tasting good).
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spoon the chicken, onions, and bell pepper mix into each biscuit..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Put in the oven until biscuits are light brown, approximately 12-14 mins.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			serve with salsa on top...or not.  taste good either way..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
