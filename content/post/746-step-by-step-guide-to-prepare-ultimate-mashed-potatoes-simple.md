##### The ingredients needed to make Mashed Potatoes (simple):

1. Take 12 small golden potatoes
1. Make ready 1 cup milk
1. Get 4 tbsp butter
1. Make ready  salt
1. Make ready  ground black pepper

##### Instructions to make Mashed Potatoes (simple):

1. Cook the potatoes.  (I microwaved 4 at a time for 4 minutes. Be sure to poke holes in the potatoes if you also do this. )
1. Heat milk and butter on the stove until the butter has melted. Low heat.
1. Quarter potatoes,  and add to pan.
1. Mash the potatoes.
1. Add salt and pepper.
1. Stir to desired consistency.
