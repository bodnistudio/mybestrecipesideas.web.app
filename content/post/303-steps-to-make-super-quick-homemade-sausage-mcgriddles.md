1. Prepare  pancake syrup

##### Steps to make Sausage Mcgriddles:

1. Cut 1 inch thick pieces of sausage. Flatten slightly. I crumble the sausage with my spatula through the cooking process. Cook in pan until done
1. Prepare another skillet for your pancakes. I use a little oil, to make the edges of the pancakes crispy.
1. Add pancake mix to pan...then add crumbled sausage on top.
1. Add a slice of cheese
1. Then another sausage pancake on top.
