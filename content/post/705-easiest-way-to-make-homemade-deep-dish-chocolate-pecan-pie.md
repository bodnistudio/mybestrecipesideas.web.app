##### The ingredients needed to make Deep Dish Chocolate Pecan Pie:

1. Make ready  pie
1. Prepare 1 9&#34; deep pie crust, I make homemade but also will use store bought frozen pie crust
1. Get 2 1/2 tbsp all-purpose flour
1. Prepare 4 large eggs
1. Prepare 1 1/4 cup granulated sugar
1. Take 1 cup dark corn syrup
1. Prepare 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
1. Make ready 4 oz bakers chocolate, unsweetened
1. Make ready 2 cup pecans
1. Prepare 1/2 tsp instant espresso
1. Take 1 pinch salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Deep Dish Chocolate Pecan Pie:

1. In a large bowl, add all ingredients except chocolate. Mix on a medium speed for 2 minutes.
1. In a double boiler, melt the chocolate squares. Let cool just a few minutes so it doesn&#39;t seize. Add into mixture and mix 1 minute.
1. Fold in the pecans.
1. Fill pie crust. I do not pre bake the store bought crusts. Even when frozen.
1. In a preheated 350°F. Oven, place pie on baking tray and bake 50-60 minutes. Middle of pie will still be a bit jiggly.
1. Let cool 60 minutes before serving. This goes great with a vanilla bean ice cream or fresh whipped cream, or both!
