#mycookbook:

1. Marinate the shrimp with all ingredients mentioned above under ‚Äòmarination‚Äô.
1. Cover/wrap it with cling wrap and place it in the fridge.

1. Cut 1 big onion into half. Chop half of it finely. And chop the other half into thin slices.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan and saut√© the sliced onions till they get caramelised. Do not over brown them.
