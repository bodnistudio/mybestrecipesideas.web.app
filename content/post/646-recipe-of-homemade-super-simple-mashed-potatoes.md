##### The ingredients needed to make Super Simple Mashed Potatoes:

1. Take 1 potato (diced)
1. Get 2 bay leaves
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp butter
1. Take 1/4 cup milk
1. Prepare 1 salt
1. Get 1 black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Super Simple Mashed Potatoes:

1. Bring water to a boil in small pot
1. Throw in potatoes, pinch of salt, and bay leaves
1. Cook for 15 minutes then drain
1. Melt butter and milk in microwave for 45 seconds
1. Stir milk and butter until blended
1. Mash potatoes and stir in milk and butter mixture
1. Potatoes should be creamy. As salt and pepper to taste
