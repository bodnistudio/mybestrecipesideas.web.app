##### Steps to make Mango ice cream:

1. First put the milk in a pan and cook on low heat for about 20 minutes, The milk has to be cooked so much that one cup of milk is cooked and half a cup is left. let it cool. Evaporated milk is ready.
1. Then put ice in a bowl. And place an empty bowl on top of the ice and pour the cream into that empty bowl.whisk it with a fork.
1. Add powdered sugar to the cream and whisk well with a fork.
1. When the cream is fluffy, add evaporated milk and whisk more.
1. Whisk the Mango slice with a fork. Now add mango liquid and mix.
1. Now add powdered milk and mix.
1. Now finally add the mango essence and mix well. If you don&#39;t have mango essence, add vanilla essence.
1. Mango Ice Cream Liquid is ready. Put it in an airtight container and keep it in the freezer for 8 hours.
1. After 8 hours, the mango ice cream is ready
