##### The ingredients needed to make Re-skinned Potatoes:

##### Steps to make Re-skinned Potatoes:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. Peel Potatoes
1. Wrap chicken skins around potatoes, comoletely covering their surface area.
1. Add Salt, Pepper, and Garlic Salt.
1. Top with Olive Oil as needed to protect the skin.
1. Put the re-skinned potatoes on to a pan lined with a piece of waxed paper. Place the potatoes with the ends of the skin facing down.
1. Cook in the oven 45 minutes or until the skin is golden brown and a fork easily passes through it.
