##### The ingredients needed to make Temari Sushi:

1. Take 360 grams Hot cooked white rice
1. Get 1 Sushi vinegar
1. Make ready 1 ＊Egg for kinshi tamago
1. Prepare 1/2 ＊Cucumber
1. Prepare 3 slice ＊Ham
1. Take 3 slice each ＊Tuna, salmon (for sashimi)
1. Prepare 1 ＊Negitoro (minced fatty tuna with scallions)
1. Prepare 1 ＊Nori (dried seaweed sheet)
1. Prepare 1 ＊Mayonnaise




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Temari Sushi:

1. Combine cooked rice with the sushi vinegar to make sushi rice! Use a hand fan to air it out while using a rice paddle to mix (airing it out will help adjust the texture of the rice and make it look glossier).
1. Prepare the ingredients. First, roll the rice into bite-sized pieces and wrap with plastic wrap.
1. Make an egg crepe, then cut it into ribbons for kinshi tamago.
1. Slice the cucumber with a slicer and arrange the slices into a flower design. Add a bit mayonnaise underneath the cucumber slices to help them stick to the rice.
1. Using a heart-shaped cutter, cut the ham into hearts.
1. Put some wasabi between the rice and tuna or salmon.
1. With the negitoro, stuff it inside the rice, then create a design on the outer surface of the rice ball using thin strips of nori.
