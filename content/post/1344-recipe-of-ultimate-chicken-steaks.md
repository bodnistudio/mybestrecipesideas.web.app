##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Steaks:

1. Get 2 Chicken pieces
1. Take 2 TBS Tanduri Masala
1. Take 2 TBS Ginger Garlic paste
1. Take 1 TBS kashmiri Red Chilli
1. Take 2 TBS Sabz Mirch &amp; Podina paste
1. Prepare 1 TBS garam Masala
1. Take 2 Boiled Potatoes
1. Prepare  Carrots, cucumber, cut in slice
1. Get  Salt For taste
1. Take 70g Butter
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Milk on room Temperature
1. Make ready  Corn flour
1. Get  Black pepper
1. Get  Garlic powder
1. Prepare  Chat Masala

##### Steps to make Chicken Steaks:

1. Marinate chicken fillets with Tikka Masala, and All ingredients for 2,3 hours
1. Add some oil in fry pan and cook chicken fillets for 20 minutes or until Chicken is completely cooked
1. In other pan for white sauce
1. Add butter, when butter is melt, add corn flour and staring for 2 minutes
1. Now Add milk, salt, Garlic powder, Black pepper
1. Sauce is ready
1. For Mashed Potatoes
1. Add 2 boiled potatoes and mashed it completely
1. Now Add salt, 2 Tbsp black pepper, 2 Tbsp Chat Masala, 2 Tbsp oil, mix it
1. Carrots, cucumber, Shimla mirch, cut these veggies into slices and fry them into chilli and soya sauce for 5 minutes
