##### The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Caserole:

1. Get 5 eggs
1. Get 1/2 cup milk
1. Take 1 bacon
1. Prepare 1 lb Breakfast sausage
1. Take 1 shredded cheese
1. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
1. Prepare 1 can Cresent rolls
1. Get 1 clove green onion chopped




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Breakfast Caserole:

1. preheat oven to 375
1. mix in bowl eggs milk green onion salt and pepper, set aside.
1. cook bacon and sausage and set aside
1. cool bacon and sausage when bacon cools chop into pieces
1. layer baking dish with Cresent roll dough
1. partially cook hash browns or tater tots and then slightly mash
1. layer on top of dough the hash browns then sausage then sprinkle with cheese, then add bacon crumbles and lastly the egg mixture
1. bake in oven for 20 mins then let set for 5
1. add more cheese the last 5 mins of baking
