##### The ingredients needed to make Aryca&#39;s Easter Ham:

1. Make ready 6 1/2 lb ham, smoked
1. Make ready 16 oz pineapple rings
1. Prepare 1 quart pineapple juice
1. Get 1 cup light brown sugar
1. Prepare 1/4 cup whole cloves
