##### The ingredients needed to make Rolled Flank Steak Kebabs:

1. Make ready 1.5 Pounds Flank Steak
1. Get 2 Tablespoons Cardamom
1. Get 2 Tablespoons Turmeric
1. Get 1 Tablespoon Black Pepper
1. Prepare To Taste Thick Sea Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Rolled Flank Steak Kebabs:

1. Wash the Flank Steak with plenty of water. Add the seasoning and rub the whole steak. Add Thick Salt to Taste.
1. Roll the Flank Steak tightly and insert the skewers. Cut in one inch thick kebabs.
1. Grill the kebabs at high heat for 6 min on each side. Add some more Salt while grilling.
1. Serve and enjoy!
