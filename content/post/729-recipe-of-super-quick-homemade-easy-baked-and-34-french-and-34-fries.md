##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Baked &#34;FRENCH&#34; Fries:

1. Take 3 each Large -Potatoes
1. Take 6 cup Boiling water
1. Take 1 can Vegetable oil (non-stick) cooking spray
1. Make ready 1 tsp Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Baked &#34;FRENCH&#34; Fries:

1. Preheat oven to 450
1. Boil water
1. Peel potatoes and cut 4 inches long and 1/4 inch wide
1. Place peeled potatoes in already boiling water for 3 minutes
1. Drain potatoes and pat dry with a paper towel
1. Spray vegetable oil evenly over potatoes
1. Salt to taste
1. Spread pieces on one shallow layer baking pan
1. Bake 30-35 minutes or until lightly browned
