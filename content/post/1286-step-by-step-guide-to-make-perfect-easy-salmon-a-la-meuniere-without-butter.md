##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Salmon à la Meunière without Butter:

1. Prepare 2 pieces Raw salmon filet
1. Get 1 dash Salt and pepper
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Flour
1. Take 1 tsp Oil
1. Prepare 2 slice Lemon slice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Easy Salmon à la Meunière without Butter:

1. Sprinkle both sides of the salmon with salt and pepper. I used Krazy Salt and coarsely ground black pepper.
1. Coat with flour. Heat oil in a pan, and pan-fry both sides until lightly browned. Cover with a lid, and cook over medium heat for 2 minutes, then flip and cook another 2 minutes over low heat.
1. Serve on a plate with lemon slices.
