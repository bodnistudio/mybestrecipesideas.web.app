##### The ingredients needed to make Doll Festival Sushi Cake:

1. Take 360 ml Plain cooked rice
1. Make ready 1 packet Chirashi sushi seasoning mix
1. Get 8 slice Cured ham
1. Prepare 1/2 Cucumber
1. Make ready 1/4 Carrot
1. Make ready 1 slice Ham
1. Get 1 slice Sliced cheese
1. Get 1 Thin omelet




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Doll Festival Sushi Cake:

1. Thinly slice the carrot into your choice of shapes. Boil.
1. Cut the ham and cheese into any shapes.
1. Slice the cucumber with a peeler.
1. Finely shred the thin omelet.
1. Cut the cured ham slices in half and roll them to form roses. Use 2 slices to make the large rose for the center of the cake.
1. Cover the cake pan in plastic wrap. Layer the finely shredded omelet on top. Combine the chirashi sushi mix with the rice and place it on top of the omelet.
1. Flip it over onto a plate. Place the cured ham roses on top and then decorate with the remaining ingredients from Steps 1-3. Twist the cucumbers once.
