<h3>Steps to make Chicken pot pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat oven to 375 degrees..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bring large pot of water to boil. Boil chicken breasts until cooked through. Once cooled, shred the chicken breasts..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spray a pie pan with nonstick cooking spray. Line pie pan with one of the pie crusts..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken, cream of mushroom soup and frozen vegetables. Stir until combined. Add salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste. Stir until combined..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour chicken mixture into pie pan. Even out with a spoon. Add second pie crust to top. Trim excess pie crust with a knife. Pinch edge all the way around with a fork or finger..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour..
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
