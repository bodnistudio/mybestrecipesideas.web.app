##### The ingredients needed to make Deconstructed Chicken Pot Pie:

1. Get 2 chicken breasts
1. Take 2 cups broccoli
1. Make ready 2 cups carrots
1. Take 2 (10.5 oz) cans cream of chicken
1. Prepare  salt and pepper
1. Prepare 1 roll flaky layers biscuits




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Deconstructed Chicken Pot Pie:

1. Cut chicken, broccoli, and carrots into bite-size pieces.
1. Put chicken and salt/pepper into crock pot and mix.
1. Add broccoli, carrots, and cream of chicken and stir.
1. Close lid and cook on high for 3 hours.
1. Bake biscuits in oven as directed on package.
1. Serve and enjoy!
