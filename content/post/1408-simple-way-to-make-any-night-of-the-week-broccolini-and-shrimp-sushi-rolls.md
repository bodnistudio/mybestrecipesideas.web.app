##### The ingredients needed to make Broccolini and Shrimp Sushi Rolls:

1. Prepare 2 rice bowls&#39; worth Hot cooked white rice
1. Get 1 tsp Salt
1. Make ready 8 boiled Peeled shrimp
1. Take 4 shoots Broccolini
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
1. Make ready 1 sheet Toasted nori




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Broccolini and Shrimp Sushi Rolls:

1. Roll the rice into a ball about the size of a large mini tomato. Wet your hands and lightly coat in salt.
1. Divide the nori into 6 long strips, and make 6 long and narrow sushi wraps.
1. Remove the hard stems from the broccolini, cut to 3-4 cm lengths, parboil in salt water (not listed), then drain.
1. Roll the rice in the nori seaweed, top with the shrimp, broccolini, mayonnaise, and they are done.
