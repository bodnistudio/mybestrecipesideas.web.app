##### The ingredients needed to make Leftover Chicken Soup:

1. Get 1 tbsp Butter
1. Get 1 clove Garlic, minced
1. Take 1/2 cup Celery, chopped
1. Take 1/2 cup Onion, chopped
1. Take 1/2 cup Carrot, chopped
1. Get 2 box Chicken Broth(32oz)
1. Take 1/2 lb Chicken, cooked
1. Make ready 2 1/2 cup Egg Noodles
1. Take 1/2 tsp Basil
1. Get 1/2 tsp Oregano
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Take 1 Cracked Pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Leftover Chicken Soup:

1. I used leftover chicken chicken from roasting a whole chicken the day before. You can also cut up chicken breasts and cook them up instead
1. Melt butter in large pot. Add garlic and cook for 30sec. Add the rest of veggies to pot. Cook for about 5 minutes. Stir occasionaly
1. Pour in chicken broth. Stir in chicken, noodles, and seasonings. Bring to boil
1. Reduce heat and simmer for 20min
1. Enjoy!
