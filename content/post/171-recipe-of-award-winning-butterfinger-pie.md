<h3>The ingredients needed to make Butterfinger Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 cup of peanut butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 2 tsp of vanilla. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 (12.5 oz) of bag fun sized butterfinger crushed (save 2 for on top). </li>
	
		<li>{Take 8 oz of cool whip. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 of chocolate ready made pie crust. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Butterfinger Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Beat cream cheese and peanut butter until smooth.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add in vanilla and powdered sugar.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Fold in candy bars and cool whip.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Spread in pie crust and top with last of candy.
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Refrigerate for 4 hours.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
