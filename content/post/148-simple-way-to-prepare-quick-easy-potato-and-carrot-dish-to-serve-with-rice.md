##### Steps to make Easy Potato and Carrot Dish to Serve with Rice:

1. Cut the potatoes into matchstick shaped pieces about 4 to 5 cm long and about 5 mm wide pieces. Soak the slices in water (but not for too long).
1. Cut the carrots to about the same size as the potatoes.
1. Pour vegetable oil in a frying pan on medium heat and add the potatoes and carrots. Add sake when the potatoes become transparent. Let the sake cook down.
1. Turn up the heat a little and add soy sauce. Fry until it gives off a nice roasted aroma, then sprinkle in the sesame seeds. You&#39;re all done!
