##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Cheesy Potatoes:

1. Make ready 2 cup whipping cream
1. Take 1 1/2 cup shredded chedder cheese
1. Prepare 1/4 cup green onions/onions
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Take 1 tsp black pepper
1. Take 4 cup hash browns




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Simple Cheesy Potatoes:

1. preheat oven to 350
1. mix thawed hash browns, whipping cream, onions, and only 1 1/4 of shredded cheese in large mixing bowl.
1. pour into a 11x7 casserole dish
1. sprinkle left over shredded cheese on top
1. cook on 350 for 35-45 minutes.
