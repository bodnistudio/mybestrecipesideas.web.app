##### Instructions to make Shrimp Pizza:

1. Sauté shrimp in butter and garlic
1. Fry bacon and crumble after cooling
1. Prepare pizza dough as per instructions on package
1. Spread alfredo sauce over dough
1. Spread approximately 1/3 of the cheese mixture on top of the sauce
1. Place shrimp evenly over the whole pizza
1. Evenly distribute the bacon, onion and peppers over the pizza
1. Spread remaining cheese over everything
1. A touch of salt, pepper and oregano over all.
1. Finish baking for approximately 10mins @ 400°.
1. Enjoy!!!
