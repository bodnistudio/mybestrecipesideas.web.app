##### The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Steaks in Red Wine Teriyaki Sauce:

1. Make ready 250 grams Ground beef and pork mix
1. Take 1/2 Onion
1. Take 2 tbsp Panko
1. Make ready 2 tbsp Milk
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Take 1 Salt and pepper
1. Get 1/2 clove Garlic
1. Prepare  ♢Sauce♢
1. Prepare 3 tbsp Soy sauce
1. Make ready 3 tbsp Red wine
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Mirin
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Sugar
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Hamburger Steaks in Red Wine Teriyaki Sauce:

1. Finely chop the onions and garlic, and saute in a bit of oil until they wilt. Soak the panko in milk.
1. Add step 1, the ground meat, egg, and salt and pepper into a bowl and knead thoroughly. Divide into 3 equal portions, form into small oval patties, and lightly press down on the center of the patties with 2 fingers.
1. Add step 2 to a pan coated with oil, cook over a medium-strong heat, and flip over once they have browned. Cover with a lid and steam-cook over a low heat until the burgers have cooked through.
1. Arrange the hamburgers onto a plate, wipe the grease from the pan, add in all of the ingredients for the sauce, and boil down for about 1 minute over a strong heat.
1. Top the hamburgers with the sauce, and you are done .
