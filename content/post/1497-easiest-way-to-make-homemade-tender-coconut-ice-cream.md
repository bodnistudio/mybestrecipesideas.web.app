##### The ingredients needed to make Tender coconut ice cream:

1. Make ready 1 cup non dairy whipping cream

1. Prepare 1/2 cup coconut water
1. Prepare 1/2 cup condensed milk
1. Get 2 table spoon sugar

##### Steps to make Tender coconut ice cream:

1. Take the chilled whipping cream in a big bowl and start beating with a electric beater still it forms soft peak. Add sugar to it and mix well
1. In a mixing jar make 1/2 cup coconut water and soft kernel of 2 tender coconut and make smooth paste
1. Mix the coconut kernel paste and the condensed milk into the cream and mix well. And keep it in the fridge for 2 hours
1. Cut the rest coconut kernel in small pieces and keep aside
1. After two hours put the chopped coconut kernel into the cream mixture and mix well with a fork
1. Put the mixture in a air tight container and keep it in the fridge for 7 to 8 hours or overnight.
1. Your delicious tender coconut ice cream is ready to serve
