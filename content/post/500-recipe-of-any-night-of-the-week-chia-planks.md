##### The ingredients needed to make Chia Planks:

1. Prepare 1 cup Chia Seeds
1. Get 1 cup old fashion oats
1. Prepare 1/3 cup Bran Bud cereal
1. Prepare 1 tbsp pumpkin spice
1. Get 1 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
1. Take 2 tbsp honey
1. Get 1 cup hot water
1. Make ready 6 pitted figs - minced
1. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla extract




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chia Planks:

1. Heat oven to 325 and line medium cookie sheet with parchment paper.
1. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl
1. Mix wet ingredients in separate bowl,   let sit 10 minutes so figs get soft.
1. Pour wet into dry and stir till well mixed. Pour out on to lined pan. Spread mixture about to about a quarter inch thick.
1. Bake 30 minutes. Rove from oven cut into planks, carefully turn planks over and bake 30 more minutes. Let completely cool on cookie sheet.
