##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious Salmon Steak:

1. Prepare 1 piece of Salmon Steak per person (about 500g)
1. Prepare 50 ml olive oil (does about 3 steaks)
1. Prepare  Club House salmon seasoning
1. Make ready 1 Lime
1. Prepare bunch of Green beans (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Delicious Salmon Steak:

1. in a small bowl, mix olive oil and seasoning
1. With a small cheese grater, add lime zest to taste
1. Cut lime in half and squeeze lime juice to taste, blend well
1. put salmon steaks on a large plate, and add olive oil marinade all over with a small brush
1. put steaks in the fridge and let the marinade soak in for at least 30 minutes
1. add some olive oil to a cooking pan, and start cooking the salmon steaks, skin side in the Pan
1. cook until steaks are whiter on the outside, and feel ferm when pressed on with a fork (undercooked salmon is not dangerous to eat, and some might even prefer it that way)
1. (Optional) Serve with fresh green beans, or cooked in butter!
