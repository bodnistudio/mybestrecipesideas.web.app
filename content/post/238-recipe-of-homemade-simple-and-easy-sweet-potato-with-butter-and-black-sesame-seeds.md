##### The ingredients needed to make Simple and Easy Sweet Potato with Butter and Black Sesame Seeds:

1. Take 100 grams Sweet potato
1. Get 5 grams Butter (Margarine)
1. Get 1/2 tsp or more Sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp Honey
1. Prepare 1 tsp or more Black sesame seeds




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Simple and Easy Sweet Potato with Butter and Black Sesame Seeds:

1. Slice sweet potato into 5-7mm half circles (or if it&#39;s large, cut into quarters). Soak in water then drain.
1. Place sweet potatoes in a microwavable dish and cover with plastic wrap. Microwave for about 2 minutes until softened. (My microwave is 900 W)
1. Heat the butter or margarine in a pan then add sugar.
1. Transfer the sweet potatoes from Step 2 to the pan and cook until golden brown, coating with butter. Drizzle in the honey.
1. Toss with black sesame seeds. I recommend a generous amount.
1. Serve on a plate. Bon appetit!
