##### Steps to make Corn Bread Pizza:

1. Take a suji add fresh cream in it and mix it well and add little water in it and make a thick paste and keep it rest for 15 minutes.
1. After 15 minutes now add more little water in it.And mix it well.Now add all the vegetables in it along with corn and mix it well.Add all the spices too.
1. Now take one slice of bread and with the help of steel glass give it a round shape.Now cover the slice top with the suji paste and like this make all the slices.
1. Now take a tawa,grease ghee on it and keep the slice paste side on the tawa and cook it on the medium low flame and then after 5 minutes flip the side and crisp it for 2 minutes.
1. In this way we cook all the slices.And our corn bread pizza is ready to serve.Serve it with ketchup.
