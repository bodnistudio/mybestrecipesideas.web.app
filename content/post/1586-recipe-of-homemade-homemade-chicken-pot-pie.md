##### Instructions to make Homemade chicken pot pie:

1. Preheat oven to 425.
1. Put potatoes and vegetables in microwave safe bowl and heat until vegetables are tender. About 5-7 minutes.
1. Roll out the pie crust in a pie pan.
1. Mix all remaining ingredients and put in pie pan.
1. Cover with remaining pie crust.
1. Bake for 30 - 40 minutes or until pie crust is golden brown. I covered the edges with aluminum foil so the edges did not get too brown.
1. Remove from oven. Let sit for 10 - 15 minutes. Then serve and enjoy.
