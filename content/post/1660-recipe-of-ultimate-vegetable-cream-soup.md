##### Steps to make Vegetable Cream Soup:

1. Chop the onions and the carrots into small pieces. Add to hot large skillet (or a small pot) with a layer of cooking oil and cook until soft.
1. Chop the garlic and add it to the skillet when the onions are browned and continue cooking for 1-2 minutes.
1. Add some water to the pot and the can of veggies if you have it (or any veggies you want to). If you don&#39;t have any veggies you can supliment the quantity of potatoes and courgettes and optionally add a vegetable cube or stock instead of water.
1. Finally add the roughly chopped potatoes (peeled) and courgettes (with skin). And add water/stock to the skillet until you have a stew like percentage of water to solids.
1. Season with salt and pepper and boil slowly for about 30 minutes (until potatoes crush easily with a wooden spoon).
1. Using a blender of any kind blend the soup. No cream needed but can optionally be added. The potatoe should make the soup creamy.
