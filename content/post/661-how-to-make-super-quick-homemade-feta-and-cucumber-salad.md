##### The ingredients needed to make Feta and Cucumber Salad:

1. Take 2 Diced English Cucumbers
1. Get 1/2 Cup Pomegranate pearls
1. Get 50 Grams Feta Cheese(crumbled)
1. Take 2 teaspoon Lemon Juice
1. Take 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
1. Prepare  Microgreens handful (optional)
1. Get to taste Salt and Pepper


This is an easy salad to throw together, the base is tomatoes, cucumbers, and feta cheese. We used cherry and pear tomatoes and cucumbers from the garden, and added some fresh mint, oregano, and shallots. Dressed with lemon juice and olive oil. You could also add some Kalamata olives and arugula.

##### Steps to make Feta and Cucumber Salad:

1. In a mixing bowl, add cucumber and pomegranate, give a gentle toss to mix.
1. Add in the feta crumbles, mix again.
1. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, season with salt and pepper, mix till incorporated.
1. Transfer to a bowl, garnish with some micro-greens and serve.
