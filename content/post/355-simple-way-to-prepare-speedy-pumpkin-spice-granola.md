<h3>The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Spice Granola:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 1/2 cups of rolled oats. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of roughly chopped pecans. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 cup of unsalted pumpkin seeds. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/2 cup of pumpkin puree. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of pure maple syrup. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 cup of melted coconut oil (could also use melted unsalted butter). </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2 tbsp. of brown sugar. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 tbsp. of pumpkin pie spice. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1 tsp. of vanilla extract. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/2 tsp. of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 2/3 cup of dried cranberries (optional). </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Steps to make Pumpkin Spice Granola:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, maple syrup, coconut oil (or butter), pumpkin pie spice, brown sugar, vanilla and salt until combined..
