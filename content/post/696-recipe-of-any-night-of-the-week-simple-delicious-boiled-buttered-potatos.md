##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Delicious boiled buttered Potatos:

1. Take 8 medium sized red potatos, peeled and simmered in chicken stock until tender 15 to 25 minutes depending on size, a knife should slide in with no resistance.
1. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper and salt to taste
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp cajun seasoning OR chili powder, depending on your preference
1. Take 1 tsp hot sauce, such as franks brand
1. Prepare 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
1. Take 6 green onions, chopped
1. Get 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

##### Instructions to make Simple Delicious boiled buttered Potatos:

1. Melt butter add all seasonings, carefully combine with hot drained potatos, serve hot.
