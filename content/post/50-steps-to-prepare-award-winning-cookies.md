##### The ingredients needed to make Cookies:

1. Get 3/4 cup maida
1. Get 1/2 cup sugar
1. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp vanilla essance
1. Get Pinch baking powder
1. Get Pinch red colour
1. Make ready 1 tbsp milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Cookies:

1. Firstly mix butter and sugar in a bowl.
1. Now mix maida,baking powder and vanilla essence. Make a crumble dough.
1. Add little milk for binding. Divide the dough in two parts.
1. In one part add pinch of red colour. Make a soft dough with little milk.
1. Roll the dough in thickeness of 1/2 cm.make the shape with cookie cutter.
1. Now brush some water in every shape and set one upon one and wrap in butter paper and keep in freezer for 20 to 30 minutes.
1. Then take in from freezer and cover with white batter.
1. Set the dough in freezer again for 30 minutes. Now cut them in cookies of 1/2 cm thikness.
1. Bake them in preheated kadai for 20 minutes. Yummy cookies are ready.
