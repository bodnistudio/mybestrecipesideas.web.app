<h3>The ingredients needed to make Chicken Pot Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>{Get 4 of boneless, skinless, chicken breasts, cooked and shredded. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 2 C. of frozen mixed vegetables, thawed. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 3/4 C. of chicken broth. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/4 C. of flour. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/3 C. of butter. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1/3 C. of chopped onion. </li>
	
		<li>{Take 1 C. of milk, divided. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1 C. of Bisquick. </li>
	
		<li>{Prepare 1 of egg. </li>
	
		<li>{Make ready 1/2 tsp. of salt. </li>
	
		<li>{Get 1/4 tsp. of black pepper. </li>
	
</ol>
<p>
	
</p>

<h3>Instructions to make Chicken Pot Pie:</h3>

<ol>
	
		<li>
			Melt butter in skillet over medium-high heat until bubbly, add chopped onion and cook for 3-4 min or until onion is fragrant and soft..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Add to the skillet flour, salt and pepper. Stir in chicken broth and 1/2 C. milk slowly and continue stirring until sauce has thickened..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Bring chicken and vegetables to skillet and mix well..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			In a small bowl combine bisquick, remaining milk and egg..
			
			
		</li>
	
		<li>
			Pour filling into baking dish and pour Bisquick batter over top and spread to even out. Pop it in the oven for 30 min. Enjoy!.
			
			
		</li>
	
</ol>
