##### The ingredients needed to make Decorative Sushi Rolls:

1. Take 180 to 270 grams Freshly cooked rice
1. Make ready 1 Sushi vinegar
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Take 1 Cucumber
1. Prepare 8 slice Smoked salmon
1. Prepare 1 Sashimi slices




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Decorative Sushi Rolls:

1. Thinly slice cucumbers lengthwise with a peeler and use 1/2 an egg to make an egg crepe, then cut into long, thin slices.
1. Fold (as if cutting) the seasoned rice vinegar into the freshly cooked rice to make sushi rice.
1. Form the sushi rice into balls with a slightly flat surface on the bottom and top. Wrap with cucumber, egg, or smoked salmon.
1. Top with your favorite garnishes (raw tuna, grilled egg, avocado, etc.) and serve!
1. This could be a festive way to dress up a table for Christmas, Doll&#39;s Festival, or on birthdays.
