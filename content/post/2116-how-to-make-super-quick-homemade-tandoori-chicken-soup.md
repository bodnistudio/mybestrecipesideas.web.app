##### The ingredients needed to make Tandoori Chicken Soup:

1. Get  whole ingredients
1. Take 1 large baked or roasted chicken carcass
1. Prepare 1 oz minced ginger
1. Get 1 gallon boiling water
1. Prepare 2 cup uncooked rice
1. Make ready 1 packages frozen peas and carrots
1. Take 3 cup applesauce
1. Prepare 4 clove fresh garlic
1. Take  spices
1. Get 4 tbsp paprika
1. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp salt
1. Take 1 1/2 tbsp ground black pepper
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp cloves
1. Get 2 tbsp each marjoram leaves, tarragon leaves, ground cardamom, ground coriander seed
1. Make ready 1 dash crushed red pepper to taste

##### Steps to make Tandoori Chicken Soup:

1. boil gallon water in large pot
1. add chicken carcass and dripping to boiling water and boil for 2 hours on medium heat until meat falls from bone
1. carefully remove bones. allow stock to cool if need be before removing bones
1. add all spices to stock and simmer for 1 hour
1. add remaining ingredients to stock and simmer covered for and additional hour
1. allow to cool, then serve
