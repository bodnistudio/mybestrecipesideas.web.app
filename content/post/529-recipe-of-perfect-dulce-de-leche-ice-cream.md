##### The ingredients needed to make Dulce de leche ice-cream:

1. Take 396 gram or 14 0z Caramelized Condensed milk –  (Readymade Or Homemade)
1. Take 3 cup Full fat milk –
1. Take 2 cup Full Cream –
1. Prepare 2/4 tsp Vanilla extract –
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Pecans  Chopped Toasted (Optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Dulce de leche ice-cream:

1. How to make Dulce de leche...
1. Take one tin Condensed milk, Remove the Plastic lid.Fill a Pressure Cooker with water, half way through. - Lie down the Can into the Pressure Cooker and close the lid. - Switch on the gas on high, wait for steam to come and then put on weight. - When you hear the first whistle simmer the flame and let it cook 30 -40 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame and let it cool to room temperature
1. How to make ice cream
1. Bring milk and cream just to a boil in a 3 quart heavy Pan over moderate heat, then remove from heat and whisk in Dulce de leech until dissolved. - Whisk in vanilla and then fold in Pecans, transfer ice cream mixture to an airtight container and put in freezer to harden. - After one hour take out the mixture of the freezer and whisk again, Repeat this Process 3-4 times during between every one hour and finally keep in the Freezer till ice-cream set.
1. Yammy delicious ice-cream is ready
