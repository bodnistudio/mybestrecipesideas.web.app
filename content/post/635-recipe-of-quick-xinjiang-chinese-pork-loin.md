##### The ingredients needed to make Xinjiang (Chinese) Pork Loin:

1. Take 1 3/4 lb pork loin
1. Prepare 4 tbsp soy sauce
1. Make ready 1 tsp ground cumin
1. Make ready 1 tbsp dried red chili peppers (chopped)
1. Take 1 tsp ground black pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Xinjiang (Chinese) Pork Loin:

1. Cut four slices (1/4 inch deep) into the the pork loin.
1. Mix soy
1. Mix soy sauce, cumin, black pepper, and red chili peppers in small bowl.
1. Pour marinade over pork loin, making sure the marinate soaks into the slices. Marinate in refrigerator for 2 hours.
1. Chop onions and bell peppers into slices. Set aside.
1. Bake marinated pork loin at 150 ° C for one hour. After thirty minutes, place onions and peppers around pork loin to soak up the juices.
1. Slice up and serve. (Best with Xinjiang-style green beans or potatoes.)
