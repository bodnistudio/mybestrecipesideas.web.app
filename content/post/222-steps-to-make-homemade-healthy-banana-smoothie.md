##### Instructions to make Healthy banana smoothie:

1. Cut the banana into slices.
1. In a blender, add yogurt,sliced bananas(use half of it because other half will be used for topping), gulkand,almonds and betel leaves.
1. Blend the mixture well.
1. Now add water and honey(optional) to it and blend it again.
1. Pour the mixture in a glass or smoothie jar and serve it chilled with banana toppings.
