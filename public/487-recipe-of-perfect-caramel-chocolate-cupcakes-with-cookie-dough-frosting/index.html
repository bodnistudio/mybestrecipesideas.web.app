Instructions to make caramel chocolate cupcakes with cookie dough frosting:
  1. preheat oven to 350º
  2. Completely melt butter add brown sugar whisk together
  3. whisk in cocoa
  4. Whisk in egg and vanilla well
  5. Put 1 cup of flour in with baking soda and powder whisk together
  6. Add milk whisk together
  7. Add the remaining half a cup of flour in
  8. Fold in caramel then bake for 20 minutes
  9. Let cupcakes cool before frosting.
  10. Cream butter and brown sugar
  11. Mix in vanilla
  12. Mix in flour
  13. Mix in milk
  14. Fold in choco chips
