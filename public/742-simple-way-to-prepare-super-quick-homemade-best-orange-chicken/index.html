The ingredients needed to make Best Orange Chicken:
  1. Get 2-3 lbs chicken, cut in pieces
  2. Take 1 1/2 C pulp-free orange juice
  3. Prepare 1/2 C honey
  4. Get 1 tsp dry mustard
  5. Take 2 tsp onion powder
  6. Take 1 tblsp Worcestershire sauce
  7. Make ready 2 tblsp cornstarch powder

Instructions to make Best Orange Chicken:
  1. Dredge chicken in flour and fry lightly until just golden -do NOT use salt- then cover to keep warm and set aside…if you are using bone-in chicken the frying process will take a lot longer and frankly I don't recommend it
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine orange juice and honey, whisk in the mustard and onion powder and Worcestershire…cook on medium high heat until it begins to bubble
  3. Make a slurry of the cornstarch and a little cold water, stir into the sauce and continue cooking for a few minutes. You may need to add another teaspoon of slurry -the goal is to thicken the sauce so it coats the back of a spoon
  4. Add the cooked chicken and turn to coat..I like to let it stay in the sauce long enough to get medium hot
  5. And there you have it! There is plenty of sauce to both coat the chicken and have enough to drizzle over rice or noodles…ENJOY!! ❤️❤️
