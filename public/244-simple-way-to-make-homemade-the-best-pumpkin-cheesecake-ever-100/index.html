The ingredients needed to make The Best Pumpkin Cheesecake Ever!!! 100:
  1. Prepare crust
  2. Get 1 3/4 cup graham cracker crumbs
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp light brown sugar
  4. Get 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1 stick melted salted butter
  6. Get filling
  7. Get 3 packages (8 oz) cream cheese, room temperature
  8. Make ready 15 oz can pureed pumpkin
  9. Get 3 large eggs
  10. Make ready 1 large egg yolk
  11. Take 1/4 cup sour cream
  12. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sugar
  13. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  14. Take 1 tsp 1/8th teaspoon fresh ground nutmeg
  15. Prepare 1 tsp 1/8th ground cloves
  16. Make ready 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  17. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make The Best Pumpkin Cheesecake Ever!!! 100:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. For crust: double the batch for two pies. With a food processor grind up the graham crackers or smash with rolling pin in a bag. Then mix the sugar and cinnamon. Mix well. Then add the melted butter and mix till thoroughly wet. Now press into pans.
  3. For filling: beat cream cheese till smooth. Add the puree, eggs, egg yolk, sour cream, sugar and the spices. Add flour and vanilla, beat together until well combined.
  4. Port into crust, place in oven for 1 hour. Remove and let sit for 15 minutes at least but preferably one hour. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 4 hours.
