The ingredients needed to make Ravioli with tomato and basil sauce:
  1. Prepare 500 gr ravioli or pasta with spinach and cheese filling
  2. Make ready 500 ml tomato paste
  3. Make ready 5 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  4. Get 2 tbsp dried basil
  5. Make ready 1 can button mushroom
  6. Take 1 handful fresh basil (I didn't have it)
  7. Prepare 1 tap salt
  8. Take 1/2 tsp pepper
  9. Make ready As needed Water to boil ravioli
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp cooking oil
  11. Prepare 1 cup water

Instructions to make Ravioli with tomato and basil sauce:
  1. Boil the water in a pot, add ravioli while the water is boiling, cook ravioli well.
  2. In the meantime, we cook the tomato and basil sauce. Preheat the saute pan with cooking oil, put chopped garlic in and stir it until half cooked.
  3. Add mushroom, stir them we until well done.
  4. Add tomato paste, salt, and pepper, mix them well.
  5. Add a cup of water and stir it well to the perfect thickness (not too thick and not too watery) sprinkle fresh and dried basil and cook for a minute.
  6. When the ravioli cooked well, drain the water and mix it to the sauce well.
  7. Ravioli with tomato and basil sauce is ready to enjoy.
