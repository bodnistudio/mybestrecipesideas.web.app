The ingredients needed to make Simply Baked chicken breasts:
  1. Get 4 chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 tsp chicken bouillon granules or 1 bullillon cube
  3. Take 1/2 cup boiling water
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp parsley flakes
  5. Take Seasoned salt
  6. Prepare Lemon pepper
  7. Take 4 Tbsp melted butter

Steps to make Simply Baked chicken breasts:
  1. Place chicken in baking pan skin side up
  2. Brush each breast with melted butter
  3. Season to taste with seasoned salt and lemon pepper
  4. Disolve chicken bouillon in boiling water
  5. Add parsley to boiling water and stir
  6. Pour boiling water mixture into bottom of pan
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 60 to 90 minutes. Baste every 20 minutes while cooking.
  8. Serve au jus or with pan gravy
