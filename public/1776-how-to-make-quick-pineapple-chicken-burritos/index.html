The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Chicken Burritos:
  1. Make ready 1 pound fried chicken breast
  2. Prepare 1 cup fresh / canned pineapple chunks
  3. Make ready 1 cup diced fresh tomatoes, two medium
  4. Get 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  5. Take 1 cup sour cream
  6. Get To taste kosher salt
  7. Make ready 6 large flour tortillas for burritos
  8. Prepare 1 small lime, optional

Instructions to make Pineapple Chicken Burritos:
  1. Slice the top off the pineapple plant this in the ground and you can grow you a pineapple bush from the top.
  2. Peel the pineapple and cut up what you want to use.
  3. Dice the tomatoes
  4. Chop the chicken
  5. Toast the tortillas and add sour cream in the center.
  6. Add the chicken, tomatoes, and Pineapple pieces.
  7. Add the lettuce. This where you would squeeze a bit of lime juice over the inside before folding it up. Now heat a skillet or pan. Fold the tortilla inwards towards the mixture in the middle of the tortilla.
  8. Now roll it up. Repeat till all are done.
  9. Add the burrito flap side down on the hot skillet. This will cause the flap not to unfold as easy. It will also give a bit of crunch.
  10. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
