Steps to make Spicy cream of tomato soup:

  2. Add pepper to taste
  3. Roasted
  4. Boil up 4 pints or so of water, add stock and lime leaves

  6. Take off the heat, add cream to taste
  7. Heat through for a further 10 mins.
  8. Serve and enjoy
