The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready 125 g flour
  2. Prepare 75 g blueband
  3. Take 75 g sugar
  4. Get 1 pinch salt
  5. Make ready 60 g chocolate chip
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Assemble all the ingredients in order.
  2. Cream the blueband and sugar. Till you get a pale mixture
  3. Add one egg and mix well. Till the egg is well incorporated
  4. You will such a mixture after mixing
  5. Add baking soda, salt to flour then sieve/ sift. Then add to the mixture
  6. Mix while folding till you get a smooth mixture. Then add the vanilla essence and mix again
  7. Add the chopped chocolate chip and mix to be incorporated.
  8. After mixing refrigerate for 20mins as you preheat the oven to 180degrees.
  9. Prepare the baking tray and arrange the batter in your preferred shape
  10. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes. Ready
