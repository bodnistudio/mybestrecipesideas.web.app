The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup instant oatmeal
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  3. Get 1/3 cup melted butter
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  7. Take 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Take 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  10. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  11. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder

Steps to make Oatmeal Cookies:
  1. Mix oatmeal, flour, baking soda, baking powder in a bowl. You can also add the choco chips here if you intend to make choco chip oatmeal cookies.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Then mix in the sugars, oil, melted butter, cinnamon, and vanilla. If you are using unsalted butter, add a pinch to this step.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients bowl. Mix well.
