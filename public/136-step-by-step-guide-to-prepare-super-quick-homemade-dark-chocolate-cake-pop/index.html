The ingredients needed to make Dark Chocolate Cake Pop:
  1. Take 300 g Dark Chocolate
  2. Prepare 264 g Oreos
  3. Make ready 226 g Cream cheese
  4. Take 1 pinch Salt
  5. Make ready 2 tbs Almond crumbs
  6. Get 2 tbs Candy sprinkles

Steps to make Dark Chocolate Cake Pop:
  1. Crush the oreo cookies into fine crumbs
  2. Add cream cheese and salt to the cookie mixture and stir until evenly combined
  3. Line a tray with parchment paper. Roll the mixture into small tight balls and place on the parchment paper
  4. Freeze until slightly hardened, around 30 minutes
  5. Melt the chocolate under indirect heat
  6. Insert a wooden stick into the frozen balls and dip the balls into the melted chocolate until coated
  7. Sprinkle the cake pops with almond crumbs and candy sprinkles
  8. Fix the cake pops into a cup filled with beans
  9. Freeze until chocolate hardens, about 15 minute
