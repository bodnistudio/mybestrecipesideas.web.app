The ingredients needed to make Chicken curry:
  1. Get 1 kg chicken
  2. Make ready 1 cup onion paste
  3. Take 1 cup tomato puree
  4. Take whole spices:
  5. Take 4-5 cloves
  6. Get 5-6 black pepper corns
  7. Take 2-3 green cardamom pods
  8. Get 1 bay leaf
  9. Make ready 1 stick cinnamon
  10. Take other spices:
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp Kashmiri mirchi powder
  14. Take 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  15. Get to taste coriander powder
  16. Get to taste salt
  17. Prepare as needed vegetable oil

Steps to make Chicken curry:
  1. Heat oil in a wok
  2. Add all the whole spices and crackle them.
  3. Add onion paste and saute for 5 minutes.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add turmeric, Kashmiri mirch, and coriander powder, mix well.
  6. Add tomato puree and salt to taste. Cook till mushy.
  7. Add garam masala and chicken pieces, mix well. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Add 2-3 cups of water and cook for 5 minutes or till chicken is done. Now our Chicken curry is ready
