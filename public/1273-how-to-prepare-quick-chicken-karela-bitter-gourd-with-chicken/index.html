Steps to make Chicken karela (bitter gourd with chicken):
  1. Peel & deseed bitter gourd then chop into small pieces with the help of the knife. -On strainer,add bitter gourd,salt and mix well,cover and let it rest for 30 minutes then wash thoroughly 3-4 times and squeeze to remove excess water.
  2. In frying pan,heat cooking oil and fry bitter gourd until golden brown and crispy & set aside.
  3. In wok,add cooking oil and chicken..fry chicken until it perfectly fry…add onion fry until light golden. -Add garlic paste and mix well. -Add tomatoes,green chilllies and mix well. -
  4. Add cumin seeds,salt,turmeric powder,coriander powder,red chilli powder,mix well and cook for 2-3 mins-Cover and cook on low flame for 5 minutes then cook on high flame for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Now add fried bitter gourd and mix well. -Add garam masala powder,cover and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes. -Add fresh coriander,mix & serve!
