  1. Get what you need

  5. Take 1 egg

Steps to make corned beef hash:
  1. fry onion till soft
  2. add cabbage to soften
  3. add corned beef
  4. once corned beef has softened add 2 cups of rice and chicken stock cube
  5. add 4 cups of water
  6. place lid on pan and simmer till rice is light and fluffy
  7. fry off an egg with runny yoke and serve on top
