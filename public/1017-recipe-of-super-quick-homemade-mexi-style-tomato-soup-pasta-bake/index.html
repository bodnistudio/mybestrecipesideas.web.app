Steps to make Mexi style tomato soup pasta bake:
  1. Cook pasta accordingly. Set oven at 400.
  2. Add condensed tomato soup , a can of milk, cumin, and salsa. Simmer and stir. Cook while pasta is cooking.
  3. Melt butter in a large pan. Add cooked pasta. Add chicken , bacon, and cilantro. Mix . Add tomato sauce.
  4. Add cheese. Place in oven and cook 10 minutes. Serve hot and enjoy.
