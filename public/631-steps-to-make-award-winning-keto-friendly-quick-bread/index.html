The ingredients needed to make Keto Friendly Quick Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups almond flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup flaxseed(I used ground)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp coconut flour
  4. Make ready 3/4 tbsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 2/4 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp psyilium husk powder
  7. Make ready 6 eggs
  8. Get 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  9. Get 1/4 cup water
  10. Take 1 1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  11. Prepare 7 drops liquid sweetener
Steps to make Keto Friendly Quick Bread:
  1. Preheat the oven 350F line 9” x 5” loaf pan with parchment paper.in a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, flaxseed,coconut flour, baking soda, salt and psyilium husk powder
  2. In a med. bowl, whisk together the eggs. Cream, water, vinegar, and liquid sweetener. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and stir to combine. Do not over mix.
  3. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake until a skewer inserted into center cons out clean, about 50 minutes.
  4. Cool before serving.
