The ingredients needed to make Tomato Basil Bisque:
  1. Make ready 1 can Tomato Basil Bisque
  2. Take 1 pinch Garlic Salt
  3. Take 1 pinch Black Pepper
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Olive oil
  5. Prepare 2 cup Diced Chicken
  6. Take 1 tbsp Sauteed Onions
  7. Prepare 1 cup Corn
  8. Take 1 dash Basil

Steps to make Tomato Basil Bisque:
  1. Pour soup and heat at med high until boil, then simmer on low and cover
  2. Season, dice and cook chicken in pan with oil
  3. Cook corn to liking
  4. Dice onions, add to cooked chicken and sauteed in oil briefly for about 2-3 minutes
  5. Lastly add to soup and let simmer for an additional 3-5 minutes
  6. Garish with basil (optional)
