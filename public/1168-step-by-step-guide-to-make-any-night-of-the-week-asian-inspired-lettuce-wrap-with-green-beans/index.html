The ingredients needed to make Asian inspired lettuce wrap with green beans:
  1. Get 2 chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 1 head lettuce (any lettuce with large leaves will work)
  3. Make ready 2 cans green beans (I used fresh)
  4. Take 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  5. Get 1/2 onion
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon fresh garlic
  7. Get 1 tablespoon butter
  8. Take 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  9. Take to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Asian inspired lettuce wrap with green beans:
  1. To make sauce- 4 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoon honey, 1 tablespoon peanut butter, 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon ginger.
  2. Coat chicken breasts in 1 tablespoon curry paste. Cook the chicken and onion together until done and shred the chicken completely.
  3. Toss chicken in sauce until coated.
  4. Sautee green beans in the other 1 tablespoon red curry paste, salt and pepper, soy sauce and butter until cooked through.
  5. Lay out the lettuce leaves and place chicken inside and roll up.
  6. Optional- add rice into the wraps, I used the Knorr rice sides Asian BBQ and it was delicious. Can also make white rice, add ginger and soy sauce to get a similar taste.
  7. Enjoy!
