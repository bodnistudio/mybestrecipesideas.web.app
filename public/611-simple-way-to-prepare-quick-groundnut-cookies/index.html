The ingredients needed to make Groundnut cookies:
  1. Take 4 cup flour
  2. Take 1 sachet butter
  3. Take 1 and half teaspoon baking powder
  4. Get 4 tablespoon powdered milk
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  6. Make ready 3 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Get 1/2 nutmeg
  8. Take 1/2 cup fried groundnut
  9. Get 1 large egg
  10. Prepare little water for mixing

Steps to make Groundnut cookies:
  1. Preheat oven temp 150c.
  2. Seive flour in a clean bowl,add baking powder,milk,sugar,grate nutmeg into the bowl mix very correctly.
  3. Add vanilla extract,crush groundnut and add to the bowl and mix.
  4. Add butter and mix very well,break egg and mix everything together.
  5. Add little water and mix very well the dough will be very soft
  6. Roll on a flat surface with rolling pin,cut dough with cookies cutter.Oil tray place the cut dough on tray bake for 15min temp reduce to 100c.
