Instructions to make Bread Basket With Chicken Cheese kabab Buns:
  1. Strain the plain flour
  2. Add plain flour,yeast,sugar
  3. Salt,oil
  4. Add eggs and mix it
  5. Add warm milk knead by hand until smooth and elastic
  6. Cover the dough and let rise in a warm place for about 60 minutes
  7. After that punch the dough
  8. Cover the metal bowl with aluminuim foil and grease with oil
  9. Divide the dough into four peices
  10. We have to use 3/4 portion of dough for making basket and 1/4 portion use for making buns
  11. Take one peice of dough and make press the dough ball and flattend it make a round shape
  12. And cut into strips
  13. Take 6 strips around the metal bowl
  14. Start to weave the dough hide the dough beneaths the dough
  15. Connect with another dough if it is not long enough
  16. Trim the excess dough
  17. Brush the egg yolk on top
  18. Bake into preheated pateela for 30 mins
  19. Bake until golden brown
  20. Basket is ready
  21. Make more ropes for the braided dough
  22. Make two braided dough
  23. Two for the ring top
  24. Brush egg yolk and bake for 35 minutes on preheated pateela medium to low flame
  25. Ring top is ready
  26. Take 8 small balls
  27. Take leftover chicken cheese kabab cut into small pieces
  28. In the center of the dough spread pizza sauce add kabab filling press and seal all edges
  29. Sprinkle oregano on top set all them similarly
  30. Bake into preheated pateela for 40 minutes medium to low flame
  31. Chicken cheese kabab buns is ready
  32. Make basket put into fresh coriander and buns
  33. Serve it
