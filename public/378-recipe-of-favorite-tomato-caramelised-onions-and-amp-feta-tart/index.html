Instructions to make Tomato, Caramelised Onions & Feta Tart:
  1. For the pastry crust – In a bowl add flour mcheddor cheese, parmesan cheese,basil, paprika and salt,mix well
  2. Add butter cubes and using finger tips mix into the flour untl a sand like consistency is reached
  3. Add beaten eggs,cold water as required and gently bring it together slightly.Tip onto a clean floured surface
  4. Gently bring the dough together untilit forms a rough ball.
  5. Flatten the dough slightly into a disc.
  6. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for an hour.
  7. For the tart topping– In a nonstick skillet add olive oil and butter.Add the sliced onions,sugar and dried basil, saute on medium heat until the onions soften,caramalise and turn sticky.Remove the onions in a bowl.
  8. To the onions add garlic powder,bread crumbs,parmesan cheese,salt and pepper. Mix all the ingredients together.
  9. Remove the pastry crust from the refrigerator and roll to the size of a tart pan(9")
  10. Spread the onion bread crumb mixtureon the crust.Top with sliced tomatoes,feta and black olives.
  11. Drizzle with olive oil on top along with an extra pinch of sea salt.
  12. Bake in a pre heated oven at 200 degree centigrade for 20- 25 until the pastry is crisp and golden minutes
  13. Serve warm or at room temperature with a salad on the side.
