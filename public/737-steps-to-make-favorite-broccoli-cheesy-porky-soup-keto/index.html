The ingredients needed to make Broccoli cheesy porky soup (keto):
  1. Prepare 1/2 medium onion
  2. Take 1 can Progresso 3 cheese starter
  3. Make ready 5 slice uncooked bacon
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup honey ham
  5. Prepare 4 cup broccoli
  6. Get 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Take 2 cup whole milk
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cup water

Steps to make Broccoli cheesy porky soup (keto):
  1. Put whole can of Progresso cheese starter into pot with water and milk. put on stove on low heat
  2. cut bacon up to 4 times so that all pieces are between .5 to 1.5 inches. put in a frying pan on low and mix as needed
  3. once bacon is 3/4 cooked, chop onion and add to bacon. add to pot of liquid ingredients and stir, stay on low. turn off heat to frying pan where bacon was cookinh.!
  4. cut up honey ham into quarter inch pieces and throw into pot with other ingredients
  5. cook broccoli in microwave or boil. you can either chop or completely blend the broccoli, it's really a matter of resource and preference. now add the broccoli plus the shredded cheese to the pot and simmer 5 minutes.
  6. turn off your stove and let the soup sit for an additional 10 minutes.
