The ingredients needed to make Blueberry cheese Cake no bake:
  1. Prepare 450 grams cream cheese
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup buttermilk
  3. Take 1 cup crush graham
  4. Take 250 1/2 ml all purpose cream
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp powdered gelatin
  6. Get 1/2 cup water
  7. Get 3/4 cup condensed milk
  8. Get 1 piece egg, beaten

Instructions to make Blueberry cheese Cake no bake:
  1. Mix butter and graham. Press it in cake pan. Chill for 20 mins
  2. Mix the condensed milk and all purpose cream. Add cream cheese until the mixture is smooth
  3. Dissolve gelatin in water for 5 to 10 mins
  4. In a pan pour the cream cheese mixture and simmer for 5 to 7 mins in medium fire with constant stirring
  5. Gradually add the gelatin mixture. Mix until it boils. Remove from fire. Add the beaten egg gradually
  6. Pour evering to the cake pan
  7. Chill overnight. Serve
  8. You can add blueberry topping, strawberry topping..
