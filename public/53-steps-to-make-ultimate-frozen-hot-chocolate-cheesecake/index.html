The ingredients needed to make Frozen Hot Chocolate Cheesecake:
  1. Prepare Oreo or chocolate crust
  2. Prepare 16 oz. Cream cheese
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  4. Get 1/4 cup chocolate sauce
  5. Make ready 3 hot chocolate mixes
  6. Take Pinch salt
  7. Make ready 2 cups heavy cream
  8. Prepare 1 cup mini marshmallows

Steps to make Frozen Hot Chocolate Cheesecake:
  1. Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy
  2. Add powdered sugar, chocolate sauce, hot chocolate mixes and salt. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add half of heavy cream and mix until smooth. Add rest of heavy cream and mix until extra fluffy.
  4. Fold in mini marshmallows and put mixture in crust.
  5. Freeze for 4 hours.
