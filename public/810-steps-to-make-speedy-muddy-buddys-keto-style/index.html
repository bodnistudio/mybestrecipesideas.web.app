The ingredients needed to make Muddy Buddys - Keto Style:

  2. Take 1 cup Lilly's Sugar Free Chocolate Chips
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Natural Creamy Peanut Butter
  4. Get 6 Tbsp Butter
  5. Make ready 2 tsp Vanilla
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  7. Take 1/8 tsp Salt
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup Swerve Confectioners Monk Fruit

Steps to make Muddy Buddys - Keto Style:
  1. Empty Pork Rinds into gallon zip lock bag and break into pieces (Check's Cereal piece sizes) and transfer into bowl.
  2. Melt in a double broiler the chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter until smooth.
  3. Mix in Vanilla, Cinnamon, and Salt.
  4. Take chocolate mixture out of double broiler and put into a large bowl. Mix in pork rinds and stir/mix until completely coated.
  5. Put all contents into a zip lock bag and add monk fruit. Zip up bag and shake until all coated.
  6. Put in a bowl and serve. Store leftovers in refrigerator.
