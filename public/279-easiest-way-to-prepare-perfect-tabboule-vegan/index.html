The ingredients needed to make Tabboule (vegan):
  1. Prepare 1 tomato (not too ripe)
  2. Get 1 bunch parsley
  3. Prepare 1 small onion
  4. Prepare 1 lemon
  5. Take 1 tbsp burghul
  6. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare Olive oil
