  2. Take Olive oil
  3. Make ready 1 onion, sliced
  4. Make ready 1 green pepper, sliced
  5. Prepare 6 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Take 1/2 cup red wine
  7. Make ready 2 cans (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes
  8. Make ready 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  9. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  10. Get 1 tablespoon oregano

  12. Make ready 1 cup heavy cream
