The ingredients needed to make Oreo Cheesecake With Raspberries & Blueberry Syrup:
  1. Take Cheesecake Base
  2. Get 15 Oreos (with filling)
  3. Make ready 30 grams Butter
  4. Make ready Cream Cheese Filling
  5. Take 500 grams Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  6. Get 1000 ml Whipping Cream
  7. Prepare 1 cup Powdered Sugar
  8. Prepare Vanilla Extract
  9. Take Blueberry Sauce
  10. Prepare 250 grams Blueberries
  11. Take Water (Depends how thick sauce you want)
  12. Get dash Lemon Juice
  13. Make ready Sugar (Depends how sweet sauce you want)
  14. Get 6 grams Cornstarch
  15. Prepare Decoration
  16. Make ready Roughly chopped Oreos
  17. Make ready 1 Blueberry Sauce
  18. Get 100 grams Raspberries
Instructions to make Oreo Cheesecake With Raspberries & Blueberry Syrup:
  1. Blend your Oreos in a blender.
  2. Melt your butter and mix it with Oreos.
  3. Put your Oreo mixture in a cake tray and bake it at 180°C/350°F for 5-10 minutes
  4. Whip up your cream unitill it doubles in volume.
  5. Whip your cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  6. Mix together your cream cheese mixture and whipped cream.
  7. Optional: You can add some oreos to your mixture
  8. Put all your ingredients for the blueberry sauce together in a sauce pan over medium to high heat.
  9. Let it cook for a few minutes to thicken up. And pour it over your cake.
  10. Add crushed Oreos and raspberries to decorate and put the cake in a fridge to chill for a few hours. Then serve your delicious cake! Enjoy!!
