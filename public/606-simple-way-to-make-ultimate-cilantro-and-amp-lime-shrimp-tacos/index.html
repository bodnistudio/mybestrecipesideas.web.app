The ingredients needed to make Cilantro & Lime Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Get 1 Shrimp
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  3. Make ready 1 Lime
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp cilantro
  5. Take 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  6. Get 1 clove garlic, chopped
  7. Make ready 1/2 spicy pepper
  8. Take 1/4 onion, chopped
