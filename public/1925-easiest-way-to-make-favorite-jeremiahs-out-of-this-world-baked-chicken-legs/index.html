The ingredients needed to make Jeremiah’s Out-of-this-World Baked Chicken Legs:
  1. Get 10 chicken legs
  2. Make ready 1 cup cooking oil
  3. Get 3/4 tbsp steak seasoning
  4. Make ready 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tbsp lemon pepper
  7. Take 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  8. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 tsp parsley
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp Chinese 5-Spice
Instructions to make Jeremiah’s Out-of-this-World Baked Chicken Legs:
  1. Add the seasonings to the oil and mix very well.
  2. Stir well occasionally during a two hour marinade.
  3. Pre-heat oven at 425°. Put the chicken legs in a large bowl and pour the seasoned oil over top and stir until the chicken is completely coated.
  4. Place legs on a flat sheet and drizzle any leftover seasoned oil on top of the chicken. Line your sheet with foil.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, turning once, or until done. Serve hot.
