The ingredients needed to make Mu Shu Chicken Lettuce Wraps:
  1. Get wraps
  2. Take 4 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Take 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  4. Get 2 tsp rice wine vineagar
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  6. Get 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
  7. Prepare 3 clove minced garlic
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp finely grated fresh ginger
  9. Make ready 8 oz chicken, cooked and shredded. I use store rotisserie chicken for ease of use
  10. Take 9 oz package prepared coleslaw, I prefer the broccoli coleslaw
  11. Get 1 small head of bibb or boston lettuce. You will use 12 outer leaves from head of lettuce
  12. Get 1/4 cup green onions, sliced on bias
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped peanuts

Instructions to make Mu Shu Chicken Lettuce Wraps:
  1. Place all liquids ,garlic, red pepper flakes and ginger into large pan.
  2. Bring to low boil over meat heat and then turn heat off.
  3. Add chicken, stir well and then add coleslaw. Cover, set a side.
  4. Prepare lettuce leaves on plate. You need 12, full untorn leaves.
  5. Divide chicken mixture amongst lettuce leaves
  6. Divide green onions and nuts over top and serve.
