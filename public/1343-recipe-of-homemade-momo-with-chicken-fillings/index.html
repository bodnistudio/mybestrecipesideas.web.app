The ingredients needed to make Momo with chicken fillings:
  1. Get 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup boiled chicken minced

  4. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Get To taste salt
  6. Prepare As needed oil
  7. Get 2 tbsp tomato sauce

Steps to make Momo with chicken fillings:
  1. Knead a soft dough with flour salt and water
  2. Take small portion of the dough and roll into round shape
  3. Mix the minced chicken with garlic soy sauce, black pepper powder and salt
  4. Now fill into the round Puri's and fold
  5. Take a steamer steam it for 20 minutes
  6. Serve it with dips,tomato sauce and green chutney
