The ingredients needed to make Rich Tomato Soup:
  1. Take 4 Tomatoes big
  2. Make ready 1 Carrot
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  4. Make ready Bay leaf 1
  5. Prepare 1 Black cardamom
  6. Make ready Cloves 4
  7. Make ready 6 Black pepper
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Get 1 tbsp Cornflour
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sugar
  11. Make ready 4 cups Water
  12. Get 4 tsp Cream fresh for 4 cups
  13. Get 2 tbsp Bread croutons
Instructions to make Rich Tomato Soup:
  1. Cut tomatoes and carrot in pieces.Put in a cooker with 2 glasses of water and all the sabut spices and salt. Add ginger and garlic paste.
  2. Boil it 4 or 5 whistles.Cool down and seive.
  3. Take out all sabut masalas and discard them. Grind tomatoes in a mixie.
  4. Mix with seived water and keep for boiling.
  5. Mix cornflour in 2 tbsp water and add to the boiling soup.It will become thick.Now add black pepper to taste.
  6. Pour into bowls.Decorate with bread croutons and cream.
