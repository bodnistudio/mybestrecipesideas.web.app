Steps to make Chicken wet fry for Easter:
  1. Wash your half chicken clean and add a cup of water and salt, leave it to boil for thirty minutes
  2. Put oil of your pan and add onions, grounded ginger and garlic
  3. Add tomatoes then half tea spoon of salt
  4. Add paprika and hoho and leave the mixture to cook, make sure it doesn't burn
  5. Add your already boiled chicken and a glass of water
  6. Leave it to boil for 15 minutes then it will be ready to serve
  7. Serve with rice or ugali
