The ingredients needed to make Tomato bisque:
  1. Take 28 oz and Marzano peeled tomatoes crushed by hand
  2. Make ready 2 cups chicken stock
  3. Prepare 3 large garlic cloves chopped
  4. Get 2 medium carrots chopped
  5. Make ready Half yellow onion chopped
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  7. Get Salt and sugar
  8. Make ready Heavy cream or half and half

Instructions to make Tomato bisque:
  1. Melt the butter and add the carrots and onions until onions are soft, then add garlic until fragrant
  2. Add the tomatoes, chicken stock,2 tbsp dried basil, 2 bay leaves and a few pinches of salt and sugar, cover and simmer for about 30 minutes on low.
  3. Pull off the heat and let cool for a bit. Use a stick blender until smooth and then taste for seasoning, put the pot on medium heat and add salt to taste, finish with a touch of cream or to taste.
