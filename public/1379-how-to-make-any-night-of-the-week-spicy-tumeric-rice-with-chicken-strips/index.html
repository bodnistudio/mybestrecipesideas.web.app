The ingredients needed to make Spicy tumeric rice with chicken strips:
  1. Prepare 3 cups pre boiled rice
  2. Make ready 1 large onion
  3. Take 1 tablespoon tumeric
  4. Take 1 tabledspoon mixed spices
  5. Get 1 chicken breast
  6. Take 1 green capsicum
  7. Get 2 carrots
  8. Prepare 1 cup preboiled green peas
  9. Take Cooking oil
  10. Make ready to taste Salt
  11. Take 1 teaspoon cummin seeds
  12. Get 1 teaspoon rosemary leaves

Instructions to make Spicy tumeric rice with chicken strips:
  1. Cut the chicken breast into strips and prepare other ingredients
  2. Pour the strips in a pan add in some oil and let the strips cook until browned. Then set aside
  3. Fry the onions in oil
  4. Add in the carrots and capsicum
  5. Add in salt, tumeric, cumin, mixed spices and rosemary leaves
  6. Add in the peas and stir
  7. Add in the chicken strips
  8. Add the rice bit by bit as you stir till well blended with the other ingredients
  9. Turn the heat low for 1 min. Serve and enjoy
