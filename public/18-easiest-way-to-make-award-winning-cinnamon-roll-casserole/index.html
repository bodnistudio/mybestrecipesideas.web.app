The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon roll casserole:
  1. Make ready 1 can refrigerate cinnamon roll
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Get 1/4 cup almond milk
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon cinnamon ground
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon butter
  7. Get 1/2 frozen banana optional
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup pecan optional

Instructions to make Cinnamon roll casserole:
  1. Remove cinnamon roll from package and cut them
  2. Spread the butter in the bread pan
  3. Spread the cinnamon roll evenly in the pan topping with pecan and frozen banana
  4. In a separate bowl combined eggs milk vanilla and cinnamon whisk and pour over cinnamon roll
  5. Cook at 350 f for 25 to 30 minutes until center is set, serve with icing that came with package enjoy 😉
