The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Tomatoes:
  1. Prepare 1/2 lb ground beef
  2. Make ready 1/2 onion; diced
  3. Get 1 stalk celery; diced
  4. Make ready 1/2 carrot; diced
  5. Prepare 3 garlic cloves; diced
  6. Make ready 4 tomatoes preferably beefsteak tomatoes
  7. Prepare 1 bellpepper
  8. Get 1 Yukon gold potato; cut into wedges
  9. Make ready 3/4 ths cup short-grain rice
  10. Get 1-2 tbsp tomato paste
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  12. Prepare 1 cup chicken broth (water is fine)
  13. Take Mint
  14. Prepare Parsley
  15. Prepare Dill
  16. Prepare Feta cheese

Instructions to make Stuffed Tomatoes:
  1. Cut the tops off the tomatoes and bell pepper. Keep them as they will be your lid. Excavate the tomato insides and set aside in a bowl.
  2. Sauté the onions and garlic until they reduce in size and turn slightly brown. Add in the celery and carrots and cook until reduced/browned. Adding salt helps!
  3. Brown the ground beef until no longer pink. Add the sugar. More salt.
  4. Add the tomato paste and rice and sauté for 2 mins.
  5. Preheat oven to 370F.
  6. Pour in the tomato insides and 1 cup of chicken broth. Simmer for 10 mins. Taste, add more salt and pepper if needed.
  7. Before we stuff, mix in the parsley, mint, and dill.
  8. Fillerup! I put potato wedges in between the veggies. Drizzle olive oil and yes - salt & pepper! Pour in half cup of water to the pan.
  9. Cover lightly with foil and bake for 1 hr.
  10. Uncover and broil until slightly charred.
  11. Serve with feta cheese on top. Enjoy!
