The ingredients needed to make Fresh Peach Cheesecake Crumble Bars:
  1. Get FOR CRUMB MIXTURE FOR CRUST AND TOPPING
  2. Prepare 2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 2 cup oats, I used Quaker old fashioned oats
  5. Get 2 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup iight brown sugar, packed
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  9. Make ready PEACH CHEESECAKE LAYER
  10. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
  11. Make ready 2/3 cup confectioner's sugar
  12. Take 2 large 3ggs, beaten
  13. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  14. Get 5 cup about 6 medium fresh peaches, peeled, pit removed and sliced in about 1/4 inch slices
  15. Get 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  16. Prepare CHOCOLATE DRIZZLE
  17. Make ready 12 oz semisweet chocolate, melted right before using

Instructions to make Fresh Peach Cheesecake Crumble Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 9 by 13 inch baking pan with baking spray
  2. In a bowl with a fork mix flour, salt, baking soda, oats , light brown sugar, cinnamon and butter until moistened and combined
  3. Press half of the flour/ oat mixture into prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes then cool while making filling
  4. In a bowl beat cream cheese, confectioner's sugar, vanilla, eggs and cinnamon until smooth.
  5. Spread cream cheese filling evenly over baked crust
  6. Evenly line up peach slices on filling
  7. Sprinkle remaing flour/oat crumble evenly over peaches
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until filling is just set and topping is golden
  9. Cool on rack 30 minutes before adding chocolate drizzle. Melt chocolate for drizzle in microwave or double boiler. Drizzle over top. Chill until set
  10. Cut into bars
