Instructions to make Vanilla cheesecake topped with chocolate and caramel:
  1. Crush your biscuits until very fine
  2. Melt your butter and add to the biscuits
  3. Arrange you're biscuits in the bottom of the cheesecake pan equally and press them, put in the freezer until you make cream layer
  4. Whip cream cheese, sugar, vanilla extract alone
  5. Whip cream alone
  6. Add cream to cream cheese mixture and mix
  7. Dissolve your gelatin in warm water and add slowly to the mixture
  8. Pour over the biscuits layer ang put it in the fridge for 5 hours at least
