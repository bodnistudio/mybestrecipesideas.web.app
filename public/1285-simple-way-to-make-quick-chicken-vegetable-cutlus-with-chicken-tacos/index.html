Instructions to make Chicken vegetable cutlus with chicken tacos:
  1. First make cutlus,in a bowl add chicken salt chilli powder chilli flakes boiled potatoes green coriander green chillies and mix well,make shape of kababs
  2. Make dilute paste of maida by adding water and salt in it,heat oil in pan dip kabab in maida and put it in oil, and fry them until becomes golden from both sides,put them on side
  3. Now make chicken filling,heat oil in pan,put onion in it when they become transculant add ginger garlic and saute it for 2,3 mints
  4. Then add boiled shedderd chicken and cook it for 3,4 mints,then add all spices mix them
  5. Then add finely chopped vegetables corns boiled potatoes add soya sauce chilli sauce vinegar black pepper and mix well filling is done
  6. Cut toping ingredients
  7. Fill tacos shell with chicken filling
  8. In a plate first spread some sauce in it then put kabab some cabbage leaves cover it with second kabab and put tacos on the top place cabbage carrot and tomato slices on the top
  9. Chicken vegetable cutlus with chicken tacos are ready i serve this with ketchup
  10. Note you can dip kabab in beaten egg before frying instead of using maida,you can use chicken tikka for tacos filling
