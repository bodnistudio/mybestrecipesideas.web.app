The ingredients needed to make Keto lemon cheesecake bombs:
  1. Prepare 6 oz cream cheese
  2. Get 1/2 stick salted butter
  3. Take 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 2 Tbsp Lemon paste
  5. Take 2 Tbsp granulated sugar
  6. Get 2 Tbsp water
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut flour
  8. Make ready Coating
  9. Prepare 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  10. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut shreds
