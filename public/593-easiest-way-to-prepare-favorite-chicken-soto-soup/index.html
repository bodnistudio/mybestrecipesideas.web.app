The ingredients needed to make Chicken Soto Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 kg chicken
  2. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  3. Make ready 4 kaffir lime leaves
  4. Prepare 2 lemongrass
  5. Make ready 2 cm galangal
  6. Prepare 1 lt water
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1 tsp sugar
  9. Take 1/2 tsp peper
  10. Take 3 spring onions
  11. Prepare 2 Celery stalks
  12. Take leaves Celery
  13. Prepare Rice noodles
  14. Get 2 potatoes
  15. Prepare 3 boiled eggs
  16. Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
  17. Make ready Grounded spices:
  18. Make ready 1/2 red onion
  19. Get 5 garlic cloves
  20. Take 3 cm turmeric
  21. Make ready 2 cm ginger
  22. Prepare 4 candle nuts
  23. Take 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  24. Get Chili sauce :
  25. Get 10 birdeye chilies
  26. Make ready 5 garlic cloves
  27. Make ready 3 candle nuts
  28. Take 1/2 tap salt
  29. Prepare Water to boil
Steps to make Chicken Soto Soup:
  1. Blend the spices that need to be ground in a blender until smooth.
  2. Preheat a pot with cooking oil, pour the grounded spices and stir until cooked. Add lemongrass, bay leaves, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, salt, sugar, and pepper. Stir them well.
  3. After around 3 mins, add the water and chicken into a pot, stir it until the water blends with the spices, add celery stalks, cover the pot and let the chicken cooked really well.
  4. Meanwhile we prepare for the other stuff, cut the potatoes into 4 and slice thinly like chips and fry them until crispy, put aside.
  5. Peel boiled eggs and cut into half. Put aside.
  6. Boil some water and cook the rice noodle for 3 mins until it soften, drain the water with the strainer, put aside.
  7. Chop celery leaves, put aside.
  8. If you like spicy we will make the chili sauce for it, boil the chilies, fry garlic and candle nuts with a little bit of oil for 3 mins (don't let them burn) Mash the garlic and candle nuts while hot, add boiled chilies, mash them well, add salt and taste it.
  9. After the chicken cooked properly, take them out of the soup and shred them, put aside.
  10. When everything is ready we prepare a bowl to serve it, put rice noodle, shredded chicken, and potato chips, pour the hot soup on top of them, add boiled egg, sprinkle chopped celery leaves, and put chili sauce on the side if you like it spicy.
  11. You can add rice on the side too. Happy cooking
