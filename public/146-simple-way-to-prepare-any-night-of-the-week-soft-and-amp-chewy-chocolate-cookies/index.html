The ingredients needed to make Soft & Chewy Chocolate Cookies:
  1. Prepare 113 gr butter (room temperature)
  2. Take 100 gr white sugar
  3. Prepare 100 gr brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Make ready 20 gr cocoa powder
  6. Get 172 gr flour
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Get White chocolate chunks
Steps to make Soft & Chewy Chocolate Cookies:
  1. In a bowl, well mix together butter, white sugar and brown sugar then add egg, mix until creamy (spatula / whisk mix is ok)
  2. Pour dry ingredients; flour+cocoa powder, baking powder and baking soda
  3. Add white chocolate chunks and mix dough with spatula
  4. Divide dough into small portions (30-40 gr each, scoop by the rounded tablespoon onto the prepared sheet pan. Give some space
  5. Pre-heat oven then bake the cookies at 180 deg C around 7-10 minutes. Let cool on the pan for a few minutes then use a cooling rack to cool cookies completely.
  6. Enjoy! Store baked cookies in a jar at room temperature.
