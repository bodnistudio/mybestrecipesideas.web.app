The ingredients needed to make Low Carb Strawberry Cheesecake Bites:
  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Make ready 150 grams Cream Cheese (150g, 5.3 oz, full fat)
  3. Prepare 70 grams Strawberries (fresh or frozen, 70g, about 4 medium berries)
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  5. Get 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  6. Get 20 grams Powdered Erythritol (20g)
  7. Take Alternative Ingredients
  8. Take 20 grams Just Like Sugar (baking, 20g) or 10-15 drops Liquid Stevia
  9. Prepare 60 grams Extra Virgin Coconut Oil, 60g (butter substitute)

Steps to make Low Carb Strawberry Cheesecake Bites:
  1. Place ingredients into bowl of stand mixer (or other bowl), leave cream cheese, butter, and frozen strawberries to warm up to room temperature for 1 hour
  2. Add vanilla and sweetener. For a better sweet flavor, mix two types of sweetener together. Such as 10g Erythritol and 10g Just Like Sugar.
  3. Using stand or hand mixer, start to mix components together until smooth even pink color, 5-10 minutes on low speed.
  4. Using a mini muffin pan or candy silicon molds, spoon mixture into 12 total molds.
  5. Place in freezer for 1-2 hours until chilled. Store in freezer. Can leave in fridge overnight to soften before eating if you like.
