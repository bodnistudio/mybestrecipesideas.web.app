The ingredients needed to make Grilled Fresh Broccoli:
  1. Prepare 2 medium heads of broccoli with stems attached
  2. Take 2 tablespoons salted butter, melted
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil, I used chili infused extra virgin olive oil
  4. Take 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  5. Get 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  6. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, minced
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon black pepper and talt to taste
  8. Get 1 teaspoon sriracha hot sauce
  9. Get 2 tablespoons fresh grated romano cheese
  10. Take For Serving
  11. Prepare 1 recipe of sriracha hollindaise sauce, found in my profile and in general search
Instructions to make Grilled Fresh Broccoli:
  1. Make basting sauce
  2. In a bowl combine melted butter, olive oil, lemon and lime juice, garlic, srirahca, pepper, salt snd romano, stir to blend
  3. Cut woody ends of broccoli but keep heads intact
  4. Slice broccoli down center of stalk , all the way through. Each head will become two with one flat side.
  5. Blanch broccoli in either chicken broth or salted water for 2 minutes
  6. Brush with basting sauce all over, if you have time place broccoli in a ziplock bag with the basting ssuce and marinate it, refrigerated up to overnight
  7. Grill over direct hest untl brown in spots and tender
  8. Serve with sriracha hollindaise sauce
