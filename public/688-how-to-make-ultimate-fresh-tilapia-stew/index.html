The ingredients needed to make Fresh Tilapia stew:
  1. Take 1 medium size fresh fish
  2. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  3. Prepare 1 medium size Onion
  4. Take 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  5. Get 1 clove garlic
  6. Get to taste Salt

Instructions to make Fresh Tilapia stew:
  1. First boil the fish after dividing it into 2 equal parts for about 1 hr with 2 cups of water added
  2. Take a pan and add the cooking oil then the chopped onions until the color is light brown then add garlic, tomatoes then cover with lid for tomatoes to soften
  3. When ready you can now add the fish with the remaining soup it has inside the pan, then stir slowly with salt to taste, cover again for about 10 minutes
  4. Your fish is now ready to serve with hot brown or white ugali
