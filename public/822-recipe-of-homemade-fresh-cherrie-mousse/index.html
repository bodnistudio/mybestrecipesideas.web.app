The ingredients needed to make Fresh Cherrie Mousse:
  1. Prepare 1 cup fresh cold cherrie juice, see attached recipe in direction step #2
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup boiling water
  3. Get 1 small box black cherry jello
  4. Get 1 3/4 cup cold heavy whipping cream
  5. Take 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
  6. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp pure cherry extract
  8. Take GARNISH
  9. Get whipped cream
  10. Get 1/4 cup shaved dark chocolate
  11. Take 6 fresh cherries
Instructions to make Fresh Cherrie Mousse:
  1. Dissolve jello powder in boiling water, stirring to be be sure it all dissoves
  2. Add chilled cherrie juice, recipe attached below and stir to combine. Chill in refigerator just until cold but not set - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/342477-fresh-cherrie-juice
  3. Whip cream until it thickens then add sugar and extracts, beat just until soft peaks begin to form
  4. Beat cold cherrie mixture into crem until combined and fthickened, it will continue to set in refigerator
  5. Spoon into serving glasses and Refigerate at least 4 to 6 hours to set uo
  6. Garnish with whipped cream, chocolate shavings and a cherry
