The ingredients needed to make Almond cookies:
  1. Make ready 200 gr oat flour
  2. Prepare 120 gr honey
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Get Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 10 gr extract of vanilla
  6. Get 1 tsp extract of amaretto
  7. Get 50 gr soya butter
  8. Get 5 almonds in pieces
  9. Take Extra almonds for the decoration
