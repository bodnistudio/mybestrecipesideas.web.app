Instructions to make Easy lazy strawberry cheesecake:
  1. Add the boiling water to your jello mix well until all dissolved. (You may microwave it till fully dissolved) then allow to cool down to room temprature
  2. Slice your swiss roll and add it to the bottom of a cheesecake pan
  3. Mix your whipping cream with cold water as instructed on the envelope.
  4. Add your cream cheese to the whipping cream and mix with an electric blender till fully mixed
  5. Add the jello to the cream-cheese mix and mix well
  6. Add sugar to preferred taste
  7. Pour over the sliced Swiss roll you may decorate it with fresh strawberries and let it to set in your fridge for an hour at least
