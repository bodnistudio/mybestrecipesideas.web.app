The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Bacon Gnocchi:
  1. Take 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts diced
  2. Get 16 oz package of gnocchi
  3. Get 2 cups mozzarella
  4. Get 6 slices bacon
  5. Take 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  6. Get 6 oz tomato paste
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup heavy cream
  8. Get 2 cloves garlic minced
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp paprika
  10. Get 1 tsp sugar

Instructions to make Chicken & Bacon Gnocchi:
  1. Put a pot of salted water on to boil for the gnocchi. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. In a large skillet cook bacon untill crisp and remove. Drain most of the bacon grease and put the garlic and paprika in, cook for 30 seconds and then add the chicken.
  3. Cook chicken through. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and sugar and simmer for 5 min.
  4. Meanwhile when the water boils add the gnocchi, when the gnocchi floats remove it with a slotted spoon and add to skillet.
  5. Remove from heat, add cream, stir together and put into a baking dish. Cover with mozzarella and bake for 10-15 mins or until cheese is golden.
  6. Sprinkle with parmesan and serve.
