Recipe of Ultimate Vegan Jackfruit BBQ

The ingredients needed to make Vegan Jackfruit BBQ:
  1. Take 1 Can Young Green Jackfruit Drained
  2. Prepare 1 Cup Smoky BBQ Sauce (Homemade BBQ Sauce Recipe Coming Soon)
  3. Make ready 1/2 of a Small Spanish Onion Diced
  4. Take 1/4 of a Red Bell Pepper Diced
  5. Prepare 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  6. Get 1 Tsp Chili Powder
  7. Take 1 1/2 Tsp Paprika
  8. Take 1/2 Tbsp Lime Juice
  9. Make ready 1/2 Cup Lager (The Cheaper the Better)
  10. Get 1 Very Small Pinch of Salt
  11. Get Black Pepper
  12. Take Olive Oil
  13. Prepare Hamburger Buns
Steps to make Vegan Jackfruit BBQ:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add in the diced onion and pepper and season with a very small pinch of salt and pepper. Remember that your BBQ sauce is very salty so you do not want to overdo it with the salt. It is just to get them to start sweating.
  3. Sauté the onion and pepper until the onions begin to turn translucent. When they do, dump in the drained jackfruit and lower the heat to medium-low.
  4. The jackfruit will likely come in large triangular chunks out of the can. Use a wooden spoon to break them apart as best you can. It might put up a fight, but keep at it! They will soften as you cook, but you want the pieces to be as small as possible.
  5. When the jackfruit is as small as it will go, add the garlic powder, chili powder, and paprika and mix until everything is coated with the seasoning.
  6. Allow to cook for 2-3 minutes and then de-glaze with your lime juice. Stir until the strong scent of lime dissapates and then pour in the lager.
  7. Turn the heat down to a simmer and allow the lager to cook off for about 5-10 minutes stirring occasionally to avoid sticking.
  8. As it cooks, the jackfruit will become easier and easier to further break apart with the spoon. Once it reaches the general consistency of pulled pork, add the BBQ sauce and mix in until the whole thing resembles a pan full of pulled pork BBQ.
  9. Allow to cook in the sauce for another 5 minutes or so before removing from the heat and serving on hamburger buns with a dollop of extra BBQ sauce on top.
