The ingredients needed to make Honey lime chicken:
  1. Prepare 8 chicken thighs bone in skin on
  2. Make ready chili powder
  3. Get cumin
  4. Make ready Salt
  5. Make ready Pepper
  6. Take Olive oil
  7. Get 1/2 cup honey
  8. Take 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  9. Take 2 limes 1 juiced 1 sliced
  10. Take 3 cloves garlic minced

Instructions to make Honey lime chicken:
  1. Pre heat oven to 375
  2. Line a baking pan with aluminum foil
  3. Sprinkle chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper on chicken and coat tops and bottoms of chicken thighs.
  4. Bake for about 1 hour till chicken is nice and brown and skin is crispy. Brush on a little olive oil about halfway through baking.
  5. In a heavy high sided skillet add honey, soy sauce, lime juice and garlic and a pinch of salt
  6. Bring mixture to a boil and add chicken and a few lime slices and spoon on sauce and cook till sauce is thickened and reduced and spoon on some more sauce till ready to serve.
