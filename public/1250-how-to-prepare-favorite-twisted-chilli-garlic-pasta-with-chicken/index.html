The ingredients needed to make Twisted Chilli Garlic Pasta with Chicken:
  1. Prepare 1 packet twisted macaroni (boiled)
  2. Get 200 gm chicken breast cubes
  3. Get 1 cup National chilli garlic ketchup
  4. Get 4 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Get 2 tbsp worstershire sauce(optional)
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Make ready to taste Black pepper powder
  8. Make ready 4-5 garlic cloves crushed
  9. Get 1/4 cup oil
  10. Get 1 capsicum julienne cut
  11. Make ready 1 carrot julienne cut
  12. Take 3/4 cup cabbage shredded
  13. Take 1/4 cup spring onions julienne cut
Steps to make Twisted Chilli Garlic Pasta with Chicken:
  1. In a non-stick wok add oil and crushed garlic. Sauté for 10 seconds and add the chicken cubes and sauté on medium high flame till chicken changes it's colour and leaves a little bit water. 1. Now add salt and black pepper powder to taste. Mix well. 1. Now add the sauces and mix well. Add the vegetables and leave the spring onions for end.
  2. Add boiled macaroni and mix everything well. You can check the salt and spices at this time, if you want you can add any thing. 1. Now turn off the flame and add spring onions on the top. Mix well or dish out as it is.
  3. Serve hot with drinks and fries.
