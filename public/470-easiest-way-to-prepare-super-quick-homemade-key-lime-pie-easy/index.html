The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie EASY:
  1. Make ready Crust
  2. Get 1.5 cups fine crushed graham crackers
  3. Take 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 6 TBS melted butter
  5. Make ready Filling
  6. Prepare 28 oz sweet condensed milk (2 cans)
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup sour cream
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup key lime juice
  9. Get Zest of 2 Limes or 4 Key Limes
  10. Get Whipped cream topping
  11. Take 1 cup heavy cream
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  13. Get 1 tsp vanilla
