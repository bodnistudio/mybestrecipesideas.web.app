The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef and Cabbage:
  1. Prepare 1 Corned Beef brisket (with the spice packet)
  2. Make ready 1 (12 oz) Guinness black lager beer
  3. Prepare 2.5 cups Water
  4. Get 1 Onion sliced
  5. Make ready 6 Garlic cloves smashed
  6. Take Creole mustard
  7. Get 3 Tblsp butter
  8. Make ready Carrots
  9. Get Red potatoes (small)
  10. Get Green Cabbage sliced
  11. Prepare Thyme
  12. Prepare Bayleaves
  13. Take Whole grain mustard dipping sauce (optional)
Instructions to make Corned Beef and Cabbage:
  1. Place the onion, garlic, and butter in the pot
  2. Place your brisket (fat side down) on top of that with the lifting handles
  3. Rub Creole mustard all over the top of brisket and add the spice packet
  4. Next pour your beer and water in the sides (I only had Coors light)
  5. Close the pot and make sure you put the valve in the sealing position and set it for 120 minutes on pressure cook (I had a 4.10 pound brisket)
  6. Once the brisket is done lift it out of the pot to rest and cover with foil
  7. Next add in your potatoes, carrots, cabbage, bay leaves and thyme, close the lid and set on pressure cook for 3 minutes
