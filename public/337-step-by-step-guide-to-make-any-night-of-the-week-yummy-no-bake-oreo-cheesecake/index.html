The ingredients needed to make Yummy No Bake Oreo Cheesecake:
  1. Take 500 g cream cheese
  2. Take 40 g sugar
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup orange huice
  4. Take 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  5. Get 2 cups whipping cream
  6. Get 40 g sugar
  7. Make ready 2 Oreos (crushed)
  8. Make ready 4 tablespoons melted butter
  9. Take 20 g gelatin
Instructions to make Yummy No Bake Oreo Cheesecake:
  1. Mix your crushed Oreos with melted butter (till it looks like wet sand).
  2. Press the mixture into a lined springform pan using a wooden spatula to form a base and set aside.
  3. Whip the cream with 40g sugar on high speed till it reaches stiff peak and set aside.
  4. Bloom the gelatin and let cool slightly.
  5. Pour the gelatin into the cream cheese mixture and combine well.
  6. Pour the mixture over the base in the spring form pan and put in your freezer for about 2-3 hours or till it sets.
  7. Mix the cream cheese, lemon juice, orange juice and 40g sugar till smooth.
  8. Fold in about half of the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture.
  9. Cut into slices and serve.
