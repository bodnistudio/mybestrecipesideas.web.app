The ingredients needed to make Quick Can-Asian Noodles:
  1. Get 1 pkg Fresh chow mein noodles
  2. Take 1 medium red bell pepper, sliced thin
  3. Make ready 1 large carrot, shaved or sliced thin
  4. Get 1 zucchini, sliced thin
  5. Make ready 2 chicken breast, sliced or diced (seasoned with basil, S&P)
  6. Prepare 2 green onions, chopped
  7. Get Sauce
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup soya sauce
  9. Prepare Just shy of 1/4 chicken stock
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp water
  11. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  12. Get 2 tsp hoisin
  13. Take 1 tsp sriracha
  14. Make ready 1/2 a green onion that you chopped for above recipe

Steps to make Quick Can-Asian Noodles:
  1. Boil noodles super quick… drop in boiling water for 2-3min then remove and set aside
  2. In screaming hot wok, add 1 tbsp olive oil and brown/cook chicken
  3. Add all your vegetables and stirfry for 2 min. Add 1/4 of your sauce to the veg.
  4. Add the noodles, then pour remaining sauce over everything and toss together.
  5. Serve immediately with diced green onions for garnish♡ and squeeze of lime
