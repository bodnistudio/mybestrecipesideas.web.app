The ingredients needed to make White chocolate cheesecake w/ chocolate crust:
  1. Make ready 2 packages cream cheese (16oz)
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 8 oz white chocolate chips
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  7. Get 1 small box chocolate Graham crackers

Instructions to make White chocolate cheesecake w/ chocolate crust:
  1. Pre heat oven 350°.
  2. Crunch up Graham crackers until all are crumbs. (regular Graham crackers work too)
  3. Melt butter and combine with crumbs. Press evenly on bottom of pan. (9in cake pan works perfectly)
  4. Bake crust 10minutes and set aside.
  5. Mix cream cheese and sugar with mixer. Add vanilla extract.
  6. Stir eggs in one at a time.
  7. Melt chocolate and mix together with cheese cake batter.
  8. Pour over crust and bake 25-30minutes.
  9. Let cool and serve :)
