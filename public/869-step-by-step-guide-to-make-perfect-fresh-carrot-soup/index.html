The ingredients needed to make Fresh Carrot soup:
  1. Get 5 lb fresh carrots
  2. Make ready 2 stick Celery
  3. Take 5 each medium white onions
  4. Prepare 1 gallon vegetable broth
  5. Make ready 3 clove garlic cloves
  6. Prepare 1 bunch fresh basil
  7. Get 1 tsp dill weed
  8. Get 1 tsp rosemary
  9. Take 1 tsp oregano
  10. Prepare 1 tsp ground cumin
  11. Take 1/2 tsp curry powder
  12. Make ready 2 tsp paprika
  13. Make ready ground black pepper
  14. Take 2 bay leaf
  15. Prepare 2 cup almond milk
  16. Make ready piece cilantro

Instructions to make Fresh Carrot soup:
  1. Cut carrots, onions and celery
  2. Saute carrots, onions and celery till soft or translucent with 3 tablespoons of watter.
  3. Add the vegetables to the pot and add the vegetable broth
  4. Add the rest of ingredients except milk and cilantro and bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 25 minutes or till tender.
  5. Add the milk and boil for 1 min
  6. Add to food processor or blender until smooth. Enjoy garnish with cilantro
