The ingredients needed to make Oreo Cheesecake non bake:
  1. Get For the Oreo Base
  2. Take 2 box Oreo's (crushed to crumble)
  3. Get 4 tsp butter, melted
  4. Prepare Top cream
  5. Get 250 grams Philadelphia cream cheese (room temp)
  6. Prepare 300 ml whipping cream
  7. Get 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  8. Prepare 2 envelope gelatine
  9. Prepare 60 ml hot water
  10. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 1 milk chocolate, finely grated (to serve)
Steps to make Oreo Cheesecake non bake:
  1. Set aside a cheesecake tin…
  2. In a food processor add the Oreo biscuits and process till crumbled….. add melted butter…. mix in to combined.
  3. Mix should hold together when a handful clumps together when squeezed in palm of hand…. if it seems to dry add more butter… very little amounts at a time otherwise it may become to greasy.
  4. Press mixture into the cheesecake tin base firmly… set aside in fridge.
  5. In a large mixing bowl cream Philadelphia cream cheese with electric beaters until soft. (room temp makes this easier)
  6. Add the can of condensed milk and continue beating with electric mixer, slowly add in the whipping cream… keep beating until thickened…
  7. Dissolve gelatine in hot water…. beat into cream mix
  8. Finally beat in vanilla extract making sure everything is evenly combined..
  9. Poor the cream mix on top of the earlier prepared Oreo base… cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until set… for best results over night…
  10. Carefully remove from cheesecake tin onto a serving plate… I like to finish it off with finely grated milk chocolate….. enjoy ?
