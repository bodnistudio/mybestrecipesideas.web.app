The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Caesar Chicken:
  1. Take 3 lbs Bone in Chicken Thighs
  2. Take 2 tablespoons Canola Oil
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  4. Take 18 oz bottle of Caesar Dressing
  5. Take 1/2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
  6. Prepare 1 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup Milk
  8. Get 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Shredded Mild Cheddar Jack Cheese
  10. Take 1 Tbsp Parsley flakes-dried
  11. Take 1 1/2 Tbsp Onion Powder
  12. Get 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  13. Get 1/2 Tbsp Oregano-dried
  14. Take 1/2 Tbsp Basil -dried
  15. Take 1 Tbsp Pepper
  16. Take In Baking Dish:
  17. Take 2 Tbsp Pepper
  18. Prepare 2 Tbsp Salt
  19. Make ready 1 cup Canola Oil - about
Instructions to make Cheesy Caesar Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. In 13x9 baking dish add enough Canola Oil to just cover the bottom of the pan. Add in the 2 Tbsp Salt & Pepper & swirl around. Remove the skin from the chicken& place in dish rolling the chicken in the oil mixture to ensure each chicken piece is covered in the oil, salt & pepper.
  3. In a mixing bowl mix in all the ingredients but the cheese. Whisk until combined. Pour over the chicken. Cover with foil.
  4. Bake the chicken in a 425°F oven for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, remove the foil sprinkling 1/2 cup of the cheese directly onto the chicken. Increase oven temperature to 450°F.
  5. Continue cooking at 450°F until chicken is done (about another 30 minutes). When finished sprinkle the rest of the cheese directly onto the chicken. Should serve 4-6.
