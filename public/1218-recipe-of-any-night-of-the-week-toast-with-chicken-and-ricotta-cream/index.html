Steps to make Toast with chicken and ricotta creamðŸ’š:
  1. For the shallot oil and fried shallots sliced thinly shallots and put them in microwave-proof form and add oil. Cook on the fool power for 6-8 minutes. Start check on it after 6 minutes. Then strain oil in another cup and keep the onions for the serving.
  2. For the ricotta cream just mix ricotta,peas,zest and juice of lemon,shallot oil,mint,salt and pepper on taste. Mash everything with potato masher or fork.
  3. For the dressing mix shallot oil,lemon juice,mustard and salt/pepper on taste. Whisk everything.
  4. Add salt and pepper on chicken breast from both sides and brush with shallot oil. Fry on grill pan or grill or normal pan for 4-5 minutes from both sides. Then cut in pieces.
  5. Bread toast on dry pan 2-3 minutes from both sides, also brush a little bit with shallot oil.
  6. For serving put cream on bread then add a little bit of sauce, then put chicken slices ; again put the sauce and then fried onions on top. Enjoy!
