Instructions to make Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. In a bowl, cream butter, peanut butter and sugars.
  2. Beat in eggs.
  3. Sift the dry ingredients and stir into butter mixture. Put dough in fridge for an hour.
  4. Roll dough into small balls and place on baking sheets. flattening each with a fork. Making criss cross.
  5. Bake for 10mins at 180. Cool before serving!
