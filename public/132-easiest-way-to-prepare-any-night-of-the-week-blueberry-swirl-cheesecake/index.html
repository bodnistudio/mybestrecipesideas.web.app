The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Swirl Cheesecake:
  1. Get For the Crust
  2. Get 2 cups grahm cracker crumbs
  3. Take 6 tablespoon melted butter
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  5. Get For Blueberrie swirl
  6. Take 2 cups fresh blueberries, or frozen if fresh are not available
  7. Prepare 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  8. Get 1 tablesppon water
  9. Take 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  10. Get 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  11. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon salt
  12. Prepare For Cheesecake
  13. Get 3-8 ounce pacages of cream cheese, at room temperature
  14. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  15. Take 1 cup sour cream, at room temperature
  16. Take 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  17. Take 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  18. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon salt
  19. Take 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  20. Prepare Garnish
  21. Take fresh whipped cream and fresh blueberries
Steps to make Blueberry Swirl Cheesecake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Spray a 9 inch springform oan with bakers spray. Have a baking sheet ready to hold cheesecake covered with foil
  2. Make Blueberry swirl sauce
  3. Combine water, lemon juice and cornstarch in a small bowl until smooth
  4. In a saucepan heat blueberries, sugar and salt, cook over low heat until blueberries begin to soften and become juicy
  5. Add the cornstarch mixture and smash most of the bleberrieswith a spoon and cook until thickened about 5 minuted, pour into a bowl and cool to room temperature
  7. Make crust
  8. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar and melted butter in a bowl until moistened
  9. Press into bottom and sides of springform oan and freeze while preparing filling
  10. Make Cheesecake Filling
  11. In a large bowl beat cream cheese until smooth
  12. Add sugar and beat until light and fluffy, beat in lemon, salt and vanilla
  13. Add eggs one at a time beating in after each egg
  14. Stir in sour cream until well combined
  15. Pour into prepared pan
  16. Drop spoonfulls of Blueberry sauce on batter
  17. Swirl sauce by cutting through batter with a skewer or thin knife
  18. Place cheesecake on foil lined pan and bake for 50 to 60 minutes until just slightly jiggly in center. Turn oven off and let cheesecake sit in oven with the door closed for 1 hour. The remove to a rack and cool completely before covering and refrigerating overnight
  20. Remove sides of springform pan
  22. Garnish with whipped cream and fresh blueberries
