Steps to make Cheesecake in Chef:
  1. Put the cream cheese into the mixing bowl.
  2. Put the sour cream into the mixing bowl.
  3. Put the white sugar into the mixing bowl.
  4. Put the yogurt into the mixing bowl.
  5. Put the unsalted butter into the mixing bowl.
  6. Combine the milk.
  7. Put the cake flour into the mixing bowl.
  8. Combine the corn starch.
  9. Put the brown sugar into the mixing bowl.
  10. Combine the egg whites.
  11. Combine the egg yolks.
  12. Put the lemon juice into the mixing bowl.
  13. Stir for 7 minutes.
  14. Liquify the contents of the mixing bowl.
  15. Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the baking dish.
  16. Bake the cake mixture.
  17. Watch the cake mixture until baked.
