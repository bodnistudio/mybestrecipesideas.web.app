The ingredients needed to make Pistacio and Basbusa Cheese Cake:
  1. Get Biscuit mix:
  2. Prepare 1 pck tea biscuit/digestive biscuit
  3. Take 2 tblspn nestle cream
  4. Prepare 50 g butter
  5. Take Cream mix:
  6. Prepare 1 can yogurt
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Get 1 cup powdered milk
  9. Make ready 4 pcs kiri square cheese
  10. Make ready 1 tspn vanilla
  11. Take 1 can nestle cream
  12. Get Basbusa mix:
  13. Take 1/4 cup powdered milk
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  15. Get 1/4 cup oil
  16. Get 1/4 cup pistacio nut powder
  17. Prepare 1/2 cup semolina
  18. Take 1/2 cup nestle cream
  19. Prepare 1 egg
Instructions to make Pistacio and Basbusa Cheese Cake:
  1. Mix the biscuit,nestle cream and butter in bowl. Then put in moulding pan. Press the biscuit good in the pan. Then put in freezer for a minute to become cold.
  2. Put all the ingredients of cream mix in blender then blend it good before putting up the biscuit.. then put in over for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix all the ingredients of basbusa mixture then put the top of cream. Then put inside the oven for only 20 minutes.
  4. If finish put in freezer for an hour to become cold. Before serve put pistacio nut powder at the top then put rose flower for design. Then serve.
