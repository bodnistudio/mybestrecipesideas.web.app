The ingredients needed to make Brad's steak roll up's with heirloom tomato relish:
  1. Prepare For the steak
  2. Take 1 1/2 lbs top round steak, sliced very thin
  3. Get Ricotta cheese
  4. Take Hard salami, deli sliced
  5. Get Sliced American cheese
  6. Prepare 1 lb bacon
  7. Make ready Sea salt, black pepper, and garlic powder
  8. Prepare For the relish
  9. Take 2 lbs heirloom tomatoes, diced

  11. Get 1/2 red bell pepper, diced

  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp celery seed
  14. Take 1 tsp minced garlic
  15. Get 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  16. Make ready 1 tbs brown sugar
  17. Make ready 1 tsp kosher salt
  18. Get 1/8 tsp ea; cinnamon, cloves nutmeg, allspice,

Instructions to make Brad's steak roll up's with heirloom tomato relish:
  1. Mix all ingredients for the relish and let sit in the fridge until dinner is ready.
  2. Lay the thin strips of steak out on a cutting board. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread around 1 1/2 tbs ricotta out on all add 2 slices of salami, and 1 slice of American cheese. Tightly roll the steaks up.
  4. Wrap with a slice of chilled bacon.
  5. Place on a preheated grill and cook 90-120 seconds on all 4 sides. Remove from grill and let rest 3 minutes.
  6. Serve with tomato relish on top. Enjoy.
