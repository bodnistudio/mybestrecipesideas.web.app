The ingredients needed to make Peshawari Chicken Chapli Kabab:
  1. Get Chapli Kabab Masala
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp whole coriander seeds
  3. Get 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
  4. Prepare 3 dried red chillies
  5. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  7. Make ready 3 tsp powdered dried pomegranate seeds
  8. Take 1 tbsp aamchur(dry mango) powder
  9. Get Kabab
  10. Prepare 1 kg boneless chicken minced
  11. Make ready 1 onion finely chopped
  12. Make ready 1 tomato finely chopped
  13. Prepare 1 egg raw
  14. Make ready 1 egg scrambled
  15. Get 1 green chilli finely chopped
  16. Get 3 tbsp coriander leaves finely chopped
  17. Make ready 3 tbsp besan/gram flour
  18. Make ready 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  19. Prepare to taste Salt
  20. Prepare 5 Tomato round slices
  21. Prepare 3 tbsp refined oil
Steps to make Peshawari Chicken Chapli Kabab:
  1. Dry roast coriander seeds, cumin and red chillies and grind them in a mixer
  2. Mix the ground spices, pepper, pomegranate powder, amchur, garam masala to make the Kabab masala
  3. Add salt, Kabab masala, scrambled egg, raw egg, onion, ginger garlic paste, green chillies, besan and coriander leaves to the minced chicken and mix well.
  4. Keep the mix in the refrigerator for an hour. After one hour, Mix the chopped tomatoes lightly to the Kabab mix.
  5. Make patties and shallow fry in oil with tomato slices placed on top of the patties. Flip them and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney!
