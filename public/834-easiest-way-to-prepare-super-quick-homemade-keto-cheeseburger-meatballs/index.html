The ingredients needed to make Keto Cheeseburger Meatballs:
  1. Make ready 1.5 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup almond flour
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cups Mexican blend shredded cheese
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  6. Get 1 TBS Worcestershire sauce
  7. Prepare Optional additional ingredients
  8. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Take 3 TBS ketchup (sugar free for keto)
  10. Make ready 1 TBS Dijon or yellow mustard
  11. Make ready 3 TBS sweet or dill relish
  12. Get Dipping sauce
Instructions to make Keto Cheeseburger Meatballs:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Add all ingredients into large bowl and mix together evenly
  3. Roll into 2" balls and place on parchment lined baking sheet. Insert toothpick into each for easier removal and eating
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is golden
