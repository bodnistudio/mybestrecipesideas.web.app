Steps to make Keto cabbage pizza:
  1. Place the cabbage, green pepper and garlic in a food processor and grate until fine.
  2. Remove and drain the water out with a clean kitchen cotton towel
  3. Place in a bowl and add the eggs, parmesan cheese, baking powder, chilli and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Divide mixture in two. Place in a rectangular or round microwavable turperware and microwave on high for about six minutes or until firm
  5. On a stove top, place a nonstick pan with a tablespoon of coconut oil. Place the firm pizza and fry for two minutes on each side
  6. Top with your desired toppings and cheese
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
