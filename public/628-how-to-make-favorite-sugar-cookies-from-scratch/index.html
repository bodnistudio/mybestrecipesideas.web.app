The ingredients needed to make Sugar cookies from scratch:
  1. Take 2 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 cup butter softened
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla
