The ingredients needed to make Fresh Cherry No Bake Cheesecake:
  1. Make ready For Crust
  2. Get 2 cups crushed oreo cookies
  3. Take 5 tablespoons butter melted
  4. Get For Cheesecake Filling
  5. Prepare 16 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  6. Get 1 cup granulated sugat
  7. Make ready 1 large 6 ounce box of black cherry gelatin
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cups boiling water
  9. Prepare 6-8 punces fresh or frozen thawed cherries
  10. Prepare 1 teapoon vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup mini chocolate chios
  13. Get For Chocolate Ganache Topping
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  16. Prepare For Garnish w
  17. Take white chocolate shavings
  18. Prepare whole cherries
  19. Make ready fresh whipped cream
Instructions to make Fresh Cherry No Bake Cheesecake:
  1. Make Crust
  2. Combine cookie crumbs and butter in a bowl
  3. Press into a 9 inch non stick sprayed springform pan, freeze while preparing filling
  4. In a bowl dissolve gelatin in boiling water, refrigerate just until cold but mot setting up
  5. Chop 1/2 cup of the cherries, pitted
  6. In a bowl beat cream cheese with sugar and vanilla until smooth
  7. In another bowl beat cream until it holds its shape
  8. Beat cold gelatin into cream cheese mixture until blended
  9. Fold in whipped cream chopped cherries and mini chips
  10. Pour into prepated crust and smooth top, refrigerate at least 6 to 8 hours until set
  11. Make Chocolate ganache Topping
  12. Heat cream to a simmer in the microwave or stovetop. Pour over chovolate chips in a bowl, let sit 1 minute then stir smooth
  13. Remove sides from springform pam
  14. Let chocolate sit until a thick but still able to pour, drizzle over set cheesecake. Refrigerate until set
  15. Garnish each slice with whipped cream and a cherry
