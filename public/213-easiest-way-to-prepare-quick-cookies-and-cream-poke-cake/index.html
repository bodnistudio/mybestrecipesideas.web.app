The ingredients needed to make Cookies and cream poke cake:
  1. Prepare 1 box chocolate cake mix
  2. Get 1 box cookie and cream pudding 4.2 oz big box
  3. Get 1 1/2 cups milk
  4. Take 1 cup chocolate cookie crushed (or more cookie divided optional)
  5. Prepare 8 oz whipped topping (divided optional)
  6. Make ready 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk (optional)

Steps to make Cookies and cream poke cake:
  1. Make cake to box Directions.
  2. Cool cake completely, then use a round spoon handle to poke holes all over the cake about an inch apart.
  3. In large bowl add putting mix and milk whisk until no lumps fold in whipped topping and cookie crumbs
  4. Pour sweet condensed milk evenly over cake and into the holes then top with pudding mixture evenly…. sprinkle extra crushed cookies on the top.
  5. Refrigerate up to 3 hours or overnight
  6. Notes- I divided the whipped topping in half, with the pudding mixture, after adding to the cake I then added the rest of the whipped topping and more crushed cookies on top of that… I didn’t use the sweetened condensed milk, I thought it be to sweet so I skipped that part….
