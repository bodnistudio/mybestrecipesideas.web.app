The ingredients needed to make Slow cooked corned beef with creamy mash:
  1. Take 1 large corned silverside.
  2. Take 6 clove garlic
  3. Make ready 1 dozen peppercorns
  4. Prepare 1 dash malt vinegar
  5. Get 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp golden syrup
  7. Get 1 pinch salt

  9. Prepare 8 large potatoes
  10. Make ready 2 tsp dried chives
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp sour cream
  12. Take 1 tbsp butter
  13. Make ready 1 pinch salt and pepper to season

Instructions to make Slow cooked corned beef with creamy mash:
  1. Rinse corned silverside under tap, place in slow cooker and fill with enough water to just cover meat
  2. Add peppercorns, salt, sliced garlic, dash of vinegar, golden syrup and brown sugar to the water.
  3. Turn slow cooker on high, leave for at least 8.
  4. Serve with salad or corn and mashed potatoes.
  5. *Mashed potatoes. Peel and boil potatoes
  6. Mash with sour cream, butter, chives and salt and pepper
