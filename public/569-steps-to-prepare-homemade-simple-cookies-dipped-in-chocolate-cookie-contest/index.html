Steps to make Simple cookies dipped in chocolate #cookie contest:
  1. Assemble and weigh your ingredients
  2. Sieve flour in a bowl add sugar and mix
  3. Add margarine or butter to the flour mixture and rub in
  4. Add milk little at a time as u knead until u get a not so hard dough
  5. Wrap with cling film n refridgerate for 10 minutes
  6. Shape as desired and bake in a preheated oven at 180degrees until they brown
  7. Prepare your chocolate ganache by placing the chocolate bar in a bowl add heavy cream place the bowl in a sufuria with boiling water allow it to melt as u stir
  8. When the cookies are ready dip half the cookie in the chocolate ganache and set them on a rack to dry
  9. Add your sprinkles and enjoy
