The ingredients needed to make Chicken and tomato lasagne with bechamel sauce:
  1. Make ready 9 sheets lasagne
  2. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 1 onion, finely chopped
  4. Prepare 1 garlic clove, crushed
  5. Take 3 1/2 oz mushrooms wiped and sliced
  6. Prepare 12 oz chicken, cut into chunks
  7. Prepare 2/3 cup red wine, diluted with 1/3 cup water
  8. Prepare 1 cup sieved tomatos
  9. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  10. Get Bechamel sauce
  11. Prepare 5 tbsp butter
  12. Make ready 1/3 cup flour
  13. Get 2 1/2 cups milk
  14. Get 1 egg, beaten
  15. Prepare 1 cup cheese (or more if you love cheese)
  16. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Chicken and tomato lasagne with bechamel sauce:
  1. Boil lasagne sheets until ready. Heat the oil, add the onions and garlic,cook for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the mushrooms and chicken and cook until the meat browns.
  3. Add the wine, bring to boil and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomatoes and sugar and cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Bechamel sauce: melt butter, stir in flour cook for 2 minutes, remove from heat, gradually add in the milk, boil until thickened, cool slightly and add the egg and half the cheese, salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Arrange in a baking dish and sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Bake until done.
