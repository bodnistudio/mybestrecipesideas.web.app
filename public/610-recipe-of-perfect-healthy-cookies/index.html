  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoon milk
  6. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Get chocolate as topping

Steps to make Healthy cookies:
  1. Preheat microwave to 150c.Grease baking tray and keep aside.
  2. Get a clean bowl,sieve wheat flour,add baking powder,sugar,milk,vanilla,grate nut and add mix very well.
  3. Add butter mix, break egg and mix too.Add little water and mix correctly it will form a soft dough.
  4. Cut small portion and roll on a flat surface with rolling pin,then cut to desired shape.
  5. Place on the oiled tray reduce temp to 100c then place in the microwave bake for 10min.
  6. Continue process until exhausted.Allow to cool then add chocolate at surface.Serve in a clean plate so tasty.
