The ingredients needed to make 3 - Step Coca Cola Chicken 🐔 Strips:
  1. Take 1 large pack chicken strips, or chicken breast cut into strips
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 can cola
  4. Take 1/2 cup ketchup
  5. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Get 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  7. Take 1/4 teaspoon adobo
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  9. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  10. Get 1 onion, sliced
Instructions to make 3 - Step Coca Cola Chicken 🐔 Strips:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken strips……Set to the side……
  2. In a large pot combine your olive oil, cola, ketchup, black pepper, crushed red pepper, adobo, oregano, minced garlic and sliced onions, bring to a boil for about 2 minutes…….
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add in your chicken strips, stirring to evenly coat all strips…….continue to cook and stir until chicken is completely cooked…….at this point you may set your heat from medium to high heat……..Serve with your favorite side dish and enjoy 😉!
