Steps to make Chicken in a can:
  1. Punch holes around the bottom of the can ( approx. 1" from the bottom and 1" apart.
  2. Add chicken into coffee can, legs up. Its okay if legs stick out a little. Place on top of charcoal.
  3. Place the charcoal in the campfire in selected area.
  4. Let cook for approx 3 hours. After cooking the chicken will fall right off the bone ;) good eating!
