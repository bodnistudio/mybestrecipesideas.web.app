The ingredients needed to make Chili Chicken Rice Casserole:
  1. Take 2 cups cooked rice
  2. Get 2 boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
  3. Get 2-3 Tbsp olive oil
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Chili powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
  6. Get 1 Tbsp Garlic powder
  7. Get 1 Tbsp Onion powder
  8. Take 1 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp pepper
  10. Prepare 1 can Rotel Chili Fixings
  11. Make ready 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
  12. Make ready 1 small can Cream Style Corn
  13. Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  14. Take 1 tbsp dried cilantro
  15. Make ready 6-9 slices pepper jack cheese
  16. Make ready 1-1 1/2 cups fried onions
Steps to make Chili Chicken Rice Casserole:
  1. Cut up chicken into pieces and toss with all the spices.
  2. Add oil to pan and heat on medium high heat. Add chicken and brown on all sides. While on medium high add in the Rotel to help deglaze the pan tossing all together.
  3. Add in the cream of chicken soup and cream style corn. Stir to combine.
  4. Add in the cilantro and stir to combine.
  5. Add in the shredded cheese. Stir to combine.
  6. Add in the cooked rice, stir to combine.
  7. Add to casserole dish and layer top with jalapeño pepper jack cheese. Preheat oven to 350.
  8. Top with the fried onions. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes until bubbly.
  9. Enjoy! Chili cheesy crunch flavor!
