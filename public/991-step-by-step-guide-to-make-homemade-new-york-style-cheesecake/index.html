The ingredients needed to make New York style Cheesecake:
  1. Take Crust
  2. Make ready 100 g crackers
  3. Get 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  4. Get 1/8 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 57 g melted butter
  6. Prepare Cheese filling
  7. Prepare 340 g cream cheese
  8. Get 10 Tbsp sugar
  9. Prepare 2 large eggs
  10. Get 1/2 large yolk
  11. Get 3/4 cup sour cream
  12. Get 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  13. Prepare 2 g vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions to make New York style Cheesecake:
  1. Crush the crackers in a food processor. Add in the sugar and salt and mix them.
  2. Melt the butter and add it into the food processor for everything to be well combined.
  3. Grease up the pan and cover the bottom and the sides with parchment paper.
  4. Pour in ur crush mixture and press it down into the pan using the bottom of a cup and smooth out the edges with the tip of a spoon
  5. Set ur oven to prehead at 175°C
  6. Grab a bowl, add in ur softened cream cheese and sugar. Cream the cheese and sugar mixture using an electric mixer.
  7. Pour the sour cream, lemon juice and vanilla extract and mix
  8. Add in the eggs one at a time and mix well between each step and then add the ½yolk and mix
  9. Scrape the bowl to make sure everything is well combined
  10. Pour the mixture over ur crust and then tap the pan over the counter to get rid of bubbles
  11. Back at 165° for an hour
  12. After it's all cooled down, cover it in plastic wrap and put it in the fridge for 24 hours to set completely before eating
